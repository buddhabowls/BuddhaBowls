## Matcha Green Tea Overnight Oats

This recipe is my go-to when I need a little extra pep in my step. It’s like having your matcha and breakfast all in one, and who doesn’t love that?

To make these oats, I mix rolled oats with almond milk and a teaspoon of matcha powder. After a thorough stir, I let it hang out in the fridge overnight, soaking up all that matcha goodness.

When morning rolls around, I serve it up in a coconut bowl (because it just feels right) and top it with fresh kiwi slices and shredded coconut.

## Instructions

- Mix oats, almond milk, and matcha powder. Refrigerate overnight. Top with fresh kiwi slices and shredded coconut.
