## **How to Make Homemade Pot Brownies: A Friendly Guide**

If you’re looking for a delicious and fun way to enjoy your cannabis, homemade pot brownies are a classic choice. These tasty treats are easy to make, and with the right recipe, you can create a batch that’s perfectly tailored to your preferences. Whether you’re a seasoned baker or a newbie in the kitchen, this guide will walk you through the process step-by-step.

## Why Pot Brownies?

Pot brownies have been a staple in cannabis culture for decades, and for good reason. They’re discreet, portable, and provide a longer-lasting experience compared to smoking or vaping. Plus, who doesn’t love the rich, chocolatey goodness of a brownie?

Before we dive into the recipe, it’s important to note that consuming edibles can have a stronger and more prolonged effect than other methods of cannabis consumption. Start with a small portion and wait at least 1-2 hours before deciding if you need more. Always consume responsibly!

### What You’ll Need

To make pot brownies, you’ll need a few key ingredients and tools. Here’s what you’ll need to get started:

#### Ingredients:

- 1/2 cup (1 stick) of cannabis-infused butter (cannabutter) or cannabis-infused oil

- 1 cup granulated sugar

- 2 large eggs

- 1 teaspoon [Vanilla Extract](https://amzn.to/4aIhdue)

- 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder

- 1/2 cup all-purpose flour

- 1/4 teaspoon salt

- 1/4 teaspoon baking powder

#### Tools:

- Mixing bowls

- Whisk or hand mixer

- Spatula

- 8×8-inch baking pan

- Parchment paper (optional)

- Oven

### Step-by-Step Instructions

#### Step 1: Prepare Your Cannabutter or Cannabis Oil

The key ingredient in pot brownies is cannabis-infused butter or oil. If you haven’t made this yet, don’t worry—it’s simple! Here’s a quick overview:

- **Decarboxylate Your Cannabis**: Preheat your oven to 240°F (115°C). Spread your ground cannabis evenly on a baking sheet and bake for about 30-40 minutes. This process activates the THC or CBD in the cannabis.

- **Infuse the Butter or Oil**: In a saucepan, melt your butter or heat your oil on low. Add the decarboxylated cannabis and simmer for 2-3 hours, stirring occasionally. Strain the mixture through a cheesecloth to remove plant material, and your cannabutter or oil is ready!

If you’re short on time, you can purchase pre-made cannabutter or infused oil from a dispensary.

#### Step 2: Preheat Your Oven

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease your baking pan or line it with parchment paper for easy removal later.

#### Step 3: Mix Wet Ingredients

In a large mixing bowl, combine your cannabutter (or oil) and sugar. Mix until smooth and creamy. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the [Vanilla Extract](https://amzn.to/4aIhdue).

#### Step 4: Add Dry Ingredients

In a separate bowl, whisk together the cocoa powder, flour, salt, and baking powder. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, stirring until just combined. Be careful not to overmix!

#### Step 5: Pour and Bake

Pour the brownie batter into your prepared baking pan, spreading it evenly with a spatula. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with just a few moist crumbs.

#### Step 6: Cool and Cut

Let the brownies cool completely in the pan before cutting them into squares. This step is crucial to ensure they hold their shape.

### Tips for Success

- **Start Low and Go Slow**: If you’re new to edibles, cut your brownies into smaller portions to control your dosage. Remember that it can take up to two hours to feel the effects.

- **Store Properly**: Keep your pot brownies in an airtight container in the fridge for up to a week, or freeze them for longer storage.

- **Label Clearly**: If you’re sharing your kitchen with others, make sure your brownies are clearly labeled as “infused” to avoid any accidental consumption.

### Enjoy Responsibly

Homemade pot brownies are a delightful way to enjoy cannabis, but always consume them responsibly. Avoid driving or operating heavy machinery after consuming edibles, and keep them out of reach of children and pets.

With this simple recipe and a little bit of patience, you’ll be well on your way to creating delicious homemade pot brownies that are sure to impress your friends—or just treat yourself! 

**Happy baking!**
