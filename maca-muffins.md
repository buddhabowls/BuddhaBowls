## Maca Muffins

**Course:** Dessert

**Servings:** 12 muffins

### Ingredients:

- 1 1/2 cups flour

- 1/2 cup sugar

- 1/2 cup mashed banana

- 1/4 cup milk

- 2 tbsp maca powder

- 1 egg

### Instructions:

- Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).

- Mix all ingredients until combined.

- Pour into muffin tins and bake for 20-25 minutes.
