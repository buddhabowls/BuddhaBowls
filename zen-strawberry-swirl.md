Find your zen with this refreshing Zen Strawberry Swirl! The creamy coconut yogurt pairs beautifully with strawberries, creating a soothing dessert perfect for any occasion. Enjoy it in a coconut bowl for tranquility!

## Zen Strawberry Swirl Recipe

Course: Dessert
Cuisine: Vegan
Servings: 2

## Ingredients

- 2 cups frozen strawberries,

- 1 cup coconut yogurt,

- 1 tbsp agave syrup,

- Toppings: fresh mint, coconut flakes

## Instructions

- Blend strawberries and coconut yogurt until creamy.

- Drizzle with agave syrup and top with mint and coconut flakes.

Add a splash of vanilla for extra flavor.
