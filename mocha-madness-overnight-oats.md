## Mocha Madness Overnight Oats

This recipe is my go-to when I need a little extra pep in my step. It’s like having your coffee and breakfast all in one, and who doesn’t love that?

To make these oats, I mix rolled oats with a strong brew of coffee, chocolate milk, and a dash of cocoa powder. After a thorough stir, I let it hang out in the fridge overnight, soaking up all that mocha goodness.

When morning rolls around, I serve it up in a coconut bowl (because it just feels right) and top it with chocolate shavings and a sprinkle of cocoa powder.

## 

**Course:** Breakfast

**Prep Time:** 5 mins

**Cook Time:** 0 mins

**Total Time:** 5 mins

**Servings:** 1

## Ingredients

- Rolled oats, coffee, chocolate milk, cocoa powder

## Instructions

- Stir oats, coffee, chocolate milk, and cocoa powder. Refrigerate overnight. Top with chocolate shavings and a sprinkle of cocoa powder.
