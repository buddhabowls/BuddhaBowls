Coconut Oil & Coffee Mask

♻️ Sustainable Ritual:
Uses upcycled coffee grounds
Biodegradable coconut bowl
100% plastic-free application

☕ Materials Needed:

  • 3 tbsp coffee grounds
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil
  • Coconut half-shell bowl
  • Bamboo spoon (optional)

🌿 Preparation Steps:

  1. Collect cooled coffee grounds from morning brew
  2. In coconut bowl, mix grounds with coconut oil
  3. Add coconut sugar if using for extra exfoliation
  4. Stir with bamboo spoon until paste forms
  5. Test on elbow before full application
  6. Massage onto damp skin using circular motions
  7. Rinse with warm water, pat dry

🌴 Bowl Care Tips:
• Clean with coarse salt scrub after use
• Dry upside down to prevent cracking
• Refresh monthly with coconut oil polish
