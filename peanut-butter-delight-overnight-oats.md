## Peanut Butter Delight Overnight Oats

This recipe is my ode to the creamy, nutty goodness that is peanut butter. It’s the perfect breakfast for those mornings when you need a little extra comfort and indulgence.

To make these oats, I mix rolled oats with almond milk, a generous dollop of peanut butter, and a splash of [Maple Syrup](https://amzn.to/3KUS5oa) for that hint of sweetness. After a thorough stir, it’s time to let the fridge work its magic overnight.

In the morning, I serve it up in a coconut bowl, because it just feels right, and top it with sliced bananas and a sprinkle of chopped peanuts for that irresistible crunch.

These Peanut Butter Delight Overnight Oats are like a warm hug in a bowl, and they never fail to make my mornings a little brighter. So go ahead, treat yourself to a breakfast that’s as nutty as you are!

## 

**Course:** Breakfast

**Prep Time:** 5 mins

**Cook Time:** 0 mins

**Total Time:** 5 mins

**Servings:** 1

## Ingredients

- Rolled oats, almond milk, peanut butter, [Maple Syrup](https://amzn.to/3KUS5oa)

## Instructions

- Combine oats, almond milk, peanut butter, and [Maple Syrup](https://amzn.to/3KUS5oa). Refrigerate overnight. Top with sliced bananas and chopped peanuts.
