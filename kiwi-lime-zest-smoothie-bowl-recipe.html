Kiwi Lime Zest Smoothie Bowl

Enjoy a refreshing and healthy Kiwi Lime Zest Smoothie Bowl, perfect for breakfast or a snack! This vibrant smoothie bowl is packed with nutrients and served in beautiful coconut bowls.

Key Ingredients

  • 2 ripe kiwis, peeled and chopped
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 1 cup fresh spinach
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
  • Toppings: sliced kiwi, granola, coconut flakes

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine the chopped kiwis, lime juice, spinach, almond milk, and honey.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour the smoothie into coconut bowls.
  4. Top with sliced kiwi, granola, and coconut flakes as desired.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!
