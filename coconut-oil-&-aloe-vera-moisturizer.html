Tropical Biome Moisturizer

🌴 Lipid Phase Components

  • Coconut oil (45% lauric acid)
  • Macadamia seed oil
  • Vitamin E acetate

Function: Rebuilds ceramide matrix
Penetration depth: 80μm

🌿 Aqua Phase Components

  • Aloe barbadensis leaf gel
  • Hyaluronic acid (0.5%)
  • Panthenol (Pro-V B5)

Function: TEWL reduction
Hydration duration: 12h+

🔬 Formulation Protocol

  1. Prepare dual-phase emulsion:
    • Oil phase: Melt coconut oil (40°C)
    • Water phase: Whip aloe gel with glycerin
  2. Combine phases using coconut shell spatula
  3. Add actives in this order:
    1. Antioxidants
    2. Humectants
    3. Essential oils
  4. Store in blue glass (blocks 450nm light)
