Maca Oatmeal

Ingredients

  • 1 cup oats
  • 2 cups water or milk
  • 2 tbsp maca powder
  • Sweetener to taste (e.g., honey, maple syrup, or stevia)
  • Optional toppings: fresh fruits, nuts, or seeds

Instructions

  1. In a pot, bring the water or milk to a boil.
  2. Add the oats and reduce heat to a simmer.
  3. Stir in the maca powder and cook for about 5 minutes, or until the oats are soft.
  4. Remove from heat and let it sit for a minute.
  5. Sweeten to taste and add any optional toppings.
  6. Serve warm and enjoy!
