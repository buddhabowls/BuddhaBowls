## Pink Matcha Latte

Simply a layer of blended pink pitaya powder and milk on the bottom.
Then, coconut matcha cold foam is poured on top!

## INGREDIENTS:

**Your pink matcha latte shopping list:**

Coconut milk
Pink pitaya powder
Matcha green tea powder (optional)

## Pink Pitaya Powder

The powder version packs all of the same nutritional benefits as the fresh version with the added benefit of being shelf-stable for easy storage. It is loaded with antioxidants, fiber, and vitamin C to help support overall health. Plus, it tastes great too!

## Coconut Milk

You can use any kind of milk that you want! I used vanilla coconut milk in mine, which makes it totally dairy free.

## Pink Matcha Latte

Organic Dragon Fruit (Hylocereus Undatus) aka “Pink Matcha” grows in Central America and South East Asia. It is naturally pink and is bursting with vitamins and minerals. 

## Iced Matcha Pink Drink

First, fill a glass with ice.

Next, grab a small [Blender](https://amzn.to/4oSJQHl). In the [Blender](https://amzn.to/4oSJQHl), add 1 teaspoon pink pitaya powder and 1 cup of coconut milk.

Blend for 1-2 minutes till it is totally combined and the milk is pink.

Pour the pink milk half way up your glass.

Next, in a cup add, 1 teaspoon matcha green tea powder, 1 tablespoon of the hard part from the refrigerated can of coconut milk, and ½ cup coconut milk.
Use your milk frother to blend until the matcha foam is totally frothy and smooth.

Scoop your matcha foam on top of the pink milk.
