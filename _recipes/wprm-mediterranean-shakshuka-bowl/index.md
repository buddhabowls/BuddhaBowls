ingredients:
  - "6 eggs"
  - "1 can diced tomatoes"
  - "1 bell pepper"
  - "1 onion"
  - "2 cloves garlic"
  - "1 tsp cumin"
  - "1 tsp paprika"
  - "1/4 cup feta cheese"
  - "2 tbsp olive oil"
  - "1/4 cup fresh parsley"
  - "Salt and pepper to taste"
instructions:
  - "Sauté onion, pepper, and garlic in olive oil."
  - "Add tomatoes and spices, simmer for 10 minutes."
  - "Create wells in the sauce and crack eggs into them."
  - "Cover and cook until eggs are set."
  - "Top with feta and parsley"
