ingredients:

  - "2 eggs (poached)"
  - "1/2 cup cheese curds"
  - "1/4 cup bacon (cooked and crumbled)"
  - "1/2 cup breakfast gravy"
  - "2 tbsp chives"
instructions:
  - "Layer crispy hash browns in bowls."
  - "Top with cheese curds and crumbled bacon."
  - "Add poached eggs."
  - "Pour warm breakfast gravy over the top."
  - "Garnish with chives."
