ingredients:
  - "2 large sweet potatoes (sliced lengthwise and toasted)"
  - "2 eggs (poached)"
  - "1 avocado (mashed)"
  - "1/4 cup cherry tomatoes (halved)"
  - "2 tbsp pumpkin seeds"
  - "2 tbsp almond butter"
  - "1 tbsp honey"
  - "1 tsp chia seeds"
instructions:
  - "Toast sweet potato slices until tender."
  - "Arrange in bowls."
  - "Top half with mashed avocado, cherry tomatoes, and a poached egg."
  - "Top the other half with almond butter, honey, pumpkin seeds, and chia seeds."
  - "Serve savory and sweet sides together."
