  - "4 nori sheets (cut into strips)"
  - "2 eggs (thin omelette)"
  - "1 avocado (sliced)"
  - "1/4 cup cucumber (julienned)"
  - "2 tbsp smoked salmon"
  - "2 tbsp cream cheese"
  - "1 tbsp sesame seeds"
  - "Soy sauce for serving"
instructions:
  - "Cook sushi rice and let cool."
  - "Make thin omelette and slice."
  - "Assemble bowls with rice, nori strips, omelette slices, avocado, cucumber, smoked salmon, and cream cheese."
  - "Sprinkle with sesame seeds."
  - "Serve with soy sauce."
