ingredients:
  - "4 slices bread (cubed)"
  - "2 eggs"
  - "1/2 cup milk"
  - "1 tsp vanilla extract"
  - "1 tsp cinnamon"
  - "2 tbsp butter"
  - "1/4 cup mixed berries"
  - "2 tbsp maple syrup"
  - "2 tbsp chopped nuts"
instructions:
  - "Mix eggs, milk, vanilla, and cinnamon."
  - "Soak bread cubes in mixture."
  - "Cook in butter until golden."
  - "Serve in bowls topped with berries, nuts, and maple syrup."
