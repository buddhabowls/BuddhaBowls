  - "2 tbsp dried cranberries"
  - "2 tbsp maple syrup"
  - "1 tsp cinnamon"

  - "Top with sliced banana, mixed nuts, and dried cranberries."
  - "Drizzle with maple syrup and sprinkle cinnamon."
