ingredients:

  - "2 soft-boiled eggs"
  - "1/2 cup cucumber (sliced)"
  - "1/4 cup red onion (thinly sliced)"
  - "2 tbsp capers"
  - "2 slices rye bread (toasted)"
  - "2 tbsp cream cheese"
  - "Fresh dill for garnish"
instructions:
  - "Arrange smoked salmon, soft-boiled eggs, cucumber, and red onion in bowls."
  - "Sprinkle with capers."
  - "Serve with toasted rye bread spread with cream cheese."
  - "Garnish with fresh dill."
