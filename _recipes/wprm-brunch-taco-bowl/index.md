ingredients:
  - "4 small corn tortillas (cut into wedges)"
  - "4 eggs (scrambled)"
  - "1 cup black beans"
  - "1/2 cup salsa"
  - "1/4 cup shredded cheese"
  - "1 avocado (sliced)"
  - "2 tbsp cilantro"
  - "Lime wedges"
instructions:
  - "Bake tortilla wedges until crispy."
  - "Layer beans, scrambled eggs, salsa, and cheese in bowls."
  - "Top with crispy tortilla chips, avocado slices, and cilantro."
  - "Serve with lime wedges."
