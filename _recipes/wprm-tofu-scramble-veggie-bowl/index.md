ingredients:
  - "1 block firm tofu"
  - "1 bell pepper (diced)"
  - "1 onion (diced)"
  - "2 cups spinach"
  - "1 tsp turmeric"
  - "1 tsp garlic powder"
  - "1/4 cup nutritional yeast"
  - "Salt and pepper to taste"
instructions:
  - "Crumble tofu and sauté with vegetables and spices."
  - "Add spinach and cook until wilted."
  - "Stir in nutritional yeast."
  - "Serve in bowls."
