ingredients:
  - "2 cups baby spinach"
  - "2 eggs (poached)"
  - "1/2 avocado (sliced)"
  - "1/4 cup quinoa"
  - "1/4 cup edamame"
  - "2 tbsp pumpkin seeds"
  - "Green goddess dressing (1/4 cup Greek yogurt"
  - "1/4 cup mixed herbs"
  - "1 tbsp lemon juice"
  - "1 clove garlic)"
instructions:
  - "Make dressing by blending yogurt, herbs, lemon juice, and garlic."
  - "Layer spinach and quinoa in bowls."
  - "Top with poached eggs, avocado slices, and edamame."
  - "Sprinkle with pumpkin seeds."
  - "Drizzle with green goddess dressing."
