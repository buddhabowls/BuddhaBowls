ingredients:

  - "4 cups chicken or vegetable broth"
  - "2 eggs (soft-boiled)"
  - "1 cup spinach"
  - "1/4 cup corn"
  - "2 slices bacon (cooked and crumbled)"
  - "2 green onions (sliced)"
  - "1 tsp sesame oil"
instructions:
  - "Cook ramen in broth according to package instructions."
  - "Add spinach and corn in the last minute of cooking."
  - "Serve in bowls topped with soft-boiled eggs, crumbled bacon, green onions, and a drizzle of sesame oil."
