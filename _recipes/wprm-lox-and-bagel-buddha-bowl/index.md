ingredients:

  - "4 oz smoked salmon"
  - "1/4 cup cream cheese"
  - "1/4 red onion (thinly sliced)"
  - "2 tbsp capers"
  - "1/4 cucumber (diced)"
  - "1 tbsp fresh dill"
  - "Lemon wedges"
instructions:
  - "Arrange bagel cubes in bowls."
  - "Top with smoked salmon, dollops of cream cheese, red onion, capers, and cucumber."
  - "Garnish with fresh dill and serve with lemon wedges."
