ingredients:
  - "4 eggs"
  - "1 can diced tomatoes"
  - "1 bell pepper (diced)"
  - "1 onion (diced)"
  - "2 cloves garlic (minced)"
  - "1 tsp cumin"
  - "1 tsp paprika"
  - "1/4 cup feta cheese"
  - "2 tbsp fresh parsley"
  - "Pita bread for serving"
instructions:
  - "Sauté onion, pepper, and garlic."
  - "Add tomatoes and spices, simmer for 10 minutes."
  - "Create wells in the sauce and crack eggs into them."
  - "Cover and cook until eggs are set."
  - "Top with feta and parsley. Serve with pita bread."
