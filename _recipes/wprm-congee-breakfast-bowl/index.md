ingredients:
  - "1 cup rice"
  - "6 cups chicken broth"
  - "2 eggs (soft-boiled)"
  - "1/4 cup shredded chicken"
  - "2 tbsp green onions"
  - "1 tbsp ginger (minced)"
  - "1 tbsp soy sauce"
  - "1 tsp sesame oil"
instructions:
  - "Cook rice in chicken broth until it reaches a porridge-like consistency."
  - "Stir in minced ginger."
  - "Serve in bowls topped with soft-boiled eggs, shredded chicken, and green onions."
  - "Drizzle with soy sauce and sesame oil."
