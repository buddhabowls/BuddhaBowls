ingredients:

  - "1 frozen banana"
  - "2 tbsp peanut butter"
  - "1/2 cup almond milk"
  - "1/4 cup granola"
  - "1 sliced banana"
  - "2 tbsp chopped peanuts"
  - "1 tbsp honey"
instructions:
  - "Blend acai, frozen banana, peanut butter, and almond milk."
  - "Pour into bowls."
  - "Top with granola, sliced banana, chopped peanuts, and a drizzle of honey."
