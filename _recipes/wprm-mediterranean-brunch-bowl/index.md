ingredients:
  - "1 cup cooked quinoa"
  - "2 eggs (poached)"
  - "1/2 cup cherry tomatoes (halved)"
  - "1/4 cup cucumber (diced)"
  - "1/4 cup feta cheese"
  - "2 tbsp Kalamata olives"
  - "2 tbsp hummus"
  - "1 tbsp olive oil"
  - "1 tsp za'atar spice"
instructions:
  - "Layer quinoa in bowls."
  - "Top with poached eggs, tomatoes, cucumber, feta, and olives."
  - "Add a dollop of hummus."
  - "Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle za'atar."
