  - "4 oz smoked salmon (cubed)"
  - "1/2 avocado (sliced)"
  - "1/4 cup edamame"
  - "1/4 cup cucumber (diced)"
  - "2 soft-boiled eggs"
  - "2 tbsp soy sauce"
  - "1 tbsp sesame seeds"
instructions:
  - "Cook sushi rice and let cool."
  - "Arrange rice in bowls."
  - "Top with smoked salmon, avocado, edamame, cucumber, and soft-boiled eggs."
  - "Drizzle with soy sauce and sprinkle sesame seeds."
