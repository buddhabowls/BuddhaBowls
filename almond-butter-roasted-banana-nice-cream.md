## 🧘 Mindful Moment
> Delight in the warm, caramelized flavors of roasted bananas blended with almond butter. This nice cream is a comforting treat that encourages mindfulness.

## 💚 Health Benefits
- Almonds: heart-healthy
- Bananas: high in potassium, good for energy.

## 🥗 Ingredients
- 3 ripe bananas
- 2 tbsp almond butter
- 2 tbsp roasted banana pieces

## 👩‍🍳 Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. Slice and roast 2 bananas for 20 minutes until caramelized.
3. Freeze roasted bananas overnight.
4. Blend frozen bananas, roasted banana pieces, and almond butter until smooth.
5. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.

## 💡 Tips
- Blend with ice for a thicker, frosty texture.
