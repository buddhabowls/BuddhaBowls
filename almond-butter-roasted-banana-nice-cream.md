ingredients:
  - 3 ripe bananas
  - 2 tbsp almond butter
  - 2 tbsp roasted banana pieces

instructions:
  - Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  - Slice and roast 2 bananas for 20 minutes until caramelized.
  - Freeze roasted bananas overnight.
  - Blend frozen bananas, roasted banana pieces, and almond butter until smooth.
  - Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.
