## Honey Fig Overnight Oats 

This recipe is my tribute to the irresistible combination of [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke) and figs. It’s like having a gourmet breakfast in a bowl, but without the fuss.

To create this sweet delight, I mix rolled oats with almond milk, chopped figs, and a generous drizzle of [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke). After a good stir, it’s time to let the fridge do its thing overnight.

In the morning, I serve it in a coconut bowl (because it makes me feel like I’m at a fancy brunch) and top it with fresh fig slices and a drizzle of [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke).

These Honey Fig Overnight Oats are like a sweet hug for your taste buds, and they never fail to make my mornings a little brighter. So go ahead, treat yourself to a breakfast that’s as sweet as you are!

## Honey Fig Overnight Oats 

**Course:** Breakfast

**Prep Time:** 5 mins

**Cook Time:** 0 mins

**Total Time:** 5 mins

**Servings:** 1

## Ingredients

Rolled oats,

almond milk,

chopped figs,

[Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke)
## Instructions

Mix oats, almond milk, chopped figs, and [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke).

Refrigerate overnight.

Top with fresh fig slices and a drizzle of [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke).
