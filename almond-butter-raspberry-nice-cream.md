## 🧘 Mindful Moment
> Enjoy the tartness of raspberries blended with the smoothness of almond butter. This recipe encourages you to embrace the contrasts in life and the beauty of balance.

## 💚 Health Benefits
- Raspberries are high in fiber and antioxidants
- almond butter adds protein.

## 🥗 Ingredients
- 3 ripe bananas
- 2 tbsp almond butter
- 1 cup raspberries

## 👩‍🍳 Instructions
1. Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
2. Place frozen bananas in a blender.
3. Add almond butter and raspberries.
4. Blend until smooth.
5. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.

## 💡 Tips
- Experiment with different sweeteners like agave or honey.
