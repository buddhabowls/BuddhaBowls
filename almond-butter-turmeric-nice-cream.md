## 🧘 Mindful Moment
> Harness the power of turmeric in this unique almond butter nice cream. Its warm, earthy flavor promotes well-being and mindfulness with every scoop.

## 💚 Health Benefits
- Almonds: nutrient-dense
- Turmeric: anti-inflammatory, antioxidant properties.

## 🥗 Ingredients
- 3 ripe bananas
- 2 tbsp almond butter
- 1/2 tsp ground turmeric

## 👩‍🍳 Instructions
1. Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
2. Place frozen bananas in a blender.
3. Add almond butter and ground turmeric.
4. Blend until smooth.
5. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.

## 💡 Tips
- Use a blender tamper to help mix thick mixtures.
