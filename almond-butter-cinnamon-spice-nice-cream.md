## 💚 Health Benefits
- Cinnamon helps regulate blood sugar
- almond butter provides protein and healthy fats.

## 🥗 Ingredients
- 3 ripe bananas
- 2 tbsp almond butter
- 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp nutmeg

## 👩‍🍳 Instructions
1. Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
2. Place frozen bananas in a blender.
3. Add almond butter, cinnamon, and nutmeg.
4. Blend until smooth.
5. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.

## 💡 Tips
- Top with granola for a crunchy texture.
