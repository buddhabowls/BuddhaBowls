Coconut Oil & Shea Butter Hair Mask

🌀 Curl Enhancement:
• Apply to soaking wet hair for maximum definition
• Use praying hands application method
• Diffuse on low heat for volume
• Refresh curls with leftover mixture

🛢️ Formula Components:

  • ½ cup (120g) raw shea butter
  • 3 tbsp (45ml) coconut oil
  • 1 tbsp (15ml) argan oil
  • Coconut shell + wooden spatula
  • Thermal cap or warm towel

🔥 Activation Protocol:

  1. Melt shea butter in coconut shell over steam bath
  2. Remove from heat - whip with coconut oil until creamy
  3. Cool to 45°C before adding essential oils
  4. Apply to sectioned hair using 3-step process:
    • Roots: Scalp massage with fingertips
    • Mid-lengths: Rake-through technique
    • Ends: Sealing with "shingling" method
  5. Cover with silk bonnet - process 30-45 minutes
  6. Rinse with lukewarm water followed by ACV rinse
  7. Air dry or use microfiber towel wrap
