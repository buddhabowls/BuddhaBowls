## Mini Pita Pizzas

Pizza night just got a whole lot more exciting with these Mini Pita Pizzas! They’re quick, easy, and come with a delightful coconut twist that will have everyone coming back for more.

I remember the first time I made these pita pizzas. It was a busy weeknight, and I needed something fast but satisfying. Using pita bread as the base was a stroke of genius, and adding a sprinkle of coconut flakes took them to the next level.

To make these mini pizzas, preheat your oven to 375°F. Spread tomato sauce on four pita breads, sprinkle with one cup of shredded mozzarella and a quarter cup of coconut flakes. Add your favorite toppings like pepperoni, olives, or bell peppers, and bake for 10 minutes. Serve them in a coconut bowl for that extra touch of fun. These pizzas are perfect for a quick dinner or a party appetizer, and they’re sure to be a hit with the whole family!

## Mini Pita Pizzas Recipe

Course: Dinner

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 10 minutes

Total Time: 20 minutes

Servings: 4
## Ingredients

- 4 pita breads

- 1 cup tomato sauce

- 1 cup shredded mozzarella

- 1/4 cup coconut flakes

- Toppings: pepperoni, olives, bell peppers

## Instructions

- Preheat oven to 375°F.

- Spread tomato sauce on pitas, sprinkle with mozzarella and coconut flakes.

- Add desired toppings and bake for 10 minutes.
