## Joyful Watermelon Wonder Recipe

Course: Dessert
Cuisine: Vegan
Servings: 2

## Ingredients

- 2 cups frozen watermelon,

- 1 banana,

- 1/2 cup coconut yogurt,

- Toppings:

- mint leaves,

- watermelon slices

## Instructions

- Blend watermelon, banana, and coconut yogurt until smooth.

- Serve in [Coconut Bowls](https://buddhabowls.org/product/coconut-bowls/) and top with mint and watermelon slices.

Use fresh watermelon for a more intense flavor.
