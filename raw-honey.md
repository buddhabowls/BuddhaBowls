1. Raw Honey and Lemon Salad Dressing:

 – 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil

 – 2 tablespoons raw [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke)

 – 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice

 – 1 teaspoon lemon zest

 – Salt and pepper to taste
 Simply whisk together the olive oil, raw [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke), lemon juice, and lemon zest until well combined. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Drizzle over your favorite salad and enjoy!

2. Raw Honey Glazed Carrots:

 – 1 pound carrots, peeled and sliced into sticks

 – 2 tablespoons raw [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke)

 – 1 tablespoon butter

 – Salt and pepper to taste

 – Fresh parsley for garnish
 In a skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the carrots and sauté until they start to soften. Drizzle the raw [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke) over the carrots and continue cooking until the [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke) forms a glaze. Season with salt and pepper, then garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

3. Raw Honey and Almond Granola:

 – 3 cups rolled oats

 – 1 cup sliced almonds

 – 1/2 cup raw [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke)

 – 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted

 – 1 teaspoon [Vanilla Extract](https://amzn.to/4aIhdue)

 – 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

 – Pinch of salt
 Preheat your oven to 300°F (150°C). In a large bowl, combine the oats and almonds. In a separate bowl, whisk together the raw [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke), melted coconut oil, [Vanilla Extract](https://amzn.to/4aIhdue), cinnamon, and salt. Pour the [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke) mixture over the oats and almonds, stirring until everything is well coated. Spread the mixture onto a baking sheet and bake for 30-35 minutes, stirring occasionally, until golden brown. Allow the [Granola](https://amzn.to/4iPqYY5) to cool completely before breaking it into clusters.

Raw Honey Tahini Dressing:

– 1/4 cup tahini

– 2 tablespoons raw [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke)

– 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice

– 2-3 tablespoons water

– Salt and pepper to taste
In a small bowl, whisk together the tahini, raw [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke), and lemon juice. Gradually add water until you reach your desired consistency. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
