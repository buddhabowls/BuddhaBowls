Mediterranean Rice Bowl

A refreshing and healthy Mediterranean Rice Bowl filled with vibrant ingredients, perfect for a light meal or lunch.

Mediterranean Rice Bowl Recipe

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 25 minutes

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 cup jasmine rice
  • 1/4 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup red onion, diced
  • 1/4 cup olives, sliced
  • 2 tbsp hummus
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Rinse the jasmine rice under cold water and cook according to package instructions (usually about 15 minutes).
  2. While the rice is cooking, dice the cucumber, halved cherry tomatoes, and red onion.
  3. Once the rice is cooked, fluff it with a fork and let it cool slightly.
  4. In a bowl, layer the cooked jasmine rice with the diced vegetables and sliced olives.
  5. Top with a dollop of hummus and garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

Nutrition

Calories: Approximately 400

Protein: 10g

Carbs: 65g

Fat: 12g

Fiber: 8g
