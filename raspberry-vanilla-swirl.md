## Raspberry Vanilla Swirl Smoothie Bowl

 

Good morning, breakfast lovers! Today, we’re swirling into the Raspberry Vanilla Swirl: a smoothie bowl that’s as delightful as it sounds. Packed with raspberries and a hint of vanilla, this breakfast is sure to bring a smile to your face. Let’s get started!

Our star ingredients include fresh raspberries, vanilla yogurt, almond milk, and a touch of [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke). Together, they create a creamy, dreamy delight that’s both refreshing and satisfying.

Time to blend! Toss the raspberries, yogurt, almond milk, and [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke) into your [Blender](https://amzn.to/4oSJQHl). Blend until smooth, but not so smooth that your [Blender](https://amzn.to/4oSJQHl) starts dreaming of dessert.

Pour your raspberry vanilla creation into the coconut bowl and let the topping fun begin. Add fresh raspberries for extra sweetness, a sprinkle of [Granola](https://amzn.to/4iPqYY5) for crunch, and maybe a few almonds for an extra boost. This is your chance to create a masterpiece that’s almost too pretty to eat.

The Raspberry Vanilla Swirl is perfect for those days when you need a little extra pep in your step. It’s creamy, refreshing, and just the right amount of sweet. So, grab your coconut bowl, take a bite, and let the breakfast bliss begin!

### Course

- **Breakfast / Snack**

### Cuisine

- **Healthy / Smoothie Bowl**

### Servings

- **2 servings**

## Ingredients

- 1 cup fresh raspberries

- 1 cup vanilla yogurt

- 1 cup almond milk

- 1 tablespoon [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke)

- Toppings (optional): sliced almonds, [Granola](https://amzn.to/4iPqYY5), additional raspberries, or coconut flakes

## Instructions

- **Blend the Base**: In a [Blender](https://amzn.to/4oSJQHl), combine the fresh raspberries, vanilla yogurt, almond milk, and [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke). Blend until smooth and creamy.

- **Adjust Consistency**: If the mixture is too thick, add more almond milk until you reach your desired consistency.

- **Serve**: Pour the smoothie mixture into bowls.

- **Add Toppings**: Top with sliced almonds, [Granola](https://amzn.to/4iPqYY5), additional raspberries, or coconut flakes as desired.

- **Enjoy**: Serve immediately and enjoy your refreshing smoothie bowl!
