Black Bean and Rice Bowl

A hearty and nutritious Black Bean and Rice Bowl, perfect for a quick meal packed with flavor and healthy ingredients.

Black Bean and Rice Bowl Recipe

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Total Time: 40 minutes

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 cup brown rice
  • 1/2 cup black beans, rinsed
  • 1/4 cup corn
  • 1/4 cup bell peppers, diced
  • 1/2 avocado, sliced
  • 2 tbsp salsa verde

Instructions

  1. Rinse the brown rice under cold water and cook according to package instructions (usually about 30 minutes).
  2. While the rice is cooking, mix the black beans, corn, and diced bell peppers in a bowl.
  3. Once the rice is cooked, fluff it with a fork and let it cool slightly.
  4. In serving bowls, layer the cooked brown rice with the black bean mixture and top with sliced avocado.
  5. Drizzle with salsa verde before serving.

Nutrition

Calories: Approximately 500

Protein: 15g

Carbs: 80g

Fat: 15g

Fiber: 12g
