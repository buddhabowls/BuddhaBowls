Almond Butter Peach Nice Cream Recipe Ingredients
  • 3 ripe bananas,
  • 2 tbsp almond butter,
  • 1 cup diced peaches
    • Instructions
  • Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
  • Place frozen bananas in a blender.
  • Add almond butter and diced peaches.
  • Blend until smooth.
  • Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.

    • Enjoy the juicy sweetness of peaches blended with almond butter. This recipe encourages you to savor the moment, appreciating the fleeting nature of summer fruits.

    Peaches are rich in vitamins and antioxidants; almond butter adds creaminess.
