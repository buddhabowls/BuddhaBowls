## Anti-Inflammatory Buddha Bowl

Anti-Inflammatory Buddha Bowl Recipe

Ingredients:

1 cup [Turmeric](https://amzn.to/4aIhdsd) rice,

1/2 cup roasted cauliflower,

1/2 cup chickpeas (cooked),

1/4 cup red onion (sliced),

1/4 cup pomegranate seeds,

Lemon-[Tahini Sauce](https://amzn.to/44p9Fau)
Instructions:

Cook rice with [Turmeric](https://amzn.to/4aIhdsd) until done.

Roast cauliflower at 400°F for 20 minutes.

Combine [Turmeric](https://amzn.to/4aIhdsd) rice, cauliflower, chickpeas, red onion, and pomegranate seeds in a bowl.

Drizzle with lemon-[Tahini Sauce](https://amzn.to/44p9Fau).
Notes:

Add a handful of spinach for extra greens.

Use purple cauliflower for extra color.
