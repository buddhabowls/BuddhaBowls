## Zero Waste Bamboo Toothbrushes

Our bamboo toothbrushes offer a path to oral care that respects the earth.

With over 1 billion plastic toothbrushes in landfills in the United States alone, that will take 500+ years to break down. This is 50 million pounds of waste that sometimes ends in waterways and oceans.

Our bamboo toothbrush is a great substitute of plastic toothbrush while getting the same brushing experience with a traditional plastic brush that you’re used to using!

## Bamboo

**SMOOTH & NATURAL BAMBOO HANDLE**
