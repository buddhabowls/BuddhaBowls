Light a candle and let your affirmations shine bright!

🕯️ Light Up Your Affirmations! 🕯️ Light a candle infused with your affirmations! As it burns, focus on your intentions and let the light guide your path to self-love! 💖✨ #CandleMagic #Affirmations

Ingredients:

– White candle

– A piece of paper with your affirmation

– A small bowl of water

Instructions:

1. Write your affirmation on the paper and place it under the candle.

2. Light the candle and focus on your affirmation.

3. Let it burn down safely.
