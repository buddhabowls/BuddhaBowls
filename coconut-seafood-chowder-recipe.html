Coconut Seafood Chowder

A rich and creamy coconut seafood chowder served in a coconut bowl, perfect for a comforting and luxurious meal.

Coconut Seafood Chowder Recipe

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 25 minutes

Total Time: 40 minutes

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 lb mixed seafood (shrimp, scallops, fish)
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 2 medium potatoes, diced
  • 1 cup corn (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups vegetable or seafood broth
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, sautéing until translucent.
  2. Add the diced potatoes and corn, stirring to combine. Cook for about 5 minutes.
  3. Pour in the vegetable or seafood broth and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and let simmer until the potatoes are tender, about 15 minutes.
  4. Stir in the coconut milk and mixed seafood. Cook for an additional 5-7 minutes, or until the seafood is cooked through.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot in coconut bowls, garnished with fresh parsley if desired.
