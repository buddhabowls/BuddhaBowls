## Herbal Detox Tub Tea

### Ingredients:

- Dried nettle, Dried peppermint, Epsom salt, Sea salt

### Instructions:

- 1. Mix 1/2 cup of dried nettle, 1/2 cup of dried peppermint, 1/2 cup of Epsom salt, and 1/4 cup of sea salt. 2. Place in a muslin bag and add to the tub as it fills. 3. Soak for 20-30 minutes.
