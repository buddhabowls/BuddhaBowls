## Nut Butter Pancakes

**Course:** Breakfast

**Cuisine:** American

**Servings:** 4 pancakes

### Ingredients:

- 1 cup flour

- 1 cup milk

- 1/2 cup nut butter

- 1 egg

### Instructions:

- In a bowl, mix all ingredients until combined.

- Cook on a skillet over medium heat until bubbles form, then flip.

NOTES: Top with syrup or fruit.
