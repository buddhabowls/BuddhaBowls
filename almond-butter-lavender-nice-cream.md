## 🧘 Mindful Moment
> Experience the calming essence of lavender in this nice cream. Allow each bite to promote relaxation and mindfulness, reminding you to take time for yourself.

## 💚 Health Benefits
- Lavender can promote relaxation
- almond butter provides healthy fats.

## 🥗 Ingredients
- 3 ripe bananas
- 2 tbsp almond butter
- 1 tsp dried lavender flowers

## 👩‍🍳 Instructions
1. Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
2. Place frozen bananas in a blender.
3. Add almond butter and dried lavender.
4. Blend until smooth.
5. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.

## 💡 Tips
- Use a little lemon juice to brighten the flavors.
