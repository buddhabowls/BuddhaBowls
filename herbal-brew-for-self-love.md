Sip on this herbal brew to boost your self-love vibes.

☕ Sip on Self-Love! ☕ Brew a delightful herbal tea with hibiscus and rose petals to boost your self-love vibes! Sip slowly and affirm your worth as you enjoy every drop! 🌹💖 #SelfLove #HerbalTea

Ingredients: – Hibiscus flowers – Rose petals – Honey – Lemon slice

Instructions:

1. Boil water and steep hibiscus and rose petals.

2. Add [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke) and a slice of lemon.

3. While sipping, affirm, “I am worthy of love.”
