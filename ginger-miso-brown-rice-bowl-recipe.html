Ginger Miso Brown Rice Bowl

A nutritious and flavorful Ginger Miso Brown Rice Bowl that combines fresh vegetables and wholesome ingredients, perfect for a healthy meal.

Ginger Miso Brown Rice Bowl Recipe

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Total Time: 40 minutes

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 cup brown rice
  • 1/2 cup snap peas
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/4 cup edamame, shelled
  • 2 tbsp ginger miso dressing
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions

  1. Rinse the brown rice under cold water and cook according to package instructions (usually about 30 minutes).
  2. While the rice is cooking, lightly steam the snap peas and shredded carrots until they are tender-crisp, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. Once the rice is cooked, fluff it with a fork and let it cool slightly.
  4. In a bowl, layer the cooked brown rice with steamed snap peas, shredded carrots, and edamame.
  5. Drizzle with ginger miso dressing and sprinkle with sesame seeds before serving.

Nutrition

Calories: Approximately 450

Protein: 14g

Carbs: 75g

Fat: 10g

Fiber: 8g
