## Nut Butter Oatmeal

**Course:** Breakfast

**Cuisine:** American

**Servings:** 2

### Ingredients:

- 1 cup oats

- 2 cups water

- 2 tbsp nut butter

- 1 banana

### Instructions:

- Cook oats in water until soft.

- Stir in nut butter and top with banana.

NOTES: Great for a filling breakfast!
