## Sunflower Seed Butter

**Course:** Spread

**Cuisine:** American

**Servings:** 1 cup

### Ingredients:

- 2 cups sunflower seeds

- 1/2 tsp salt

- 1 tbsp [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke) (optional)

### Instructions:

- Roast sunflower seeds at 350°F (175°C) for 10 minutes.

- Let cool, then blend in a food processor until smooth.

- Add salt and [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke) if desired.

NOTES: Great for school lunches!
