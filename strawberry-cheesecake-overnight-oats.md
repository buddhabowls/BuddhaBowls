Hello, dessert lovers! If you’re craving something sweet and indulgent for breakfast, these Strawberry Cheesecake Overnight Oats are just what you need.

## Strawberry Cheesecake Overnight Oats

This recipe is my ode to the creamy, dreamy goodness of cheesecake. It’s like having dessert for breakfast, but without the guilt.

To whip up these oats, I mix rolled oats with Greek yogurt, a dollop of cream cheese, and a handful of fresh strawberries. After a quick stir, it’s time to let the fridge work its magic overnight.

In the morning, I serve it in a coconut bowl (because it adds that extra touch of elegance) and top it with crushed graham crackers and strawberry slices.

These Strawberry Cheesecake Overnight Oats are like a little slice of heaven, and they never fail to make my mornings a little sweeter. So go ahead, treat yourself to a breakfast that’s as indulgent as you are!

## Strawberry Cheesecake Overnight Oats

**Course:** Breakfast

**Prep Time:** 5 mins

**Cook Time:** 0 mins

**Total Time:** 5 mins

**Servings:** 1

## Ingredients

- Rolled oats, Greek yogurt, cream cheese, strawberries

## Instructions

- Mix oats, Greek yogurt, cream cheese, and strawberries. Refrigerate overnight. Top with crushed graham crackers and strawberry slices.
