Soy Wax Herbal Candle

🌱 Core Formula

  • 1 lb organic soy wax flakes
  • 15ml eucalyptus essential oil
  • 2 tbsp dried lavender buds
  • Reused mason jar + wood wick

🌀 5-Step Crafting

  1. Melt wax (65°C max)
  2. Steep herbs 15min (strain optional)
  3. Blend in essential oil
  4. Pour into jar, arrange herb decor
  5. Cure 48hrs for full aroma

🌼 Seasonal Swaps

  • Spring: Lemon balm + chamomile
  • Summer: Mint + rosemary
  • Fall: Sage + orange peel
  • Winter: Pine + cinnamon
