**DIY Peppermint Candle Ideas**

If you’re feeling crafty, why not make your own peppermint candles? It’s easier than you might think and allows you to customize the scent exactly to your liking. Here’s a quick guide to get started:

**What You’ll Need:**

- Soy wax flakes - Peppermint essential oil - Candle wicks - Glass jars or tins - A double boiler (or a heat-safe bowl and pot)

**Instructions:**

1. Melt the soy wax flakes in a double boiler until fully liquefied.

2. Remove from heat and stir in 10-15 drops of peppermint essential oil (adjust based on how strong you want the scent).

3. Place the wick in the center of your jar or tin and secure it with a wick holder or chopstick.

4. Carefully pour the melted wax into the container, holding the wick in place.

5. Allow the wax to cool and harden completely before trimming the wick and lighting your candle. Not only is this a fun project, but it also makes for thoughtful gifts during the holiday season!
