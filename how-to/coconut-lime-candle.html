Coconut Lime Candle

Ingredients:

  • Coconut oil, Beeswax, Lime essential oil, Candle wicks, Glass jars

Instructions:

  1. 1. Melt coconut oil and beeswax in a double boiler. 2. Stir in lime oil. 3. Secure wicks in jars and pour wax. 4. Allow to cool.
