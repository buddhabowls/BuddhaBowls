  • 1 lb organic soy wax flakes
  • 25 ml eucalyptus radiata essential oil
  • Handful dried eucalyptus leaves
  • Recycled apothecary jar + hemp wick
  1. Melt wax at low heat (58°C)
  2. Steep crushed leaves 20min (strain optional)
  3. Blend oil while cooling to 50°C
  4. Layer whole leaves during pouring
  5. Cure 48hrs for full therapeutic effect
