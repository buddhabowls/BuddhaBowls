Welcome to your guide for a simple house blessing and cleansing ritual! This practice helps to clear negative energies and invite positive vibrations into your home. Let’s create a peaceful and harmonious space!

Simple House Blessing/Cleansing

What You Will Need:

  • White sage or palo santo
  • Salt (sea salt or Himalayan salt)
  • Essential oil (like lavender or frankincense)
  • Water
  • A small candle (white or green)
  • Coconut Bowl
  • Your favorite crystals (optional)

Steps to Perform the Blessing:

  1. Start by setting your intention. Take a moment to focus on what you want to achieve with this blessing.
  2. Fill the coconut bowl with a bit of salt and water. This will serve as a cleansing agent.
  3. Light the sage or palo santo and let the smoke fill the space. Move from room to room, allowing the smoke to cleanse each area.
  4. As you cleanse, visualize any negative energy leaving your home.
  5. After cleansing, place the coconut bowl in a central location in your home.
  6. Add a few drops of essential oil to the water in the bowl for added positive energy.
  7. Light the candle and say a blessing or affirmation, inviting peace and harmony into your home.
  8. Optionally, place your favorite crystals around the coconut bowl to enhance the energy.
  9. Take a moment to meditate or reflect on the positive energy you’ve invited into your space.

Regularly performing this blessing can help maintain a positive atmosphere in your home.

Feel free to repeat this ritual whenever you feel the need to refresh your space!
