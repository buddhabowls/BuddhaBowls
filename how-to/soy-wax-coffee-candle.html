Soy Wax Coffee Candle

☕ Naked Ingredients

  • 1 lb organic soy wax flakes
  • 2 tbsp used coffee grounds
  • 15ml coffee bean essential oil
  • Recycled mug + cotton wick

🌀 4-Step Crafting

  1. Melt wax in double boiler
  2. Mix in cooled coffee grounds
  3. Stir oil counter-clockwise 3x
  4. Pour into mug, cure 24hrs

🌱 Barista Pro Tips

  • Morning Magic: Add cinnamon stick wick decor
  • Scent Boost: Layer grounds at jar bottom
  • Texture Play: Mix fine+coarse grind sizes
