#CandleAlchemy #SpiceGlow

🌰 Cozy Ingredients

  • 1 lb organic soy wax flakes
  • 20ml cinnamon bark essential oil
  • 3-4 cinnamon sticks
  • Repurposed tin + wood wick

🌀 5-Step Crafting

  1. Melt wax in spice-scented pan
  2. Add crushed cinnamon stick powder
  3. Blend oil while wax cools to 55°C
  4. Arrange whole sticks as decor
  5. Cure 72hrs for intense aroma

🎄 Festive Hacks

  • Holiday Twist: Add orange peel dust
  • Gift Idea: Tie with jute + dried citrus
  • Scent Boost: Layer ground + stick cinnamon
