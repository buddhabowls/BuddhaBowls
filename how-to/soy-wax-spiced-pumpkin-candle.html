Soy Wax Spiced Pumpkin Candle

🍂 Harvest Ingredients

  • 1 lb organic soy wax flakes
  • 2 tbsp pumpkin pie spice blend
  • 15ml cinnamon + 5ml nutmeg oil
  • Small pie tin + crackling wood wick

🌀 6-Step Crafting

  1. Melt wax with spice blend (65°C)
  2. Strain solids for smooth burn
  3. Blend oils while cooling to 55°C
  4. Pour into tin, add spice dusting
  5. Cure 96hrs - patience = potency
  6. Top with mini cinnamon stick

🎃 Pro Tips

  • Pumpkin Magic: Add dehydrated puree chips
  • Color Boost: Steep turmeric + paprika
  • Scent Layering: Vanilla base + spice top
