🏝️ Core Formula

  • 1 lb coconut-soy wax blend
  • 15ml vanilla + 10ml coconut oil
  • 2 tbsp organic shredded coconut
  • Upcycled glass jar + wood wick

🌀 5-Step Crafting

  1. Melt wax with coconut oil (58°C)
  2. Whip in vanilla oil until frothy
  3. Sprinkle shredded coconut layers
  4. Top with whole vanilla bean piece
  5. Cure 72hrs - patience = perfection
