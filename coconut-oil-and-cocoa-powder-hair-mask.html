Coconut Oil & Cocoa Powder Hair Mask

🎨 Color Science:
• Cacao's theobromine enhances natural pigment
• Coffee adds red undertones to dark hair
• ACV closes cuticles for light reflection
• Coconut oil prevents color stripping

🍫 Formula Components:

  • ½ cup (60g) raw cacao powder
  • ¼ cup (60ml) coconut oil
  • 2 tbsp (30ml) raw apple cider vinegar
  • Coconut shell + bamboo whisk
  • Plastic-free hair clips

🧑🍳 Preparation Protocol:

  1. Melt coconut oil in coconut shell (45°C water bath)
  2. Gradually whisk in cacao powder until lump-free
  3. Add ACV last to preserve acidity
  4. Apply to dry hair using quadrant method:
    • Divide hair into 4 sections
    • Use brush to coat from roots to ends
    • Focus on sun-exposed areas for color protection
  5. Wrap in thermal cap (30-45 minutes)
  6. Rinse with cold water followed by herbal rinse
  7. Air dry away from direct sunlight

🌰 Pro Enhancements:
• Add 1 tsp cinnamon for warm undertones
• Mix with henna for permanent color boost
• Use green tea instead of ACV for cool tones
• Add 5 drops vanilla EO for scent camouflage
• Store in coconut shell (1 week refrigerated)
