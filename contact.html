Frequently Asked Questions

What are your shipping times?

We ship most orders within 2-3 business days. Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days, while expedited shipping takes 2-3 business days. You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships.

Are your products eco-friendly?

Yes! All our coconut bowls are handcrafted from sustainable coconut shells. They're 100% biodegradable and compostable, making them an excellent eco-friendly choice for conscious consumers.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all products. If you're not completely satisfied, simply contact us for a return label and we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days of receiving your return.

Do you offer bulk orders?

Absolutely! We offer special pricing for bulk orders of 50+ units. Please contact our sales team at sales@buddhabowls.com for a custom quote.

How do I care for my coconut bowl?

Hand wash your bowl with warm water and mild soap. Avoid the dishwasher and prolonged soaking. To maintain the natural finish, occasionally apply food-grade coconut oil. Your bowl will develop a beautiful patina over time.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide! International shipping rates vary by location. You can calculate shipping costs at checkout, and we'll handle all customs documentation for you.
