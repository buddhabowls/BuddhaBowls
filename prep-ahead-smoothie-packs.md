Here are some simple prep-ahead single-serve smoothie pack ideas that you can easily dump into a [Blender](https://amzn.to/4oSJQHl). Each pack can be prepared in advance and stored in the freezer for quick and convenient smoothies.

### Smoothie Pack Ideas

#### 1. **Berry Banana Blast**

- **Ingredients**:

- 1 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)

- 1 banana, sliced

- 1 tablespoon [chia seeds](https://amzn.to/4q7GcdC)

- Optional: 1 tablespoon [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke) or [Maple Syrup](https://amzn.to/3KUS5oa)

- **Instructions**:

- Combine all ingredients in a freezer-safe bag or container.

- Freeze until ready to use.

#### 2. **Tropical Green Smoothie**

- **Ingredients**:

- 1 cup spinach or kale

- 1/2 cup pineapple chunks

- 1/2 cup mango chunks

- 1/2 banana, sliced

- Optional: 1 tablespoon coconut flakes

- **Instructions**:

- Place all ingredients in a freezer-safe bag or container.

- Freeze until ready to use.

#### 3. **Chocolate Peanut Butter Delight**

- **Ingredients**:

- 1 banana, sliced

- 1 tablespoon cocoa powder

- 1 tablespoon peanut butter (or [Almond Butter](https://amzn.to/4oN8Mj6))

- 1/2 cup almond milk (add when blending)

- **Instructions**:

- Combine all solid ingredients in a freezer-safe bag or container.

- Freeze until ready to use.

#### 4. **Oatmeal Berry Smoothie**

- **Ingredients**:

- 1/2 cup rolled oats

- 1 cup mixed berries

- 1/2 banana, sliced

- 1 tablespoon flaxseeds

- **Instructions**:

- Mix all ingredients in a freezer-safe bag or container.

- Freeze until ready to use.

#### 5. **Peach Mango Smoothie**

- **Ingredients**:

- 1 cup frozen peaches

- 1/2 cup frozen mango

- 1/2 cup Greek yogurt (add when blending)

- Optional: 1 tablespoon [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke)

- **Instructions**:

- Combine all ingredients in a freezer-safe bag or container.

- Freeze until ready to use.

### Blending Instructions

When you’re ready to make your smoothie:

- Take a smoothie pack out of the freezer.

- Dump the contents into your Ninja Bullet [Blender](https://amzn.to/4oSJQHl).

- Add any required liquids (like almond milk, yogurt, or water).

- Blend until smooth!

### Tips

- **Storage**: Use freezer-safe bags or containers to prevent freezer burn.

- **Customization**: Feel free to mix and match ingredients based on your preferences and dietary needs.

- **Add-ins**: Consider adding protein powder, greens powder, or other supplements as desired.

These smoothie packs are a great way to save time and ensure you have healthy options ready to go! 

**Enjoy your smoothies!**
