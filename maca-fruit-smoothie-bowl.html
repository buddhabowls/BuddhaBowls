🌈 Maca Fruit Power Bowl

🥄 10min Prep | 🥣 380 kcal | 🌿 Vegan

This Instagram-worthy bowl combines maca's natural energy boost with antioxidant-rich fruits. Perfect for morning vitality or post-yoga fuel!

🍌 Base Ingredients

  • 1 cup almond milk (unsweetened)
  • 1 frozen banana
  • 2 tbsp maca powder
  • ½ cup frozen mango

🎨 Topping Canvas

Granola
Berries
Almonds
  • ¼ cup protein granola
  • ½ cup mixed berries
  • 1 tbsp sliced almonds
  • 1 tsp coconut flakes
  • Edible flowers (optional)

👩🍳 Chef's Instructions

  1. Freeze serving bowl 10min before prep
  2. Blend base ingredients until thick
  3. Pour into chilled bowl
  4. Artistically arrange toppings
  5. Add final drizzle (honey/maple syrup)

💡 Pro Tips

  • Use frozen banana for ice-cream texture
  • Soak chia seeds 10min for gel consistency
  • Layer toppings by color contrast
  • Add maca gradually - strong earthy flavor
