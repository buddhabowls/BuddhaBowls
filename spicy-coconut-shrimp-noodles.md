## Spicy Coconut Shrimp Noodles Recipe

**Prep Time:** 20 minutes

**Cook Time:** 15 minutes

**Total Time:** 35 minutes

**Servings:** 2

## Ingredients

- 8 oz rice noodles

- 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined

- 1 can coconut milk

- 2 tablespoons chili paste (adjust to taste)

- 2 cloves garlic, minced

- 1 tablespoon ginger, grated

- 2 tablespoons lime juice

- Fresh cilantro for garnish

## Instructions

- Cook the rice noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.

- In a large skillet, heat a splash of oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, sautéing until fragrant.

- Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook until they turn pink, about 3-4 minutes.

- Stir in the chili paste and coconut milk, mixing well to combine. Let it simmer for about 5 minutes.

- Add the cooked rice noodles to the skillet and toss to coat them in the sauce.

- Finish with lime juice and adjust seasoning if needed. Serve hot in [Coconut Bowls](https://buddhabowls.org/product/coconut-bowls/), garnished with fresh cilantro.
