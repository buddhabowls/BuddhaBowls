Aloha, friends! If you’re dreaming of palm trees and sandy beaches, I’ve got just the thing to transport you to paradise: Tropical Paradise Overnight Oats.

## Tropical Paradise Overnight Oats

This recipe is like a mini vacation in a bowl. I mean, who wouldn’t want to start their day with the flavors of pineapple and coconut dancing on their taste buds? It’s like a piña colada, but, you know, healthy and breakfast-appropriate.

To whip up this tropical delight, I mix rolled oats with creamy coconut milk and toss in some juicy pineapple chunks. The secret to making it extra special? A sprinkle of shredded coconut that adds a lovely texture.

Once it’s all mixed up, I let it chill in the fridge overnight. In the morning, I grab my trusty coconut bowl (because it just feels right) and serve it up with some mango slices and toasted coconut flakes.

Every spoonful is like a burst of sunshine, and I can’t help but smile as I eat it. So, if you’re looking to escape the ordinary and add a little tropical flair to your breakfast routine, give these Tropical Paradise Overnight Oats a try. Mahalo!

## 

**Course:** Breakfast

**Prep Time:** 5 mins

**Cook Time:** 0 mins

**Total Time:** 5 mins

**Servings:** 1

## Ingredients

- Rolled oats, coconut milk, pineapple chunks, shredded coconut

## Instructions

- Combine oats, coconut milk, and pineapple chunks. 

- Refrigerate overnight. 

- Top with mango slices and toasted coconut flakes.
