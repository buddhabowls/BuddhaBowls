Drift off to dreamland with this soothing herbal sachet.

😴 Sweet Dreams Ahead! 😴 Create a soothing herbal sachet with chamomile and lavender to help you drift off to dreamland! Place it under your pillow and let the calming scents work their magic! 🌙💤 #SleepWell #HerbalMagic

Ingredients:

– Dried chamomile

– Dried lavender

– A small cloth bag

Instructions:

1. Fill the cloth bag with chamomile and lavender.

2. Place it under your pillow or near your bed.

3. Breathe in the calming scents as you sleep.
