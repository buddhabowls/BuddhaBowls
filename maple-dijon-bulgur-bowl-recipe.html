Maple Dijon Bulgur Bowl

A hearty Maple Dijon Bulgur Bowl featuring roasted vegetables and a sweet tangy dressing, perfect for a nutritious meal.

Maple Dijon Bulgur Bowl Recipe

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 25 minutes

Total Time: 35 minutes

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 cup bulgur
  • 1/2 cup Brussels sprouts, halved
  • 1/4 cup red onion, sliced
  • 1/4 cup pecans, chopped
  • 2 tbsp maple dijon dressing

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. On a baking sheet, toss the halved Brussels sprouts and sliced red onion with a drizzle of olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast for about 20 minutes or until tender and slightly caramelized.
  3. While the vegetables are roasting, rinse the bulgur under cold water and cook according to package instructions (usually about 12-15 minutes).
  4. Once the bulgur is cooked, fluff it with a fork and let it cool slightly.
  5. In a bowl, layer the cooked bulgur with the roasted Brussels sprouts, red onion, and chopped pecans.
  6. Drizzle with maple dijon dressing before serving.

Nutrition
