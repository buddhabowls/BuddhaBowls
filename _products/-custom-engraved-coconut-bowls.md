Custom Engraved Coconut Bowls Original handcrafted 100% natural and made entirely from real rescued organic coconuts shells after they have been extracted for their meat and water in an effort to reduce waste. Billions of coconuts harvested each year for the coconut oil, water and flesh industries, see 99% of their shells discarded and burned as waste. Burning coconut shells not only misuses natural resources, it also contributes significantly to CO2 &amp; Methane emissions. Our Organization has been up-cycling these shells into beautiful sustainable bowls made from real coconuts just for you (and Mother Nature).
