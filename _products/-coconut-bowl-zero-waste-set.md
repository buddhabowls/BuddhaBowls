Introducing the bowl that started it all! Original handcrafted coconut bowls, 100% natural and made entirely from real rescued organic coconuts shells after they have been extracted for their meat and water in an effort to reduce waste. Billions of coconuts harvested each year for the coconut oil, water and flesh industries, see 99% of their shells discarded and burned as waste. Burning coconut shells not only misuses natural resources, it also contributes significantly to CO2 &amp; Methane emissions. With billions of plastic containers also ending up as landfill every year, it’s important that as a society we begin to reject plastic solutions in favor of eco-friendly alternatives. Our Organization has been up-cycling these shells into beautiful sustainable bowls made from real coconuts just for you (and Mother Nature). By purchasing our products, you are actively supporting global sustainability. Mother Nature thanks you, and so do we. Join the millions that have begun choosing natural solutions to plastic pollution. Joining the global movement that's making the world a better place one coconut at a time. Each coconut bowl has its own size, shape, pattern, and color that make it uniquely beautiful just like YOU!
