## Maple Pecan Overnight Oats

This recipe is my tribute to the irresistible combination of [Maple Syrup](https://amzn.to/3KUS5oa) and pecans. It’s like having a stack of pancakes in a bowl, but without the hassle of flipping.

To create this nutty delight, I mix rolled oats with oat milk, a generous drizzle of [Maple Syrup](https://amzn.to/3KUS5oa), and a handful of chopped pecans. After a good stir, it’s time to let the fridge do its thing overnight.

In the morning, I serve it in a coconut bowl (because it makes me feel like I’m at a fancy brunch) and top it with toasted pecans and an extra drizzle of [Maple Syrup](https://amzn.to/3KUS5oa).

These Maple Pecan Overnight Oats are like a sweet hug for your taste buds, and they never fail to make my mornings a little brighter. So go ahead, treat yourself to a breakfast that’s as sweet as you are!

## Maple Pecan Overnight Oats

**Course:** Breakfast

**Prep Time:** 5 mins

**Cook Time:** 0 mins

**Total Time:** 5 mins

**Servings:** 1

## Ingredients

- Rolled oats,

- oat milk,

- [Maple Syrup](https://amzn.to/3KUS5oa),

- chopped pecans

## Instructions

- Combine oats, oat milk, and [Maple Syrup](https://amzn.to/3KUS5oa).

- Refrigerate overnight.

- Top with toasted pecans and a drizzle of [Maple Syrup](https://amzn.to/3KUS5oa).
