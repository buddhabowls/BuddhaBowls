Citrus Farro Bowl

A refreshing Citrus Farro Bowl featuring vibrant ingredients and a zesty dressing, perfect for a light and nutritious meal.

Citrus Farro Bowl Recipe

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Total Time: 40 minutes

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 cup farro
  • 1/4 cup oranges, segmented
  • 1/4 cup spinach, chopped
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp citrus dressing
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Rinse the farro under cold water and cook according to package instructions (usually about 30 minutes).
  2. While the farro is cooking, mix together the segmented oranges, chopped spinach, and sliced red onion in a bowl.
  3. Once the farro is cooked, fluff it with a fork and let it cool slightly.
  4. In serving bowls, layer the cooked farro with the orange mixture.
  5. Drizzle with citrus dressing and garnish with fresh mint leaves before serving.

Nutrition

Calories: Approximately 420

Protein: 12g

Carbs: 70g

Fat: 10g

Fiber: 8g
