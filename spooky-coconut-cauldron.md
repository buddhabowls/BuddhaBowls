## Spooky Coconut Cauldron

Get ready for a frightfully delicious meal! This Spooky Coconut Cauldron is packed with nutritious ingredients that will keep you energized for all your Halloween festivities.

## Spooky Coconut Cauldron Recipe

**Course:** Lunch

**Cuisine:** Healthy

**Servings:** 2

### Ingredients

- 1 cup quinoa, 1 cup black beans, 1 cup roasted sweet potatoes, 1 avocado, 1/2 cup corn, 1/4 cup cilantro, 1 lime (juiced), Salt and pepper

### Instructions

- Cook quinoa according to package instructions. Combine with black beans, roasted sweet potatoes, corn, and cilantro. Drizzle with lime juice and season to taste.

Add ghost-shaped tortilla chips for fun!
