ingredients:
  - 3 ripe bananas
  - 2 tbsp almond butter
  - 2 tbsp salted caramel sauce
  - pinch of sea salt

instructions:
  - Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
  - Place frozen bananas in a blender.
  - Add almond butter and salted caramel sauce.
  - Blend until smooth.
  - Serve immediately, topped with a pinch of sea salt.
