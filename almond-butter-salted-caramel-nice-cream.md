## 💚 Health Benefits
- Salt enhances flavor
- almond butter provides healthy fats.

## 🥗 Ingredients
- 3 ripe bananas
- 2 tbsp almond butter
- 2 tbsp salted caramel sauce
- pinch of sea salt

## 👩‍🍳 Instructions
1. Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
2. Place frozen bananas in a blender.
3. Add almond butter and salted caramel sauce.
4. Blend until smooth.
5. Serve immediately, topped with a pinch of sea salt.

## 💡 Tips
- Try adding a scoop of nut butter for an extra protein punch.
