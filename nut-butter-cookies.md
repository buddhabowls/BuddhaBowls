## Nut Butter Cookies

**Course:** Dessert

**Cuisine:** American

**Servings:** 12 cookies

### Ingredients:

- 1 cup nut butter

- 1 cup sugar

- 1 egg

### Instructions:

- Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).

- Mix all ingredients until combined.

- Drop spoonfuls onto a baking sheet and bake for 10 minutes.

NOTES: Great for a sweet treat!
