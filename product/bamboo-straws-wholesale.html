Care & Maintenance

Cleaning

  • Rinse immediately after use with warm water
  • Use the included cleaning brush for thorough cleaning
  • Hand wash with mild soap if needed
  • Do not use dishwasher
  • Avoid abrasive scrubbers

Drying & Storage

  • Air dry completely after washing
  • Store in a cool, dry place
  • Keep away from direct sunlight
  • Store upright in a container for easy access
  • Ensure completely dry before storage

For Business Use

  • Provide cleaning brushes to customers if offering reusable straws
  • Store in bulk containers for easy dispensing
  • Highlight eco-friendly benefits to customers
  • Consider offering as a premium service option
  • Educate customers on proper care and reuse
