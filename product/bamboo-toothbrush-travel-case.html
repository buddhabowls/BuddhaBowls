Care & Maintenance

Cleaning Your Case

  • Wipe down inside and outside with a damp cloth as needed
  • For deeper cleaning, use mild soap and water
  • Let air dry completely before storing
  • Avoid harsh scrubbers or abrasive materials
  • Do not soak or submerge for extended periods

Cleaning Your Toothbrush

  • Rinse thoroughly with water after each use
  • Ensure toothbrush is completely dry before placing in case
  • Replace toothbrush every 3-4 months for optimal oral health
  • Handle bristles gently to prevent fraying
  • Store in a cool, dry place between uses

Storage Tips

  • Keep case dry: Prolonged moisture can cause warping or cracking
  • Store in cool, dry place away from humidity
  • Avoid direct sunlight and excessive heat
  • Keep away from steamy bathrooms when not in use
  • Ensure toothbrush is dry before closing case

End of Life

  • When no longer needed, the entire case is compostable
  • Add to your home compost bin
  • Or place in green waste for municipal composting
  • No plastic waste—completely biodegradable
  • With proper care, case can last for years
