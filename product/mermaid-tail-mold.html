Care & Maintenance

Cleaning

  • Wash with warm soapy water immediately after use
  • Use a soft cloth or sponge to gently clean the mold
  • Pay special attention to the detailed scale areas
  • Rinse thoroughly to remove all soap residue
  • Pat dry with a clean towel

Drying

  • Air dry completely before storing or using again
  • Place on a clean, flat surface to dry
  • Ensure all moisture is removed to prevent mold growth
  • Can be hung or stored upright if needed

Storage

  • Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight
  • Keep away from extreme heat or cold
  • Store flat or upright - silicone won't warp
  • Keep in a clean container to prevent dust accumulation
  • Avoid storing near sharp objects that could puncture the mold

Longevity Tips

  • High-quality silicone is designed to last for years
  • Avoid using abrasive cleaners or scrubbers
  • Don't expose to temperatures above 400°F (200°C)
  • Handle gently to maintain intricate details
  • With proper care, the mold will remain in excellent condition
