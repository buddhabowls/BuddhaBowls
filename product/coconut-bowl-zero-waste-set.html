The Bowl That Started It All

The Problem

Billions of coconuts are harvested each year for the coconut oil, water, and flesh industries. Tragically, 99% of their shells are discarded and burned as waste. Burning coconut shells not only misuses natural resources, it also contributes significantly to CO2 and methane emissions. Meanwhile, billions of plastic containers end up in landfills every year.

Our Solution

We began up-cycling these shells into beautiful, sustainable bowls made from real coconuts. Each bowl is handcrafted from rescued organic coconut shells, giving them new life and purpose. By purchasing our products, you're actively supporting global sustainability and reducing plastic pollution.

Your Impact

Care & Maintenance

Cleaning

  • Hand wash with mild soap and warm water
  • Use a soft cloth or sponge to gently clean
  • Dry immediately with a soft cloth to prevent moisture damage
  • Do not soak for extended periods
  • Not recommended for dishwasher

Maintenance

  • Occasionally apply food-safe oil (mineral or coconut oil)
  • This maintains shine and prevents cracking
  • Apply oil every 2-3 months or as needed
  • Wipe excess oil with a soft cloth
  • Store in a cool, dry place

Utensil Care

  • Hand wash wooden utensils with mild soap
  • Dry immediately after washing
  • Avoid soaking for extended periods
  • Apply food-safe oil occasionally to maintain finish
  • Store in a cool, dry location

Bamboo Straw Care

  • Rinse immediately after use with the cleaning brush
  • Use the included cleaning brush for thorough cleaning
  • Air dry completely before storage
  • Store in the included pouch
  • Reusable for years with proper care
