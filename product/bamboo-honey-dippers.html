What is a Honey Dipper?

A honey dipper is a small utensil designed to scoop and drizzle honey with precision. Its unique design features grooves or ridges that help hold the honey until you're ready to release it. With a quick twist of the wrist, you can drizzle honey exactly where you want it—without the sticky mess of using a spoon or squeezing a bottle.

Care & Maintenance

Cleaning

  • Hand wash immediately after use with warm water and mild soap
  • Use a soft brush or cloth to gently clean the grooves
  • Rinse thoroughly to remove all honey residue
  • Dry immediately with a clean, soft cloth
  • Do not soak for extended periods
  • Never use a dishwasher - high heat can damage bamboo

Maintenance

  • Apply food-safe mineral oil occasionally (every 2-3 months)
  • This helps maintain the natural luster and prevent drying
  • Wipe excess oil with a soft cloth
  • Store in a cool, dry place when not in use
  • Avoid storing in humid environments

Important Notes

  • Avoid prolonged soaking in water
  • Do not expose to extreme heat or direct sunlight
  • Do not use in the dishwasher
  • Keep away from extended moisture exposure
  • With proper care, will last for many years

Storage Tips

  • Store in a cool, dry location
  • Keep in a drawer or utensil holder
  • Display in a decorative container for easy access
  • Avoid storing near heat sources or direct sunlight
  • Perfect for both storage and display
