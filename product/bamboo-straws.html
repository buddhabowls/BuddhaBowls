Care & Maintenance

Daily Cleaning

  • Rinse immediately after use with warm water
  • Use the included brush to clean the inside
  • Scrub gently to remove any residue
  • Rinse thoroughly with clean water
  • Stand upright to air dry

Deep Cleaning

  • Soak in warm soapy water for 15-20 minutes
  • Use brush to scrub the inside thoroughly
  • Rinse with clean water multiple times
  • For stubborn stains, use a mixture of baking soda and water
  • Air dry completely before storage

Storage

  • Store in a clean, dry place
  • Keep in a cup or container upright
  • Avoid prolonged moisture exposure
  • Keep away from direct sunlight
  • Store brush separately or in a pouch

Important Notes

  • Not recommended for dishwasher
  • Avoid prolonged soaking (more than 1 hour)
  • Do not use abrasive scrubbers
  • Inspect regularly for damage
  • Replace if any splinters appear
