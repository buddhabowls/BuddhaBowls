Environmental Impact:

Care & Maintenance

Cleaning

  • Hand wash immediately after use with warm soapy water
  • Use a soft cloth or sponge to gently clean
  • Rinse thoroughly to remove all soap residue
  • Pat dry with a clean towel immediately
  • Do not soak for extended periods
  • Not recommended for dishwasher

Maintenance

  • Occasionally apply food-safe mineral or coconut oil
  • This helps maintain the natural luster of the shell
  • Apply oil every 2-3 months or as needed
  • Wipe excess oil with a soft cloth
  • Store in a cool, dry place

Usage Tips

  • Safe for hot and cold foods and beverages
  • Perfect for smoothie bowls, salads, and grain bowls
  • Can be used for serving and entertaining
  • Ideal for plant-based foods and recipes
  • Complements all styles of home decor

Storage

  • Store in a cool, dry location
  • Keep away from extreme heat or direct sunlight
  • Display in a decorative holder for easy access
  • Avoid storing near moisture sources
  • With proper care, will last for many years
