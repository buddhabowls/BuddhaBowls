Candle Care & Safety

Burning Instructions

  • Trim wick to ¼ inch before each use
  • Allow wax to melt completely on first burn (up to 2 hours)
  • Never leave burning candle unattended
  • Keep away from drafts, fans, and air vents
  • Keep out of reach of children and pets
  • Burn for no more than 4 hours at a time

Cleaning & Reuse

  • Once candle is finished, clean the shell gently
  • Hand wash with warm, soapy water
  • Pat dry with a soft cloth
  • Repurpose as a decorative bowl or planter
  • Apply food-grade oil to maintain appearance

Storage

  • Store in a cool, dry place
  • Keep away from direct sunlight
  • Avoid extreme temperature changes
  • Store upright to prevent wax from shifting

Safety Warnings

  • Do not move candle while lit or wax is hot
  • Keep away from flammable materials
  • Never use water to extinguish the flame
  • Use a candle snuffer or blow out gently
  • Do not touch the shell while burning - it may be hot
