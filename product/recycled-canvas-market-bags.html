Care & Maintenance

Washing

  • Machine wash on gentle cycle with cold water
  • Use mild detergent to preserve fabric quality
  • Avoid bleach and harsh chemicals
  • For stubborn stains, pre-soak briefly before washing
  • Wash with similar colors to prevent color bleeding

Drying

  • Air dry completely after washing
  • Hang or lay flat in a well-ventilated area
  • Avoid direct sunlight for extended periods
  • Do not use a dryer - heat can damage the canvas
  • Ensure bags are completely dry before storage

Storage

  • Store in a cool, dry place
  • Keep away from moisture and humidity
  • Fold or roll bags for compact storage
  • Keep in a convenient location for easy access
  • Consider keeping bags in your car for shopping trips

Longevity Tips

  • Rotate between multiple bags to extend lifespan
  • Avoid overloading beyond reasonable capacity
  • Inspect regularly for any wear or damage
  • Repair small tears promptly to prevent larger damage
  • With proper care, bags will last for years
