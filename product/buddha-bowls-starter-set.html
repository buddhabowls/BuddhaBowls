Care & Maintenance for All Items

Coconut Bowls

  • Hand wash with mild soap and warm water
  • Avoid prolonged soaking to prevent warping
  • Air dry completely before storage
  • Apply food-grade oil occasionally to maintain shine
  • Can be used for hot and cold foods
  • Not recommended for dishwasher

Wooden Utensils

  • Hand wash with mild soap and warm water
  • Dry immediately after washing
  • Avoid soaking for extended periods
  • Apply food-safe oil occasionally
  • Store in a cool, dry place
  • Not recommended for dishwasher

Bamboo Straws

  • Rinse immediately after use with the cleaning brush
  • Use the included cleaning brush for thorough cleaning
  • Air dry completely before storage
  • Store in the included pouch
  • Can be hand washed or placed in dishwasher
  • Reusable for years with proper care

Travel Pouch

  • Hand wash with mild soap and water
  • Air dry completely
  • Store in a cool, dry place
  • Perfect for organizing and protecting your items
  • Can be used for storage or travel
