Care & Maintenance

Washing Instructions

  • Machine wash on gentle cycle with cold water
  • Alternatively, hand wash with mild soap and warm water
  • Use gentle detergent to preserve fabric quality
  • Avoid bleach and harsh chemicals
  • For stubborn stains, soak briefly before washing

Drying

  • Line dry completely after washing
  • Air dry in a well-ventilated area
  • Avoid direct sunlight for extended periods
  • Do not use a dryer - heat can damage the mesh
  • Ensure bags are completely dry before storage

Storage

  • Store in a cool, dry place
  • Keep away from moisture and humidity
  • Fold or roll bags for compact storage
  • Keep in a convenient location for easy access
  • Consider keeping one set in your car for shopping trips

Longevity Tips

  • Rotate between multiple sets to extend lifespan
  • Avoid overloading beyond 35 lbs capacity
  • Inspect regularly for any wear or damage
  • Repair small tears promptly to prevent larger damage
  • With proper care, bags will last for years
