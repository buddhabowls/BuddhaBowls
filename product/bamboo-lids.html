Care & Maintenance

Cleaning

  • Hand wash with warm, lightly soapy water immediately after use
  • Use a soft cloth or sponge to gently clean the surface
  • Avoid harsh scrubbers that may damage the finish
  • Do not put in the dishwasher

Drying

  • Pat dry with a soft cloth immediately after washing
  • Allow to air dry completely before storing
  • Ensure silicone gasket is completely dry

Storage

  • Store in a cool, dry place
  • Keep away from direct sunlight
  • Avoid prolonged exposure to moisture
  • Store upright or with gasket facing up

Maintenance

  • Apply food-grade mineral oil monthly to maintain shine
  • Rub oil in with a soft cloth and buff away excess
  • This helps preserve the bamboo and prevent cracking

Usage Tips

  • Can be used with both hot and cold foods
  • Avoid extreme temperature changes
  • Perfect for mason jars and glass containers
  • Straw hole lids convert jars into reusable beverage tumblers
