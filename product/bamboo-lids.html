Care & Maintenance

Cleaning

  • Hand wash with warm, lightly soapy water immediately after use
  • Use a soft cloth or sponge to gently clean the surface
  • Avoid harsh scrubbers that may damage the finish
  • Do not put in the dishwasher

Drying

  • Pat dry with a soft cloth immediately after washing
  • Allow to air dry completely before storing
  • Ensure silicone gasket is completely dry

Storage

  • Store in a cool, dry place
  • Keep away from direct sunlight
  • Avoid prolonged exposure to moisture
  • Store upright or with gasket facing up

Maintenance

  • Apply food-grade mineral oil monthly to maintain shine
  • Rub oil in with a soft cloth and buff away excess
  • This helps preserve the bamboo and prevent cracking

Usage Tips

  • Can be used with both hot and cold foods
  • Avoid extreme temperature changes
  • Perfect for mason jars and glass containers
  • Straw hole lids convert jars into reusable beverage tumblers

Frequently Asked Questions

What sizes do these lids fit?

Our bamboo lids are available in two sizes: Regular Mouth (70mm) and Wide Mouth (86mm). The straw hole version fits regular mouth jars. Please check your jar size before ordering.

Are these lids food-safe?

Yes! All our bamboo lids are treated with food-grade mineral oil and are completely safe for food storage. The silicone gasket is also food-safe.

Can I put these in the dishwasher?

We recommend hand washing only. Dishwashers can damage the bamboo and the silicone gasket. Hand wash with warm, soapy water and dry immediately.

How do I maintain the bamboo?

Apply food-grade mineral oil monthly to keep the bamboo looking beautiful and prevent cracking. Simply rub the oil in with a soft cloth and buff away any excess.

Are these lids durable?

Yes! Our bamboo lids are handcrafted from high-quality, sustainable bamboo and are built to last for years with proper care. They're backed by our 30-day satisfaction guarantee.

Do the straw hole lids work with all straws?

The straw hole diameter is 0.55 inches, which fits most standard straws including our bamboo straws. They work perfectly with regular and wide straws.
