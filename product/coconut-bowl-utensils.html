Care & Maintenance

Cleaning

  • Hand wash immediately after use with warm soapy water
  • Use a soft cloth or sponge to gently clean
  • Rinse thoroughly to remove all soap residue
  • Pat dry with a clean towel immediately
  • Do not soak for extended periods
  • Not recommended for dishwasher

Maintenance

  • Occasionally apply food-safe mineral oil
  • This helps maintain the natural luster of the wood
  • Apply oil every 2-3 months or as needed
  • Wipe excess oil with a soft cloth
  • Store in a cool, dry place

Important Notes

  • Avoid prolonged soaking in water
  • Do not expose to extreme heat
  • Do not use in the dishwasher
  • Keep away from extended moisture exposure
  • With proper care, will last for many years

Storage Tips

  • Store in a cool, dry place
  • Keep in a drawer or utensil holder
  • Display in a decorative container for easy access
  • Avoid storing near heat sources
  • Perfect for both storage and display
