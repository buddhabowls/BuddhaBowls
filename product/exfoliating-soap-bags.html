Care & Maintenance

How to Use

  • Insert your favorite bar of soap into the sisal bag
  • Secure the drawstring closure at the top
  • Wet the bag under warm water to activate the soap
  • Gently rub the bag over your skin in circular motions
  • Focus on areas needing extra exfoliation (elbows, knees, heels)
  • Rinse your skin thoroughly with water and pat dry

Drying & Storage

  • After each use, rinse the bag under water to remove soap residue
  • Hang the bag in a well-ventilated area to dry completely
  • Ensure complete dryness between uses to prevent mold or mildew
  • Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight
  • Keep in a bathroom caddy or hanging organizer

Maintenance Tips

  • Wash the bag occasionally with warm water and mild soap
  • Remove any soap buildup or residue
  • Allow to air dry completely after washing
  • Replace bags every 3-6 months or as needed
  • With proper care, each bag lasts several months

Customizing Your Experience

  • Adjust pressure during use to customize exfoliation intensity
  • Pair with soaps formulated for your specific skin type
  • Use gentle pressure for sensitive skin
  • Increase pressure for tougher skin areas
  • Experiment to find your ideal exfoliation level
