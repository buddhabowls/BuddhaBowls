Care & Maintenance Instructions

Cleaning

  • Hand wash with warm, soapy water
  • Use soft cloth or sponge
  • Avoid harsh scrubbers or abrasive materials
  • Do not use dishwasher
  • Rinse thoroughly after each use

Drying & Storage

  • Pat dry immediately after washing
  • Allow to air dry completely
  • Store in dry location away from moisture
  • Avoid prolonged soaking
  • Store in cool, dry place

For Business Use

  • Provide care instructions to customers
  • Recommend hand washing for longevity
  • Highlight natural variation in each bowl
  • Emphasize eco-friendly benefits to customers
