The Perfect Cup of Tea

1

Scoop Your Tea

Add your favorite loose-leaf tea to the infuser basket. Use approximately 1 teaspoon per 8 oz of water for optimal flavor.

2

Secure the Lid

Close the infuser securely to prevent leaves from escaping during steeping.

3

Add Hot Water

Place the infuser in your cup or teapot and add hot water. Temperature varies by tea type (175-212°F).

4

Steep & Enjoy

Let your tea steep for 3-5 minutes depending on your preference. Remove the infuser and savor your perfect cup!

Care & Maintenance

Cleaning

  • Rinse immediately after use with warm water
  • Use a soft cloth or soft-bristled brush to gently clean
  • For stubborn residue, soak briefly in warm soapy water
  • Rinse thoroughly to remove all soap residue
  • Pat dry with a soft cloth
  • Dishwasher safe on top rack for convenience

Maintenance

  • Food-grade stainless steel resists rust and tarnish
  • Rose gold plating is durable and long-lasting
  • Avoid abrasive scrubbers that may damage the finish
  • Do not use harsh chemicals or bleach
  • With proper care, will maintain its beauty for years

Storage

  • Store in a cool, dry place
  • Keep away from direct sunlight to preserve finish
  • Store in a drawer or tea caddy for protection
  • Ensure completely dry before storing
  • Compact size makes storage easy

Tips for Best Results

  • Use filtered water for the best tea flavor
  • Adjust steeping time based on tea type and preference
  • Use approximately 1 teaspoon of loose-leaf tea per 8 oz of water
  • Remove infuser promptly to prevent over-steeping
  • Experiment with different teas to find your favorites
