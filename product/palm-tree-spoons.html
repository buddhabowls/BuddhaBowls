Care & Maintenance

Cleaning

  • Hand wash with mild soap and warm water
  • Use a soft cloth or sponge to clean
  • Avoid abrasive scrubbers or harsh chemicals
  • Dry immediately after washing
  • For stubborn residue, soak briefly in warm soapy water
  • Gently wipe dry with a soft cloth

Maintaining the Finish

  • Store in a dry location away from moisture
  • Avoid prolonged exposure to water
  • Keep away from extreme heat
  • Periodically polish with a soft cloth to maintain shine
  • Avoid stacking with other metals to prevent scratching
  • Store in a utensil organizer or drawer

Important Notes

  • Not recommended for dishwasher use
  • Avoid prolonged soaking in water
  • Do not use with acidic foods for extended periods
  • Keep away from harsh chemicals and bleach
  • With proper care, spoons will maintain beauty for years

Storage Tips

  • Store in a cool, dry place
  • Use a utensil organizer or drawer divider
  • Keep away from direct sunlight
  • Avoid storing with rubber bands or tight containers
  • Consider using a soft cloth to wrap for storage
