Care Instructions

  • Machine wash on gentle cycle or hand wash
  • Line dry completely before storage
  • Keep away from direct sunlight
  • Holds up to 35 lbs

Bamboo Toothbrush

  • Rinse with water after each use
  • Store in a dry toothbrush holder
  • Replace every 3-4 months
  • 100% biodegradable

Coconut Bowls

  • Hand wash with warm, soapy water
  • Avoid prolonged soaking
  • Air dry completely
  • Apply food-grade oil monthly
