Care & Maintenance

Cleaning

  • Wipe down with a damp cloth to remove dust or residue
  • Use mild soap and warm water for deeper cleaning
  • Dry immediately with a soft cloth
  • Avoid using harsh scrubbers or abrasive materials
  • For push-to-dispense models, gently clean the mechanism

Maintenance

  • Apply a light coat of food-safe mineral oil occasionally
  • This helps preserve natural luster and extend lifespan
  • Oil every 2-3 months or as needed
  • Wipe excess oil with a soft cloth
  • Store in a cool, dry place when not in use

Important Notes

  • Avoid soaking in water to prevent warping
  • Do not expose to extreme heat or direct sunlight
  • Not recommended for dishwasher use
  • Keep away from prolonged moisture
  • With proper care, will last for years

Storage Tips

  • Store in a cool, dry location
  • Keep away from humid environments
  • Display proudly on your counter or table
  • Avoid storing near heat sources
  • Perfect for both storage and display
