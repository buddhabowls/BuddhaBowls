Care & Usage

How to Use

  • Fill infuser with loose leaf tea
  • Place in cup or mug
  • Pour hot water (200-212°F)
  • Steep for 3-5 minutes
  • Remove infuser and enjoy

Cleaning

  • Dry completely before storage
