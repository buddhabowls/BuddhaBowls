The Wooden Matcha Cup: A Blend of Tradition and Elegance

The art of preparing and enjoying matcha is steeped in centuries of tradition, and the tools used play a crucial role in enhancing the experience. Among these tools, the wooden matcha cup stands out as a timeless and elegant choice for tea enthusiasts who value both functionality and beauty.

Crafted from high-quality, natural wood, the wooden matcha cup combines simplicity with sophistication. Its smooth, polished surface feels warm and inviting in your hands, creating a connection to nature with every sip. Unlike ceramic or glass alternatives, wooden cups add a rustic charm to your tea ritual, making the experience even more special.

Care & Maintenance

Cleaning Instructions

  • Gently hand wash with mild soap and water after each use
  • Use a soft cloth or sponge
  • Rinse thoroughly with clean water
  • Pat dry immediately with a soft cloth
  • Do not soak or leave standing in water

What to Avoid

  • Do not place in the dishwasher
  • Avoid prolonged soaking or submersion
  • Do not use abrasive scrubbers or harsh chemicals
  • Avoid extreme temperature changes
  • Keep away from direct heat sources

Maintenance

  • Periodically apply organic coconut oil to maintain finish
  • Store in a cool, dry place
  • Allow to air dry completely before storage
  • With proper care, the cup will develop a rich patina over time
  • This aging process adds character and tells the story of your tea moments

Natural Variations

  • All natural products - color and pattern variations are normal
  • Each cup is unique due to natural wood grain patterns
  • Variations add to the beauty and character of each piece
  • No two cups are exactly alike
