Care & Maintenance

Cleaning Your Brush

  • Rinse thoroughly with water after each use
  • Remove any food particles or debris
  • For stubborn residue, soak in warm soapy water for 10-15 minutes
  • Gently scrub the bristles with your fingers
  • Rinse completely with clean water

Drying & Storage

  • Squeeze out excess water gently
  • Stand brush upright to air dry completely
  • Store in a well-ventilated area
  • Keep away from direct heat or sunlight
  • Allow bristles to dry between uses

End of Life

  • When bristles wear out, the entire brush is compostable
  • Add to your home compost bin
  • Or place in green waste for municipal composting
  • No plastic waste - completely biodegradable
  • Typically lasts 3-6 months with regular use

Important Notes

  • Not recommended for dishwasher
  • Avoid prolonged soaking in hot water
  • Do not use with harsh chemical cleaners
  • Keep away from extreme heat
  • Replace when bristles become worn or frayed
