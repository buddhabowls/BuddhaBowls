## 🧘 Mindful Moment
> Savor the unique flavors of figs paired with almond butter. This recipe encourages you to appreciate the diversity of nature's offerings and the stories they tell.

## 💚 Health Benefits
- Figs are high in fiber and antioxidants
- almond butter adds protein.

## 🥗 Ingredients
- 3 ripe bananas
- 2 tbsp almond butter
- 1/2 cup chopped figs

## 👩‍🍳 Instructions
1. Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
2. Place frozen bananas in a blender.
3. Add almond butter and chopped figs.
4. Blend until smooth.
5. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.

## 💡 Tips
- Blend in some spinach for a nutrient boost without altering flavor.
