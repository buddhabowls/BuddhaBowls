## Maca Chia Pudding

**Course:** Breakfast

**Servings:** 2

### Ingredients:

- 1/4 cup [chia seeds](https://amzn.to/4q7GcdC)

- 1 cup almond milk

- 2 tbsp maca powder

- 1 tbsp [Maple Syrup](https://amzn.to/3KUS5oa)

### Instructions:

- Mix all ingredients in a bowl.

- Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight until thickened.
