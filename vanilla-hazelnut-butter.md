## Vanilla Hazelnut Butter

**Course:** Spread

**Cuisine:** American

**Prep Time:**

**Servings:** 1 cup

### Ingredients:

- 2 cups hazelnuts

- 1/2 tsp salt

- 1 tsp [Vanilla Extract](https://amzn.to/4aIhdue)

### Instructions:

- Roast hazelnuts at 350°F (175°C) for 10 minutes.

- Let cool, then blend in a food processor until smooth.

- Add salt and vanilla

- Blend until combined.

NOTES: Great for desserts and snacks.
