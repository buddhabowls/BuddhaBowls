An Asian-inspired bowl with soba noodles and sesame ginger dressing for a flavorful meal.

## Asian Sesame Ginger Bowl Recipe

 Course: Main

 Cuisine: Asian

 Prep Time: 10 minutes

 Cook Time: 10 minutes

 Total Time: 20 minutes

 Servings: 2
 
## Ingredients

- 1 cup cooked soba noodles
- 1/2 cup shredded cabbage
- 1/2 cup edamame
- 1/4 cup carrots
- 1/4 cup green onions
- 2 tbsp [Sesame Oil](https://amzn.to/3XPoGi8)
- 1 tbsp [Soy Sauce](https://amzn.to/48Vf7mR)
- 1 tbsp ginger
- 1 tbsp sesame seeds

## Instructions

- Cook soba noodles;
- Julienne carrots;
- Combine noodles, cabbage, edamame, carrots, onions in a bowl;
- Drizzle with [Sesame Oil](https://amzn.to/3XPoGi8), [Soy Sauce](https://amzn.to/48Vf7mR), and ginger;
- Sprinkle sesame seeds

Diet: Gluten-Free, Vegan
