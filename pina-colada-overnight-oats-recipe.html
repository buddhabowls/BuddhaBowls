Pina Colada Overnight Oats

A tropical twist on overnight oats with the flavors of pina colada, served in a coconut bowl for a fun and tasty breakfast.

Pina Colada Overnight Oats Recipe

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Cook Time: 0 minutes

Total Time: 8 hours

Servings: 1

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup fresh pineapple, diced
  • 1/2 teaspoon rum extract
  • Optional toppings: shredded coconut, additional pineapple, or nuts

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the rolled oats, coconut milk, diced pineapple, and rum extract. Stir well to combine.
  2. Transfer the mixture to a coconut bowl or jar, cover, and refrigerate overnight (or for at least 8 hours).
  3. In the morning, give the oats a good stir. If the mixture is too thick, add a splash more coconut milk to reach your desired consistency.
  4. Top with additional pineapple, shredded coconut, or nuts if desired, and enjoy your delicious pina colada overnight oats!
