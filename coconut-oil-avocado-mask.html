Coconut Oil Avocado Mask

🥑 Pro Tip: Use lemon juice in mix to prevent browning
⏳ Shelf Life: 24hrs in coconut bowl (press plastic wrap on surface)

🌿 Ingredients:

  • ½ ripe avocado (pit reserved)
  • 1 tbsp coconut oil
  • 1 tsp raw honey
  • Chilled coconut bowl
  • Cold jade roller (optional)

🍃 Ritual Steps:

  1. Mash avocado in coconut bowl until creamy
  2. Mix in coconut oil and honey thoroughly
  3. Add essential oils last to preserve nutrients
  4. Apply thick layer with avocado pit as applicator
  5. Leave on 15-20 minutes (store mask in fridge)
  6. Massage off with coconut oil-soaked cloth
  7. Finish with cold jade roller session

🌱 Zero-Waste Hack:
• Use avocado pit as natural exfoliator
• Compost leftover mask in garden
• Clean coconut bowl with coffee grounds scrub
• Store tools in coconut shell between uses
