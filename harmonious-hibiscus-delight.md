## Harmonious Hibiscus Delight Recipe

Course: Dessert
Cuisine: Vegan
Servings: 2

## Ingredients

- 2 cups frozen hibiscus tea ice cubes,

- 1 banana,

- 1/2 cup almond milk,

- Toppings:

- edible flowers,

- berries

## Instructions

- Blend hibiscus ice cubes, banana, and almond milk until smooth.

- Serve in [Coconut Bowls](https://buddhabowls.org/product/coconut-bowls/) and top with flowers and berries.

Hibiscus tea can be brewed fresh for a stronger flavor.
