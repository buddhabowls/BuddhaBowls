## Intention-Filled Crystal Bracelet

Ingredients:

– Beads (choose your favorite crystals)

– Elastic cord

– Scissors

Instructions:

1. String the beads onto the elastic cord.

2. Tie a knot and wear it as a reminder of your intentions.

3. Charge it with your energy by holding it in your hands.
