## Mango Coconut Overnight Oats 

To create this tropical delight, I mix rolled oats with creamy coconut milk and a generous helping of mango puree. After a good stir, I let it chill in the fridge overnight.

Come morning, I serve it up in a coconut bowl (because it just feels right) and top it with mango chunks and shredded coconut.

Every spoonful is like a burst of sunshine, and I can’t help but smile as I eat it. So if you’re ready to escape the ordinary and add a little tropical flair to your breakfast routine, give these Mango Coconut Overnight Oats a try. Mahalo!

## Mango Coconut Overnight Oats 

**Course:** Breakfast

**Prep Time:** 5 mins

**Cook Time:** 0 mins

**Total Time:** 5 mins

**Servings:** 1

## Ingredients

- Rolled oats, coconut milk, mango puree

## Instructions

- Combine oats, coconut milk, and mango puree. Refrigerate overnight. Top with mango chunks and shredded coconut.
