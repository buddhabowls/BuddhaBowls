## Radiant Raspberry Dream Recipe

Course: Dessert
Cuisine: Vegan
Servings: 2

## Ingredients

- 2 cups frozen raspberries,

- 1 banana,

- 1/2 cup almond milk,

- 1 tbsp [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke),

- Toppings: dark chocolate shavings, berries

## Instructions

- Blend raspberries, banana, almond milk, and [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke) until smooth.

- Serve in [Coconut Bowls](https://buddhabowls.org/product/coconut-bowls/) and top with chocolate and extra berries.

Frozen bananas work well for added creaminess.
