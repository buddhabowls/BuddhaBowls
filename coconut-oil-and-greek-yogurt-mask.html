Coconut Oil & Greek Yogurt Mask

🦠 Microbial Magic:
• Live yogurt cultures balance skin biome
• Coconut bowl's lauric acid boosts preservation
• Chilled application reduces inflammation

🥄 Materials Needed:

  • 2 tbsp (30g) Greek yogurt
  • 1 tbsp (15ml) coconut oil
  • Pre-chilled coconut bowl
  • Silicone mask brush
  • Cold compress (optional)

🧊 Preparation Steps:

  1. Chill coconut bowl in refrigerator 30 minutes
  2. In chilled bowl, whip yogurt and coconut oil
  3. Gently fold in colloidal oatmeal
  4. Add essential oils last to preserve cultures
  5. Apply thick layer to clean face with brush
  6. Relax 15 minutes (use cold compress if sensitive)
  7. Rinse with cool herbal tea infusion

🌡️ Temperature Control:
• Maintain yogurt's probiotics with chilled bowl
• Discard unused mixture after 24 hours
• Freeze leftovers in coconut bowl ice cubes
• Thaw cubes as needed for quick applications
