## Affirmation Stones for Kids

Ingredients:

– Small, smooth stones

– Paint or markers

Instructions:

1. Have kids decorate stones with positive words.

2. Let them carry the stones as reminders of their strengths.

3. Encourage them to repeat the words daily.
