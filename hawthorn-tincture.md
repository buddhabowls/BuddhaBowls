## Hawthorn Tincture

### Ingredients:

- Dried hawthorn berries,

- Vodka (high-proof),

- Jar

### Instructions:

- Fill a jar with dried hawthorn berries.

- Pour vodka over the berries until covered.

- Seal and store in a cool, dark place for 4-6 weeks, shaking occasionally.

- Strain and bottle the tincture.
