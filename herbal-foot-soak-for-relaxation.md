## Herbal Foot Soak for Relaxation

### Ingredients:

– Epsom salt

– Dried herbs (like peppermint or chamomile)

– A basin of warm water

### Instructions:

1. Fill the basin with warm water and add Epsom salt and herbs.

2. Soak your feet for 20 minutes while relaxing.

3. Feel the tension melt away!
