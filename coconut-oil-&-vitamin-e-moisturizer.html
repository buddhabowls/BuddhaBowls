Coconut Oil & Vitamin E Moisturizer

🌅 Golden Hour Ritual Tip:
Prepare at sunset for maximum relaxation. The warm golden hue enhances the sensory experience while vitamin E works overnight to repair daily damage.

🥥 Sacred Ingredients:

  • ¼ cup virgin coconut oil
  • 2 vitamin E capsules (400IU each)
  • 1 tbsp jojoba oil
  • 5 drops lavender essential oil
  • Coconut bowl + bamboo stirrer
  • Amber glass jar

🧘♀️ Preparation Ritual:

  1. Place coconut bowl in warm water bath (40°C)
  2. Add coconut oil to bowl - watch it melt into liquid gold
  3. Pierce vitamin E capsules and squeeze contents into oil
  4. Stir clockwise 7 times with bamboo stick
  5. Add jojoba oil while whispering self-love affirmations
  6. Finish with lavender oil - inhale deeply 3 times
  7. Pour into amber jar while still warm
  8. Seal with intention for radiant skin
🌙 Moon Phase Application Guide:
• New Moon: Apply to face during meditation
• Full Moon: Full-body massage under moonlight
• Waning Moon: Focus on elbows/knees
• Waxing Moon: Daily facial hydration ritual

💫 Self-Care Enhancements:

  • Morning: Apply before yoga with gua sha massage
  • Evening: Use as meditation oil on pulse points
  • Bath: Add 1 tsp to bathwater for silky skin
  • Hair: Use as overnight scalp treatment
  • Storage: Keep in coconut bowl for 1 week, then transfer to jar
