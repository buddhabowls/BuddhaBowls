## Manifestation Oil

Ingredients:

– Coconut oil

– Patchouli essential oil

– Bergamot essential oil – A small glass vial

Instructions:

1. Mix coconut oil with a few drops of patchouli and bergamot.

2. Shake well and say your intention aloud.

3. Use as a perfume or anoint your manifestation journal.
