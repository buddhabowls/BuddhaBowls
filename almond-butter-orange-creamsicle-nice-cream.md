## 🧘 Mindful Moment
> Transport yourself to summer with the nostalgic flavors of orange creamsicle. This nice cream is a cheerful reminder to enjoy the sweet moments.

## 💚 Health Benefits
- Almonds: good for skin
- Orange: high in Vitamin C, boosts immune function.

## 🥗 Ingredients
- 3 ripe bananas
- 2 tbsp almond butter
- zest and juice of 1 orange

## 👩‍🍳 Instructions
1. Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
2. Place frozen bananas in a blender.
3. Add almond butter, orange zest, and juice.
4. Blend until smooth.
5. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.

## 💡 Tips
- Try adding matcha powder for a green tea flavor.
