Coconut Chickpea Curry

A rich and creamy vegan coconut chickpea curry served in a coconut bowl, bringing the flavors of India to your table.

Coconut Chickpea Curry Recipe

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 25 minutes

Total Time: 35 minutes

Servings: 3

Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 2 medium tomatoes, diced
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat a splash of oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, sautéing until fragrant.
  2. Add the diced tomatoes and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in the curry powder, cumin, and turmeric, cooking for another minute.
  4. Add the chickpeas and coconut milk, stirring to combine. Bring to a simmer.
  5. Reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld. Stir occasionally.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot in coconut bowls, garnished with fresh cilantro if desired.
