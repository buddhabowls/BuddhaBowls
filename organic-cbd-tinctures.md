### Most frequent questions and answers

												[What is a ]()[CBD](https://mission-farms-cbd.sjv.io/LKJB43) tincture?

					Our ancestors have used tinctures in medicine for centuries. A tincture combines extracts with a base for oral use, cooking, or baking. Most commonly, CBD companies infuse alcohol with CBD oil to make tinctures. We use a less abrasive medium, such as organic extra virgin olive oil or organic medium-chain triglycerides (MCT) oil.

												[How are our ]()[CBD](https://mission-farms-cbd.sjv.io/LKJB43) tinctures flavored?
