This Ally McBeal’s Almond Bowl is a fresh and nutritious meal!

Get stylish with this Ally McBeal’s Almond Bowl! Packed with greens and flavors, it’s a great way to enjoy a healthy lunch. Perfect for a 90’s-inspired meal!

## Ally McBeal’s Almond Bowl Recipe

Course: Lunch
Cuisine: Healthy
Servings: 2

## Ingredients

- 1 cup mixed greens,

- 1/2 cup sliced almonds,

- 1/4 cup feta cheese,

- Olive oil,

- Balsamic vinegar

## Instructions

- Combine all ingredients in a coconut bowl and drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.

Add grilled chicken for extra protein!
