Hey there, pear lovers! If you’re a fan of warm, cozy flavors, you’re going to adore these Spiced Pear Overnight Oats.

## Spiced Pear Overnight Oats

This recipe is like having a slice of spiced pear pie for breakfast, but without the guilt and with all the deliciousness.

To whip up these oats, I mix rolled oats with pear juice, diced pears, and a generous sprinkle of cinnamon and nutmeg. After a quick stir, I let it chill in the fridge overnight, soaking up all those warm, spicy flavors.

Come morning, I serve it up in a coconut bowl (because it adds that extra touch of charm) and top it with sliced pears and a sprinkle of cinnamon.

Every spoonful is like a taste of autumn, and I can’t help but feel all warm and fuzzy inside as I eat it. So if you’re ready to start your day with a little slice of pear heaven, give these Spiced Pear Overnight Oats a try. You won’t be disappointed!

## Spiced Pear Overnight Oats

**Course:** Breakfast

**Prep Time:** 5 mins

**Cook Time:** 0 mins

**Total Time:** 5 mins

**Servings:** 1

## Ingredients

- Rolled oats, pear juice, diced pears, cinnamon, nutmeg

## Instructions

- Mix oats, pear juice, diced pears, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Refrigerate overnight. Top with sliced pears and a sprinkle of cinnamon.
