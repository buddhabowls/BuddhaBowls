## 🧘 Mindful Moment
> Transport yourself to a tropical paradise with this luscious mango almond butter nice cream. It’s a blissful treat that encourages you to embrace the moment.

## 💚 Health Benefits
- Almonds: supports brain health
- Mango: rich in vitamins A and C, boosts immunity.

## 🥗 Ingredients
- 3 ripe bananas
- 2 tbsp almond butter
- 1 cup diced mango

## 👩‍🍳 Instructions
1. Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
2. Place frozen bananas in a blender.
3. Add almond butter and diced mango.
4. Blend until smooth.
5. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.

## 💡 Tips
- Add a swirl of nutella for a decadent treat.
