ingredients:
  - 3 ripe bananas
  - 2 tbsp almond butter
  - 1 cup pitted cherries

instructions:
  - Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
  - Place frozen bananas in a blender.
  - Add almond butter and cherries.
  - Blend until smooth.
  - Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.
