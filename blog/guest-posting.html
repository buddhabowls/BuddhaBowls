Guest Posting: How To Get Featured

Guest blogging, also called “guest posting,” is the act of writing content for another company’s website. Generally, guest bloggers write for similar blogs within their industry in order to: Attract traffic back to their website.

How To Grow Your Brand Using Guest Posting

Guest posting is a fantastic way to grow your online presence and reach a wider audience. By contributing high-quality content to other websites, you can increase your visibility, drive traffic to your own site, and establish yourself as an authority in your niche. Here are some tips on how to effectively grow using guest posting:

1. Identify your target audience:

Before you start guest posting, it's important to have a clear understanding of who your target audience is. This will help you identify the right websites to contribute to and ensure that your content resonates with the readers.

2. Research potential websites:

Look for websites that are relevant to your niche and have a strong online presence. Consider factors such as domain authority, traffic, and engagement to determine which sites are worth contributing to.

3. Develop high-quality content:

When pitching guest post ideas to websites, make sure that your content is well-researched, informative, and valuable to the readers. Focus on providing actionable tips, insights, and solutions to common problems within your industry.

4. Craft a compelling pitch:

When reaching out to website owners or editors, it's essential to craft a compelling pitch that highlights your expertise and the value you can provide to their audience. Personalize your pitch for each website and clearly outline the topic you'd like to write about.

5. Follow the guidelines:

Many websites have specific guidelines for guest contributors, so make sure to familiarize yourself with these before submitting your pitch or article. This can include requirements for word count, formatting, and style.

6. Build relationships:

Guest posting is not just about getting your content published; it's also an opportunity to build relationships with other professionals in your industry. Engage with the website's audience by responding to comments on your posts and interacting with other contributors.

7. Promote your guest posts: 

Once your guest posts are published, make sure to promote them across your own social media channels and email list. This will help drive traffic back to the host website and increase your visibility.

8. Monitor your results:

Keep track of the performance of your guest posts by monitoring metrics such as traffic, engagement, and conversions. This will help you understand which websites are driving the most value for your business. By following these tips, you can effectively grow your online presence using guest posting and establish yourself as a thought leader in your industry. It's a long-term strategy that requires consistency and dedication, but the benefits in terms of increased visibility and credibility are well worth the effort.

Guest Posting Tips

Posts should be 1,000 – 1,500+ words in length We find this is a sweet spot with our readership, and you’ll fit in with the rest of our blog this way. Longer is good, too. Break up your content, so it’s easy to read. This means including headings, subheadings, bullets, and any other visual elements to increase the readability of your piece. Write in a natural, conversational tone. We want to share content that is authoritative, but not robotic. We’re producing blog content — not Wikipedia pages, so don’t shy away from showing off your sense of humor. That said, if your guest post was a movie, the highest rating we’d want to see on it is PG-13.

Guest Posting Promotion

Please feel free to promote your post through your social media, newsletter, or link to it from your blog. You can even include a snippet of the post on your blog, with a link back to the original content on our site (this is important so that we both avoid duplicate content penalties).
