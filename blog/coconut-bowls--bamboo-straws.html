The perfect pairing for mindful sipping and supping, our coconut bowls and bamboo straws embody eco-harmony.

Our Mission

Billions of coconuts harvested each year for the coconut oil, water and flesh industries, see 99% of their shells discarded and burned as waste.

Our Organization has been up-cycling these shells into beautiful sustainable bowls made from real coconuts just for you.
(and Mother Nature!) Our Mission Our coconut bowls are eco-friendly and sustainable alternatives to traditional bowls. They are made from real coconut shells that would otherwise be discarded as waste. These natural bowls are perfect for serving smoothie bowls, salads, and other delicious meals. Not only are they stylish and unique, but they also contribute to reducing environmental waste. Coconut bowls are a great way to add a touch of nature to your dining experience while promoting sustainability.

1. Use coconut bowls as a sustainable alternative to plastic or ceramic bowls for serving smoothie bowls, salads, or snacks.

2. Decorate coconut bowls with colorful patterns or designs to create unique and eco-friendly tableware.

3. Offer coconut bowls as a natural and biodegradable option for food vendors and restaurants looking to reduce their environmental impact.

4. Utilize coconut bowls for DIY crafts such as making candle holders, plant pots, or decorative storage containers.

5. Promote coconut bowls as a trendy and environmentally conscious gift or souvenir option for eco-minded consumers.
