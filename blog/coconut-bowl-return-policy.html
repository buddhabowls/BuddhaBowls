Embracing the principle of impermanence, our Coconut Bowl Return Policy ensures your contentment and allows for a harmonious exchange if needed. We understand that sometimes, despite our best intentions, a product may not align with your needs or expectations. Our return policy is designed with compassion and fairness in mind, aiming to make the process as smooth and stress-free as possible. If you find yourself unsatisfied with your purchase, you have 30 days from the date of delivery to initiate a return. We ask that you treat the return process with the same mindfulness you would apply to any aspect of your sustainable lifestyle. This means ensuring the bowl is cleaned and carefully packaged for its journey back to us. Upon receiving the returned item, our team will mindfully assess its condition. If the bowl is in its original state, we'll gladly process a full refund or exchange, whichever you prefer. We see each return as an opportunity for growth and learning, and welcome your feedback to help us continually improve our products and services. Rest assured, returned bowls are never wasted – they find new homes through our donation program to local community centers and eco-education initiatives.
