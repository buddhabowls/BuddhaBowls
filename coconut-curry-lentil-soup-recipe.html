Coconut Curry Lentil Soup

A hearty and flavorful vegan coconut curry lentil soup, served in a coconut bowl for an exotic touch.

Coconut Curry Lentil Soup Recipe

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Total Time: 45 minutes

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 cup red lentils
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, spinach)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat a splash of oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion, garlic, and ginger, sautéing until the onion is translucent.
  2. Add the curry powder and stir for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Stir in the red lentils, mixed vegetables, and vegetable broth. Bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 25 minutes, or until the lentils are soft.
  5. Pour in the coconut milk and stir well. Cook for an additional 5 minutes.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot in coconut bowls for an exotic touch.
