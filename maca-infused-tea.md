Adaptogenic Maca Tea Recipe | Stress-Relief Elixir

☕ 8min Total | 🌿 Adaptogen | 🧘♀️ Stress Support

This earthy infusion combines maca’s hormone-balancing properties with traditional herbal wisdom. Ideal for afternoon slumps or evening relaxation.

## 🍵 Maca Adaptogenic Tea

## 🍂 Ingredients

- 1 cup filtered water

- 1 tsp maca powder (gelatinized)

- 1 tsp raw [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke)

- ½ tsp cinnamon

- 1 tsp MCT oil (enhances absorption)

- Optional: Cardamom pinch/Reishi powder

## 🫖 Brewing Guide

- Use thermometer for optimal water temp

- Pre-mix maca with splash of water

- Whisk vigorously to prevent clumping

- Add fat source for nutrient absorption

- Steep covered for 5 minutes

### 🌱 Maca Benefits

- Supports adrenal function

- Rich in amino acids (18 types)

- Contains iron + vitamin C combo

- May enhance mood stability

### 💡 Preparation Wisdom

#### 🟠 Golden Milk Version

Add ¼ tsp [Turmeric](https://amzn.to/4aIhdsd) + black pepper

#### 🟣 Evening Relaxation

Mix with chamomile + ashwagandha
