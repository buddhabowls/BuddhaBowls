Cultivate gratitude with a focused meditation practice.

🙏 Cultivate Gratitude! 🙏 Take a moment to practice gratitude meditation! Focus on three things you’re thankful for and let the feelings of gratitude fill your heart! 🌈💖 #Gratitude #Meditation

Ingredients:

– A quiet space

– A comfortable seat

Instructions:

1. Sit comfortably and close your eyes.

2. Think of three things you’re grateful for.

3. Focus on the feelings of gratitude for a few minutes.
