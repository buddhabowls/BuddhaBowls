Pamper yourself with this delightful self-care ritual.

💆‍♀️ Pamper Yourself! 💆‍♀️ Treat yourself to a delightful self-care ritual with your favorite essential oils! Relax and rejuvenate your spirit with soothing scents! 🌿💖 #SelfCare #EssentialOils

Ingredients:

– Your favorite essential oils

– A warm towel

– A bowl of warm water

Instructions:

1. Add a few drops of essential oils to the warm water.

2. Soak a towel in the mixture and wring it out.

3. Place it on your face and relax for a few minutes.
