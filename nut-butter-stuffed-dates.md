Are you looking for a delicious and healthy snack that’s quick and easy to make? Look no further than nut butter stuffed dates! These little treats are not only tasty, but they’re also packed with nutrients to give you a natural energy boost. Whether you need a pick-me-up between meals or a sweet treat to satisfy your cravings, nut butter stuffed dates are the perfect solution.

To make nut butter stuffed dates, all you need are a few simple ingredients: dates and your favorite nut butter. You can use any type of nut butter you like, such as [Almond Butter](https://amzn.to/4oN8Mj6), peanut butter, or [Cashew Butter](https://amzn.to/4j45Z3Z). The combination of sweet, chewy dates and creamy nut butter creates a delicious flavor and texture that’s sure to satisfy your taste buds.

To prepare nut butter stuffed dates, start by carefully slicing each date lengthwise to create a small opening. Be sure not to slice all the way through the date – you want to create a pocket for the nut butter. Once you’ve sliced all of the dates, use a small spoon to fill each one with a generous amount of nut butter. You can also get creative and add additional toppings like chopped nuts, shredded coconut, or a sprinkle of cinnamon for extra flavor.

Not only are nut butter stuffed dates delicious, but they also offer a range of health benefits. Dates are naturally sweet and high in fiber, making them a great source of sustained energy. They’re also packed with essential nutrients like potassium, magnesium, and vitamin B6. Nut butter adds healthy fats, protein, and additional vitamins and minerals to the mix, making this snack a well-rounded option for anyone looking to fuel their body with wholesome ingredients.

These little bites are perfect for anyone with a busy lifestyle. Whether you’re on the go, at work, or just need a quick snack at home, nut butter stuffed dates are convenient and easy to take with you wherever you need to go. They’re also a great option for kids – they’ll love the sweet and creamy combination, and you’ll love knowing they’re getting a nutritious snack.

If you’re hosting a gathering or party, nut butter stuffed dates make a great addition to any snack table. They’re simple to prepare in advance, and they’re sure to be a hit with your guests. Plus, they’re naturally gluten-free and vegan, making them suitable for a variety of dietary preferences.

In addition to being a delicious snack on their own, nut butter stuffed dates can also be used as a versatile ingredient in other recipes. You can chop them up and add them to oatmeal or yogurt for a sweet and satisfying breakfast. They can also be used as a natural sweetener in homemade energy bars or blended into smoothies for added sweetness and creaminess.

So, whether you’re looking for a quick and easy snack, a healthy treat for your kids, or a versatile ingredient to add to your favorite recipes, nut butter stuffed dates are the perfect choice. With their delicious flavor, nutrient-packed ingredients, and versatility, they’re sure to become a staple in your kitchen. Give them a try and enjoy the natural goodness of this simple yet satisfying snack!

## Nut Butter Stuffed Dates

**Course:** Snack

**Cuisine:** American

**Servings:** 12 servings

### Ingredients:

- 12 medjool dates

- 1/2 cup nut butter

### Instructions:

- Slice dates open and remove pits.

- Fill with nut butter.

NOTES: Great for a quick snack!
