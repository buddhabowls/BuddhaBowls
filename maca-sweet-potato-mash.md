## Maca Sweet Potato Mash

**Course:** Side Dish

**Servings:** 4 servings

### Ingredients:

- – 2 tbsp maca powder – 1/4 cup milk – Salt to taste

### Instructions:

- 1. Boil sweet potatoes until tender. 2. Mash and mix in maca powder, milk, and salt.
