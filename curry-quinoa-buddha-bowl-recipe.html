Curry Quinoa Buddha Bowl

A vibrant and nutritious Curry Quinoa Buddha Bowl packed with flavor and healthy ingredients, perfect for a wholesome meal.

Curry Quinoa Buddha Bowl Recipe

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 25 minutes

Total Time: 35 minutes

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1/2 cup chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1/4 cup roasted cauliflower
  • 1/4 cup fresh spinach
  • 2 tbsp curry sauce
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Rinse the quinoa under cold water and cook according to package instructions (usually about 15 minutes).
  2. While the quinoa is cooking, roast the chickpeas and cauliflower in the oven at 400°F (200°C) for about 20 minutes, tossing with curry sauce halfway through.
  3. Once the quinoa is cooked, fluff it with a fork and let it cool slightly.
  4. In a bowl, layer the cooked quinoa with roasted chickpeas, cauliflower, and fresh spinach.
  5. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.

Nutrition

Calories: Approximately 450

Protein: 15g

Carbs: 70g

Fat: 10g

Fiber: 12g
