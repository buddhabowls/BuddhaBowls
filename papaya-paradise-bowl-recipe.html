🥭 Papaya Paradise Smoothie Bowl

🌴 Tropical Breakfast Bliss

This vibrant bowl combines the digestive benefits of papaya with the creaminess of coconut milk, sweetened naturally with honey. Perfect for summer mornings or post-workout refreshment!

📝 Ingredients

  • 2 cups ripe papaya (cubed)
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp fresh lime juice
  • 1 tbsp raw honey
  • 1/2 cup frozen mango
  • Toppings: Granola, chia seeds, coconut flakes, mint

👩🍳 Preparation

  1. Blend base ingredients until smooth
  2. Pour into chilled bowls
  3. Arrange toppings artistically
  4. Drizzle with extra honey (optional)
