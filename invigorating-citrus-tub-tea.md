## Invigorating Citrus Tub Tea

Print Recipe

### Ingredients:

- Dried orange peels, Dried lemon peels, Epsom salt, Rosemary

### Instructions:

- Mix 1 cup of dried orange peels and 1/2 cup of dried lemon peels with 1/2 cup of Epsom salt and 1 tablespoon of dried rosemary.

- Place in a muslin bag and hang under the faucet as the tub fills.

- Soak for 20-30 minutes.
