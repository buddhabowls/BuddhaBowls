Almond Butter Almond Joy Nice Cream

Servings: 2
Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 0 mins
Total Time: 10 mins
Course: Dessert
Cuisine: American

Inspired by the classic candy bar, this dessert features almond butter, chocolate, and coconut for a delightful treat.

🥗

Health Benefits

  • Almonds: nutrient-rich
  • Coconut: aids in digestion
  • Dark chocolate: rich in antioxidants.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 2 tbsp almond butter
  • 1/4 cup shredded coconut
  • 2 tbsp chocolate chips
  • 2 tbsp chopped almonds

Instructions

  1. 1

    Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.

  2. 2

    Place frozen bananas in a blender.

  3. 3

    Add almond butter and shredded coconut.

  4. 4

    Blend until smooth.

  5. 5

    Stir in chocolate chips and chopped almonds.

  6. 6

    Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.

Chef Tips

Use frozen bananas for a creamy texture.

Mindful Moment

Revel in the classic flavors of chocolate and coconut with this almond butter nice cream. It’s a nostalgic treat that invites you to enjoy every moment.
