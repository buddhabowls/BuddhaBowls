## Herbal Incense for Meditation

### Ingredients:

– Dried herbs (like sandalwood or lavender)

– Charcoal discs

### Instructions:

1. Crush the dried herbs into a fine powder.

2. Place on a lit charcoal disc in a fire-safe bowl.

3. Enjoy the aromatic experience during meditation.
