Mango Black Rice Bowl

A vibrant and nutritious Mango Black Rice Bowl that combines sweet mango, crunchy vegetables, and creamy coconut dressing for a delightful meal.

Mango Black Rice Bowl Recipe

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Total Time: 40 minutes

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 cup black rice
  • 1/4 cup mango chunks
  • 1/4 cup bell peppers, diced
  • 1/4 cup edamame, shelled
  • 2 tbsp coconut dressing
  • Lime wedge for garnish

Instructions

  1. Rinse the black rice under cold water and cook according to package instructions (usually about 30 minutes).
  2. While the rice is cooking, mix together the mango chunks, diced bell peppers, and shelled edamame in a bowl.
  3. Once the black rice is cooked, fluff it with a fork and let it cool slightly.
  4. In serving bowls, layer the cooked black rice with the mango mixture.
  5. Drizzle with coconut dressing and garnish with a lime wedge before serving.

Nutrition

Calories: Approximately 400

Protein: 12g

Carbs: 70g

Fat: 10g

Fiber: 8g
