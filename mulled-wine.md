# Mulled Wine

Warm the soul with our mulled wine recipe, a mindful indulgence for cool evenings.

Are you looking for a cozy drink to warm you up during the chilly winter months? Look no further than mulled wine! This delightful beverage is a favorite during the holiday season and is sure to be a hit at any gathering. In this post, we’ll explore what mulled wine is, its origins, and share some delicious recipes for you to try at home.

## What is Mulled Wine?

Mulled wine, also known as spiced wine, is a beverage typically made with red wine along with various mulling spices and sometimes raisins. It is served hot or warm and is perfect for sipping on a cold evening. The spices used can vary, but common ingredients include cinnamon, cloves, star anise, and citrus zest. Some recipes also call for additional spirits such as brandy or rum to add an extra kick.

## Origins of Mulled Wine

The tradition of mulling wine dates back to ancient times when the Romans would heat their wine and add spices to ward off the cold. Over the centuries, the practice spread throughout Europe and became especially popular during the Middle Ages. Mulled wine has been a staple of holiday celebrations in many countries and continues to be enjoyed around the world today.

## Mulled Wine Recipes

Now that you know a bit about mulled wine, let’s dive into some delicious recipes for you to try at home. Whether you prefer a traditional approach or want to experiment with unique flavors, there’s a mulled wine recipe for everyone.

### Classic Mulled Wine

Ingredients:

– 1 bottle of red wine

– 1 orange, sliced

– 8 whole cloves

– 2 cinnamon sticks

– 2 star anise

– 1/4 cup [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke) or sugar

– 1/2 cup brandy (optional)
Instructions:

1. In a large pot, combine the red wine, orange slices, cloves, cinnamon sticks, star anise, and [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke) or sugar.

2. Heat the mixture over low heat, stirring occasionally, until it is hot but not boiling.

3. If using brandy, stir it in just before serving.

4. Ladle the mulled wine into mugs or heatproof glasses, making sure to include some of the orange slices and spices in each serving.
### Spiced Cranberry Mulled Wine

Ingredients:

– 1 bottle of red wine

– 2 cups cranberry juice

– 1/2 cup fresh cranberries

– 1/4 cup [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke) or sugar

– 2 cinnamon sticks

– 6 whole cloves

– 1 orange, sliced

– 1/2 cup brandy (optional)
Instructions:

1. In a large pot, combine the red wine, cranberry juice, fresh cranberries, [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke) or sugar, cinnamon sticks, cloves, and orange slices.

2. Heat the mixture over low heat, stirring occasionally, until it is hot but not boiling.

3. If using brandy, stir it in just before serving.

4. Serve the mulled wine in mugs or heatproof glasses, garnishing each serving with a few extra cranberries and a cinnamon stick.
### Tips for Making Mulled Wine

– Use a good quality red wine for the best flavor. A medium-bodied red such as Merlot or Cabernet Sauvignon works well.

– Feel free to adjust the sweetness to your taste by adding more or less [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke) or sugar.

– If you prefer a non-alcoholic version, you can substitute the red wine with grape juice or apple cider and omit the brandy.

One of the best things about mulled wine is that it’s incredibly easy to make. All you need is a bottle of red wine, some spices, and a bit of sweetener. You can even add in your own twist by including additional ingredients like ginger, nutmeg, or even a splash of brandy for an extra kick. Simply combine all the ingredients in a pot, heat gently (but don’t boil!), and let the flavors meld together for about 20 minutes. The result is a delicious concoction that will have your guests coming back for more.
