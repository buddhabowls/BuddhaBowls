## Nut Butter Energy Balls

**Course:** Snack

**Cuisine:** American

**Servings:** 12 balls

### Ingredients:

- 1 cup nut butter (peanut or almond) 

- 1 cup oats 

- 1/4 cup [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke) 

- 1/4 cup chocolate chips

### Instructions:

- In a bowl, mix all ingredients until combined.

- Roll into balls and refrigerate for 30 minutes.

NOTES: Great for on-the-go snacks.
