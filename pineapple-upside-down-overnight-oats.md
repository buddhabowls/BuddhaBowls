## Pineapple Upside-Down Overnight Oats

This recipe is my ode to the classic pineapple upside-down cake. It’s like having your favorite dessert in a bowl, but without the guilt and with all the deliciousness.

To whip up these oats, I mix rolled oats with coconut milk and a generous helping of pineapple chunks. After a quick stir, it’s time to let the fridge work its magic overnight.

In the morning, I serve it in a coconut bowl (because it adds that extra touch of elegance) and top it with caramelized pineapple slices and a sprinkle of toasted coconut.

These Pineapple Upside-Down Overnight Oats are like a little slice of paradise, and they never fail to make my mornings a little sweeter. So go ahead, treat yourself to a breakfast that’s as indulgent as you are!

## 

**Course:** Breakfast

**Prep Time:** 5 mins

**Cook Time:** 0 mins

**Total Time:** 5 mins

**Servings:** 1

## Ingredients

- Rolled oats, pineapple juice, crushed pineapple

## Instructions

- Combine oats, pineapple juice, and crushed pineapple. Refrigerate overnight. Top with pineapple rings and maraschino cherries.
