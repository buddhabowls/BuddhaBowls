## Pink Pitaya Frozen Yogurt Recipe

**Prep Time:** 10 minutes

**Cook Time:** 0 minutes

**Total Time:** 4-6 hours (freezing time)

**Servings:** 4

## Ingredients

- 2 cups Greek yogurt,

- 3 tbsp pink pitaya powder,

- 1/4 cup [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke),

- 1 tsp [Vanilla Extract](https://amzn.to/4aIhdue),

- Pinch of salt,

- Toppings: fresh fruit, [Granola](https://amzn.to/4iPqYY5)

## Instructions

- In a bowl, mix Greek yogurt, pitaya powder, [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke), [Vanilla Extract](https://amzn.to/4aIhdue), and salt until well combined.

- Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to manufacturer’s instructions.

- If you don’t have an ice cream maker, pour the mixture into a freezer-safe container and freeze for 4-6 hours, stirring every hour.

- Serve with fresh fruit and [Granola](https://amzn.to/4iPqYY5).

## Notes

For a dairy-free version, use coconut yogurt instead of Greek yogurt. Experiment with different sweeteners like agave or [Maple Syrup](https://amzn.to/3KUS5oa).
