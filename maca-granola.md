## Maca Granola

**Course:** Breakfast

**Servings:** 4 servings

### Ingredients:

- – 2 cups oats

- – 1/2 cup nuts

- – 1/4 cup [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke)

- – 2 tbsp maca powder

### Instructions:

- Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).

- Mix all ingredients in a bowl.

- Spread on a baking sheet and bake for 20 minutes, stirring halfway.
