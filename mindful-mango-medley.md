## Mindful Mango Medley Recipe

Course: Dessert
Cuisine: Vegan
Servings: 2

## Ingredients

- 2 cups frozen mango

- 1 banana

- 1/2 cup almond milk

- Toppings: mint leaves, coconut flakes

## Instructions

- Blend mango, banana, and almond milk until smooth.

- Serve in [Coconut Bowls](https://buddhabowls.org/product/coconut-bowls/) and top with mint and coconut.

Add a squeeze of lime for a refreshing twist.
