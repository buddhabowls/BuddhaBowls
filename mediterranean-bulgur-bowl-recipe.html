Mediterranean Bulgur Bowl

A fresh and vibrant Mediterranean Bulgur Bowl packed with wholesome ingredients and flavors, perfect for a light meal.

Mediterranean Bulgur Bowl Recipe

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 25 minutes

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 cup bulgur
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup cucumbers, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onions, diced
  • 1/4 cup olives, sliced
  • 2 tbsp hummus
  • Fresh dill for garnish

Instructions

  1. Rinse the bulgur under cold water and cook according to package instructions (usually about 12-15 minutes).
  2. While the bulgur is cooking, dice the cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, and red onions.
  3. Once the bulgur is cooked, fluff it with a fork and let it cool slightly.
  4. In serving bowls, layer the cooked bulgur with the diced tomatoes, cucumbers, red onions, and olives.
  5. Top with hummus and garnish with fresh dill before serving.

Nutrition

Calories: Approximately 350

Protein: 10g

Carbs: 55g

Fat: 10g

Fiber: 8g
