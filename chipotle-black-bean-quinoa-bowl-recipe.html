Chipotle Black Bean Quinoa Bowl

A spicy and satisfying Chipotle Black Bean Quinoa Bowl that's packed with flavor and nutrients, perfect for a hearty meal.

Chipotle Black Bean Quinoa Bowl Recipe

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 25 minutes

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1/2 cup black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1/4 cup corn (fresh or frozen)
  • 1/4 cup bell peppers, diced
  • 1/4 avocado, sliced
  • 2 tbsp chipotle sauce
  • Lime wedge for garnish

Instructions

  1. Rinse the quinoa under cold water and cook according to package instructions (usually about 15 minutes).
  2. While the quinoa is cooking, mix the black beans, corn, and diced bell peppers in a bowl.
  3. Once the quinoa is cooked, fluff it with a fork and let it cool slightly.
  4. In a bowl, layer the cooked quinoa with the black bean mixture and top with sliced avocado.
  5. Drizzle with chipotle sauce and garnish with a lime wedge before serving.

Nutrition

Calories: Approximately 450

Protein: 15g

Carbs: 70g

Fat: 12g

Fiber: 10g
