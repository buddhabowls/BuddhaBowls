## 🧘 Mindful Moment
> Refresh your palate with the zesty lime and creamy coconut in this nice cream. It’s the perfect blend for a mindful moment of indulgence.

## 💚 Health Benefits
- Almonds: heart-healthy
- Lime: high in Vitamin C
- Coconut: aids in digestion.

## 🥗 Ingredients
- 3 ripe bananas
- 2 tbsp almond butter
- zest and juice of 1 lime
- 1/4 cup shredded coconut

## 👩‍🍳 Instructions
1. Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
2. Place frozen bananas in a blender.
3. Add almond butter, lime zest and juice, and shredded coconut.
4. Blend until smooth.
5. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.

## 💡 Tips
- Mix in some crushed nuts for added crunch.
