## Tranquil Tropical Treat Recipe

Course: Dessert
Cuisine: Vegan
Servings: 2

## Ingredients

- 2 cups frozen mango,

- 1 banana,

- 1/2 cup coconut water,

- Toppings: shredded coconut, pineapple chunks

## Instructions

- Blend mango, banana, and coconut water until creamy.

- Serve in [Coconut Bowls](https://buddhabowls.org/product/coconut-bowls/) and top with coconut and pineapple.

Use fresh mango for a more intense flavor.
