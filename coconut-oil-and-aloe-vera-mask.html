Coconut Oil and Aloe Vera Mask

🌴 You'll Need:

  • 2 tbsp (30ml) coconut oil
  • 1 tbsp (15ml) fresh aloe vera gel
  • 1 tsp (5ml) raw honey
  • Double boiler (optional)
  • Clean glass jar for storage

🍃 Preparation Steps:

  1. Gently melt coconut oil if solid (low heat/double boiler)
  2. Whisk aloe gel into warm oil until emulsified
  3. Mix in honey and essential oils if using
  4. Test on inner elbow - wait 15 minutes
  5. Apply to clean face with brush or fingers
  6. Leave on 15-20 minutes
  7. Rinse with cool water

💧 Pro Tips:
• Use within 3 days when fresh
• Avoid citrus oils if sensitive
• Best applied before showers for easy rinse-off
• Boost hydration with 1 tsp rosewater
