## Asian-Inspired Coconut Buddha Bowl

Dive into this vibrant Asian-Inspired Coconut Buddha Bowl! With its colorful ingredients and delicious dressing, it’s a perfect way to enjoy a healthy meal. The combination of soba noodles, fresh veggies, and sesame flavors will leave you feeling energized and satisfied.

## Asian-Inspired Coconut Buddha Bowl Recipe

**Course:** Lunch

**Cuisine:** Healthy

**Servings:** 2

### Ingredients

- 1 cup soba noodles

- 1/2 cup edamame

- 1/2 cup shredded carrots

- 1/2 cup red cabbage

- 1/4 cup sesame seeds

- 2 tbsp [Soy Sauce](https://amzn.to/48Vf7mR)

- 1 tbsp [Sesame Oil](https://amzn.to/3XPoGi8)

- Green onions for garnish

### Instructions

- Cook soba noodles according to package instructions. 

- In [Coconut Bowls](https://buddhabowls.org/product/coconut-bowls/), layer noodles, edamame, carrots, and cabbage.

- Drizzle with [Soy Sauce](https://amzn.to/48Vf7mR) and [Sesame Oil](https://amzn.to/3XPoGi8), and top with sesame seeds and green onions.

Try adding tofu for extra protein.
