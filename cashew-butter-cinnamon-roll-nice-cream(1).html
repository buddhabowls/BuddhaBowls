Cashew Butter Cinnamon Roll Nice Cream Recipe

A creamy vegan dessert combining the warm spices of cinnamon rolls with rich cashew butter, perfect for healthy indulgence. Ready in 10 minutes with just 4 simple ingredients!

Prep Time: 5 mins | Freeze Time: 6 hours | Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas (spotty yellow peels)
  • 2 tbsp organic cashew butter
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg
  • Pinch of sea salt (optional)

Instructions

  1. Slice bananas into ½" coins and freeze overnight on parchment-lined tray
  2. Add frozen banana coins to high-speed blender
  3. Top with cashew butter, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt
  4. Blend using tamper until smooth (about 2-3 minutes)
  5. Serve immediately in eco-friendly coconut bowls

Chef's Tips

  • Freeze bananas at peak ripeness for maximum sweetness
  • Use runny cashew butter for easier blending
  • Add 1 tsp vanilla extract for French toast variation
