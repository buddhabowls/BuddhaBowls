## Acai Granola Breakfast Bowl Recipe

**Course:** Brunch

**Cuisine:** Brazilian-inspired

**Servings:** 2

## Ingredients

- 2 frozen acai packets

- 1 banana

- 1/2 cup almond milk

- 1 cup mixed berries

- 1/2 cup [Granola](https://amzn.to/4iPqYY5)

- 2 tbsp [Almond Butter](https://amzn.to/4oN8Mj6)

- 1 tbsp [chia seeds](https://amzn.to/4q7GcdC)

- 1 tbsp [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke)

## Instructions

- Blend acai, banana, and almond milk. 

- Pour into bowls. 

- Top with mixed berries, [Granola](https://amzn.to/4iPqYY5), [Almond Butter](https://amzn.to/4oN8Mj6), [chia seeds](https://amzn.to/4q7GcdC), and a drizzle of [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke).

**TIPS:**

Add protein powder for a more filling meal. Use coconut milk for a tropical flavor.
