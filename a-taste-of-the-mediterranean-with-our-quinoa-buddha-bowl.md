## A Taste of the Mediterranean with Our Quinoa Buddha Bowl

Hey there, culinary explorers! Today, we’re diving into the fresh and vibrant flavors of the Mediterranean with a delicious Quinoa Buddha Bowl. This bowl is a delightful medley of fresh veggies, creamy feta, and zesty lemon, all served in a charming coconut bowl. Let’s get started! 

## The Inspiration Behind the Bowl

Imagine strolling along the sun-drenched coastlines of Greece, where the air is filled with the scent of fresh herbs and the sea breeze. That’s the inspiration behind this Mediterranean Quinoa Bowl. I wanted to capture those bright, refreshing flavors that remind me of my travels. 

## Crafting the Mediterranean Quinoa Bowl 

This bowl is all about balance—crunchy cucumbers, juicy tomatoes, and tangy feta, all brought together with a simple lemon dressing.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup cooked quinoa 

- 1/2 cup cucumber, diced 

- 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved 

- 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled 

- 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, sliced 

- 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced 

- 2 tablespoons lemon juice 

- 1 tablespoon olive oil 

- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

- **Cook the Quinoa:** Prepare your quinoa according to the package instructions and let it cool. 

- **Prep the Veggies:** Dice the cucumber, halve the cherry tomatoes, and slice the red onion. 

- **Assemble the Bowl:** In your coconut bowl, layer the quinoa, cucumber, tomatoes, feta, olives, and onion.

- **Dress It Up:** Whisk together the lemon juice and olive oil, then drizzle over the bowl. Season with salt and pepper.
