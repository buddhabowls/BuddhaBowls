## Strawberry Coconut Balls Recipe:

Now that you know the health and sustainability benefits of coconut, let’s dive into the delicious recipe for strawberry coconut balls. These delectable treats are not only easy to make but are also perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth in a healthy way.

### Ingredients:

– 1 cup of shredded coconut
– 1/2 cup of fresh strawberries
– 1/4 cup of almond flour
– 2 tablespoons of [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke) or [Maple Syrup](https://amzn.to/3KUS5oa)
– 1 teaspoon of [Vanilla Extract](https://amzn.to/4aIhdue)
– Pinch of salt
– Coconut bowls for serving

### Instructions:

1. In a food processor, combine the shredded coconut, fresh strawberries, almond flour, [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke) or [Maple Syrup](https://amzn.to/3KUS5oa), [Vanilla Extract](https://amzn.to/4aIhdue), and a pinch of salt.
2. Pulse the mixture until it comes together and forms a sticky dough.
3. Using your hands, roll the dough into small balls and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
4. Place the strawberry coconut balls in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes to allow them to firm up.
5. Once chilled, serve the strawberry coconut balls in sustainable [Coconut Bowls](https://buddhabowls.org/product/coconut-bowls/) for an extra touch of eco-friendly elegance.
