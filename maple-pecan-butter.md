## Maple Pecan Butter

**Course:** Spread

**Cuisine:** American

**Prep Time:**

**Servings:** 1 cup

### Ingredients:

- 2 cups pecans 

- 1/2 tsp salt 

- 2 tbsp [Maple Syrup](https://amzn.to/3KUS5oa)

### Instructions:

- Roast pecans at 350°F (175°C) for 8-10 minutes.

- Let cool, then blend in a food processor until smooth.

- Add salt and [Maple Syrup](https://amzn.to/3KUS5oa); blend until combined.

NOTES: Great for oatmeal toppings.
