Peanut Butter Chai Nice Cream Recipe

Overview

Servings: 2

Course: Dessert

Cuisine: American

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Freezing Time: Overnight

Nutritional Information

Calories: 240 per serving

Protein: 6g

Fat: 9g

Carbohydrates: 36g

Sugar: 14g

Fiber: 4g

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1/2 tsp ground cardamom
  • 1/4 tsp ground cloves

Instructions

  1. Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
  2. Place frozen bananas in a blender.
  3. Add peanut butter, ground cardamom, and ground cloves.
  4. Blend until smooth.
  5. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.
