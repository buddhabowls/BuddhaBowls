Colorful Buddha Bowl Recipes

Rainbow Veggie Bowl

Ingredients: Red cabbage, carrots, bell peppers, edamame, quinoa, sesame dressing.

This bowl is as colorful as it is nutritious, featuring a variety of fresh vegetables.

Tropical Buddha Bowl

Ingredients: Mango, avocado, black beans, brown rice, cilantro lime dressing.

Bring a taste of the tropics to your table with this refreshing and vibrant bowl.

Autumn Harvest Bowl

Ingredients: Roasted sweet potatoes, kale, cranberries, pecans, farro, maple vinaigrette.

This seasonal bowl is bursting with fall flavors and colors, perfect for cozy gatherings.

Why Color Matters

Eating a variety of colorful foods not only makes your meals visually appealing but also ensures you’re getting a range of nutrients. Each color represents different vitamins and minerals that are essential for your health.
