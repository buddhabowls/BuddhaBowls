## What Even Is Pink Pitaya Powder?

Okay, so here’s the deal: pink pitaya is another name for dragon fruit, specifically the hot-pink variety with white or magenta flesh. The fruit is freeze-dried and ground into a fine powder, creating a product that’s basically edible glitter for grown-ups. It’s packed with antioxidants, vitamins, and fiber, but let’s be honest—most of us are here for the color.

This powder is so pink it makes Barbie’s Dreamhouse look like it needs a fresh coat of paint. It’s the kind of pink that makes flamingos jealous. Add it to anything, and suddenly your food looks like it belongs on the Instagram feed of a lifestyle influencer who owns too many houseplants.
