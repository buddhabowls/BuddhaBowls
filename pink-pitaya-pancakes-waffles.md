Are you ready to add a pop of color and a burst of flavor to your breakfast routine? Look no further than these delicious pink pitaya powder pancakes and waffle recipes! 

Whether you’re looking to impress your brunch guests or simply treat yourself to a vibrant and tasty morning meal, these recipes are sure to hit the spot.

Pink pitaya powder, also known as dragon fruit powder, is not only visually stunning with its vibrant pink hue, but it also offers a subtle sweetness and a host of health benefits. Packed with antioxidants, vitamins, and fiber, pitaya powder is a fantastic addition to any recipe. Plus, it’s a fun way to incorporate more plant-based ingredients into your diet.

Without further ado, let’s dive into these mouthwatering recipes that will have you reaching for seconds (and maybe even thirds)!

## Pink Pitaya Powder Pancakes

Ingredients:
– 1 cup all-purpose flour
– 2 tablespoons pink pitaya powder
– 2 tablespoons sugar
– 1 teaspoon baking powder
– 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
– 1/4 teaspoon salt
– 1 cup buttermilk
– 1 large egg
– 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
– 1 teaspoon [Vanilla Extract](https://amzn.to/4aIhdue)

Instructions:
1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, pink pitaya powder, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, egg, melted butter, and [Vanilla Extract](https://amzn.to/4aIhdue).
3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. It’s okay if the batter is slightly lumpy.
4. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat and lightly grease with butter or cooking spray.
5. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown on the other side.
6. Serve the pancakes warm with your favorite toppings, such as fresh fruit, whipped cream, or [Maple Syrup](https://amzn.to/3KUS5oa).

## Pink Pitaya Powder Waffles

Ingredients:
– 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
– 3 tablespoons pink pitaya powder
– 3 tablespoons sugar
– 1 tablespoon baking powder
– 1/2 teaspoon salt
– 1 1/4 cups milk
– 1/3 cup vegetable oil
– 2 large eggs
– 1 teaspoon [Vanilla Extract](https://amzn.to/4aIhdue)

Instructions:
1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, pink pitaya powder, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, vegetable oil, eggs, and [Vanilla Extract](https://amzn.to/4aIhdue).
3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. It’s okay if the batter is slightly lumpy.
4. Preheat your waffle iron according to the manufacturer’s instructions and lightly grease with non-stick spray.
5. Pour the recommended amount of batter onto the waffle iron and cook until golden and crisp.
6. Serve the waffles hot off the iron with your favorite toppings, such as Greek yogurt, [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke), or a sprinkle of powdered sugar.
