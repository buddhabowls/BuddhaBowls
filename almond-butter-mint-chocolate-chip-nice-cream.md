## 🧘 Mindful Moment
> Refresh your palate with the coolness of mint and the richness of chocolate. This recipe invites you to embrace the joy of contrasts and the refreshing moments in life.

## 💚 Health Benefits
- Mint aids digestion
- dark chocolate is rich in antioxidants.

## 🥗 Ingredients
- 3 ripe bananas
- 2 tbsp almond butter
- 1/4 cup chopped mint leaves
- 1/4 cup mini chocolate chips

## 👩‍🍳 Instructions
1. Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
2. Place frozen bananas in a blender.
3. Add almond butter and mint leaves.
4. Blend until smooth.
5. Stir in mini chocolate chips.
6. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.

## 💡 Tips
- Top with shredded coconut for a tropical feel.
