Simplify your transaction karma. Pay easily with Cash App for a smooth, mindful purchasing experience.

It’s fast & simple to pay using Cash App. The #1 financial app in the United States.

## Mobile

To complete a Cash App payment for an online transaction on a mobile browser:

- Select Cash App Pay as your payment method during checkout. 

- You’ll be redirected to Cash App and your account will link automatically.

- Tap Continue. You’ll be redirected back to the website and your $Cashtag will display on the site’s checkout screen.

- Tap Place order on the site to complete the transaction.

## Desktop

To complete a Cash App payment for an online transaction on a desktop browser:

- Select Cash App Pay as your payment method during checkout to see your QR code.

- On your mobile device, log in to Cash App and tap the Payments ($) tab on your home screen.

- Tap the scan icon to open the QR scanner.

- Hold the scanner over the QR code to scan it. Your $Cashtag will then display on the site’s checkout screen.

- Select Place order on the site to complete the transaction.
