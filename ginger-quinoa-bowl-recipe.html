Ginger Quinoa Bowl

A delicious and nutritious Ginger Quinoa Bowl packed with flavor and vibrant ingredients, perfect for a healthy meal.

Ginger Quinoa Bowl Recipe

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 25 minutes

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1/2 cup tofu, cubed
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/4 cup snap peas
  • 2 tbsp ginger dressing
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions

  1. Rinse the quinoa under cold water and cook according to package instructions (usually about 15 minutes).
  2. While the quinoa is cooking, heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and sear the cubed tofu until golden brown, about 5-7 minutes, adding 1 tbsp of ginger dressing halfway through.
  3. Lightly steam the shredded carrots and snap peas until tender, about 3-4 minutes.
  4. Once the quinoa is cooked, fluff it with a fork and let it cool slightly.
  5. In a bowl, layer the cooked quinoa with seared tofu, steamed carrots, and snap peas.
  6. Drizzle with additional ginger dressing and sprinkle with sesame seeds before serving.

Nutrition

Calories: Approximately 450

Protein: 15g

Carbs: 65g

Fat: 12g

Fiber: 8g
