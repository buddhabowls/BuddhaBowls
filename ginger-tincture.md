## Ginger Tincture

### Ingredients:

- Fresh ginger root,

- Vodka (high-proof),

- Jar

### Instructions:

- Chop fresh ginger root and fill a jar halfway.

- Pour vodka over the ginger until fully submerged.

- Seal the jar and let it sit in a cool, dark place for 4-6 weeks, shaking occasionally.

- Strain and bottle the tincture.
