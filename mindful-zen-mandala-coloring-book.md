## Why Coloring Mandalas is So Relaxing

Coloring isn’t just for kids anymore. Adult coloring books have surged in popularity over the past few years, and for good reason. They offer a simple, yet powerful, way to practice mindfulness. Mandalas, in particular, are intricate circular designs that have been used for centuries in spiritual traditions to promote meditation and inner peace. When you color a mandala, your mind focuses on the patterns and colors, helping you stay present in the moment. It’s like a mini meditation session with a splash of creativity!
