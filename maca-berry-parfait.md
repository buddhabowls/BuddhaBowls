## Maca Berry Parfait

### Ingredients:

- 1 cup (240g) plain Greek yogurt

- 2 tbsp (15g) maca powder

- ½ cup (75g) mixed berries (fresh or frozen)

- ¼ cup (30g) [Granola](https://amzn.to/4iPqYY5)

- 1 tsp (5ml) [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke) (optional)

### Instructions:

- In medium bowl, whisk yogurt and maca powder until fully combined

In serving glass, layer ingredients in this order:

- Bottom: ⅓ yogurt mixture

- Middle: ½ berries

- Next: ½ [Granola](https://amzn.to/4iPqYY5)

- Repeat layers

- Top with remaining yogurt

- Drizzle with [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke) if using

- Serve immediately or chill up to 2 hours
