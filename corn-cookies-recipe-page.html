Corn Cookies Recipe

Delightful, sweet, and chewy corn cookies that everyone will love!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup corn kernels (fresh or canned)
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Add the egg and vanilla extract; mix well.
  4. In another bowl, combine cornmeal, flour, baking powder, and salt.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, mixing until just combined.
  6. Fold in the corn kernels.
  7. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  8. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the edges are golden.
  9. Let cool on a wire rack before serving.

Tips for Perfect Corn Cookies

  • Use fresh corn for a sweeter flavor.
  • Experiment with adding chocolate chips or nuts for extra texture.
  • Store cookies in an airtight container to maintain freshness.

Why You'll Love These Corn Cookies

These corn cookies are not only delicious but also easy to make. They are perfect for snacks, desserts, or even as a unique addition to your Buddha bowls. Enjoy the delightful combination of sweet and savory flavors!
