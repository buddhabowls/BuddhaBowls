## Maca Coconut Energy Bites

**Course:** Snack

**Servings:** 12 bites

### Ingredients:

- 1 cup dates

- 1/2 cup shredded coconut

- 2 tbsp maca powder

- 1/4 cup nuts

### Instructions:

- Blend all ingredients in a food processor until combined. 

- Roll into balls and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
