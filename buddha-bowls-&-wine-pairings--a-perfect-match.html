Buddha Bowl Recipes

Chickpea & Avocado Buddha Bowl

Ingredients: Chickpeas, avocado, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, tahini dressing.

This refreshing bowl is packed with protein and healthy fats.

Thai Peanut Tofu Buddha Bowl

Ingredients: Tofu, brown rice, broccoli, carrots, Thai peanut sauce.

A delicious blend of flavors that transports you to Thailand.

Greek Quinoa Buddha Bowl

Ingredients: Quinoa, olives, feta cheese, red onion, lemon vinaigrette.

This bowl offers a Mediterranean twist with fresh ingredients.

Wine Pairings for Buddha Bowls

Enhance your Buddha bowl experience with these wine pairings:

  • Sauvignon Blanc: Crisp and refreshing, it pairs well with the chickpea and avocado bowl.
  • Riesling: The sweetness balances the spiciness of the Thai peanut tofu bowl.
  • Pinot Grigio: A light and zesty option that complements the Greek quinoa bowl.
  • Chardonnay: A fuller-bodied choice that works well with richer ingredients like tofu and peanut sauce.
