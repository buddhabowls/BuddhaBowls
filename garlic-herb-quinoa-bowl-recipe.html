Garlic Herb Quinoa Bowl

A delicious Garlic Herb Quinoa Bowl packed with savory flavors and fresh ingredients, perfect for a healthy meal.

Garlic Herb Quinoa Bowl Recipe

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 20 minutes

Total Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1/2 cup sautéed mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup spinach, chopped
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 tbsp garlic herb dressing
  • Fresh thyme for garnish

Instructions

  1. Rinse the quinoa under cold water and cook according to package instructions (usually about 15 minutes).
  2. While the quinoa is cooking, heat a pan over medium heat and sauté the mushrooms with garlic herb dressing until tender, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. In a bowl, layer the cooked quinoa with the sautéed mushrooms, chopped spinach, and halved cherry tomatoes.
  4. Garnish with fresh thyme before serving.

Nutrition

Calories: Approximately 400

Protein: 12g

Carbs: 60g

Fat: 10g

Fiber: 8g
