Coconut Oil & Rosemary Hair Mask

🔬 Science Spotlight: Rosemary inhibits DHT (hair loss hormone) while coconut oil penetrates hair shaft
📆 Ideal Use: 2x weekly for 3 months minimum
🌡️ Temperature: 40°C (104°F) optimal for activation

🌱 Formula Components:

  • ¼ cup (60ml) fractionated coconut oil
  • 10 drops rosemary EO (diluted)
  • 1 tbsp (15ml) cold-pressed castor oil
  • Coconut bowl + dropper bottle
  • Scalp massager tool

💆♀️ Activation Ritual:

  1. Warm coconut oil in shell over double boiler (3 mins)
  2. Remove from heat - mix in castor oil and aloe vera
  3. Cool to 40°C before adding rosemary essential oil
  4. Section hair - apply using dropper along part lines
  5. Massage scalp 5 mins with ceramic tool
  6. Wrap hair in warm microfiber towel (20-30 mins)
  7. Rinse with cool water - no shampoo needed

📈 Growth Protocol:
• Track progress with monthly ponytail circumference checks
• Alternate with protein treatments weekly
• Combine with rosemary water spritz on off days
• Store mixture in amber dropper bottle (6 weeks max)
