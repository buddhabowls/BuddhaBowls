Greek Orzo Bowl

A delightful Greek Orzo Bowl filled with fresh ingredients and topped with creamy tzatziki, perfect for a light and satisfying meal.

Greek Orzo Bowl Recipe

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 10 minutes

Total Time: 20 minutes

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 cup orzo
  • 1/4 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup red onion, diced
  • 1/4 cup olives, sliced
  • 2 tbsp tzatziki sauce
  • Fresh dill for garnish

Instructions

  1. Rinse the orzo under cold water and cook according to package instructions (usually about 8-10 minutes).
  2. While the orzo is cooking, dice the cucumber, halve the cherry tomatoes, and dice the red onion.
  3. Once the orzo is cooked, drain it and let it cool slightly.
  4. In a bowl, layer the cooked orzo with the diced cucumber, halved cherry tomatoes, diced red onion, and sliced olives.
  5. Drizzle with tzatziki sauce and garnish with fresh dill before serving.
