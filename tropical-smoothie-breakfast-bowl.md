  - 1/2 cup coconut milk
  - 1/2 cup pineapple
  - 1/2 cup mango

  - 1 tbsp coconut flakes

  - Blend coconut milk, pineapple, and mango until smooth.
  - Pour into a bowl and top with granola and coconut flakes.
