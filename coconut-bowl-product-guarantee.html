What's NOT Covered

To ensure fairness to all customers, the following are not covered by our guarantee:

Normal Wear & Tear

Minor scratches, discoloration, or surface wear from normal use is considered natural aging of the material and is not covered.

Misuse or Abuse

Damage caused by dropping, throwing, or mishandling the product is not covered. Coconut bowls are natural products and can break if dropped.

Improper Care

Damage from improper cleaning (dishwasher use, harsh chemicals) or storage is not covered. Follow our care guide for best results.

Extreme Temperature

Damage from exposure to extreme heat, cold, or rapid temperature changes is not covered. Store in moderate conditions.

Engraved Items

Custom engraved or personalized items cannot be returned or replaced once engraved. Verify your design before ordering.

After 30 Days

Satisfaction guarantees apply only within 30 days of purchase. After 30 days, defect coverage may apply with proof of defect.

Natural Variations

Natural color variations, grain patterns, and minor imperfections are part of the charm of natural coconut shells and are not defects.

Used Items

Items that have been used, washed, or show signs of use cannot be returned. The bowl must be in original, unused condition.

What Constitutes a Defect?

We take quality seriously. Here are examples of what we consider manufacturing defects that are covered by our guarantee:

Structural Cracks

Deep cracks that go through the bowl or affect its structural integrity are considered defects. Surface cracks are normal in natural material.

Large Chips

Significant chips or missing pieces from the rim or sides are defects. Tiny edge imperfections are normal in handcrafted items.

Uneven Finish

Rough patches, splinters, or uneven finish that affects usability are defects. Minor texture variations are normal.

Food Safety Issues

If the bowl has toxic finishes or isn't food-safe as advertised, it's covered. All our bowls are certified food-safe.

Shipping Damage

Damage that occurs during shipping (broken bowl, crushed packaging) is covered. We pack carefully, but accidents happen.

Incorrect Item

If you receive the wrong item or size, we'll send the correct one immediately at no cost. This is always covered.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the guarantee valid?

The 30-day satisfaction guarantee applies from the date of purchase. Defect coverage extends to 1 year for manufacturing defects. Damage from misuse or improper care is not covered.

What if my bowl arrives damaged?

Contact us immediately with photos of the damage. We'll send a replacement right away at no cost. You don't need to return the damaged bowl first. We'll file a claim with the carrier.

Can I return a used bowl?

Bowls that have been used or washed cannot be returned for a refund due to hygiene reasons. However, if there's a defect, we may offer a replacement or partial refund.

Are natural color variations covered?

No. Natural color variations, grain patterns, and minor imperfections are part of the beauty of natural coconut shells. These are not considered defects.

What if I drop my bowl and it breaks?

Damage from dropping or mishandling is not covered by the guarantee. Coconut bowls are natural products and can break if dropped. Handle with care!

Can I return an engraved bowl?

Engraved items cannot be returned once customized. We provide a design preview before production so you can verify your engraving is correct.

What if I'm not satisfied after 30 days?

After 30 days, the satisfaction guarantee expires. However, if there's a manufacturing defect within 1 year, we'll address it. Contact us to discuss your specific situation.

Do I have to pay for return shipping?

No! We provide a prepaid return label for all returns. You won't pay anything for return shipping. For replacements, we ship at no cost to you.

How quickly will I get a replacement?

If your claim is approved, we ship your replacement immediately. Most replacements arrive within 3-5 business days. You don't need to return the defective item first.

What if I have a different issue?

Contact our support team! We're committed to customer satisfaction. Even if your issue isn't specifically covered, we'll do our best to find a solution.
