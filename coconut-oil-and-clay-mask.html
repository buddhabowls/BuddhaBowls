Coconut Oil & Clay Mask

🌍 Earth Connection:
• Clay harvested from volcanic ash beds
• Coconut bowl prevents metal interaction
• Wooden tools maintain clay's ionic charge

🏺 Materials Needed:

  • 1 tbsp (15g) bentonite clay
  • 2 tsp (10ml) coconut oil
  • 1 tbsp (15ml) apple cider vinegar
  • Coconut bowl set
  • Flat brush (natural bristle)

🌀 Ritual Steps:

  1. Sanitize coconut bowl with boiling water
  2. Combine clay and ACV in bowl - mix with wooden spatula
  3. Add melted coconut oil slowly while stirring
  4. Mix to smooth paste consistency
  5. Apply immediately with brush using upward strokes
  6. Let dry 10-15 minutes (avoid facial movements)
  7. Rinse with tepid water using circular motions

💆♀️ Application Wisdom:
• Use within 15 minutes of mixing
• Avoid metal utensils (neutralizes clay)
• Store leftover clay dry in coconut bowl
• Reactivate with coconut oil before next use
