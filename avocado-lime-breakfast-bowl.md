## Avocado Lime Breakfast Bowl Recipe

**Ingredients:**

– 2 ripe avocados
– 1 lime
– 1 cup of Greek yogurt
– 1 tablespoon of [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke)
– 1/4 cup of [Granola](https://amzn.to/4iPqYY5)
– 1/4 cup of mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries – take your pick!)
– 2 tablespoons of shredded coconut
– Fresh mint leaves for garnish

**Instructions:**

1. Start by cutting the avocados in half and removing the pits. Scoop out the flesh into a bowl and mash it with a fork until it reaches a smooth consistency.

2. Zest the lime and set the zest aside for later. Then, cut the lime in half and squeeze the juice into the mashed avocado. Mix well to combine.

3. In a separate bowl, mix the Greek yogurt with [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke) until it’s well incorporated.

4. Now it’s time to assemble our breakfast bowls! Start by spooning a generous amount of the avocado-lime mixture into each coconut bowl.

5. Next, add a dollop of the sweetened Greek yogurt on top of the avocado mixture.

6. Sprinkle a handful of [Granola](https://amzn.to/4iPqYY5) over the yogurt, followed by a generous handful of mixed berries.

7. To finish off, sprinkle some shredded coconut and the lime zest over the berries for an extra burst of flavor and texture.

8. Garnish with a few fresh mint leaves for a pop of color and freshness.
