## Lemon Air Freshener

### Ingredients:

- Lemon essential oil

- Water

- Spray bottle

### Instructions:

- Combine 1 cup of water with 15 drops of lemon essential oil in a spray bottle.

- Shake well before use.

- Spray around your home for a fresh scent.
