Coconut Oil & Egg Hair Mask

🧪 Science Breakdown:
• Egg white proteins fill hair cortex gaps
• Yolk lecithin emulsifies coconut oil
• Lavender EO increases blood flow (+31% oxygenation*)
• Honey's humectants lock in moisture

🥚 Formula Components:

  • 2 large eggs (55-60g each)
  • 3 tbsp (45ml) coconut oil
  • 1 tbsp (15ml) raw honey
  • Coconut shell + bamboo whisk
  • Thermal hair cap

🔬 Application Protocol:

  1. Whisk eggs in coconut bowl until frothy (2 mins)
  2. Melt coconut oil separately (40°C maximum)
  3. Combine ingredients using folding technique
  4. Apply to damp hair in three phases:
    • Phase 1: Scalp massage with egg mixture
    • Phase 2: Mid-lengths with coconut oil focus
    • Phase 3: Ends sealed with honey layer
  5. Process under thermal cap (20-25 minutes)
  6. Rinse with cool water (never hot!)
  7. Final cleanse with mild shampoo bar

💡 Customization Guide:
• Fine hair: Use egg whites only
• Curly hair: Add 1 tbsp yogurt
• Color-treated: Include 1 tsp ACV
• Damaged hair: Mix with 1 mashed banana
• Storage: 24hrs refrigerated in coconut shell
