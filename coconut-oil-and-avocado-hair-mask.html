Coconut Oil & Avocado Hair Mask

🧬 Cellular Repair Mechanism:
• Avocado's oleic acid rebuilds lipid layers
• Coconut oil penetrates cortex with MCTs
• Manuka honey's antioxidants prevent oxidative stress
• Macadamia oil mimics natural sebum

🌴 Formula Components:

  • 1 medium Hass avocado (200g)
  • ¼ cup (60ml) coconut oil
  • 2 tbsp (30ml) manuka honey
  • Coconut shell + stone mortar
  • Thermal processing cap

🧪 Application Protocol:

  1. Mash avocado in coconut shell using stone pestle
  2. Whip coconut oil and honey into emulsion
  3. Apply using three-temperature technique:
    • Room temp application to scalp
    • Warm mixture to 40°C for mid-lengths
    • Cool mixture for ends sealing
  4. Process under thermal cap (30-45 minutes)
  5. Rinse with cool rooibos tea infusion
  6. Air dry using tension-free method

🔬 Customization Matrix:
• High porosity: Add 1 egg white protein
• Curly hair: Include 1 tbsp yogurt
• Color-treated: Mix with 1 tsp ACV
• Dry scalp: Add 5 drops tea tree oil
• Storage: 3 days refrigerated in coconut shell
