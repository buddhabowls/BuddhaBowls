## 🧘 Mindful Moment
> Transport yourself to a tropical getaway with almond butter pineapple nice cream. Let the sweetness of pineapple remind you of the joy of adventure and exploration.

## 💚 Health Benefits
- Pineapple contains bromelain for digestion
- almond butter provides healthy fats.

## 🥗 Ingredients
- 3 ripe bananas
- 2 tbsp almond butter
- 1 cup pineapple chunks

## 👩‍🍳 Instructions
1. Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
2. Place frozen bananas in a blender.
3. Add almond butter and pineapple.
4. Blend until smooth.
5. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.

## 💡 Tips
- Add a splash of coffee for a mocha flavor.
