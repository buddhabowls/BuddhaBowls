Are you looking for a delicious and easy vegan butter recipe? Look no further! 

Making your own vegan butter at home is not only simple, but it’s also a great way to ensure that you know exactly what’s going into your food. Whether you’re vegan, lactose intolerant, or just looking to reduce your consumption of animal products, this homemade vegan butter is a fantastic alternative to traditional butter. Plus, it’s perfect for spreading on toast, using in baking, or cooking up your favorite dishes.

## Homemade Vegan Butter Recipe

### Ingredients:

– 1 cup refined coconut oil
– 1/2 cup unsweetened soy milk
– 1/4 cup refined avocado oil
– 1 teaspoon apple cider vinegar
– 1/2 teaspoon salt
– 1/4 teaspoon [Turmeric](https://amzn.to/4aIhdsd) (for color)

### Instructions:

1. In a small saucepan, melt the coconut oil over low heat. Once melted, remove from heat and let it cool for a few minutes.
2. In a [Blender](https://amzn.to/4oSJQHl), combine the soy milk, avocado oil, apple cider vinegar, salt, and [Turmeric](https://amzn.to/4aIhdsd). Blend on high speed until well combined.
3. With the [Blender](https://amzn.to/4oSJQHl) running on low speed, slowly pour in the melted coconut oil. Continue blending until the mixture is smooth and creamy.
4. Pour the mixture into a mold or airtight container and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or until firm.

### Tips for Making Vegan Butter:

– Use refined coconut oil to avoid a strong coconut flavor in your butter.
– Refined avocado oil has a neutral flavor and is perfect for this recipe.
– Feel free to adjust the salt and apple cider vinegar to suit your taste preferences.

Now that you have your homemade vegan butter, you can use it in all of your favorite recipes! Whether you’re sautéing vegetables, baking cookies, or simply spreading it on your morning toast, this vegan butter is sure to be a hit.

### Vegan Butter for Baking

If you’re specifically looking for a vegan butter that works well in baking, here’s a slightly different recipe that’s perfect for all of your baking needs.

### Ingredients:

– 1 cup refined coconut oil
– 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
– 1/4 cup refined avocado oil
– 1 teaspoon apple cider vinegar
– 1/2 teaspoon salt
– 1/4 teaspoon nutritional yeast

### Instructions:

1. Melt the coconut oil in a small saucepan over low heat. Once melted, remove from heat and let it cool for a few minutes.
2. In a [Blender](https://amzn.to/4oSJQHl), combine the almond milk, avocado oil, apple cider vinegar, salt, and nutritional yeast. Blend on high speed until well combined.
3. With the [Blender](https://amzn.to/4oSJQHl) running on low speed, slowly pour in the melted coconut oil. Continue blending until the mixture is smooth and creamy.
4. Pour the mixture into a mold or airtight container and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or until firm.

This vegan butter is perfect for all of your baking needs, whether you’re making flaky pastries, gooey brownies, or tender cakes. It’s a great way to ensure that your baked goods are not only delicious but also completely vegan.

Making your own vegan butter at home is not only easy and delicious, but it’s also a wonderful way to take control of what you’re putting into your body. With just a few simple ingredients and a little bit of time, you can create a creamy, flavorful vegan butter that’s perfect for all of your cooking and baking needs.
