## Intention Setting with Crystals

Ingredients:

– Intention crystals (like citrine or clear quartz)

– A small cloth

Instructions:

1. Hold your chosen crystal in your hand.

2. Set your intention clearly in your mind.

3. Keep the crystal with you as a reminder.
