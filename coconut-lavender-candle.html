🌺 Core Elements

  • 1 lb coconut-soy wax blend
  • 10ml lavender + 5ml coconut oil
  • 2 tbsp dried lavender buds
  • Sea glass container + braided wick

🌀 4-Step Ritual

  1. Melt wax with coconut oil (60°C)
  2. Stir in oils clockwise 5x
  3. Layer buds while pouring
  4. Cure 48hrs - patience = paradise

🌊 Pro Tips

  • Beach Vibe: Add sea salt sprinkles
  • Color Magic: Steep butterfly pea flowers
  • Moon Ritual: Charge under full moon
