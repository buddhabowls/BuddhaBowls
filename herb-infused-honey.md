## How to Infuse Honey

		Place your ingredients in the bottom of the jar and top with [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke).

Wait a few minutes, then stir it again and add more [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke).
 Continue until you’ve added all of your [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke).
