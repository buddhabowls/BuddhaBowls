## Coconut Tortillas Recipe

**Prep Time:** 20 minutes

**Cook Time:** 15 minutes

**Total Time:** 35 minutes

## Ingredients

- 2 cups coconut flour,

- 1/2 tsp salt,

- 1 cup warm water,

- Fillings: ground beef, cheese, lettuce, [Salsa](https://amzn.to/4aIhdue)

## Instructions

- Mix coconut flour and salt, gradually add water to form dough.

- Roll into tortillas and cook on a hot skillet.

- Serve with your choice of fillings.
