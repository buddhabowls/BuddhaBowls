### 1. Nourishing Buddha Bowl

**Servings:** 2
**Total Time:** 30 minutes

#### Ingredients:

- 1 cup [Brown Rice](https://amzn.to/4aLQJqd)

- 2 cups water

- 1 cup kale, chopped

- 1 cup roasted carrots, sliced

- ½ cup pumpkin seeds

- 2 tbsp tahini dressing

#### Instructions:

- Cook rice: Rinse [Brown Rice](https://amzn.to/4aLQJqd), then combine with water in a pot. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 45 minutes.

- Roast carrots: Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Toss sliced carrots with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast for 25 minutes.

- Sauté kale: In a pan, sauté kale in a little olive oil until wilted, about 5 minutes.

- Assemble bowls: Divide rice between two bowls. Top with roasted carrots, sautéed kale, and pumpkin seeds. Drizzle with tahini dressing.

#### Nutritional Breakdown (per serving):

- Calories: 480

- Protein: 15g

- Carbohydrates: 65g

- Fat: 20g

- Fiber: 10g

### 2. BBQ Tempeh Bowl

**Servings:** 2
**Total Time:** 30 minutes

#### Ingredients:

- 1 cup [Brown Rice](https://amzn.to/4aLQJqd)

- 2 cups water

- 1 block (8 oz) tempeh, sliced

- 1 cup cabbage slaw

- 1 avocado, sliced

- ½ cup BBQ sauce

#### Instructions:

- Cook rice: Rinse [Brown Rice](https://amzn.to/4aLQJqd), then combine with water in a pot. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 45 minutes.

- Cook tempeh: In a skillet, sauté tempeh slices until golden brown, about 5-7 minutes. Add BBQ sauce and cook for an additional 2 minutes.

- Assemble bowls: Divide rice between two bowls. Top with BBQ tempeh, cabbage slaw, and avocado slices.

#### Nutritional Breakdown (per serving):

- Calories: 500

- Protein: 20g

- Carbohydrates: 60g

- Fat: 20g

- Fiber: 8g

### 3. Breakfast Buddha Bowl

**Servings:** 2
**Total Time:** 15 minutes

#### Ingredients:

- 1 cup oats

- 2 cups almond milk

- 1 banana, sliced

- 1 cup mixed berries (fresh or frozen)

- ¼ cup almonds, chopped

- 1 tbsp [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke) or [Maple Syrup](https://amzn.to/3KUS5oa)

#### Instructions:

- Cook oats: In a saucepan, combine oats and almond milk. Cook over medium heat until creamy, about 5-7 minutes.

- Assemble bowls: Divide cooked oats between two bowls. Top with banana slices, mixed berries, chopped almonds, and drizzle with [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke) or [Maple Syrup](https://amzn.to/3KUS5oa).

#### Nutritional Breakdown (per serving):

- Calories: 400

- Protein: 10g

- Carbohydrates: 65g

- Fat: 15g

- Fiber: 10g

### 4. Pesto Zucchini Noodle Bowl

**Servings:** 2
**Total Time:** 20 minutes

#### Ingredients:

- 2 medium zucchinis, spiralized

- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved

- ½ cup basil pesto

- ¼ cup pine nuts

- ¼ cup Parmesan cheese (optional)

#### Instructions:

- Prepare zucchini noodles: Using a spiralizer, create noodles from zucchinis.

- Sauté noodles: In a skillet, sauté zucchini noodles for 2-3 minutes until slightly softened.

- Assemble bowls: Divide zucchini noodles between two bowls. Top with cherry tomatoes, basil pesto, and sprinkle with pine nuts and Parmesan cheese, if desired.

#### Nutritional Breakdown (per serving):

- Calories: 350

- Protein: 10g

- Carbohydrates: 30g

- Fat: 20g

- Fiber: 5g

### 5. Falafel Power Bowl

### 6. Southeast Asian Rice Bowl

**Servings:** 2
**Total Time:** 30 minutes

#### Ingredients:

- 1 cup jasmine rice

- 2 cups water

- 1 chicken breast, grilled and sliced (or tofu for a vegan option)

- 1 cup mango, diced

- ¼ cup cilantro, chopped

- Juice of 1 lime

- 2 tbsp [Soy Sauce](https://amzn.to/48Vf7mR)

#### Instructions:

- Cook rice: Rinse jasmine rice, then combine with water in a pot. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes.

- Grill chicken: Season chicken breast with salt and pepper, then grill until cooked through, about 6-7 minutes per side. Slice thinly.

- Assemble bowls: Divide rice between two bowls. Top with grilled chicken (or tofu), diced mango, and garnish with cilantro and lime juice. Drizzle with [Soy Sauce](https://amzn.to/48Vf7mR).

#### Nutritional Breakdown (per serving):

- Calories: 480

- Protein: 30g

- Carbohydrates: 65g

- Fat: 10g

- Fiber: 5g

### 7. Roasted Cauliflower Bowl

**Servings:** 2
**Total Time:** 30 minutes

#### Ingredients:

- 1 cup quinoa

- 1 cup water

- 1 head of cauliflower, cut into florets

- 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed

- 2 tbsp [Tahini Sauce](https://amzn.to/44p9Fau)

- ¼ cup parsley, chopped

#### Instructions:

- Cook quinoa: Rinse quinoa under cold water, then combine with water in a pot. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes.

- Roast cauliflower: Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Toss cauliflower florets with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast for 20 minutes.

- Assemble bowls: Divide quinoa between two bowls. Top with roasted cauliflower, chickpeas, and drizzle with [Tahini Sauce](https://amzn.to/44p9Fau). Garnish with parsley.

#### Nutritional Breakdown (per serving):

- Calories: 420

- Protein: 15g

- Carbohydrates: 60g

- Fat: 15g

- Fiber: 10g

### 8. Chili Lime Shrimp Bowl

**Servings:** 2
**Total Time:** 25 minutes

#### Ingredients:

- 1 cup [Brown Rice](https://amzn.to/4aLQJqd)

- 2 cups water

- 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined

- 1 avocado, diced

- 1 mango, diced

- Juice of 1 lime

- 1 tbsp chili powder

- Fresh cilantro for garnish

#### Instructions:

- Cook rice: Rinse [Brown Rice](https://amzn.to/4aLQJqd), then combine with water in a pot. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 45 minutes.

- Cook shrimp: In a skillet, sauté shrimp with chili powder and lime juice until pink and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes.

- Assemble bowls: Divide rice between two bowls. Top with chili lime shrimp, diced avocado, and mango. Garnish with cilantro.

#### Nutritional Breakdown (per serving):

- Calories: 500

- Protein: 30g

- Carbohydrates: 60g

- Fat: 15g

- Fiber: 6g

### 9. Vegan Taco Bowl

**Servings:** 2
**Total Time:** 30 minutes

#### Ingredients:

- 1 cup quinoa

- 2 cups water

- 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed

- 1 cup corn (frozen or canned)

- 1 avocado, diced

- ½ cup [Salsa](https://amzn.to/4aIhdue)

- Assemble bowls: Divide quinoa between two bowls. Top with the black bean mixture, diced avocado, and tortilla strips.

#### Nutritional Breakdown (per serving):

- Fat: 15g

### 10. Coconut Curry Bowl

**Servings:** 2
**Total Time:** 30 minutes

#### Ingredients:

- 1 cup coconut milk

- 1 cup vegetable broth

- 1 cup mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots)

- 1 cup cooked rice (white or brown)

- 1 tbsp curry paste

- Fresh cilantro for garnish

#### Instructions:

- Prepare curry: In a saucepan, combine coconut milk, vegetable broth, and curry paste. Bring to a simmer, then add mixed vegetables and cook until tender, about 10 minutes.

- Assemble bowls: Divide cooked rice between two bowls. Top with coconut curry and garnish with fresh cilantro.

#### Nutritional Breakdown (per serving):

- Calories: 450

- Protein: 10g

- Carbohydrates: 60g

- Fat: 20g
