## Pina Colada Overnight Oats Recipe

**Prep Time:** 5 minutes

**Cook Time:** 0 minutes

**Total Time:** 8 hours

**Servings:** 1

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup rolled oats

- 1 cup coconut milk

- 1/2 cup fresh pineapple, diced

- 1/2 teaspoon rum extract

- Optional toppings: shredded coconut, additional pineapple, or nuts

## Instructions

- In a mixing bowl, combine the rolled oats, coconut milk, diced pineapple, and rum extract. Stir well to combine.

- Transfer the mixture to a coconut bowl or jar, cover, and refrigerate overnight (or for at least 8 hours).

- In the morning, give the oats a good stir. If the mixture is too thick, add a splash more coconut milk to reach your desired consistency.

- Top with additional pineapple, shredded coconut, or nuts if desired, and enjoy your delicious pina colada overnight oats!
