A refreshing and nutrient-packed brunch bowl featuring antioxidant-rich acai, fresh fruits, and crunchy toppings for a healthy start to the day.

## Acai Smoothie Bowl Recipe

**Course:** Brunch

**Cuisine:** Brazilian-inspired

**Servings:** 2

## Ingredients

- 2 frozen acai packets, 

- 1 banana, 

- 1/2 cup almond milk, 

- 1/4 cup [Granola](https://amzn.to/4iPqYY5), 

- 1/4 cup mixed berries, 

- 2 tbsp shredded coconut, 

- 1 tbsp [chia seeds](https://amzn.to/4q7GcdC), 

- 1 tbsp [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke)

## Instructions

1. Blend acai, banana, and almond milk until smooth. 

2. Pour into bowls. 

3. Top with [Granola](https://amzn.to/4iPqYY5), mixed berries, shredded coconut, and [chia seeds](https://amzn.to/4q7GcdC). 

4. Drizzle with [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke).

Add a scoop of protein powder for a more filling meal. Use coconut water instead of almond milk for extra hydration.
