# Loyalty Points Program

Cultivate good karma with our loyalty program, rewarding your commitment to sustainable living.

		Our Loyalty Points Program is our way of showing our appreciation.
 You’ll earn points for activities on our site, like referrals and purchases.
 You can use them to earn discounts off purchases that you can use easily during checkout.
 So, the more you collect the more you save!		

## Loyalty Points Program Rewards

Our loyalty point program for buddhabowls.org offers a rewarding experience for customers who purchase our eco-friendly products. With every purchase of our [Coconut Bowls](https://buddhabowls.org/product/coconut-bowls/), [Bamboo Straws](https://buddhabowls.org/product/bamboo-straws/), or other reusable items, customers can earn loyalty points that can be redeemed for discounts on future purchases. We value our customers’ commitment to sustainability and want to show our appreciation through this program. Join us in our mission to reduce single-use plastic and promote a more eco-friendly lifestyle with our loyalty point program.

						
## Ways To Earn Points

		You can earn points for all sorts of activities, including referring friends, and making purchases.

											Anyone with an account is automatically enrolled.
***Registering for an account: **10 Reward Points
***Purchases: **5 Reward Points for every $1 spent.

***Referrals:** 10 points for every friend that signs up using your referral link.
