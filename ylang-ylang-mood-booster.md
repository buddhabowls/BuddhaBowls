## Ylang Ylang Mood Booster

### Ingredients:

- Ylang ylang essential oil,

- Carrier oil,

- Roller bottle

### Instructions:

- Mix 5 drops of ylang ylang essential oil with 10 ml of carrier oil in a roller bottle.

- Apply to pulse points when feeling stressed or anxious.
