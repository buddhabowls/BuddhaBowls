## Coconut Oil Hair Masks

I like using coconut oil because it helps to grow healthier, thicker, and longer hair.

**Moisturizes.**

The lauric acid in coconut oil has nourishing properties that are especially prone to soak into the strands of your hair. Coconut oil absorbs into your hair quickly, providing moisture to tame frizz and heal breakage. Lauric acid is a kind of fat that your hair can easily and quickly absorb.

Its vitamins and fatty acids help nourish the scalp while penetrating through its cuticles. Coconut oil also smooths cuticles on your split ends and controls frizz when in humid weather or if you have breakage.

**It Offers UV-Ray Protection**

A 30-minute coconut oil mask can do wonders to restore hair moisture, but an overnight treatment is better.

**Smooths hair and reduces frizz.**

Coconut oil can improve the health and condition of your hair by helping prevent protein loss.

## Coconut Oil Hair Masks

## How to use a coconut oil hair mask?

- Rub 2 tablespoons of lukewarm melted coconut oil into dry or damp strands.

- Gently massage the oil into each section from root to tip.

- Wear a shower cap overnight and finish by rinsing it off in the morning with water and your favorite shampoo.

- You may need two or three rinses to entirely wash out the mask.
