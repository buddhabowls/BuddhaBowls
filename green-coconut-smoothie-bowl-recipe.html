Green Coconut Smoothie Bowl

A nutrient-packed green smoothie bowl served in a coconut shell, perfect for a quick and healthy breakfast or post-workout snack.

Green Coconut Smoothie Bowl Recipe

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Cook Time: 0 minutes

Total Time: 5 minutes

Servings: 1

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh spinach
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1 kiwi, sliced (for topping)
  • Additional toppings: granola, coconut flakes, or berries (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine the spinach, banana, coconut milk, and chia seeds. Blend until smooth.
  2. Pour the smoothie into a coconut shell or bowl.
  3. Top with sliced kiwi and any additional toppings you desire, such as granola, coconut flakes, or berries.
  4. Serve immediately and enjoy your healthy breakfast or snack!
