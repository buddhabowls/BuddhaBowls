## Lavender Sleep Spray

### Ingredients:

- Lavender essential oil,

- Distilled water,

- Spray bottle

### Instructions:

- Combine 2 tablespoons of lavender essential oil with 1 cup of distilled water in a spray bottle.

- Shake well before use.

- Spray on pillows and linens before bedtime.
