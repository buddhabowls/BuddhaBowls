## Maca Banana Bread

**Course:** Dessert

**Servings:** 1 loaf

### Ingredients:

- 2 ripe bananas

- 1/2 cup sugar

- 1/4 cup butter

- 1/2 cup flour

- 2 tbsp maca powder

- 1 egg

### Instructions:

- Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).

- Mix all ingredients until combined. 

- Pour into a loaf pan and bake for 50 minutes.
