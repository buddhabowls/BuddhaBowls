## 🧘 Mindful Moment
> Delight in the sweet and tangy flavors of blueberries with almond butter. Take a moment to appreciate the health benefits of blueberries and the joy they bring.

## 💚 Health Benefits
- Blueberries are rich in antioxidants
- almond butter provides healthy fats.

## 🥗 Ingredients
- 3 ripe bananas
- 2 tbsp almond butter
- 1 cup blueberries

## 👩‍🍳 Instructions
1. Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
2. Place frozen bananas in a blender.
3. Add almond butter and blueberries.
4. Blend until smooth.
5. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.

## 💡 Tips
- Experiment with different nut butters for variety.
