## Maca Chocolate Chip Cookies

Print Recipe

**Course:** Dessert

**Servings:** 12 cookies

### Ingredients:

- – 1 cup flour – 1/2 cup sugar – 1/2 cup butter – 1/2 cup chocolate chips – 2 tbsp maca powder – 1 egg

### Instructions:

- 1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). 2. Mix all ingredients until combined. 3. Drop spoonfuls onto a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes.
