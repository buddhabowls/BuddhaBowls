## Nutty Coconut Energy Bites

Need a quick energy boost? These Nutty Coconut Energy Bites are just what you need! They’re packed with flavor and nutrients, making them the perfect snack for busy days.

The first time I made these energy bites, I was looking for a healthy snack that I could grab on the go. The combination of oats, peanut butter, and coconut flakes was a winner, and they quickly became my go-to snack.

To make these bites, mix one cup of oats, half a cup of peanut butter, a quarter cup of [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke), half a cup of coconut flakes, and half a cup of chopped nuts in a bowl. Roll the mixture into bite-sized balls and refrigerate until firm. Serve them in a coconut bowl for a fun twist. These energy bites are perfect for a quick snack, and they’re sure to keep you fueled and satisfied throughout the day!

## Nutty Coconut Energy Bites Recipe

 Course: Snack

 Prep Time: 15 minutes

 Cook Time: 0 minutes

 Total Time: 15 minutes

 Servings: 12
## Ingredients

- 1 cup oats, 1/2 cup peanut butter, 1/4 cup [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke), 1/2 cup coconut flakes, 1/2 cup chopped nuts

## Instructions

- Mix all ingredients in a bowl until well combined. Roll into bite-sized balls and refrigerate until firm.
