## Tropical Fruit Parfait Recipe

**Prep Time:** 10 minutes

**Cook Time:** 0 minutes

**Total Time:** 10 minutes

## Ingredients

- 2 cups coconut yogurt,

- 2 cups mixed tropical fruits (kiwi, mango, pineapple),

- 1/2 cup [Granola](https://amzn.to/4iPqYY5)

## Instructions

- Layer coconut yogurt, fruits, and [Granola](https://amzn.to/4iPqYY5) in glasses.

- Repeat layers and serve immediately.
