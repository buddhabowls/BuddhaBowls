Care & Maintenance

Cleaning

  • Hand wash recommended with warm water and mild soap
  • Use a soft cloth or sponge to gently clean
  • Avoid harsh scrubbers that may damage the surface
  • Do not put in the dishwasher
  • Rinse thoroughly with clean water

Drying

  • Pat dry with a soft cloth immediately after washing
  • Do not let air dry, as prolonged moisture can cause issues
  • Store only when completely dry

Storage

  • Store in a cool, dry place
  • Keep away from direct sunlight
  • Avoid prolonged water exposure
  • Store upright or stacked carefully with padding

Maintenance

  • Apply food-grade coconut oil monthly to maintain shine
  • Rub oil in with a soft cloth and buff away excess
  • This helps preserve the coconut and prevent cracking

Usage Tips

  • Can be used for hot and cold foods
  • Avoid extreme temperature changes
  • Handle with care to avoid dropping
  • Each bowl is unique and may have natural variations
  • Not recommended for dishwasher use

Frequently Asked Questions

Are these bowls food-safe?

Yes! Our coconut bowls are 100% food-safe. They're made from natural coconut shells and can be used for any type of food, both hot and cold.

How do I clean my coconut bowl?

Hand wash with warm, soapy water immediately after use. Use a soft cloth or sponge and avoid harsh scrubbers. Pat dry with a soft cloth and store in a cool, dry place.

Can I put these in the dishwasher?

No, we do not recommend putting coconut bowls in the dishwasher. Hand washing is the best way to care for your bowl and ensure its longevity.

Why is each bowl different?

Each coconut bowl is handcrafted from a natural coconut shell, making every bowl unique. These natural variations in size, shape, and texture are part of the charm and beauty of our products.

How do I maintain my bowl?

Apply food-grade coconut oil monthly to maintain shine and prevent cracking. Rub the oil in with a soft cloth and buff away any excess. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.

Are these bowls durable?

Yes! With proper care, your coconut bowl will last for years. The natural coconut shell is durable and long-lasting. Handle with care to avoid dropping, as coconut shell can crack with impact.
