## 🥗 Ingredients
- 3 ripe bananas
- 2 tbsp almond butter
- 1/4 cup cookie dough pieces

## 👩‍🍳 Instructions
1. Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
2. Place frozen bananas in a blender.
3. Add almond butter and cookie dough pieces.
4. Blend until smooth.
5. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.

## 💡 Tips
- Combine almond butter with cocoa powder for a chocolate version.
