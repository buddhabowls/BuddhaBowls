Fruit Powder Soup Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of fruit powder
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar (optional)
  • A pinch of salt
  • Fresh fruit pieces (for garnish)

Simple Steps

  1. In a pot, bring the water to a boil.
  2. Add the fruit powder and stir until dissolved.
  3. If desired, add sugar and salt, stirring well.
  4. Simmer for 5 minutes, then remove from heat.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with fresh fruit pieces.
