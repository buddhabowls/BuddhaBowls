Brew a cup of clarity with this refreshing herbal tea.

🍵 Brew Clarity! 🍵 Sip on a refreshing herbal tea with peppermint and lemon balm for mental clarity! Enjoy the soothing flavors while clearing your mind! 🌿✨ #HerbalTea #Clarity

Ingredients:

– Peppermint leaves

– Lemon balm – Honey

Instructions:

1. Steep peppermint and lemon balm in hot water.

2. Add [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke) for sweetness.

3. Sip slowly while focusing on clarity in your thoughts.
