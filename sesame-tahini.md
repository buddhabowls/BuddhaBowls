## Sesame Tahini

**Course:** Spread

**Cuisine:** Middle Eastern

**Prep Time:**

**Servings:** 1 cup

### Ingredients:

- 2 cups sesame seeds

- 1/2 tsp salt

- 1 tbsp olive oil (optional)

### Instructions:

- Toast sesame seeds at 350°F (175°C) for 8-10 minutes.

- Let cool, then blend in a food processor until smooth.

- Add salt and olive oil if desired.

NOTES: Great for hummus!
