Coconut Sunshine Mask

🌴 Natural brightening treatment
⏱ 15 minute ritual
🧴 Suitable for most skin types
🌿 3-ingredient simplicity

🍯 You'll Need:

  • 2 tbsp virgin coconut oil (melted)
  • 1 tsp fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp raw honey
  • Small mixing bowl
  • Clean brush or fingers

🌀 Creation Steps:

  1. Combine melted coconut oil and honey in bowl
  2. Whisk in lemon juice until emulsified
  3. Test on wrist - wait 5 minutes
  4. Apply thin layer to clean face
  5. Relax 10-15 minutes
  6. Rinse with lukewarm water

💡 Coconut Wisdom:
"Best used weekly - lemon may increase sun sensitivity. Store leftovers in coconut shell for 3 days max!"
