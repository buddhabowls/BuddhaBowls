Coconut Oil & Banana Mask

"Because your face deserves vacation vibes 🍌🥥
• 5-minute tropical treatment
• Edible ingredients only
• Smells like island time
#DIYskincare #CoconutMagic"

🍌 What You'll Need:

  • 1 overripe banana (the spottier the better)
  • 2 tbsp melted coconut oil
  • Coconut bowl + wooden masher
  • Soft cloth for removal

🍹 Mixing Ritual:

  1. Mash banana in coconut bowl until smooth
  2. Whisk in coconut oil until creamy emulsion
  3. Optional: Add cinnamon for circulation boost
  4. Apply thick layer avoiding eye area
  5. Relax 10 minutes (bonus: cucumber eye pads)
  6. Gently wipe off with warm cloth
  7. Follow with coconut water spritz

🍌 Pro Tip: Freeze leftover mix in ice cube tray
🥥 Shelf Life: 48 hours in coconut bowl fridge storage
🌴 Best Served: With steel drum music playlist
