## Creamy Vegan Mac and Cheese with Coconut Milk (Dairy-Free Comfort)

## Why This Belongs in Your Plant-Based Journey

- Coconut milk base supports ethical farming

- 8 simple whole-food ingredients

- 35-minute from pot to bowl

- Zero food waste potential

## Earth-Conscious Ingredients

1. 2 cups pasta shells (organic, fair-trade)
2. 1 can full-fat coconut milk (BPA-free can)
3. ½ cup nutritional yeast (plastic-free pouch)
4. 1 lemon (zest + juice)
5. 2 tbsp coconut flour
6. 1 tsp [Turmeric](https://amzn.to/4aIhdsd) (for golden hue)
7. ½ tsp smoked paprika
8. 1 garlic clove (local sourced)

## Sustainable Cooking Ritual

- **Sacred Boil**
Cook pasta in salted water (save 1 cup starch water)

- **Golden Elixir**
Whisk coconut milk, yeast, and spices over medium-low

- **Mindful Union**
Combine sauce with pasta, using reserved water

- **Final Blessing**
Stir in lemon zest/juice, rest 5 minutes

## Buddha Bowl Serving Inspiration

- Serve in [Coconut Bowls](https://buddhabowls.org/product/coconut-bowls/) with:
✨ Crispy roasted chickpeas
✨ Massaged kale ribbons
✨ Fermented hot sauce dots

- Garnish with pumpkin seed “parm”

- ♻️ Use leftovers in stuffed peppers next day

## Storage Dharma

- Glass containers: 5 days refrigerated

- Freezes well in beeswax wraps

- Reheat in solar oven or low-energy stove
