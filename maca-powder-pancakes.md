## Maca Powder Pancakes

**Course:** Breakfast

**Servings:** 4 pancakes

### Ingredients:

- 1 cup flour

- 1 tbsp baking powder

- 2 tbsp maca powder

- 1 cup milk

- 1 egg

### Instructions:

- In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder, and maca powder.

- Add milk and egg; stir until combined.

- Cook on a skillet until bubbles form, then flip.
