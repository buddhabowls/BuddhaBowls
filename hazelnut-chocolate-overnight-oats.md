[ Print Recipe](https://buddhabowls.org/wprm_print/hazelnut-chocolate-overnight-oats)## Hazelnut Chocolate Overnight Oats

Hello, Nutella fans! If you’re looking for a breakfast that’s rich, nutty, and oh-so-satisfying, these Hazelnut Chocolate Overnight Oats are calling your name.

This recipe is my tribute to the irresistible combination of hazelnuts and chocolate. It’s like having a gourmet breakfast in a bowl, but without the fuss.

To create this nutty delight, I mix rolled oats with hazelnut milk, a spoonful of cocoa powder, and a handful of chopped hazelnuts. After a good stir, it’s time to let the fridge do its thing overnight.

In the morning, I serve it in a coconut bowl (because it makes me feel like I’m at a fancy brunch) and top it with hazelnut pieces and chocolate chips.

These Hazelnut Chocolate Overnight Oats are like a sweet hug for your taste buds, and they never fail to make my mornings a little brighter. So go ahead, treat yourself to a breakfast that’s as sweet as you are!

## 

**Course:** Breakfast

**Prep Time:** 5 mins

**Cook Time:** 0 mins

**Total Time:** 5 mins

**Servings:** 1

## Ingredients

- Rolled oats, hazelnut milk, cocoa powder, chopped hazelnuts

## Instructions

Combine oats, hazelnut milk, cocoa powder, and chopped hazelnuts. Refrigerate overnight. Top with hazelnut pieces and chocolate chips.
