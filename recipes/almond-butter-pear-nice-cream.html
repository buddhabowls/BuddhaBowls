Almond Butter Pear Nice Cream Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas,
  • 2 tbsp almond butter,
  • 1 cup diced pears

Instructions

  • Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
  • Place frozen bananas in a blender.
  • Add almond butter and diced pears.
  • Blend until smooth.
  • Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.
