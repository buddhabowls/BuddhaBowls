  - 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup
  - 1/2 cup coconut milk
  - 1/4 cup plain Greek yogurt
  - 2 ripe kiwis
  - Toppings: sliced kiwi
instructions:
  - In a blender, combine the sliced kiwis, halved strawberries, frozen banana, coconut milk, Greek yogurt, and sweetener (if using).
  - Blend until smooth and creamy, adding more coconut milk if needed to reach your desired consistency.
  - Pour the smoothie into a coconut bowl.
  - Arrange the sliced kiwi, sliced strawberries, shredded coconut, chia seeds, and granola on top of the smoothie.
  - Grab a spoon and enjoy your delicious Kiwi Strawberry Coconut Smoothie Bowl!
