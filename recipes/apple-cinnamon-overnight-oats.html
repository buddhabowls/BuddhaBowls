Apple Cinnamon Overnight Oats

Hello, apple lovers! If you're a fan of classic flavors with a twist, you're going to adore these Apple Cinnamon Overnight Oats.

To whip up these oats, I mix rolled oats with apple juice, diced apples, and a generous sprinkle of cinnamon. After a quick stir, I let it chill in the fridge overnight, soaking up all those warm, spicy flavors.

Come morning, I serve it up in a coconut bowl (because it adds that extra touch of charm) and top it with apple slices and a sprinkle of cinnamon.

Every spoonful is like a taste of autumn, and I can't help but feel all warm and fuzzy inside as I eat it. So if you're ready to start your day with a little slice of apple heaven, give these Apple Cinnamon Overnight Oats a try. You won't be disappointed!

 

Course: Breakfast

Prep Time: 5 mins

Cook Time: 0 mins

Total Time: 5 mins

Servings: 1

Ingredients

  • Rolled oats, apple juice, diced apples, cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Mix oats, apple juice, diced apples, and cinnamon. Refrigerate overnight. Top with apple slices and a sprinkle of cinnamon.
