A chocolate lover's dream, combining almond butter with cocoa powder and chunks of brownie for a rich, indulgent dessert.

Almond Butter Brownie Batter Nice Cream Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas,
  • 2 tbsp almond butter,
  • 1/4 cup brownie batter pieces

Instructions

  1. Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
  2. Place frozen bananas in a blender.
  3. Add almond butter and brownie batter pieces.
  4. Blend until smooth.
  5. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.

Dive into the decadent taste of brownie batter with a healthy twist. This nice cream is a guilt-free indulgence that encourages mindful enjoyment.

Almonds: good for skin; Cocoa: rich in antioxidants, may improve mood.
