ingredients:
  - '- 1 ripe banana
  - 1 tablespoon of cocoa powder
  - 1/2 cup of frozen or fresh orange segments
  - 1/2 cup of Greek yogurt or dairy-free yogurt
  - 1/4 cup of milk or dairy-free milk
  - dark chocolate shavings
  - granola
  - sliced oranges
instructions:
  - In a blender, combine the ripe banana, orange segments, cocoa powder, Greek yogurt, and milk.
  - Blend until smooth and creamy, adding more milk if needed to reach your desired consistency.
  - Pour the smoothie into a bowl and top with sliced oranges, dark chocolate shavings, and granola.
