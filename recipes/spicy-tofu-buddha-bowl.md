description: >
  The combination of crispy tofu, tender-crisp vegetables, and spicy sauce creates a flavor explosion in every bite. The nuttiness of the brown rice complements the heat from the sriracha, making this dish a perfect balance of flavors.

ingredients:
  - '- 1 cup brown rice
  - 1 tbsp sriracha
  - 1/4 cup green beans
  - 1/4 cup red bell pepper
  - 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  - 100g tofu
  - 2 tbsp soy sauce
  - Served in a coconut bowl
  - sesame seeds
instructions:
  - Cook brown rice and steam veggies.
  - Sear tofu with soy sauce and sriracha until crispy.
  - Layer rice with veggies and tofu.
  - Garnish with sesame seeds.
notes: >
  <p>- To add an extra layer of flavor, you can marinate the tofu in the soy sauce and sriracha mixture before pan-frying it.</p>
<p>- Feel free to customize your Buddha bowl with your favorite vegetables or add a handful of fresh herbs for a burst of freshness.</p>
<p>- If you're not a fan of spicy food, you can adjust the amount of sriracha to suit your taste.</p>
