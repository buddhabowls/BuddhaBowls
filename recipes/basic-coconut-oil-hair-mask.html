Basic Coconut Oil Hair Mask

🥥 Preparation Protocol:

  1. Melt ¼ cup oil in coconut shell (40°C water bath)
  2. Test temperature on wrist (should feel warm)
  3. Section hair into quadrants using butterfly clips
  4. Apply using spiral motion:
    • Roots: Finger-tip massage (5 mins)
    • Lengths: Palm-gliding technique
    • Ends: Dip-twist sealing method
  5. Wrap with microfiber towel (20-30 mins)
  6. Rinse with cool water + apple cider vinegar spritz

💡 Enhancement Options:
• Dry hair: Add 1 tsp honey
• Oily scalp: Mix with 1 tbsp aloe vera
• Color-treated: Include 5 drops rosemary EO
• Fine hair: Combine with 1 egg white
• Storage: 6 months in amber glass
