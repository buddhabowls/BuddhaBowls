Almond Butter Pineapple Nice Cream Recipe

Tropical pineapple blended with almond butter for a refreshing and creamy treat.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas,
  • 2 tbsp almond butter,
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks

Instructions

  • 1. Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
  • 2. Place frozen bananas in a blender.
  • 3. Add almond butter and pineapple.
  • 4. Blend until smooth.
  • 5. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.

Pineapple contains bromelain for digestion; almond butter provides healthy fats.
