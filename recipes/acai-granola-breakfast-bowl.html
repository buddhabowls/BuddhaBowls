Acai Granola Breakfast Bowl Recipe

Course: Brunch

Cuisine: Brazilian-inspired

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 2 frozen acai packets
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1 cup mixed berries
  • 1/2 cup granola
  • 2 tbsp almond butter
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 tbsp honey

Instructions

  1. Blend acai, banana, and almond milk.
  2. Pour into bowls.
  3. Top with mixed berries, granola, almond butter, chia seeds, and a drizzle of honey.

TIPS:

Add protein powder for a more filling meal. Use coconut milk for a tropical flavor.
