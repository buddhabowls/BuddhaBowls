ingredients:
  - '- Ice cubes
  - chia seeds
  - fresh spinach leaves
  - kiwi
  - pink pitaya
  - spirulina powder
  - unsweetened apple juice
instructions:
  - Add pink pitaya, spinach, kiwi, apple juice, chia seeds, and spirulina (if using) to the blender.
  - Add ice cubes & Blend on high speed for 2-3 minutes until smooth and well combined.
  - Let sit for 2 minutes to allow chia seeds to expand slightly.
  - Stir gently and pour into glasses.
  - Serve immediately.
notes: >
  <p>For a sweeter taste, add a ripe banana or a tablespoon of honey. </p>
<p>Use coconut water instead of apple juice for a lower sugar content.</p>
