ingredients:
  - chopped pecans
  - maple syrup
  - peanut butter
  - ripe bananas
instructions:
  - Slice the bananas and freeze them overnight.
  - Place the frozen bananas in a blender.
  - Add the peanut butter and maple syrup.
  - Blend until smooth and creamy.
  - Stir in the chopped pecans.
  - Serve immediately for a soft texture or freeze for a firmer texture.
