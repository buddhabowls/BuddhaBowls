ingredients:
  - cashew butter
  - poppy seeds
  - ripe bananas
  - zest of 1 lemon
instructions:
  - Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
  - Place frozen bananas in a blender.
  - Add cashew butter and lemon zest.
  - Blend until smooth.
  - Stir in poppy seeds.
  - Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.
