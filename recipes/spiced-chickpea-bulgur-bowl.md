ingredients:

  - Fresh parsley for garnish
  - red onion
  - tahini sauce
instructions:
  - Rinse the bulgur under cold water and cook according to package instructions (usually about 12-15 minutes).
  - While the bulgur is cooking, roast the chickpeas in the oven at 400°F (200°C) for about 15 minutes, seasoned with your choice of spices (like cumin and paprika).
  - Once the bulgur is cooked, fluff it with a fork and let it cool slightly.
  - In a bowl, layer the cooked bulgur with roasted chickpeas, diced red onion, and halved cherry tomatoes.
  - Drizzle with tahini sauce and garnish with fresh parsley before serving.
