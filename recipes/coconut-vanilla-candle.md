ingredients:
  - coconut-soy wax blend
  - organic shredded coconut
  - Upcycled glass jar + wood wick
  - vanilla + 10ml coconut oil
instructions:
  - Melt wax with coconut oil (58°C)
  - Whip in vanilla oil until frothy
  - Sprinkle shredded coconut layers
  - Top with whole vanilla bean piece
  - Cure 72hrs - patience = perfection
