A rich and creamy body butter that deeply hydrates the skin.

This basic coconut oil body butter is perfect for dry skin and leaves your body feeling soft and smooth.

Basic Coconut Oil Body Butter

Ingredients:

  • Coconut oil,
  • Shea butter

Instructions:

  1. Melt 1/2 cup of shea butter and 1/2 cup of coconut oil in a double boiler.
  2. Remove from heat and let cool slightly.
  3. Whip with a hand mixer until fluffy.
  4. Store in a jar.
