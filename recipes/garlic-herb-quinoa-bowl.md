  - quinoa
  - sautéed mushrooms
  - spinach
instructions:
  - Rinse the quinoa under cold water and cook according to package instructions (usually about 15 minutes).
  - While the quinoa is cooking, heat a pan over medium heat and sauté the mushrooms with garlic herb dressing until tender, about 5-7 minutes.
  - In a bowl, layer the cooked quinoa with the sautéed mushrooms, chopped spinach, and halved cherry tomatoes.
  - Garnish with fresh thyme before serving.
