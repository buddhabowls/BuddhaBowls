  - Essential oil
instructions:
  - Combine 1 cup of dried rose petals with 1/2 cup of Epsom salt and 1/4 cup of baking soda.
  - Add a few drops of rose essential oil if desired.
  - Place in a muslin bag and hang under the faucet.
  - Soak for 20-30 minutes.
