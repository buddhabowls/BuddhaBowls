Almond Butter Matcha Nice Cream Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas,
  • 2 tbsp almond butter,
  • 1 tsp matcha powder

Instructions

  • 1. Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
  • 2. Place frozen bananas in a blender.
  • 3. Add almond butter and matcha powder.
  • 4. Blend until smooth.
  • 5. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.

Experience the calming effects of matcha in this delicious almond butter nice cream. Take a moment to savor the unique flavor while enjoying its health benefits, making it a perfect mindful snack.
