  - Vodka
instructions:
  - Fill a jar with dried lavender flowers.
  - Pour vodka over the flowers until covered.
  - Seal and let it sit in a cool, dark place for 4-6 weeks, shaking occasionally.
  - Strain and bottle the tincture.
