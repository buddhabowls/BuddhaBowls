A tropical twist with almond butter, lime juice, and coconut milk, creating a refreshing and creamy dessert.

Almond Butter Lime Coconut Nice Cream Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 2 tbsp almond butter
  • zest and juice of 1 lime
  • 1/4 cup shredded coconut

Instructions

  • 1. Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
  • Place frozen bananas in a blender.
  • Add almond butter, lime zest and juice, and shredded coconut.
  • Blend until smooth.
  • Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.

Refresh your palate with the zesty lime and creamy coconut in this nice cream. It’s the perfect blend for a mindful moment of indulgence.

Almonds: heart-healthy; Lime: high in Vitamin C; Coconut: aids in digestion.
