  - sesame oil
  - sesame seeds
  - soy sauce
  - spinach
instructions:
  - Press and cube tofu
  - Sauté tofu in sesame oil until golden
  - Julienne carrot
  - Wilt spinach in the same pan
  - Assemble tofu, spinach, and carrot in a bowl
  - Drizzle with soy sauce and sprinkle sesame seeds, salt, and pepper
