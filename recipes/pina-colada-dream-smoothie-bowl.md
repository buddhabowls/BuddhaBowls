ingredients:
  - '- 1 cup of frozen pineapple chunks
  - 1 ripe banana
  - 1 tablespoon of honey
  - 1/2 cup of coconut milk
  - 1/4 cup of Greek yogurt
  - Toppings: shredded coconut
instructions:
  - In a blender, combine the ripe banana, frozen pineapple chunks, coconut milk, Greek yogurt, and honey.
  - Blend until smooth and creamy, adding more coconut milk if needed to reach your desired consistency.
  - Pour the smoothie into a coconut bowl.
  - Top with shredded coconut, fresh pineapple chunks, granola, and a sprinkle of chia seeds.
