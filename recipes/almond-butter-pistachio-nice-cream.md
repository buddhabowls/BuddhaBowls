ingredients:
  - almond butter
  - crushed pistachios
  - ripe bananas
instructions:
  - Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
  - Place frozen bananas in a blender.
  - Add almond butter and crushed pistachios.
  - Blend until smooth.
  - Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.
