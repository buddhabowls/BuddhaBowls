ingredients:

  - lemon herb dressing
  - quinoa
  - zucchini
instructions:
  - Rinse the quinoa under cold water and cook according to package instructions (usually about 15 minutes).
  - While the quinoa is cooking, lightly sauté the diced zucchini in a pan over medium heat until tender, about 3-4 minutes.
  - Once the quinoa is cooked, fluff it with a fork and let it cool slightly.
  - In a bowl, layer the cooked quinoa with sautéed zucchini, halved cherry tomatoes, and chickpeas.
  - Drizzle with lemon herb dressing and garnish with fresh basil before serving.
