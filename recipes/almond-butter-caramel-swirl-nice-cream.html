Almond Butter Caramel Swirl Nice Cream Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 2 tbsp almond butter
  • 2 tbsp caramel sauce

Instructions

  1. Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
  2. Place frozen bananas in a blender.
  3. Add almond butter and caramel sauce.
  4. Blend until smooth.
  5. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.

Indulge in the rich, decadent flavors of caramel swirl nice cream. As you enjoy this treat, take a moment to reflect on the sweetness of life and the importance of balance.

Caramel adds sweetness; almond butter provides protein and healthy fats.
