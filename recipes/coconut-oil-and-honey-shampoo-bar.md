ingredients:

  - olive oil
instructions:
  - Measure lye and water separately.
  - Slowly mix lye into water.
  - Melt coconut oil and olive oil together.
  - Combine oils and lye solution, add honey, blend until trace.
  - Pour into molds and cure for 4-6 weeks.
