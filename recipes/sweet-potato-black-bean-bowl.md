  - salt
  - sweet potatoes
instructions:
  - Roast sweet potatoes at 400°F for 25 minutes
  - Rinse and drain black beans
  - Dice red bell pepper
  - Combine sweet potatoes, black beans, bell pepper, and corn in a bowl
  - Squeeze lime juice over the top
  - Garnish with cilantro, salt, and pepper
