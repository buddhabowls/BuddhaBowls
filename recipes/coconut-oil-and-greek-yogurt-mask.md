ingredients:
  - 15ml coconut oil
  - 30g Greek yogurt
  - Cold compress
  - Pre-chilled coconut bowl
  - Silicone mask brush
instructions:
  - Chill coconut bowl in refrigerator 30 minutes
  - In chilled bowl, whip yogurt and coconut oil
  - Gently fold in colloidal oatmeal
  - Add essential oils last to preserve cultures
  - Apply thick layer to clean face with brush
  - Relax 15 minutes (use cold compress if sensitive)
  - Rinse with cool herbal tea infusion
