instructions:
  - In a blender, combine the ripe banana, almond milk, cocoa powder, almond butter, honey or maple syrup, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt.
  - Blend until smooth and creamy. If the mixture is too thick, you can add a splash more almond milk to reach your desired consistency.
  - Once the smoothie base is ready, it's time to get creative with the toppings. Arrange sliced bananas, berries, shredded coconut, granola, or nuts on top of the smoothie bowl for added texture and flavor.
