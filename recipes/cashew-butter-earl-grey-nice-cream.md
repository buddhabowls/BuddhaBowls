ingredients:
  - cashew butter
  - loose leaf Earl Grey tea
  - ripe bananas
instructions:
  - Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
  - Steep Earl Grey tea in a small amount of hot water and let cool.
  - Place frozen bananas in a blender.
  - Add cashew butter and steeped Earl Grey tea.
  - Blend until smooth.
  - Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.
