Almond Butter Coconut Milk Nice Cream Recipe

A rich and creamy dessert made with almond butter and coconut milk, perfect for a dairy-free indulgence.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 2 tbsp almond butter
  • 1/4 cup coconut milk

Instructions

  • Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
  • Place frozen bananas in a blender.
  • Add almond butter and coconut milk. 4. Blend until smooth. 5. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.
