ingredients:
  - asparagus
  - cherry tomatoes
  - farro
  - Fresh mint for garnish
  - Lemon vinaigrette
  - snap peas
instructions:
  - Rinse the farro under cold water and cook according to package instructions (usually about 30 minutes).
  - While the farro is cooking, lightly steam the asparagus and snap peas until tender-crisp, about 3-4 minutes.
  - Once the farro is cooked, fluff it with a fork and let it cool slightly.
  - In a bowl, layer the cooked farro with halved cherry tomatoes, steamed asparagus, and snap peas.
  - Drizzle with lemon vinaigrette and garnish with fresh mint before serving.
