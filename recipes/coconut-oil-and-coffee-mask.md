ingredients:
  - Bamboo spoon
  - Coconut half-shell bowl
  - coconut oil
  - Coffee grounds
instructions:
  - Collect cooled coffee grounds from morning brew
  - In coconut bowl, mix grounds with coconut oil
  - Add coconut sugar if using for extra exfoliation
  - Stir with bamboo spoon until paste forms
  - Test on elbow before full application
  - Massage onto damp skin using circular motions
  - Rinse with warm water, pat dry
