ingredients:
  - coconut milk
  - matcha green tea powder
  - Pink pitaya powder
instructions:
  - First, fill a glass with ice.
  - Next, grab a small blender. In the blender, add 1 teaspoon pink pitaya powder and 1 cup of coconut milk.
  - Blend for 1-2 minutes till it is totally combined and the milk is pink.
  - Pour the pink milk half way up your glass.
  - Next, in a cup add, 1 teaspoon matcha green tea powder, 1 tablespoon of the hard part from the refrigerated can of coconut milk, and ½ cup coconut milk.
  - Use your milk frother to blend until the matcha foam is totally frothy and smooth.
  - Scoop your matcha foam on top of the pink milk.
