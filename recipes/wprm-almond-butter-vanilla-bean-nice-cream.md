ingredients:
  - almond butter
  - ripe bananas
  - seeds from 1 vanilla bean
instructions:
  - Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
  - Place frozen bananas in a blender.
  - Add almond butter and vanilla bean seeds.
  - Blend until smooth.
  - Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.
