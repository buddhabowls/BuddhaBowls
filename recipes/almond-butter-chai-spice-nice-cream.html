Almond Butter Chai Spice Nice Cream Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas, 2 tbsp almond butter, 1/2 tsp chai spice mix

Instructions

  • 1. Slice bananas and freeze them overnight. 2. Place frozen bananas in a blender. 3. Add almond butter and chai spice mix. 4. Blend until smooth. 5. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.
