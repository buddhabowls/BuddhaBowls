ingredients:
  - '- 1 ripe banana
  - 1 tablespoon of honey or maple syrup
  - 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  - 1/2 cup of almond milk
  - 1/2 cup of Greek yogurt
  - 1/2 cup of ice
  - 1/2 teaspoon of ground ginger
  - 1/4 teaspoon of ground cloves
  - 1/4 teaspoon of ground nutmeg
  - Drizzle of honey or maple syrup
  - chia seeds
  - chopped nuts
  - granola
  - shredded coconut
  - sliced banana
instructions:
  - In a blender, combine the ripe banana, almond milk, Greek yogurt, ground cinnamon, ground ginger, ground nutmeg, ground cloves, and honey or maple syrup.
  - Add the ice to the blender and blend until smooth and creamy.
  - Pour the smoothie into two coconut bowls.
  - Top with your favorite toppings such as granola, sliced banana, chia seeds, shredded coconut, chopped nuts, and a drizzle of honey or maple syrup.
  - Enjoy your delicious Gingerbread Spice Smoothie Bowl!
