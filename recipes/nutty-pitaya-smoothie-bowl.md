  - almond butter
  - banana
  - ground flax seeds
  - pink pitaya
  - unsweetened almond milk
instructions:
  - Add pink pitaya, almond butter, almond milk, and flax seeds to the blender.
  - Add banana if using for extra creaminess.
  - Add ice cubes.
  - Blend on high speed for 1-2 minutes until smooth and creamy.
  - Pour into glasses and serve immediately.
