  - 1/2 cup coconut water

instructions:
  - In a blender, combine the chopped kale, frozen pineapple chunks, ripe banana, and coconut water.
  - Blend until smooth and creamy, adding more coconut water if needed to reach your desired consistency.
  - Pour the smoothie into a coconut bowl.
  - Top with your favorite toppings such as sliced fruits, nuts, seeds, or granola.
