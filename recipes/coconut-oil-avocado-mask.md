ingredients:
  - Chilled coconut bowl
  - coconut oil
  - Cold jade roller
  - raw honey
  - ripe avocado
instructions:
  - Mash avocado in coconut bowl until creamy
  - Mix in coconut oil and honey thoroughly
  - Add essential oils last to preserve nutrients
  - Apply thick layer with avocado pit as applicator
  - Leave on 15-20 minutes (store mask in fridge)
  - Massage off with coconut oil-soaked cloth
  - Finish with cold jade roller session
