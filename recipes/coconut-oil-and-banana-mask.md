ingredients:
  - Coconut bowl + wooden masher
  - melted coconut oil
  - overripe banana
  - Soft cloth for removal
instructions:
  - Mash banana in coconut bowl until smooth
  - Whisk in coconut oil until creamy emulsion
  - Optional: Add cinnamon for circulation boost
  - Apply thick layer avoiding eye area
  - Relax 10 minutes (bonus: cucumber eye pads)
  - Gently wipe off with warm cloth
