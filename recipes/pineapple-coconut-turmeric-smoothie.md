ingredients:
  - '- 1 cup of coconut milk
  - 1 cup of frozen pineapple chunks
  - 1 ripe banana
  - 1 tablespoon of shredded coconut
  - 1 teaspoon of ground turmeric
  - 1 teaspoon of honey or maple syrup
  - A handful of ice cubes
  - A squeeze of fresh lime juice
instructions:
  - In a blender, combine the coconut milk, frozen pineapple chunks, ripe banana, shredded coconut, ground turmeric, honey or maple syrup (if using), and fresh lime juice.
  - Add the ice cubes to the blender to give the smoothie a nice, frosty texture.
  - Blend all the ingredients until smooth and creamy.
