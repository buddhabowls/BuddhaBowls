Asian Sesame Ginger Bowl Recipe

Course: Main Cuisine: Asian Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 10 minutes Total Time: 20 minutes Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked soba noodles, 1/2 cup shredded cabbage, 1/2 cup edamame, 1/4 cup carrots, 1/4 cup green onions, 2 tbsp sesame oil, 1 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp ginger, 1 tbsp sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. Cook soba noodles; Julienne carrots; Combine noodles, cabbage, edamame, carrots, onions in a bowl; Drizzle with sesame oil, soy sauce, and ginger; Sprinkle sesame seeds
