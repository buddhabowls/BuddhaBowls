instructions:
  - Start by cooking the jasmine rice according to the package instructions. Once done, fluff it with a fork and set it aside.
  - While the rice is cooking, grill the chicken until it's fully cooked and has those beautiful grill marks. You can season it with your favorite Mexican-inspired spices like cumin, chili powder, and garlic for an extra kick of flavor.
  - Next, grill the pineapple slices until they have caramelized grill marks. This step adds a sweet and smoky element to the dish, balancing out the savory flavors of the chicken and beans.
  - Warm up the black beans either on the stovetop or in the microwave. You can season them with a pinch of salt and a squeeze of lime juice for added brightness.
  - Assemble your Buddha bowl by starting with a generous scoop of jasmine rice as the base. Then, arrange the grilled chicken, pineapple, and black beans on top. Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves and drizzle with a generous squeeze of lime juice for a pop of citrusy freshness.
