instructions:
  - Start by gathering all of your ingredients. Make sure your bananas are ripe and your coconut milk is well-shaken.
  - In a blender, combine the ripe bananas, coconut milk, shredded coconut, honey, and vanilla extract.
  - Blend the mixture on high speed until it is smooth and creamy. You may need to stop and scrape down the sides of the blender to ensure that all of the ingredients are well combined.
  - Once the mixture is smooth, taste it and adjust the sweetness or flavoring to your preference. You can add more honey for extra sweetness or more vanilla extract for a stronger flavor.
  - Once you are happy with the taste, transfer the coconut base to a bowl and place it in the refrigerator to chill for at least 30 minutes. Chilling the base will help it thicken and develop a richer flavor.
  - <p>After the base has chilled, it&#39;s ready to be used as a delicious topping for your favorite Coconut Bowls or enjoyed on its own as a refreshing treat.</p>
