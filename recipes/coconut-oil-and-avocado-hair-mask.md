ingredients:
  - 30ml manuka honey
  - 60ml coconut oil
  - Coconut shell + stone mortar
  - medium Hass avocado
  - Thermal processing cap
instructions:
  - Mash avocado in coconut shell using stone pestle
  - Whip coconut oil and honey into emulsion
  - Apply using three-temperature technique:
  - Room temp application to scalp
  - Warm mixture to 40°C for mid-lengths
  - Cool mixture for ends sealing
  - Process under thermal cap (30-45 minutes)
  - Rinse with cool rooibos tea infusion
  - Air dry using tension-free method
