ingredients:
  - can coconut milk
  - curry powder
  - garlic
  - ginger
  - mixed vegetables
  - onion
  - red lentils
  - Salt and pepper to taste
  - vegetable broth
instructions:
  - In a large pot, heat a splash of oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion, garlic, and ginger, sautéing until the onion is translucent.
  - Add the curry powder and stir for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  - Stir in the red lentils, mixed vegetables, and vegetable broth. Bring to a boil.
  - Reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 25 minutes, or until the lentils are soft.
  - Pour in the coconut milk and stir well. Cook for an additional 5 minutes.
  - Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot in coconut bowls for an exotic touch.
