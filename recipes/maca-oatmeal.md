ingredients:
  - maca powder
  - oats
  - Optional toppings: fresh fruits
  - Sweetener to taste
  - water or milk
instructions:
  - In a pot, bring the water or milk to a boil.
  - Add the oats and reduce heat to a simmer.
  - Stir in the maca powder and cook for about 5 minutes, or until the oats are soft.
  - Remove from heat and let it sit for a minute.
  - Sweeten to taste and add any optional toppings.
  - Serve warm and enjoy!
