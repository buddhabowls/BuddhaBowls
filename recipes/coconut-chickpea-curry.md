ingredients:

  - cumin
  - curry powder
  - Fresh cilantro for garnish
  - garlic
  - ginger
  - medium tomatoes
  - Salt and pepper to taste
  - turmeric
instructions:
  - In a large pot, heat a splash of oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, sautéing until fragrant.
  - Add the diced tomatoes and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  - Stir in the curry powder, cumin, and turmeric, cooking for another minute.
  - Add the chickpeas and coconut milk, stirring to combine. Bring to a simmer.
  - Reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld. Stir occasionally.
  - Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot in coconut bowls, garnished with fresh cilantro if desired.
