ingredients:
  - '- 1 ripe banana
  - 1 tablespoon honey
  - 1/2 cup coconut milk
  - 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  - 1/4 cup shredded coconut
  - 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  - Toppings: sliced bananas
instructions:
  - In a blender, combine the ripe banana, coconut milk, Greek yogurt, shredded coconut, vanilla extract, and honey (if using). Blend until smooth and creamy.
  - Pour the smoothie into two coconut bowls for an extra tropical touch.
  - Top the smoothie bowls with sliced bananas, granola, additional shredded coconut, and a sprinkle of cinnamon for added flavor and texture.
  - Grab a spoon and dig in!
