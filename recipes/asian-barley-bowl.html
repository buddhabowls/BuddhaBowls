A nutritious and colorful Asian Barley Bowl packed with fresh vegetables and topped with a delicious sesame dressing, perfect for a healthy meal.

Asian Barley Bowl Recipe

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Total Time: 40 minutes

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 cup barley
  • 1/4 cup edamame, shelled
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/4 cup bell pepper, diced
  • 1/4 avocado, sliced
  • 2 tbsp sesame dressing
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions

  1. Rinse the barley under cold water and cook according to package instructions (usually about 30 minutes).
  2. While the barley is cooking, lightly steam the edamame and bell pepper until tender.
  3. Once the barley is cooked, fluff it with a fork and let it cool slightly.
  4. In a bowl, layer the cooked barley with the steamed edamame, shredded carrots, diced bell pepper, and sliced avocado.
  5. Drizzle with sesame dressing and sprinkle with sesame seeds before serving.

Nutrition

Calories: Approximately 400

Protein: 14g

Carbs: 60g

Fat: 12g

Fiber: 10g
