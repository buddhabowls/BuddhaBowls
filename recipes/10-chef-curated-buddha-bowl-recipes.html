11. Nourishing Buddha Bowl

Servings: 2
Total Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup brown rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup kale, chopped
  • 1 cup roasted carrots, sliced
  • ½ cup pumpkin seeds
  • 2 tbsp tahini dressing

Instructions:

  1. Cook rice: Rinse brown rice, then combine with water in a pot. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 45 minutes.
  2. Roast carrots: Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Toss sliced carrots with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast for 25 minutes.
  3. Sauté kale: In a pan, sauté kale in a little olive oil until wilted, about 5 minutes.
  4. Assemble bowls: Divide rice between two bowls. Top with roasted carrots, sautéed kale, and pumpkin seeds. Drizzle with tahini dressing.

Nutritional Breakdown (per serving):

  • Calories: 480
  • Protein: 15g
  • Carbohydrates: 65g
  • Fat: 20g
  • Fiber: 10g

  • 1 cup brown rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 block (8 oz) tempeh, sliced

16. Southeast Asian Rice Bowl

Servings: 2
Total Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup jasmine rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 chicken breast, grilled and sliced (or tofu for a vegan option)
  • 1 cup mango, diced
  • ¼ cup cilantro, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce

Instructions:

  1. Cook rice: Rinse jasmine rice, then combine with water in a pot. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes.

  3. Assemble bowls: Divide rice between two bowls. Top with grilled chicken (or tofu), diced mango, and garnish with cilantro and lime juice. Drizzle with soy sauce.

Nutritional Breakdown (per serving):

  • Calories: 480
  • Protein: 30g

  • Fat: 10g
  • Fiber: 5g
