ingredients:
  - 120ml fresh aloe gel
  - 15g fenugreek powder
  - 45ml coconut oil
  - Coconut shell + bamboo spatula
  - Ice globes
instructions:
  - Harvest aloe gel directly into coconut shell
  - Whip coconut oil into gel using figure-8 motion
  - Add fenugreek and essential oils last
  - Apply using three-zone technique:
  - Scalp: Part hair 1cm apart with tint brush
  - Lengths: Use praying hands application
  - Ends: Dip-wrap in leftover mixture
  - Cover with silk wrap (25-30 minutes)
  - Rinse with cold hibiscus tea rinse
  - Finish with ice globe massage
