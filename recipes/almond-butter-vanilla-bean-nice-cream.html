Almond Butter Vanilla Bean Nice Cream Recipe

Classic vanilla flavor enhanced with creamy almond butter for a smooth texture.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 2 tbsp almond butter
  • seeds from 1 vanilla bean

Instructions

  1. Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
  2. Place frozen bananas in a blender.
  3. Add almond butter and vanilla bean seeds.
  4. Blend until smooth.
  5. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.

Vanilla has antioxidant properties; almond butter is a good source of vitamin E.
