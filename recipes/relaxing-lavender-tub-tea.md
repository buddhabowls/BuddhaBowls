ingredients:

  - Epsom salt
  - Essential oil
instructions:
  - In a bowl, mix 1 cup of dried lavender with 1/2 cup of Epsom salt and 1/4 cup of baking soda.
  - Add a few drops of lavender essential oil if desired.
  - Place the mixture in a muslin bag or directly in the tub.
  - Fill the tub with warm water and soak for 20-30 minutes.
