instructions:
  - Rinse the sticky rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Soak the rice in water for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  - Drain the rice and steam it in a bamboo steamer or a rice cooker for about 20 minutes, or until tender.
  - While the rice is cooking, combine coconut milk, sugar, and a pinch of salt in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir until the sugar dissolves, but do not let it boil.
  - Once the rice is cooked, transfer it to a mixing bowl and pour about 3/4 of the coconut milk mixture over it. Stir to combine and let it sit for about 10 minutes to absorb the flavors.
  - To serve, place a portion of the sticky rice in a coconut bowl, top with fresh mango slices, and drizzle with the remaining coconut milk mixture.
