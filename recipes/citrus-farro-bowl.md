instructions:
  - Rinse the farro under cold water and cook according to package instructions (usually about 30 minutes).
  - While the farro is cooking, mix together the segmented oranges, chopped spinach, and sliced red onion in a bowl.
  - Once the farro is cooked, fluff it with a fork and let it cool slightly.
  - In serving bowls, layer the cooked farro with the orange mixture.
  - Drizzle with citrus dressing and garnish with fresh mint leaves before serving.
