  - 2 tablespoons almond butter

  - honeydew melon
instructions:
  - In a blender, combine the honeydew melon, banana, almond milk, Greek yogurt, almond butter, and honey. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  - Pour the smoothie into a bowl.
  - Top with your favorite toppings such as sliced almonds, chia seeds, shredded coconut, and fresh fruit.
