Ally McBeal's Almond Bowl Recipe

Course: Lunch
Cuisine: Healthy
Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mixed greens,
  • 1/2 cup sliced almonds,
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese,
  • Olive oil,
  • Balsamic vinegar

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a coconut bowl and drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.

Add grilled chicken for extra protein!
