ingredients:
  - '- 1 ripe banana
  - 1 tablespoon of cocoa powder
  - 1/2 cup of coconut milk
  - 1/2 cup of frozen or fresh orange segments
  - 1/4 cup of dairy-free yogurt
  - cacao nibs
  - coconut flakes
  - sliced oranges
instructions:
  - In a blender, combine the ripe banana, orange segments, cocoa powder, coconut milk, and dairy-free yogurt.
  - Blend until smooth and creamy, adding more coconut milk if needed to reach your desired consistency.
  - Pour the smoothie into a bowl and top with coconut flakes, cacao nibs, and sliced oranges.
