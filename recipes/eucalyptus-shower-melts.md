  - Silicone mold
  - water
instructions:
  - Mix 1 cup of baking soda with 10 drops of eucalyptus essential oil and enough water to create a paste.
  - Pack the mixture into silicone molds and let dry for 24 hours.
  - Use in the shower for an aromatic experience.
