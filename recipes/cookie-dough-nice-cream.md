instructions:
  - Place the frozen banana slices, almond milk, and vanilla extract in a high-speed blender or food processor. Blend until smooth and creamy, scraping down the sides as needed. Once it's reached a soft-serve consistency, transfer it to a bowl and set it aside.
  - In a separate bowl, mix together the almond flour, maple syrup, melted coconut oil, and vanilla extract until well combined. Then, gently fold in the mini chocolate chips until they're evenly distributed throughout the dough.
  - Grab your coconut bowls and scoop a generous amount of the nice cream into each one.
  - Then, sprinkle a generous amount of the cookie dough chunks on top of the nice cream.
  - Feel free to get creative and add extra toppings like more chocolate chips or a drizzle of caramel sauce.
