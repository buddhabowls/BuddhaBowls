instructions:
  - Make dressing by blending yogurt, herbs, lemon juice, and garlic.
  - Layer spinach and quinoa in bowls.
  - Top with poached eggs, avocado slices, and edamame.
  - Sprinkle with pumpkin seeds.
  - Drizzle with green goddess dressing.
