instructions:
  - Prepare your rice noodles according to the package instructions.
  - Whisk together peanut butter, soy sauce, lime juice, and sriracha.
  - In your coconut bowl, combine noodles, carrots, bell pepper, and edamame.
  - Drizzle the spicy peanut dressing over the bowl.
  - Top with chopped peanuts and cilantro.
