ingredients:
  - '- 1 cup frozen blueberries
  - 1 ripe banana
  - 1 tablespoon almond butter
  - 1 tablespoon honey
  - 1/2 cup almond milk
  - 1/4 cup Greek yogurt
  - Toppings: sliced almonds
instructions:
  - In a blender, combine the frozen blueberries, ripe banana, almond milk, Greek yogurt, almond butter, and honey.
  - Blend until smooth and creamy.
  - Pour the smoothie into a coconut bowl.
  - Top with sliced almonds, shredded coconut, and fresh blueberries.
  - Serve and enjoy!
