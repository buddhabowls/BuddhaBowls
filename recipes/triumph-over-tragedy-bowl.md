ingredients:
  - Balsamic vinegar
  - cherry tomatoes
  - feta cheese
  - millet
  - olive oil
  - salt and pepper
  - sautéed spinach
instructions:
  - Cook millet according to package instructions.
  - Sauté spinach until wilted.
  - In a bowl, layer millet, spinach, and cherry tomatoes.
  - Top with feta, drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
  - Season with salt and pepper.
