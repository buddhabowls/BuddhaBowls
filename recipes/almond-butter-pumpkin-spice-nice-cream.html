Almond Butter Pumpkin Spice Nice Cream Recipe

Seasonal pumpkin spice flavors blended with almond butter for a fall favorite.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 2 tbsp almond butter
  • 1/4 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 tsp pumpkin pie spice

Instructions

  1. Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
  2. Place frozen bananas in a blender.
  3. Add almond butter, pumpkin puree, and pumpkin pie spice.
  4. Blend until smooth.
  5. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.

Pumpkin is high in vitamins A and C; almond butter provides healthy fats.
