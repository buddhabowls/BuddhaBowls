Almond Butter Kiwi Nice Cream Recipe

A tropical delight that combines almond butter with tangy kiwi, resulting in a refreshing and fruity frozen treat.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas,
  • 2 tbsp almond butter,
  • 1 cup diced kiwi

Instructions

  • 1. Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
  • 2. Place frozen bananas in a blender.
  • 3. Add almond butter and diced kiwi.
  • 4. Blend until smooth.
  • 5. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.

Almonds: rich in magnesium; Kiwi: high in Vitamin C, supports immune function.
