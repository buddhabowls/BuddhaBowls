A chocolatey delight with almond butter, marshmallows, and nuts, reminiscent of the classic rocky road ice cream.

Almond Butter Rocky Road Nice Cream Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas, 2 tbsp almond butter, 1/4 cup mini marshmallows, 1/4 cup chopped almonds, 1/4 cup mini chocolate chips

Instructions

  • 1. Slice bananas and freeze them overnight. 2. Place frozen bananas in a blender. 3. Add almond butter. 4. Blend until smooth. 5. Stir in mini marshmallows, chopped almonds, and mini chocolate chips. 6. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.

Enjoy the classic flavors of rocky road in a healthier format. This nice cream is a fun treat that invites you to savor the experience.

Almonds: heart-healthy; Marshmallows: quick energy; Dark chocolate: rich in antioxidants.
