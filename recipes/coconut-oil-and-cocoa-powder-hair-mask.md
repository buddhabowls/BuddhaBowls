ingredients:
  - 30ml raw apple cider vinegar
  - 60g raw cacao powder
  - 60ml coconut oil
  - Coconut shell + bamboo whisk
  - Plastic-free hair clips
instructions:
  - Melt coconut oil in coconut shell (45°C water bath)
  - Gradually whisk in cacao powder until lump-free
  - Add ACV last to preserve acidity
  - Apply to dry hair using quadrant method:
  - Divide hair into 4 sections
  - Use brush to coat from roots to ends
  - Focus on sun-exposed areas for color protection
  - Wrap in thermal cap (30-45 minutes)
  - Rinse with cold water followed by herbal rinse
  - Air dry away from direct sunlight
