ingredients:
  - '- 1 ripe banana
  - 1 tablespoon honey
  - 1/2 cup almond milk
  - 1/4 cup Greek yogurt
  - 1/4 teaspoon almond extract
  - 2 tablespoons almond butter
  - Toppings of your choice
  - frozen cherries
instructions:
  - In a blender, combine the frozen cherries, banana, almond milk, Greek yogurt, almond butter, honey, and almond extract.
  - Blend until smooth and creamy. If the mixture is too thick, you can add more almond milk to reach your desired consistency.
  - Pour the smoothie into coconut bowls because, well, why not?
  - Now comes the fun part - toppings! Get creative with your toppings. You can add sliced almonds, shredded coconut, chia seeds, granola, or even more cherries.
