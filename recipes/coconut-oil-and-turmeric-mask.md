ingredients:
  - 15g manuka honey
  - 30ml coconut oil
  - 3g turmeric powder
  - Coconut bowl &amp; bamboo spoon
  - Dark-colored towel
instructions:
  - Mix turmeric and honey in coconut bowl until smooth
  - Gradually blend in coconut oil to form paste
  - Apply thin layer with brush (avoid eyebrows)
  - Let sit 15-20 minutes (no facial movements)
  - Remove with oil cleanser first, then water
