ingredients:
  - BBQ tofu
  - black beans
  - corn
  - Fresh cilantro for garnish
  - quinoa
  - red onions
instructions:
  - Rinse the quinoa under cold water and cook according to package instructions (usually about 15 minutes).
  - In a pan, sauté the cubed BBQ tofu over medium heat until heated through and slightly crispy, about 5 minutes.
  - In a bowl, mix together the corn, black beans, and diced red onions.
  - Once the quinoa is cooked, fluff it with a fork and let it cool slightly.
  - In serving bowls, layer the cooked quinoa with the sautéed BBQ tofu and the veggie mixture.
  - Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.
