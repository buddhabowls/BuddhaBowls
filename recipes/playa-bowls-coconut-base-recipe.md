instructions:
  - In a blender, combine the ripe bananas, coconut milk, shredded coconut, honey, and vanilla extract.
  - <p>Blend the mixture on high speed until it is smooth and creamy. </p>
  - <p>Taste it and adjust the sweetness or flavoring to your preference. You can add more honey for extra sweetness or more vanilla extract for a stronger flavor.</p>
  - <p>Transfer the coconut base to a bowl and place it in the refrigerator to chill for at least 30 minutes. </p>
  - <p>Add any desired toppings or enjoy plain!</p>
