ingredients:
  - '- 1 cup of coconut water
  - 1 ripe banana
  - 1 tablespoon of honey or maple syrup
  - 1-inch piece of fresh ginger
  - 1/2 cup of plain Greek yogurt
  - 2 large carrots
  - granola
  - nuts
  - seeds
  - sliced fruits
instructions:
  - In a blender, combine the chopped carrots, banana, fresh ginger, coconut water, Greek yogurt, and honey or maple syrup (if using).
  - Blend until smooth and creamy. If the mixture is too thick, you can add more coconut water to reach your desired consistency.
  - Once blended, pour the smoothie into a coconut bowl.
  - Now comes the fun part – adding your favorite toppings! Get creative and sprinkle on sliced fruits, nuts, seeds, and granola to add texture and flavor to your smoothie bowl.
