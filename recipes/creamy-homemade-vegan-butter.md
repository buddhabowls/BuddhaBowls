ingredients:
  - '- 1 teaspoon apple cider vinegar
  - 1/2 cup unsweetened soy milk
  - 1/2 teaspoon salt
  - 1/4 cup refined avocado oil
  - 1/4 teaspoon turmeric
  - refined coconut oil
instructions:
  - In a small saucepan, melt the coconut oil over low heat. Once melted, remove from heat and let it cool for a few minutes.
  - In a blender, combine the soy milk, avocado oil, apple cider vinegar, salt, and turmeric. Blend on high speed until well combined.
  - With the blender running on low speed, slowly pour in the melted coconut oil. Continue blending until the mixture is smooth and creamy.
  - Pour the mixture into a mold or airtight container and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or until firm.
