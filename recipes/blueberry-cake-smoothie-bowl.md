ingredients:
  - '- 1 cup frozen blueberries
  - 1 ripe banana
  - 1 tablespoon almond butter
  - 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup
  - 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  - 1/2 cup almond milk or your preferred milk
  - 1/2 cup Greek yogurt or a dairy-free alternative
  - Edible flowers
  - Granola or crushed nuts
  - Sliced almonds or other nuts
  - chia seeds
  - Fresh blueberries
  - shredded coconut
instructions:
  - **Blend the Base:** In a blender, combine the frozen blueberries, banana, Greek yogurt, almond milk, almond butter, vanilla extract, and honey or maple syrup. Blend until smooth and creamy. You may need to stop and scrape down the sides of the blender to ensure everything is well combined.
  - **Adjust Consistency:** If the smoothie is too thick, add a little more almond milk until you reach your desired consistency. The goal is to have a thick, spoonable mixture.
  - **Prepare the Coconut Bowl:** Using a coconut bowl adds an eco-friendly and aesthetic touch to your smoothie bowl. These bowls are sustainable and biodegradable, making them a great choice for environmentally conscious individuals.
  - **Assemble Your Bowl:** Pour the smoothie mixture into the coconut bowl. Arrange your toppings artfully on top of the smoothie. This is your chance to get creative! You can create patterns, stripes, or simply scatter the toppings for a more rustic look.
  - **Enjoy:** Grab a spoon and dig into your delicious Blueberry Cake Smoothie Bowl. The combination of creamy smoothie and crunchy toppings will provide a satisfying and nourishing start to your day.
