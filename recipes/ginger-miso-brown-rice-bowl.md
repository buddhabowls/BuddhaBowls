ingredients:
  - brown rice
  - edamame
  - ginger miso dressing
  - Sesame seeds for garnish
  - shredded carrots
  - snap peas
instructions:
  - Rinse the brown rice under cold water and cook according to package instructions (usually about 30 minutes).
  - While the rice is cooking, lightly steam the snap peas and shredded carrots until they are tender-crisp, about 3-4 minutes.
  - Once the rice is cooked, fluff it with a fork and let it cool slightly.
  - In a bowl, layer the cooked brown rice with steamed snap peas, shredded carrots, and edamame.
  - Drizzle with ginger miso dressing and sprinkle with sesame seeds before serving.
