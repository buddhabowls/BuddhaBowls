ingredients:
  - '- 1 cup coconut flour
  - 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  - 1 teaspoon paprika
  - 1 teaspoon salt
  - 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  - 1/4 cup coconut oil
  - 3 eggs
  - 4 boneless
instructions:
  - In a shallow dish, mix together the coconut flour, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper.
  - In another shallow dish, beat the eggs.
  - Dip each chicken breast into the egg mixture, then dredge it in the coconut flour mixture until fully coated.
  - In a large skillet, heat the coconut oil over medium-high heat.
  - Once the oil is hot, add the coated chicken breasts to the skillet and cook for 6-8 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and cooked through.
