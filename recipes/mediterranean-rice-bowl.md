ingredients:
  - cherry tomatoes
  - cucumber
  - Fresh parsley for garnish
  - hummus
  - jasmine rice
  - olives
  - red onion
instructions:
  - Rinse the jasmine rice under cold water and cook according to package instructions (usually about 15 minutes).
  - While the rice is cooking, dice the cucumber, halved cherry tomatoes, and red onion.
  - Once the rice is cooked, fluff it with a fork and let it cool slightly.
  - In a bowl, layer the cooked jasmine rice with the diced vegetables and sliced olives.
  - Top with a dollop of hummus and garnish with fresh parsley before serving.
