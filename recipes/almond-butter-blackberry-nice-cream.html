A fruity dessert blending almond butter with fresh blackberries, creating a naturally sweet and vibrant treat.

Almond Butter Blackberry Nice Cream Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas,
  • 2 tbsp almond butter,
  • 1 cup blackberries

Instructions

  • 1. Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
  • 2. Place frozen bananas in a blender.
  • 3. Add almond butter and blackberries.
  • 4. Blend until smooth.
  • 5. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.

Savor the sweet and tart flavors of blackberries mixed with almond butter. This nice cream is a delightful way to practice mindfulness and enjoy nature’s bounty.

Almonds: good for cholesterol; Blackberries: high in fiber, rich in vitamins.
