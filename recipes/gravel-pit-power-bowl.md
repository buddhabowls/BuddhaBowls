ingredients:
  - carrots
  - couscous
  - lemon
  - roasted broccoli
  - salt and pepper
  - sunflower seeds
  - tahini
instructions:
  - Prepare couscous as per package instructions.
  - Roast broccoli and carrots at 400°F for 20 minutes.
  - In a bowl, combine couscous, veggies, and sunflower seeds.
  - Drizzle with tahini and lemon juice.
  - Season with salt and pepper.
