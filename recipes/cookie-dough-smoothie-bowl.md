ingredients:
  - '- 1 ripe banana
  - 1 tablespoon of almond butter
  - 1 tablespoon of honey or maple syrup
  - 1 tablespoon of mini chocolate chips
  - 1/2 cup of almond milk
  - 1/2 cup of rolled oats
  - 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  - A pinch of salt
  - Optional toppings such as more chocolate chips
instructions:
  - Combine the ripe banana, rolled oats, almond milk, almond butter, honey or maple syrup, vanilla extract, and salt in a blender.
  - Blend until smooth and creamy.
  - Stir in the mini chocolate chips to create that classic cookie dough flavor.
  - Pour it into a bowl and get creative with your toppings.
