ingredients:
  - bell peppers
  - black beans

  - corn
  - Fresh cilantro for garnish
  - Lime wedge for garnish
  - salsa
instructions:
  - Rinse the brown rice under cold water and cook according to package instructions (usually about 30 minutes).
  - While the rice is cooking, mix the black beans, corn, and diced bell peppers in a bowl.
  - Once the rice is cooked, fluff it with a fork and let it cool slightly.
  - In a bowl, layer the cooked brown rice with the bean mixture and top with salsa.
  - Garnish with a lime wedge and fresh cilantro before serving.
