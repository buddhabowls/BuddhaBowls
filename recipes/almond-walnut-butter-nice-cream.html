Almond Walnut Butter Nice Cream Recipe

Course: Dessert
Cuisine: American

Ingredients

  • 2 cups frozen bananas
  • 1/2 cup almond butter
  • 1/2 cup almond milk

Instructions

  1. Blend frozen bananas until smooth.
  2. Add almond butter and almond milk; blend until creamy.

NOTES:
Great topped with sliced almonds.
