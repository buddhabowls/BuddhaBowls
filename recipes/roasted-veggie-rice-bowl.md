ingredients:

  - jasmine rice
  - roasted mixed veggies
  - spinach
  - Sunflower seeds for garnish
  - tahini sauce
instructions:
  - Rinse the jasmine rice under cold water and cook according to package instructions (usually about 15-20 minutes).
  - While the rice is cooking, roast your mixed vegetables in the oven at 400°F (200°C) for about 20-25 minutes until tender.
  - Once the rice is cooked, fluff it with a fork and let it cool slightly.
  - In serving bowls, layer the cooked jasmine rice with the roasted veggies and chopped spinach.
  - Add avocado slices on top and drizzle with tahini sauce.
  - Sprinkle with sunflower seeds before serving.
