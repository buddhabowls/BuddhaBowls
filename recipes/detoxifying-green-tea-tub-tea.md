ingredients:

  - Lemon slices
  - Sea salt
instructions:
  - Combine 1 cup of dried green tea leaves with 1/2 cup of Epsom salt and 1/4 cup of sea salt.
  - Add fresh lemon slices for a refreshing scent.
  - Place in a muslin bag and hang under the faucet.
  - Soak for 20-30 minutes.
