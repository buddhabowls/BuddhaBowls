  - A squeeze of lemon juice
  - canned chickpeas
  - ripe avocado
  - Salt and pepper to taste

  - Add lemon juice, salt, pepper, and chili flakes if you like a bit of spice.
  - Serve with whole-grain crackers or veggie sticks.
