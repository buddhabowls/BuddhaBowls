ingredients:

  - bulgur
  - Maple Dijon dressing
  - pecans
  - red onion
instructions:
  - Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  - On a baking sheet, toss the halved Brussels sprouts and sliced red onion with a drizzle of olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast for about 20 minutes or until tender and slightly caramelized.
  - While the vegetables are roasting, rinse the bulgur under cold water and cook according to package instructions (usually about 12-15 minutes).
  - Once the bulgur is cooked, fluff it with a fork and let it cool slightly.
  - In a bowl, layer the cooked bulgur with the roasted Brussels sprouts, red onion, and chopped pecans.
  - Drizzle with maple dijon dressing before serving.
