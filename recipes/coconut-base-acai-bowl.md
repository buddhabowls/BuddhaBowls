ingredients:
  - '- 1 packet of frozen acai puree
  - 1/2 cup of coconut milk
  - 1/2 cup of frozen mixed berries
  - 1/2 frozen banana
  - Toppings of your choice
instructions:
  - In a blender, combine the frozen acai puree, mixed berries, banana, and coconut milk.
  - Blend until smooth and creamy, adding more coconut milk if needed to achieve the desired consistency.
  - Pour the blended mixture into a bowl.
  - Top with your favorite toppings such as granola, sliced fruits, shredded coconut, and chia seeds.
  - Enjoy your classic coconut acai bowl!
