ingredients:
  - 15ml coconut milk
  - 30ml coconut oil
  - 45g colloidal oatmeal
  - Coconut bowl + wooden spoon
  - Muslin cloth
instructions:
  - Grind oats into fine powder (if using whole oats)
  - In coconut bowl, mix oat powder with coconut oil
  - Add coconut milk gradually to reach paste consistency
  - Test on jawline - wait 10 minutes
  - Apply using circular massage motions
  - Leave on 15 minutes or overnight as spot treatment
  - Remove with lukewarm water and soft cloth
