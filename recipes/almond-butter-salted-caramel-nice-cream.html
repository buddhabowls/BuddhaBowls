Almond Butter Salted Caramel Nice Cream Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas,
  • 2 tbsp almond butter,
  • 2 tbsp salted caramel sauce, pinch of sea salt

Instructions

  • 1. Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
  • 2. Place frozen bananas in a blender.
  • 3. Add almond butter and salted caramel sauce.
  • 4. Blend until smooth.
  • 5. Serve immediately, topped with a pinch of sea salt.

Experience the perfect balance of sweet and salty with almond butter salted caramel nice cream. Reflect on the dualities in life and how they create harmony.

Salt enhances flavor; almond butter provides healthy fats.
