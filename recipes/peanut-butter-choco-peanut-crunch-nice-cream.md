ingredients:

  - crushed peanuts
  - peanut butter
  - ripe bananas
instructions:
  - Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
  - Place frozen bananas in a blender.
  - Add peanut butter and chocolate syrup.
  - Blend until smooth.
  - Stir in crushed peanuts.
  - Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.
