instructions:
  - Arrange pita wedges around the edges of the bowls.
  - Place chicken shawarma (or falafel) in the center.
  - Top with fried eggs.
  - Add scoops of tabbouleh, hummus, and tzatziki.
  - Garnish with pickled turnips and sprinkle with sumac.
