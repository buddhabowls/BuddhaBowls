ingredients:

  - ground cardamom
  - ground cloves
  - ripe bananas
instructions:
  - Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
  - Place frozen bananas in a blender.
  - Add cashew butter, ground cardamom, and ground cloves.
  - Blend until smooth.
  - Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.
