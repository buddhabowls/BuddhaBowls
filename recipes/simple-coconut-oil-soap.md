ingredients:

  - Distilled water
  - Essential oils
  - Lye
instructions:
  - Measure the coconut oil and lye separately.
  - Heat the coconut oil until melted.
  - Mix lye with distilled water (carefully).
  - Combine lye mixture with melted oil.
  - Blend until trace.
  - Pour into molds.
  - Cure for 4-6 weeks.
