ingredients:
  - '- 1 frozen banana
  - 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  - 1/2 cup almond milk
  - 1/2 cup frozen mixed berries
  - 1/2 cup spinach
  - blueberries
  - chopped nuts
  - kiwi
  - macaron cookies
  - shredded coconut
  - sliced strawberries
instructions:
  - In a blender, combine the frozen banana, frozen mixed berries, spinach, almond milk, and chia seeds. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  - Pour the smoothie into a coconut bowl.
  - Arrange the sliced strawberries, blueberries, kiwi, shredded coconut, chopped nuts, and macaron cookies on top of the smoothie.
  - Serve immediately and enjoy!
