ingredients:
  - bulgur
  - cherry tomatoes
  - Cucumbers
  - Fresh dill for garnish
  - hummus
  - olives
  - red onions
instructions:
  - Rinse the bulgur under cold water and cook according to package instructions (usually about 12-15 minutes).
  - While the bulgur is cooking, dice the cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, and red onions.
  - Once the bulgur is cooked, fluff it with a fork and let it cool slightly.
  - In serving bowls, layer the cooked bulgur with the diced tomatoes, cucumbers, red onions, and olives.
  - Top with hummus and garnish with fresh dill before serving.
