ingredients:

  - Lye
  - oatmeal
  - olive oil
instructions:
  - Prepare lye solution.
  - Melt oils together.
  - Combine oils and lye when both are at similar temperatures.
  - Blend until trace.
  - Stir in oatmeal and lavender oil.
  - Pour into molds and cure.
