ingredients:

  - of pink pitaya powder
  - of water
instructions:
  - In a mixing bowl, whisk together the water, lemon juice, and honey/agave nectar until well combined.
  - Gradually add the pink pitaya powder while whisking continuously to avoid lumps.
  - Once the mixture is smooth, pour it into popsicle molds.
  - Insert popsicle sticks and freeze for at least 4 hours or until completely solid.
  - Enjoy a refreshing and tangy pink pitaya lemonade popsicle on a hot day!
