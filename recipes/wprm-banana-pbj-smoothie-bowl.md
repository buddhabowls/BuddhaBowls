ingredients:
  - '- 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  - 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup
  - 1/2 cup almond milk
  - 2 ripe bananas
  - 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  - Toppings: sliced bananas
instructions:
  - In a blender, combine the frozen bananas, almond milk, peanut butter, chia seeds, and honey or maple syrup.
  - Blend until smooth and creamy. If the mixture is too thick, add a little more almond milk until you reach your desired consistency.
  - Pour the smoothie into a bowl and top with sliced bananas, granola, berries, a drizzle of peanut butter, and chia seeds.
  - Enjoy your nutritious and delicious smoothie bowl!
