instructions:
  - The first step is to decide what type of cannabis infusion you’ll use. Most people use cannabis tincture or infused coconut oil for gummies because they mix easily with the other ingredients. If you don’t already have an infusion ready, you’ll need to make one beforehand. (Tip: Decarboxylate your cannabis first to activate the THC or CBD!)
  - In a saucepan, pour your fruit juice and heat it over low-medium heat. Avoid boiling as high heat can degrade the cannabinoids.
  - Gradually sprinkle in the gelatin (or agar-agar) while whisking continuously to avoid clumps. Keep stirring until the mixture is smooth.
  - Once your mixture is smooth and slightly cooled (but still warm), add your cannabis tincture or infused oil. Stir thoroughly to ensure the cannabis is evenly distributed throughout the mixture. This step is crucial for consistent dosing in each gummy.
  - Using a dropper or small spoon, carefully fill your silicone molds with the gummy mixture. Work quickly as the mixture can start to set as it cools.
  - Place the filled molds in the refrigerator for at least 1-2 hours to allow the gummies to fully set. Once firm, pop them out of the molds.
