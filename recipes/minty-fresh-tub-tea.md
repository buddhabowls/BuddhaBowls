ingredients:

  - Dried peppermint
  - Epsom salt
  - Lemon oil
instructions:
  - Combine 1 cup of dried peppermint with 1/2 cup of Epsom salt and 1/4 cup of baking soda.
  - If desired, add a few drops of lemon oil.
  - Place in a muslin bag and hang under the faucet.
  - Soak for 20-30 minutes.
