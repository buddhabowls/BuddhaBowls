15 Walnut Butter Nice Cream Recipes 1. Classic Walnut Butter Nice Cream Instructions: - In a blender, combine 2 ripe bananas, 2 tablespoons of walnut butter, and 1/4 cup of almond milk. Blend until smooth. - Pour the mixture into a freezer-safe container and freeze for at least 4 hours. - Scoop the nice cream into bowls and serve with your favorite toppings. Ingredients: - 2 ripe bananas - 2 tablespoons walnut butter - 1/4 cup almond milk Recipe Notes: - You can add a drizzle of honey or maple syrup for extra sweetness. 2. Chocolate Walnut Butter Nice Cream Instructions: - Blend together 2 ripe bananas, 2 tablespoons of walnut butter, 2 tablespoons of cocoa powder, and 1/4 cup of coconut milk until creamy. - Transfer the mixture to a freezer-safe container and freeze for at least 4 hours. - Serve the nice cream with chocolate shavings on top. Ingredients: - 2 ripe bananas - 2 tablespoons walnut butter - 2 tablespoons cocoa powder - 1/4 cup coconut milk Recipe Notes: - You can use dark chocolate for a richer flavor. 3. Vegan Walnut Butter Nice Cream Instructions: - Combine 3 frozen bananas, 3 tablespoons of walnut butter, and 1/2 cup of oat milk in a blender. Blend until smooth. - Transfer the mixture to a loaf pan and freeze for 6 hours or overnight. - Serve the nice cream with a sprinkle of chopped walnuts. Ingredients: - 3 frozen bananas - 3 tablespoons walnut butter - 1/2 cup oat milk Recipe Notes: - Make sure the bananas are ripe before freezing for the best flavor. 4. Strawberry Walnut Butter Nice Cream Instructions: - Blend together 2 cups of frozen strawberries, 1/4 cup of walnut butter, and 1/2 cup of Greek yogurt until creamy. - Transfer the mixture to a shallow dish and freeze for 3-4 hours. - Top the nice cream with fresh strawberries before serving. Ingredients: - 2 cups frozen strawberries - 1/4 cup walnut butter - 1/2 cup Greek yogurt Recipe Notes: - You can use dairy-free yogurt for a vegan option. 5. Blueberry Walnut Butter Nice Cream Instructions: - In a blender, combine 2 ripe bananas, 1 cup of frozen blueberries, 3 tablespoons of walnut butter, and 1/4 cup of almond milk. Blend until smooth. - Pour the mixture into a freezer-safe container and freeze for at least 4 hours. - Garnish the nice cream with fresh blueberries before serving. Ingredients: - 2 ripe bananas - 1 cup frozen blueberries - 3 tablespoons walnut butter - 1/4 cup almond milk Recipe Notes: - You can add a squeeze of lemon juice for a refreshing twist. 6. Coconut Walnut Butter Nice Cream Instructions: - Blend together 2 ripe bananas, 1/2 cup of coconut cream, 2 tablespoons of walnut butter, and a splash of vanilla extract until creamy. - Transfer the mixture to a freezer-safe container and freeze for at least 4 hours. - Serve the nice cream with toasted coconut flakes on top. Ingredients: - 2 ripe bananas - 1/2 cup coconut cream - 2 tablespoons walnut butter - Vanilla extract Recipe Notes: - You can add a pinch of sea salt for a salted caramel flavor. 7. Raspberry Walnut Butter Nice Cream Instructions: - In a blender, combine 2 ripe bananas, 1 cup of frozen raspberries, 3 tablespoons of walnut butter, and 1/4 cup of coconut milk. Blend until smooth. - Pour the mixture into a loaf pan and freeze for at least 6 hours. - Top the nice cream with fresh raspberries before serving. Ingredients: - 2 ripe bananas - 1 cup frozen raspberries - 3 tablespoons walnut butter - 1/4 cup coconut milk Recipe Notes: - You can add a drizzle of raspberry sauce for extra flavor. 8. Pineapple Walnut Butter Nice Cream Instructions: - Blend together 2 cups of frozen pineapple chunks, 1/4 cup of walnut butter, and 1/2 cup of coconut milk until creamy. - Transfer the mixture to a shallow dish and freeze for 3-4 hours. - Garnish the nice cream with toasted coconut flakes before serving. Ingredients: - 2 cups frozen pineapple chunks - 1/4 cup walnut butter - 1/2 cup coconut milk Recipe Notes: - You can add a splash of rum extract for a tropical twist. 9. Matcha Walnut Butter Nice Cream Instructions: - In a blender, combine 2 ripe bananas, 2 teaspoons of matcha powder, 3 tablespoons of walnut butter, and 1/4 cup of almond milk. Pistachio Walnut Butter Nice Cream Instructions: - Blend together 2 ripe bananas, 1/4 cup of pistachios, 3 tablespoons of walnut butter, and a splash of vanilla extract until creamy. - Transfer the mixture to a loaf pan and freeze for at least 6 hours. - Top the nice cream with chopped pistachios before serving.
