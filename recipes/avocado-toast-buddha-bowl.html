Avocado Toast Buddha Bowl

A trendy and nutritious brunch bowl featuring creamy avocado toast topped with protein-rich poached eggs and tangy feta cheese.

  • 1 slice whole-grain bread
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes
  • salt & pepper
  1. Toast the bread.
  2. Mash avocado and spread on toast.
  3. Top with a fried egg and cherry tomatoes.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.

For a vegan option, replace eggs and feta with roasted chickpeas and nutritional yeast.
