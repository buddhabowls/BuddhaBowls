33 Delicious Nice Cream Recipes to Satisfy Your Sweet Tooth

Are you looking for a healthier alternative to traditional ice cream? Look no further than nice cream! Nice cream is a dairy-free, vegan-friendly frozen treat that is made with ripe bananas as the base. Not only is it delicious, but it's also incredibly easy to make and can be customized with a variety of flavors and toppings. In this tutorial, we'll walk you through the basics of making nice cream and share some mouthwatering recipes for you to try at home.

Basic Nice Cream Recipe:

Ingredients:

- 4 ripe bananas, peeled, sliced, and frozen
- 1/4 cup non-dairy milk (such as almond, coconut, or oat milk)
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

1. Place the frozen banana slices, non-dairy milk, and vanilla extract in a high-speed blender or food processor.
2. Blend on high until smooth and creamy, scraping down the sides as needed.
3. If the mixture is too thick, add more non-dairy milk, 1 tablespoon at a time, until it reaches your desired consistency.
4. Serve immediately as soft-serve nice cream, or transfer to a container and freeze for 1-2 hours for a firmer texture.

Now that you have the basic recipe down, let's get creative with some delicious flavor variations!

Chocolate Nice Cream:

Ingredients:
- Basic nice cream mixture
- 2-3 tablespoons cocoa powder
- 2 tablespoons maple syrup or agave nectar
- Chocolate chips or cacao nibs for topping

Instructions:
1. Add the cocoa powder and maple syrup to the basic nice cream mixture in the blender.
2. Blend until well combined and creamy.
3. Transfer the chocolate nice cream to serving bowls and top with chocolate chips or cacao nibs for an extra chocolatey crunch.

 

Berry Blast Nice Cream:

Ingredients:
- Basic nice cream mixture
- 1 cup mixed berries (such as strawberries, blueberries, and raspberries), fresh or frozen
- 2 tablespoons honey or agave nectar
- Fresh berries and granola for topping

Instructions:
1. Add the mixed berries and honey to the basic nice cream mixture in the blender.
2. Blend until the berries are fully incorporated and the nice cream is a vibrant pink color.
3. Serve the berry blast nice cream in bowls and top with fresh berries and granola for a fruity and crunchy twist.

 

Peanut Butter Banana Nice Cream:

Ingredients:
- Basic nice cream mixture
- 1/4 cup creamy peanut butter
- 2 tablespoons honey or maple syrup
- Chopped peanuts and a drizzle of peanut butter for topping

Instructions:
1. Add the peanut butter and honey to the basic nice cream mixture in the blender.
2. Blend until the peanut butter is fully mixed in and the nice cream is smooth and creamy.
3. Scoop the peanut butter banana nice cream into bowls and top with chopped peanuts and a drizzle of peanut butter for a nutty finish.
