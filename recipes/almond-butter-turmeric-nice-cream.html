Almond Butter Turmeric Nice Cream Recipe

A healthful dessert featuring almond butter and turmeric, known for its anti-inflammatory properties and vibrant color.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 2 tbsp almond butter
  • 1/2 tsp ground turmeric

Instructions

  1. Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
  2. Place frozen bananas in a blender.
  3. Add almond butter and ground turmeric.
  4. Blend until smooth.
  5. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.
