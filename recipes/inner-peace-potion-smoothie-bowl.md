ingredients:
  - '- 1 frozen banana
  - 1 tablespoon almond butter
  - 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  - 1 teaspoon honey
  - 1/2 cup almond milk
  - 1/2 cup frozen blueberries
  - 1/2 cup frozen strawberries
  - Toppings: sliced almonds
instructions:
  - In a blender, combine the frozen banana, blueberries, strawberries, almond milk, almond butter, chia seeds, and honey.
  - Blend until smooth and creamy, adding more almond milk if needed to reach your desired consistency.
  - Pour the smoothie into a bowl and top with sliced almonds, shredded coconut, fresh berries, and granola.
  - Take a moment to admire the beauty of your creation before indulging in its deliciousness.
