ingredients:

instructions:
  - Rinse the black rice under cold water and cook according to package instructions (usually about 30 minutes).
  - While the rice is cooking, mix together the mango chunks, diced bell peppers, and shelled edamame in a bowl.
  - Once the black rice is cooked, fluff it with a fork and let it cool slightly.
  - In serving bowls, layer the cooked black rice with the mango mixture.
  - Drizzle with coconut dressing and garnish with a lime wedge before serving.
