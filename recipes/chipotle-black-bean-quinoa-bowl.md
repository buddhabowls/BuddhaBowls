  - corn
  - Lime wedge for garnish
  - quinoa
instructions:
  - Rinse the quinoa under cold water and cook according to package instructions (usually about 15 minutes).
  - While the quinoa is cooking, mix the black beans, corn, and diced bell peppers in a bowl.
  - Once the quinoa is cooked, fluff it with a fork and let it cool slightly.
  - In a bowl, layer the cooked quinoa with the black bean mixture and top with sliced avocado.
  - Drizzle with chipotle sauce and garnish with a lime wedge before serving.
