ingredients:
  - A small candle
  - coconut bowl
  - Essential oil
  - salt
  - water
  - White sage or palo santo
  - Your favorite crystals
instructions:
  - Start by setting your intention. Take a moment to focus on what you want to achieve with this blessing.
  - Fill the coconut bowl with a bit of salt and water. This will serve as a cleansing agent.
  - Light the sage or palo santo and let the smoke fill the space. Move from room to room, allowing the smoke to cleanse each area.
  - As you cleanse, visualize any negative energy leaving your home.
  - After cleansing, place the coconut bowl in a central location in your home.
  - Add a few drops of essential oil to the water in the bowl for added positive energy.
  - Light the candle and say a blessing or affirmation, inviting peace and harmony into your home.
  - Optionally, place your favorite crystals around the coconut bowl to enhance the energy.
  - Take a moment to meditate or reflect on the positive energy you’ve invited into your space.
