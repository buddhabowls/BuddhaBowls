  - Top the grains with the roasted or steamed beets, sautéed kale or spinach, sliced avocado, and your choice of cooked chickpeas or tofu.
  - Spoon a dollop of the vibrant beet hummus onto the bowl.
  - Sprinkle with your favorite toppings such as toasted nuts or seeds, microgreens, and a drizzle of balsamic glaze for extra flavor.
