ingredients:
  - '- 1 ripe banana
  - 1 tablespoon of peanut butter
  - 1 teaspoon of honey
  - 1/2 cup of almond milk
  - 1/4 cup of oats
  - drizzle of peanut butter
  - granola
  - shredded coconut
  - sliced banana
instructions:
  - In a blender, combine the ripe banana, oats, peanut butter, almond milk, and honey (if using).
  - Blend until smooth and creamy, adding more almond milk if needed to reach your desired consistency.
  - Pour the smoothie into a coconut bowl.
  - Top with sliced banana, granola, shredded coconut, and a drizzle of peanut butter.
