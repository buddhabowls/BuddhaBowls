  - fabric glue
instructions:
  - Cut a piece of fabric to fit the interior of the coconut bowl.
  - Apply fabric glue to the inside of the bowl.
  - Carefully press the fabric into the bowl, smoothing out any wrinkles.
  - Trim any excess fabric from the edges.
  - Allow the glue to dry completely before using the bowl.
