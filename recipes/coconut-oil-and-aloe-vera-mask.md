ingredients:
  - 15ml fresh aloe vera gel
  - 30ml coconut oil
  - 5ml raw honey
  - Clean glass jar for storage
  - Double boiler
instructions:
  - Gently melt coconut oil if solid (low heat/double boiler)
  - Whisk aloe gel into warm oil until emulsified
  - Mix in honey and essential oils if using
  - Test on inner elbow - wait 15 minutes
  - Apply to clean face with brush or fingers
  - Leave on 15-20 minutes
  - Rinse with cool water
notes: >
  <p>• Use within 3 days when fresh</p>
