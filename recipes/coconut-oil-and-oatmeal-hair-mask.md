ingredients:
  - 30g colloidal oatmeal
  - 30ml aloe vera gel
  - 45ml coconut oil
  - Coconut bowl + bamboo whisk
  - Scalp applicator brush
instructions:
  - Grind oats into fine powder using coconut bowl
  - Melt coconut oil in separate container (40°C max)
  - Whisk oat powder with aloe vera to form base gel
  - Slowly emulsify coconut oil into mixture
  - Apply to dry scalp using tint brush:
  - Section hair into 1-inch partings
  - Apply directly to scalp surface
  - Gently massage with silicone scalp massager
  - Cover with shower cap + warm towel (20-30 mins)
  - Rinse with cool water followed by sulfate-free shampoo
