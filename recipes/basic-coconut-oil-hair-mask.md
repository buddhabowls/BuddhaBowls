ingredients:

instructions:
  - Melt ¼ cup oil in coconut shell (40°C water bath)
  - Test temperature on wrist (should feel warm)
  - Section hair into quadrants using butterfly clips
  - Apply using spiral motion:
  - Roots: Finger-tip massage (5 mins)
  - Lengths: Palm-gliding technique
  - Ends: Dip-twist sealing method
  - Wrap with microfiber towel (20-30 mins)
  - Rinse with cool water + apple cider vinegar spritz
