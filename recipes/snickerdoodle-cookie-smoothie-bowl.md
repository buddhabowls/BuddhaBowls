  - vanilla extract
instructions:
  - In a blender, combine the ripe banana, rolled oats, Greek yogurt, almond milk, ground cinnamon, vanilla extract, and honey or maple syrup (if using). Blend until smooth and creamy.
  - Once your smoothie base is ready, it's time to get creative with the toppings. Arrange your sliced bananas, chopped nuts, and crumbled snickerdoodle cookies on top of the smoothie.
  - Finish off with a sprinkle of cinnamon for that extra touch of warmth and flavor.
