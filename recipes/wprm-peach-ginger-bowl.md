ingredients:
  - '- 1 banana
  - 1 inch piece of ginger
  - 1 tablespoon honey
  - 1/2 cup coconut milk
  - 1/2 cup granola
  - 1/4 cup Greek yogurt
  - 1/4 cup shredded coconut
  - 2 ripe peaches
  - Sliced fruit for topping
instructions:
  - In a blender, combine the peaches, ginger, banana, coconut milk, Greek yogurt, and honey. Blend until smooth.
  - Pour the smoothie into a coconut bowl.
  - Top with granola, shredded coconut, and your choice of sliced fruit.
notes: >
  <p>- Use frozen peaches and bananas for a thicker consistency.<br />
- Substitute the coconut milk with almond milk for a different flavor.</p>
