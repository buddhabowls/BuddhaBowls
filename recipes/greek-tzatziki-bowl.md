ingredients:

  - cooked farro
  - cucumber
  - feta cheese
  - lemon juice
  - olive oil
  - red onion
  - Salt and pepper to taste
  - tzatziki sauce
instructions:
  - Prepare your farro according to the package instructions and let it cool.
  - Dice the cucumber, halve the cherry tomatoes, and slice the red onion.
  - In your coconut bowl, layer the farro, cucumber, tomatoes, onion, and feta.
  - Drizzle with tzatziki sauce, olive oil, and lemon juice. Season with salt and pepper.
