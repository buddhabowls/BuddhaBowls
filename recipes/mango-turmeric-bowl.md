ingredients:
  - '- 1 frozen banana
  - 1 ripe mango
  - 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  - 1/2 cup coconut milk
  - 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  - 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  - granola
  - nuts
  - seeds
  - sliced fruits
instructions:
  - In a blender, combine the diced mango, frozen banana, coconut milk, turmeric, and ground ginger.
  - Blend until smooth and creamy.
  - Pour the smoothie into a coconut bowl.
  - Sprinkle chia seeds on top of the smoothie.
  - Add your favorite toppings such as sliced fruits, nuts, seeds, or granola.
