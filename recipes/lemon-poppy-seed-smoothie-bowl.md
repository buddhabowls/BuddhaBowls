ingredients:
  - '- 1 ripe banana
  - 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup
  - 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  - 1 tablespoon poppy seeds
  - 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  - 1/4 cup almond milk
  - Fresh fruits and nuts for topping
  - zest of 1 lemon
instructions:
  - In a blender, combine the frozen banana, Greek yogurt, almond milk, lemon juice, lemon zest, and honey or maple syrup. Blend until smooth and creamy. The consistency should be thick enough to eat with a spoon. Adjust the amount of milk if needed.
  - Once the base is smooth, stir in the poppy seeds by hand to maintain their texture.
  - Pour the smoothie into a coconut bowl. Using a coconut bowl not only enhances the presentation but also supports sustainable practices by utilizing natural materials.
  - Decorate your smoothie bowl with fresh fruits and nuts. Blueberries and strawberries complement the lemon flavor beautifully, while almonds add a nice crunch.
  - Grab a spoon and enjoy your refreshing Lemon Poppy Seed Smoothie Bowl. It's a perfect blend of flavors and textures that will leave you feeling energized and satisfied.
