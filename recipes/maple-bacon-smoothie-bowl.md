ingredients:
  - '- 1 cup of frozen mango chunks
  - 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  - 1/2 cup of Greek yogurt
  - 1/4 cup of almond milk
  - 2 ripe bananas
  - 2 tablespoons of maple syrup
  - Cooked bacon slices
  - Maple syrup for drizzling
  - Optional: additional sliced bananas
instructions:
  - In a blender, combine the ripe bananas, frozen mango chunks, Greek yogurt, almond milk, maple syrup, and vanilla extract. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  - Once the smoothie mixture is well blended, pour it into a coconut bowl.
  - Now, it's time to add the toppings! Place the cooked bacon slices on top of the smoothie bowl, creating a beautiful and savory contrast to the sweet and fruity base. Feel free to get creative with the presentation by arranging the bacon slices in an eye-catching pattern.
  - To finish off this delectable creation, drizzle some more maple syrup over the top. The extra sweetness will complement the smoky flavor of the bacon and tie all the flavors together.
  - If you'd like to add some extra crunch and variety to your smoothie bowl, consider sprinkling on some additional sliced bananas, chopped nuts, or granola. These toppings will not only add texture but also enhance the overall taste experience.
