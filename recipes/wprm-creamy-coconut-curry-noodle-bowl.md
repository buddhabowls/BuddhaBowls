ingredients:
  - A handful of spinach or bok choy
  - coconut milk
  - cooked rice noodles or ramen noodles
  - cubed tofu or chickpeas
  - Lime wedge and fresh cilantro for garnish
  - red curry paste
  - sliced bell peppers
  - vegetable broth
instructions:
  - In a saucepan, heat the coconut milk and vegetable broth over medium heat. Stir in the red curry paste until well combined.
  - Add the bell peppers and tofu or chickpeas (if using) and simmer for 5 minutes until tender. Toss in spinach or bok choy at the end to wilt slightly.
  - Place the cooked noodles in your coconut bowl and pour the curry mixture over them.
  - Garnish with a squeeze of lime juice and fresh cilantro for a fragrant finish.
