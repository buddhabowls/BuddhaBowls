ingredients:

  - pecans
  - roasted butternut squash
instructions:
  - Rinse the farro under cold water and cook according to package instructions (usually about 30 minutes).
  - While the farro is cooking, roast the butternut squash in the oven at 400°F (200°C) for about 20 minutes, or until tender.
  - Once the farro is cooked, fluff it with a fork and let it cool slightly.
  - In a bowl, layer the cooked farro with roasted butternut squash, dried cranberries, chopped pecans, and apple slices.
  - Drizzle with maple vinaigrette before serving and enjoy!
