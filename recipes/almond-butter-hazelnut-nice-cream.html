Rich and creamy, this nice cream combines almond butter with hazelnuts for a decadent dessert experience.

Almond Butter Hazelnut Nice Cream Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas,
  • 2 tbsp almond butter,
  • 1/4 cup chopped hazelnuts

Instructions

  1. Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
  2. Place frozen bananas in a blender.
  3. Add almond butter and chopped hazelnuts.
  4. Blend until smooth.
  5. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.

Indulge in the rich, nutty flavor of hazelnuts blended with almond butter. This nice cream is a luxurious way to practice mindfulness and treat yourself.

Almonds: rich in Vitamin E; Hazelnuts: heart-healthy, high in antioxidants.
