ingredients:
  - '- Ice cubes
  - chia seeds
  - maple syrup
  - pink pitaya
  - unsweetened almond milk
  - vanilla extract
  - vanilla yogurt
instructions:
  - Add pink pitaya, vanilla yogurt, almond milk, chia seeds, and vanilla extract to the blender.
  - Add maple syrup if using.
  - Add ice cubes.
  - Blend on high speed for 2-3 minutes until smooth and creamy.
  - Let sit for 5 minutes to allow chia seeds to expand slightly.
  - Stir gently and serve in glasses.
