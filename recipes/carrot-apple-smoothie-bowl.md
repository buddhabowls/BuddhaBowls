ingredients:
  - '- 1 apple
  - 1 cup of chopped carrots
  - 1 tablespoon of honey
  - 1/2 cup of almond milk
  - 1/2 cup of Greek yogurt
  - 1/2 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  - 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  - Ice cubes
instructions:
  - Start by preparing the ingredients. Wash and chop the carrots and apple, and set them aside.
  - In a blender, combine the chopped carrots, apple, Greek yogurt, almond milk, honey (if using), ground cinnamon, and vanilla extract.
  - Blend the ingredients on high speed until smooth and creamy. If the smoothie is too thick, you can add more almond milk to reach your desired consistency.
  - Add a few ice cubes to the blender and blend again until the smoothie is chilled.
  - Once the smoothie is ready, pour it into glasses and serve immediately.
