A chocolate almond butter with coconut flavor.

Enjoy the flavors of Almond Joy with this delicious nut butter recipe!

Almond Joy Nut Butter

Course: Spread

Cuisine: American

Prep Time:

Servings: 1 cup

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups raw almonds
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup shredded coconut
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Instructions:

  1. 1. Roast almonds at 350°F (175°C) for 10-12 minutes. 
  2. Let cool, then blend in a food processor until smooth.
  3. Add cocoa powder, coconut, and salt; blend until combined.

NOTES: Perfect for dipping fruits!
