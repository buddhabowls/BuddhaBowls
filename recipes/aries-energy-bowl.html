Aries Energy Bowl

Embrace your fiery spirit with this energizing smoothie bowl! Perfect for Aries, it combines the freshness of spinach with the sweetness of banana, creating a delightful start to your day. Don’t forget to add your favorite toppings for an extra crunch!

Aries Energy Bowl Recipe

Course: Breakfast

Cuisine: Healthy

Servings: 1

Ingredients

  • 1 banana,
  • 1/2 cup spinach,
  • 1/2 cup almond milk,
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter,
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds,
  • Toppings: sliced strawberries, granola

Instructions

  1. Blend all ingredients until smooth.
  2. Top with strawberries and granola.

Add protein powder for an extra boost.
