ingredients:
  - cinnamon + 5ml nutmeg oil
  - organic soy wax flakes
  - pumpkin pie spice blend
  - Small pie tin + crackling wood wick
instructions:
  - Melt wax with spice blend (65°C)
  - Strain solids for smooth burn
  - Blend oils while cooling to 55°C
  - Pour into tin, add spice dusting
  - Cure 96hrs - patience = potency
  - Top with mini cinnamon stick
