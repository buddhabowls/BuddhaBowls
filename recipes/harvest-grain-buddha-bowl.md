ingredients:

  - farro
  - Maple Dijon dressing
  - pumpkin seeds
  - roasted Brussels sprouts
  - roasted butternut squash
instructions:
  - Cook farro according to package instructions.
  - Roast Brussels sprouts and butternut squash at 400°F for 25 minutes.
  - Combine farro, Brussels sprouts, butternut squash, cranberries, and pumpkin seeds in a bowl.
  - Drizzle with maple Dijon dressing.
