A unique blend of almond butter and tahini, creating a nutty flavor and creamy texture that’s both delicious and nutritious.

Almond Butter Tahini Nice Cream Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas, 2 tbsp almond butter, 1 tbsp tahini

Instructions

  • 1. Slice bananas and freeze them overnight. 2. Place frozen bananas in a blender. 3. Add almond butter and tahini. 4. Blend until smooth. 5. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.

Explore the unique combination of almond butter and tahini in this creamy nice cream. It’s a delightful way to practice mindfulness with its rich flavors.

Almonds: supports brain health; Tahini: high in calcium, good for bone health.
