instructions:
  - Start by roasting the beets. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Wash the beets, trim off the stems, and wrap them in aluminum foil. Place the wrapped beets on a baking sheet and roast for 45-60 minutes, or until they are tender. Once they are cool enough to handle, peel the skins off.
  - In a food processor, combine the roasted beets, chickpeas, garlic, tahini, lemon juice, olive oil, cumin, salt, and pepper. Blend until smooth, scraping down the sides as needed. If the mixture is too thick, add water a tablespoon at a time until you reach your desired consistency.
  - Taste and adjust the seasoning as needed. Transfer the beet hummus to a serving bowl and drizzle with a little extra olive oil. Serve with your favorite dippers such as pita bread, vegetable sticks, or crackers.
