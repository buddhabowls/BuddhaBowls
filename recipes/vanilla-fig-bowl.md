ingredients:
  - '- 1 tablespoon of honey
  - 1/2 cup of Greek yogurt
  - 1/4 cup of granola
  - 1/4 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  - 2 fresh figs
  - A sprinkle of shredded coconut
  - A sprinkle of chia seeds
  - ripe banana
instructions:
  - In a small bowl, mash the ripe banana with a fork until it reaches a smooth consistency.
  - Add the Greek yogurt and vanilla extract to the bowl with the mashed banana, and mix well to combine all the ingredients.
  - Transfer the creamy mixture to a coconut bowl, spreading it evenly across the bottom.
  - Arrange the sliced figs on top of the yogurt mixture, creating an attractive pattern that will delight both your eyes and your taste buds.
  - Sprinkle the granola over the figs, adding a delightful crunch to each spoonful of the Vanilla Fig Bowl.
  - Drizzle the honey over the entire bowl, adding a touch of natural sweetness that perfectly complements the other flavors.
