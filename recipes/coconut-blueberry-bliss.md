ingredients:
  - coconut milk
  - frozen blueberries
  - honey
  - vanilla extract
instructions:
  - In a blender, combine the frozen blueberries, coconut milk, honey, and vanilla extract.
  - Blend until smooth and creamy.
  - Pour the smoothie into a bowl.
  - Top with additional blueberries, coconut flakes, and your favorite toppings if desired.
notes: >
  <p>Consistency: For a thicker smoothie bowl, use less coconut milk or add more frozen blueberries.</p>
