ingredients:
  - '- Scissors
  - brush
  - coconut bowl
  - decorative paper or napkins
  - Mod Podge or decoupage glue
instructions:
  - Cut out designs from decorative paper or napkins.
  - Apply a thin layer of Mod Podge to the area of the bowl you wish to decorate.
  - Carefully place the paper design onto the glued area, smoothing out any wrinkles.
  - Apply another layer of Mod Podge over the paper to seal it.
  - Let it dry completely before use.
