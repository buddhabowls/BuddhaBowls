ingredients:
  - can coconut milk
  - corn
  - Fresh parsley for garnish
  - garlic
  - medium potatoes
  - mixed seafood
  - olive oil
  - onion
  - Salt and pepper to taste
  - vegetable or seafood broth
instructions:
  - In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, sautéing until translucent.
  - Add the diced potatoes and corn, stirring to combine. Cook for about 5 minutes.
  - Pour in the vegetable or seafood broth and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and let simmer until the potatoes are tender, about 15 minutes.
  - Stir in the coconut milk and mixed seafood. Cook for an additional 5-7 minutes, or until the seafood is cooked through.
  - Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot in coconut bowls, garnished with fresh parsley if desired.
