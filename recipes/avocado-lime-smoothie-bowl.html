Enjoy a refreshing and nutritious Avocado Lime Smoothie Bowl that’s perfect for breakfast or a quick snack. This smoothie bowl is packed with healthy fats and vibrant flavors!

Avocado Lime Smoothie Bowl Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/2 ripe avocado
  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1/4 cup fresh lime juice
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • Lime zest and sliced banana for topping

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine the avocado, frozen banana, lime juice, and almond milk.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour the smoothie into a bowl.
  4. Top with lime zest and sliced banana for added flavor and presentation.

Tips for the Perfect Smoothie Bowl

For an extra creamy texture, ensure your banana is fully frozen. You can also add spinach or kale for a nutrient boost without altering the flavor significantly.

Nutritional Information

This smoothie bowl is not only delicious but also packed with nutrients. Here’s a quick breakdown:

  • Calories: ~250
  • Protein: 3g
  • Fat: 12g
  • Carbohydrates: 35g
  • Fiber: 7g
