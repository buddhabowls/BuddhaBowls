ingredients:
  - '- 1 tsp vanilla extract
  - 1/2 cup full-fat coconut milk
  - Pinch of sea salt
  - cocoa powder
  - dairy-free dark chocolate chips
instructions:
  - In a small saucepan, heat the coconut milk over medium heat until it just begins to simmer.
  - Place the chocolate chips in a heatproof bowl and pour the hot coconut milk over them.
  - Let it sit for a minute, then stir until the chocolate is completely melted and smooth.
  - Stir in the vanilla extract and sea salt.
  - Cover the bowl and refrigerate the mixture for at least 2 hours, or until it's firm enough to scoop.
  - Use a small spoon or melon baller to scoop out the chocolate mixture and roll it into 1-inch balls.
  - Roll the truffles in cocoa powder, shredded coconut, or chopped nuts to coat.
  - Store the truffles in an airtight container in the refrigerator until ready to serve.
