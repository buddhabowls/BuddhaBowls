  - Blend until smooth and creamy, adding more almond milk if necessary to reach your desired consistency.
  - Pour the smoothie into a coconut bowl.
  - Add your favorite toppings such as granola, sliced almonds, shredded coconut, chia seeds, and fresh cherries.
  - Enjoy your Cherry Vanilla Smoothie Bowl with a spoon and savor every delicious bite!
