ingredients:
  - chocolate chips
  - crushed graham crackers
  - mini marshmallows
  - peanut butter
  - ripe bananas
instructions:
  - Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
  - Place frozen bananas in a blender.
  - Add peanut butter.
  - Blend until smooth.
  - Stir in crushed graham crackers, mini marshmallows, and chocolate chips.
  - Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.
