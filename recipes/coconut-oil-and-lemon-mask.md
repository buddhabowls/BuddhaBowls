ingredients:
  - fresh lemon juice
  - raw honey
  - Small mixing bowl
  - virgin coconut oil
instructions:
  - Combine melted coconut oil and honey in bowl
  - Whisk in lemon juice until emulsified
  - Test on wrist - wait 5 minutes
  - Apply thin layer to clean face
  - Relax 10-15 minutes
  - Rinse with lukewarm water
