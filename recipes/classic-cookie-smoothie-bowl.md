ingredients:
  - '- 1 tablespoon of cacao powder
  - 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  - 1/2 cup of almond milk
  - 1/2 cup of oats
  - 2 ripe bananas
  - 2 tablespoons of almond butter
  - A pinch of salt
  - Cookie crumbles
  - chocolate chips
  - granola
  - Sliced bananas
instructions:
  - In a blender, combine the ripe bananas, oats, almond milk, almond butter, cacao powder, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  - Once the smoothie base is ready, it's time to assemble our bowl. Pour the smoothie into a coconut bowl for that extra tropical touch.
  - Now comes the fun part – adding the toppings! Sprinkle some granola on top for a delightful crunch, then add some sliced bananas for a fresh and sweet touch.
notes: >
