ingredients:
  - curry sauce
  - Fresh cilantro for garnish
  - lentils
  - quinoa
  - roasted chickpeas
  - spinach
instructions:
  - Rinse the quinoa and lentils under cold water. Cook the quinoa according to package instructions (usually about 15 minutes) and the lentils (about 20 minutes) until tender.
  - While the quinoa and lentils are cooking, roast the chickpeas in the oven at 400°F (200°C) for about 15 minutes, tossing them with curry sauce.
  - Once the quinoa and lentils are cooked, fluff the quinoa with a fork and combine them in a bowl.
  - Layer the cooked quinoa with lentils, chopped spinach, and roasted chickpeas.
  - Drizzle with extra curry sauce and garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.
