ingredients:
  - kale
  - olive oil
  - quinoa
  - Salt and pepper to taste
  - sweet potato
  - tahini
instructions:
  - Roast sweet potato cubes at 400°F for 25 minutes.
  - Cook quinoa according to package instructions.
  - Sauté kale in olive oil until wilted. In a bowl, layer quinoa, sweet potato, and kale.
  - Drizzle with tahini and season with salt and pepper.
