ingredients:

  - Dried ginger root
  - Epsom salt
  - honey
instructions:
  - Mix 1/2 cup of dried ginger root, 1/2 cup of Epsom salt, and 2-3 cinnamon sticks.
  - If desired, add honey for a soothing touch.
  - Place in a muslin bag and add to the tub as it fills.
  - Soak for 20-30 minutes.
