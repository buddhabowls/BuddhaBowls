ingredients:
  - '- 1 medium sweet potato
  - 1 ripe banana
  - 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  - 1/2 cup almond milk
  - 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  - 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  - 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  - 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  - Toppings of your choice
instructions:
  - In a blender, combine the baked sweet potato, banana, almond milk, cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, maple syrup, and vanilla extract.
  - Blend until smooth and creamy, adding more almond milk if needed to reach your desired consistency.
  - Pour the smoothie into a coconut bowl (or any bowl of your choice).
  - Top with your favorite toppings. I used granola, sliced banana, and a sprinkle of cinnamon.
  - Grab a spoon and dig in!
