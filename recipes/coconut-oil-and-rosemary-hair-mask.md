ingredients:
  - 15ml cold-pressed castor oil
  - 60ml fractionated coconut oil
  - Coconut bowl + dropper bottle
  - drops rosemary EO
  - Scalp massager tool
instructions:
  - Warm coconut oil in shell over double boiler (3 mins)
  - Remove from heat - mix in castor oil and aloe vera
  - Cool to 40°C before adding rosemary essential oil
  - Section hair - apply using dropper along part lines
  - Massage scalp 5 mins with ceramic tool
  - Wrap hair in warm microfiber towel (20-30 mins)
  - Rinse with cool water - no shampoo needed
