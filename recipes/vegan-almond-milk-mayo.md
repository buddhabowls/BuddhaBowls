ingredients:
  - '- 1 cup neutral-flavored oil
  - 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  - 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  - 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  - 1/2 teaspoon salt
  - 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
instructions:
  - In a small bowl, whisk together the almond milk and apple cider vinegar. Let it sit for a few minutes to curdle slightly.
  - Transfer the curdled almond milk mixture to a blender or food processor. Add the Dijon mustard, salt, and garlic powder.
  - With the blender running on low speed, slowly drizzle in the oil in a steady stream until the mixture emulsifies and thickens.
  - Taste the mayo and adjust the seasonings as needed.
