instructions:
  - Prepare congee and place in bowls.
  - Add steamed pork buns and soft-boiled eggs.
  - Sprinkle edamame around the bowl.
  - Drizzle with chili oil and soy sauce.
  - Garnish with green onions and sesame seeds.
