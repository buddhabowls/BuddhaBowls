ingredients:

  - Distilled water
  - Lye
  - olive oil
instructions:
  - Brew chamomile tea and let cool.
  - Measure lye and mix into cooled tea.
  - Melt coconut oil and olive oil.
  - Combine oils and lye solution, blend until trace.
  - Pour into molds and cure for 4-6 weeks.
