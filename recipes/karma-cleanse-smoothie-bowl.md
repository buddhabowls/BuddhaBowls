ingredients:
  - '- 1 ripe banana
  - 1 tablespoon of chia seeds
  - 1/2 cup of almond milk
  - 1/2 cup of frozen berries
  - 1/2 cup of kale
  - 1/2 cup of spinach
  - coconut flakes
  - granola
  - nuts
  - sliced fruits
instructions:
  - In a blender, combine the ripe banana, frozen berries, spinach, kale, almond milk, and chia seeds.
  - Blend until smooth and creamy, adding more almond milk if needed to reach your desired consistency.
  - Pour the smoothie into a bowl.
  - Top with your favorite toppings, such as granola, sliced fruits, coconut flakes, or nuts.
  - Take a moment to admire the vibrant colors and beautiful presentation of your Karma Cleanse Smoothie Bowl.
  - Enjoy mindfully, savoring each spoonful and appreciating the nourishment it provides for your body and soul.
