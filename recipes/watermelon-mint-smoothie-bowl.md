  - '- 1 frozen banana
  - 1/4 cup of coconut milk
  - 2 cups of diced watermelon
  - Toppings of your choice
  - A handful of fresh mint leaves
instructions:
  - In a blender, combine the diced watermelon, frozen banana, coconut milk, and fresh mint leaves.
  - Blend until smooth and creamy, adding more coconut milk if needed to reach your desired consistency.
  - Pour the smoothie into a coconut bowl.
  - Top with your favorite toppings, such as sliced strawberries, blueberries, shredded coconut, and chia seeds.
