ingredients:
  - can coconut milk
  - chili paste
  - Fresh cilantro for garnish
  - garlic
  - ginger
  - lime juice
  - rice noodles
  - shrimp
instructions:
  - Cook the rice noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  - In a large skillet, heat a splash of oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, sautéing until fragrant.
  - Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook until they turn pink, about 3-4 minutes.
  - Stir in the chili paste and coconut milk, mixing well to combine. Let it simmer for about 5 minutes.
  - Add the cooked rice noodles to the skillet and toss to coat them in the sauce.
  - Finish with lime juice and adjust seasoning if needed. Serve hot in coconut bowls, garnished with fresh cilantro.
