instructions:
  - In a blender, combine the fresh spinach, banana, pineapple chunks, mango chunks, and coconut water.
  - Blend until smooth and creamy, adding more coconut water if needed to reach your desired consistency.
  - Pour the green smoothie into a coconut bowl.
