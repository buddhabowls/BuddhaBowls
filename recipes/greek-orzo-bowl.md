ingredients:
  - cherry tomatoes
  - cucumber
  - Fresh dill for garnish
  - olives
  - orzo
  - red onion
  - tzatziki sauce
instructions:
  - Rinse the orzo under cold water and cook according to package instructions (usually about 8-10 minutes).
  - While the orzo is cooking, dice the cucumber, halve the cherry tomatoes, and dice the red onion.
  - Once the orzo is cooked, drain it and let it cool slightly.
  - In a bowl, layer the cooked orzo with the diced cucumber, halved cherry tomatoes, diced red onion, and sliced olives.
  - Drizzle with tzatziki sauce and garnish with fresh dill before serving.
