ingredients:

  - drops sweet orange essential oil
  - grapeseed oil

  - virgin coconut oil
  - Zest of 1 organic orange
instructions:
  - Place coconut bowl in morning sunlight for 5 minutes
  - Add coconut oil - let it soften naturally
  - Zest orange directly into bowl (clockwise motion)
  - Whisk in grapeseed oil using bamboo tools
  - Add essential oils while reciting daily intentions
  - Stir until mixture smells like
  - Transfer to jar using citrus-colored funnel
