ingredients:
  - '- 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup
  - 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  - 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  - 1/4 cup coconut flour
  - 1/4 cup coconut milk
  - 4 eggs
  - Pinch of salt
instructions:
  - Preheat your waffle iron.
  - In a bowl, whisk together the coconut flour and baking powder.
  - In a separate bowl, beat the eggs, then add the coconut milk, honey or maple syrup, vanilla extract, and salt. Mix well.
  - Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until well combined.
  - Pour the batter onto the preheated waffle iron and cook according to the manufacturer's instructions.
