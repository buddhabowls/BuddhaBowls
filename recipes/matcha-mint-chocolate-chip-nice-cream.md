ingredients:
  - dairy-free chocolate chips
  - matcha powder
  - peppermint extract
  - ripe bananas
instructions:
  - Start by placing the frozen banana slices in a high-speed blender or food processor. Blend until the bananas become creamy and smooth, resembling the texture of soft-serve ice cream.
  - Add the matcha powder and peppermint extract to the blender. Continue blending until the matcha and peppermint are fully incorporated into the banana nice cream.
  - Transfer the nice cream to a mixing bowl and fold in the dairy-free chocolate chips.
  - If you prefer a firmer texture, you can place the nice cream in a container and freeze it for an additional 1-2 hours.
