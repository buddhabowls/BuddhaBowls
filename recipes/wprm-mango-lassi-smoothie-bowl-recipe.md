ingredients:
  - '- 1 cup plain yogurt
  - 1 ripe mango
  - 1-2 tablespoons honey or agave nectar
  - 1/2 cup ice
  - Optional toppings: sliced fresh mango
instructions:
  - In a blender, combine the diced mango, yogurt, honey or agave nectar, and ice.
  - Blend until smooth and creamy, adding a splash of water if needed to reach your desired consistency.
  - Pour the mango lassi smoothie into a coconut bowl.
  - Top with sliced fresh mango, shredded coconut, chopped nuts, and chia seeds, or any other toppings you like.
  - Enjoy your mango lassi smoothie bowl with a spoon!
