ingredients:
  - 10ml coconut oil
  - 15g bentonite clay
  - 15ml apple cider vinegar
  - Coconut bowl set
  - Flat brush
instructions:
  - Sanitize coconut bowl with boiling water
  - Combine clay and ACV in bowl - mix with wooden spatula
  - Add melted coconut oil slowly while stirring
  - Mix to smooth paste consistency
  - Apply immediately with brush using upward strokes
  - Let dry 10-15 minutes (avoid facial movements)
  - Rinse with tepid water using circular motions
