ingredients:
  - '- Ice cubes
  - baby spinach
  - chia seeds
  - fresh orange juice
  - kiwi
  - pink pitaya
instructions:
  - Add pink pitaya, spinach, and kiwi to the blender.
  - Pour in orange juice and add chia seeds.
  - Add ice cubes.
  - Blend on high speed for 2-3 minutes until smooth and creamy.
  - Let sit for 2 minutes to allow chia seeds to expand slightly.
  - Stir gently and serve in glasses.
notes: >
  <p>For added nutrition, consider adding a scoop of your favorite plant-based protein powder. Pre-soak chia seeds in the orange juice for 10 minutes before blending for a smoother texture. This smoothie is an excellent way to incorporate more greens into your diet.</p>
