ingredients:
  - '- 1/2 cup almond milk
  - 1/2 cup pistachio butter
  - 1/4 cup crushed pistachios
  - 2 cups frozen bananas

  - Blend frozen bananas until smooth.
  - Add pistachio butter and almond milk; blend until creamy.
  - Stir in crushed pistachios.
