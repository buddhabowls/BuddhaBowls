ingredients:

  - 1 frozen banana
  - 1 ripe peach
  - 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup
  - 1/2 cup almond milk
  - 1/2 cup green tea
  - sliced peaches
instructions:
  - In a blender, combine the sliced peach, frozen banana, fresh spinach, green tea, almond milk, and sweetener (if using).
  - Blend until smooth and creamy, adding more liquid if necessary to reach your desired consistency.
  - Pour the smoothie into a coconut bowl.
  - Top with sliced peaches, granola, chia seeds, and shredded coconut.
  - Enjoy your delicious and nourishing Green Tea Peach Smoothie Bowl!
