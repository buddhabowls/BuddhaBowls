Floral lavender mixed with almond butter for a soothing and unique flavor.

Almond Butter Lavender Nice Cream Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas, 2 tbsp almond butter, 1 tsp dried lavender flowers

Instructions

  • 1. Slice bananas and freeze them overnight. 2. Place frozen bananas in a blender. 3. Add almond butter and dried lavender. 4. Blend until smooth. 5. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.

Experience the calming essence of lavender in this nice cream. Allow each bite to promote relaxation and mindfulness, reminding you to take time for yourself.

Lavender can promote relaxation; almond butter provides healthy fats.
