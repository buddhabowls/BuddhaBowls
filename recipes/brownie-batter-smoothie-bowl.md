ingredients:
  - '- 1 ripe banana
  - 1 tablespoon of almond butter
  - 1 tablespoon of cacao powder
  - 1 tablespoon of honey or maple syrup
  - 1/2 cup of almond milk
  - 1/4 cup of oats
  - Toppings of your choice
instructions:
  - In a blender, combine the ripe banana, oats, almond butter, cacao powder, almond milk, and honey or maple syrup (if using). Blend until smooth and creamy.
  - Once the mixture is well blended, pour it into your coconut bowls.
  - Now, it's time to get creative with the toppings! Arrange your sliced bananas, shredded coconut, cacao nibs, and a drizzle of almond butter on top of your smoothie bowl.
  - Grab a spoon and dig in! Enjoy the rich and chocolatey flavors of this brownie batter smoothie bowl.
