  A tropical coconut lover's dream, this smoothie combines the exotic flavor of pitaya with the rich, creamy taste of coconut in multiple forms. The addition of flax seeds provides omega-3 fatty acids and fiber, making this a nutritious and indulgent treat.

ingredients:
  - '- Ice cubes
  - coconut milk
  - coconut yogurt
  - flax seeds
  - pink pitaya
  - shredded coconut
  - vanilla extract
instructions:
  - Add pink pitaya, coconut yogurt, coconut milk, flax seeds, shredded coconut, and vanilla extract to the blender.
  - Add ice cubes.
  - Blend on high speed for 2-3 minutes until smooth and creamy.
  - Pour into glasses and sprinkle with additional shredded coconut if desired.
  - Serve immediately.
notes: >
  <p>For a more indulgent version, add a tablespoon of coconut cream. Freeze coconut milk ice cubes in advance for an extra creamy and frosty texture. Garnish with a sprinkle of toasted coconut flakes for added crunch and flavor.</p>
