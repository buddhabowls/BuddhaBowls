ingredients:
  - almond butter
  - chopped almonds
  - mini chocolate chips
  - mini marshmallows
  - ripe bananas
instructions:
  - Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
  - Place frozen bananas in a blender.
  - Add almond butter.
  - Blend until smooth.
  - Stir in mini marshmallows, chopped almonds, and mini chocolate chips.
  - Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.
