This nice cream features roasted bananas blended with almond butter for a warm, sweet, and creamy dessert.

Almond Butter Roasted Banana Nice Cream Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas, 2 tbsp almond butter, 2 tbsp roasted banana pieces

Instructions

  • 1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). 2. Slice and roast 2 bananas for 20 minutes until caramelized. 3. Freeze roasted bananas overnight. 4. Blend frozen bananas, roasted banana pieces, and almond butter until smooth. 5. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.

Delight in the warm, caramelized flavors of roasted bananas blended with almond butter. This nice cream is a comforting treat that encourages mindfulness.

Almonds: heart-healthy; Bananas: high in potassium, good for energy.
