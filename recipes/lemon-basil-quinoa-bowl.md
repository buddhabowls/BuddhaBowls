  - yellow squash
  - zucchini
instructions:
  - Rinse the quinoa under cold water and cook according to package instructions (usually about 15 minutes).
  - While the quinoa is cooking, sauté the diced zucchini and yellow squash in a pan over medium heat until tender, about 5-7 minutes.
  - Once the quinoa is cooked, fluff it with a fork and let it cool slightly.
  - In a bowl, layer the cooked quinoa with halved cherry tomatoes, sautéed zucchini, yellow squash, and sliced avocado.
  - Drizzle with lemon basil dressing before serving.
