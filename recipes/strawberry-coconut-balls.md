ingredients:
  - '- 1 cup of shredded coconut
  - 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  - 1/2 cup of fresh strawberries
  - 1/4 cup of almond flour
  - 2 tablespoons of honey or maple syrup
  - Coconut bowls for serving
  - Pinch of salt
instructions:
  - In a food processor, combine the shredded coconut, fresh strawberries, almond flour, honey or maple syrup, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt.
  - Pulse the mixture until it comes together and forms a sticky dough.
  - Using your hands, roll the dough into small balls and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  - Place the strawberry coconut balls in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes to allow them to firm up.
  - Once chilled, serve the strawberry coconut balls in sustainable coconut bowls for an extra touch of eco-friendly elegance.
