instructions:
  - In a well-ventilated area, mix 4 oz of lye with 10 oz of distilled water and let cool.
  - Heat 16 oz of coconut oil until melted.
  - Combine the lye mixture with the coconut oil and blend until trace.
  - Pour into molds and let cure for 4-6 weeks.
