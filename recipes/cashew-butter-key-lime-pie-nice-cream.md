ingredients:
  - cashew butter
  - ripe bananas
  - zest and juice of 1 lime
instructions:
  - Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
  - Place frozen bananas in a blender.
  - Add cashew butter, lime zest, and lime juice.
  - Blend until smooth.
  - Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.
