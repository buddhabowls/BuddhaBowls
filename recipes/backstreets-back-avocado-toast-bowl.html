Backstreet's Back Avocado Toast Bowl Recipe

Course: Lunch
Cuisine: Healthy
Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 2 slices whole grain bread,
  • 1 avocado,
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes,
  • Salt and pepper to taste,
  • Lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread, mash the avocado, and spread it in a coconut bowl.
  2. Top with tomatoes, salt, pepper, and lemon juice.

Add a poached egg for extra protein!
