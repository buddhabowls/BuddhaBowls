ingredients:
  - chickpeas
  - curry sauce
  - Fresh cilantro for garnish
  - fresh spinach
  - quinoa
  - roasted cauliflower
instructions:
  - Rinse the quinoa under cold water and cook according to package instructions (usually about 15 minutes).
  - While the quinoa is cooking, roast the chickpeas and cauliflower in the oven at 400°F (200°C) for about 20 minutes, tossing with curry sauce halfway through.
  - Once the quinoa is cooked, fluff it with a fork and let it cool slightly.
  - In a bowl, layer the cooked quinoa with roasted chickpeas, cauliflower, and fresh spinach.
  - Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.
