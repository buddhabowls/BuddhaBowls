instructions:
  - Blend the Base: Add the frozen banana slices, soaked figs, peanut butter, and vanilla extract to a high-speed blender or food processor. Blend until smooth and creamy. If the mixture is too thick, add a small splash of plant-based milk to help it along.
  - Scoop & Serve: Spoon the nice cream into coconut bowls for that extra mindful touch.
  - Optional Toppings: Get creative! Add sliced fresh figs, a drizzle of peanut butter, granola, or even a sprinkle of cacao nibs for some crunch.
  - Enjoy Mindfully: Sit back, take a deep breath, and savor every spoonful of this delicious creation.
