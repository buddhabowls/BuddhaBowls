  - Toppings: sliced apple
instructions:
  - In a blender, combine the chopped apple, rolled oats, almond milk, cinnamon, and honey or maple syrup.
  - Blend until smooth and creamy.
  - Pour the smoothie into a coconut bowl.
  - Top with sliced apple, granola, chia seeds, and a sprinkle of cinnamon.
