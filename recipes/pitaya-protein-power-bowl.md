ingredients:
  - '- Ice cubes
  - almond butter
  - pink pitaya
  - ripe banana
  - scoop vanilla protein powder
  - skim milk
instructions:
  - Add pink pitaya, banana, protein powder, skim milk, and almond butter to the blender.
  - Add ice cubes.
  - Blend on high speed for 2-3 minutes until smooth and creamy.
  - Pour into glasses and serve immediately.
notes: >
  <p>For extra protein, add an additional half scoop of protein powder. Use Greek yogurt instead of milk for a thicker, more protein-rich smoothie. Add a handful of spinach or kale for additional nutrients without altering the taste significantly.</p>
