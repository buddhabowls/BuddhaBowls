ingredients:
  - '- 1 cup frozen strawberries
  - 1 ripe banana
  - 1 tablespoon honey
  - 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
  - 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  - 1/4 cup almond milk
  - Toppings: fresh strawberries
instructions:
  - In a blender, combine the frozen strawberries, ripe banana, Greek yogurt, almond milk, vanilla extract, and honey (if using). Blend until smooth and creamy.
  - Pour the smoothie into a bowl and top with fresh strawberries, granola, shredded coconut, and a drizzle of honey.
  - Enjoy your strawberry shortcake smoothie bowl with a spoon and savor every delicious bite!
