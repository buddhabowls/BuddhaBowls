instructions:

  - Break the Cookies & Cream sandwich cookies into smaller pieces and add them to the blender. Pulse a few times to incorporate the cookie pieces into the smoothie mixture, but be sure to leave some larger chunks for added texture.
  - Pour the smoothie into a coconut bowl.
  - Top with your favorite toppings. Get creative and make it as colorful and flavorful as you like!
