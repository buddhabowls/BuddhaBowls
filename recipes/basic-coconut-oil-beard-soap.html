Basic Coconut Oil Beard Soap

This basic coconut oil beard soap bar is perfect for all beard types, leaving your beard feeling soft and clean.

  • 8 oz Coconut oil
  • 4 oz Lye
  • 8 oz Distilled water
  • 4 oz Olive oil
  • Essential oil (optional)
  1. Measure lye and water separately
  2. Slowly mix lye into water until dissolved
  3. Melt coconut oil and olive oil together
  4. Combine oils and lye solution, blend until trace
  5. Pour into molds and let cure for 4-6 weeks.
