A simple salve that moisturizes and protects the skin.

This basic coconut oil skin salve is perfect for dry patches and minor irritations.

Basic Coconut Oil Skin Salve

Ingredients:

  • Coconut oil,
  • Beeswax

Instructions:

  1. In a double boiler, melt 1/2 cup of coconut oil and 1/4 cup of beeswax together.
  2. Stir until fully combined.
  3. Pour into a container and let cool.
