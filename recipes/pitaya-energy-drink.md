ingredients:
  - '- Ice cubes
  - chia seeds
  - coconut water
  - lime juice
  - pineapple juice
  - Pink pitaya powder
instructions:
  - In a shaker bottle, combine pitaya powder, coconut water, and pineapple juice.
  - Shake vigorously until well mixed.
  - Add chia seeds and lime juice, shake again.
  - Pour over ice in a glass.
  - Let sit for 5 minutes to allow chia seeds to expand.
  - Stir before drinking.
