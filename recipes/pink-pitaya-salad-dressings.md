ingredients:
  - apple cider vinegar
  - Dijon mustard
  - honey
  - olive oil
  - Pink pitaya powder
  - Salt and pepper to taste
instructions:
  - In a jar, combine all ingredients.
  - Shake vigorously until well combined.
  - Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
  - Use immediately or store in the refrigerator.
notes: >
  <p>This dressing pairs well with fruit-based salads or mixed greens with goat cheese.<br />
