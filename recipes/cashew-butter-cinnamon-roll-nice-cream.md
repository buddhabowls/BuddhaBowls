ingredients:

  - ground cinnamon
  - ground nutmeg
  - ripe bananas
instructions:
  - Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
  - Place frozen bananas in a blender.
  - Add cashew butter, ground cinnamon, and ground nutmeg.
  - Blend until smooth.
  - Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.
