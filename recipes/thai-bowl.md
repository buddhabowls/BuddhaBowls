instructions:
  - Start by preparing the Thai Peanut Sauce. In a small bowl, whisk together the peanut butter, soy sauce, lime juice, honey, ginger, garlic, and red pepper flakes. Add water as needed to achieve your desired consistency. Set the sauce aside.
  - Assemble the Buddha Bowl by dividing the cooked jasmine rice among serving bowls. Arrange the shredded purple cabbage, spiralized carrots, sliced cucumber, edamame beans, and sliced avocado on top of the rice.
  - Drizzle the Thai Peanut Sauce over the Buddha Bowls, and garnish with chopped cilantro and crushed peanuts. Serve with lime wedges on the side for an extra burst of citrus flavor.
