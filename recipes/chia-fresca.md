ingredients:
  - '- 1-2 teaspoons of honey or agave syrup
  - 2 cups of water
  - Fresh mint leaves
  - Ice cubes
  - Juice of 1 lime
  - of chia seeds
instructions:
  - - In a mixing bowl, combine the chia seeds with water. Stir well to prevent clumping and let them sit for about 10 minutes until they form a gel-like consistency.
  - - Once the chia seeds have absorbed the water, add the lime juice and honey or agave syrup. Stir well until the sweetener is fully dissolved.
  - - Pour the Chia Fresca into a coconut bowl for a tropical presentation. Add ice cubes if desired for extra refreshment.
  - - Garnish with fresh mint leaves for an added burst of flavor and aroma. Serve immediately and enjoy your refreshing Chia Fresca!
