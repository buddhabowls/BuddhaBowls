  - Dried cranberries or raisins
  - maple syrup
  - of salt
  - pumpkin pie spice
  - pumpkin puree
  - rolled oats
  - vanilla extract
instructions:
  - In a medium saucepan, combine pumpkin puree, almond milk, rolled oats, maple syrup, pumpkin pie spice, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt.
  - Heat over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the mixture is warm and the oats are cooked (about 5-7 minutes).
  - Once heated through, remove from heat and let it sit for a minute to thicken slightly.
  - Pour the warm pumpkin mixture into a bowl.
  - Top with chopped nuts, dried cranberries, a dollop of Greek yogurt, and a sprinkle of additional pumpkin pie spice.
  - Serve warm and enjoy your Pumpkin Spice Bowl!
