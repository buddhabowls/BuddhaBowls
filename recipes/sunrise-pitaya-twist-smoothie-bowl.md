ingredients:
  - '- Ice cubes
  - fresh carrot juice
  - fresh mango chunks
  - grated carrots
  - lemon juice
  - pink pitaya
  - turmeric powder
instructions:
  - Add pink pitaya, mango chunks, grated carrots, carrot juice, lemon juice, and turmeric to the blender.
  - Add ice cubes.
  - Blend on high speed for 2-3 minutes until smooth and well combined.
  - Pour into glasses, creating a layered effect if desired.
  - Serve immediately.
