ingredients:
  - ginger dressing
  - quinoa
  - Sesame seeds for garnish
  - shredded carrots
  - snap peas
  - tofu
instructions:
  - Rinse the quinoa under cold water and cook according to package instructions (usually about 15 minutes).
  - While the quinoa is cooking, heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and sear the cubed tofu until golden brown, about 5-7 minutes, adding 1 tbsp of ginger dressing halfway through.
  - Lightly steam the shredded carrots and snap peas until tender, about 3-4 minutes.
  - Once the quinoa is cooked, fluff it with a fork and let it cool slightly.
  - In a bowl, layer the cooked quinoa with seared tofu, steamed carrots, and snap peas.
  - Drizzle with additional ginger dressing and sprinkle with sesame seeds before serving.
