instructions:
  - Prepare your soba noodles according to the package instructions.
  - Whisk together sesame oil, soy sauce, and ginger.
  - In your coconut bowl, combine noodles, cabbage, edamame, carrots, and onions.
  - Drizzle the sesame ginger dressing over the bowl.
  - Sprinkle with sesame seeds.
