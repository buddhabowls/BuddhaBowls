instructions:
  - Measure lye and water separately.
  - Slowly add lye to water, stirring until dissolved.
  - In a separate bowl, melt coconut oil and olive oil.
  - Combine oils and lye solution, blend until trace.
  - Pour into molds and let cure for 4-6 weeks.
