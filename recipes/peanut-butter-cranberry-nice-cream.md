ingredients:

instructions:
  - Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
  - Place frozen bananas in a blender.
  - Add peanut butter and orange juice.
  - Blend until smooth.
  - Stir in fresh cranberries.
  - Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.
