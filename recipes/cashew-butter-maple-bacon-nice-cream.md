ingredients:
  - cashew butter
  - cooked crumbled bacon
  - maple syrup
  - ripe bananas
instructions:
  - Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
  - Place frozen bananas in a blender.
  - Add cashew butter and maple syrup.
  - Blend until smooth.
  - Stir in crumbled bacon.
  - Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture
