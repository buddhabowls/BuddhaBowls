ingredients:
  - 120g raw shea butter
  - 15ml argan oil
  - 45ml coconut oil
  - Coconut shell + wooden spatula
  - Thermal cap or warm towel
instructions:
  - Melt shea butter in coconut shell over steam bath
  - Remove from heat - whip with coconut oil until creamy
  - Cool to 45°C before adding essential oils
  - Apply to sectioned hair using 3-step process:
  - Roots: Scalp massage with fingertips
  - Mid-lengths: Rake-through technique
  - Ends: Sealing with
  - Cover with silk bonnet - process 30-45 minutes
  - Rinse with lukewarm water followed by ACV rinse
  - Air dry or use microfiber towel wrap
