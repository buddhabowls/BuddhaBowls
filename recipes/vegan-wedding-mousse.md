ingredients:
  - '- 1 can of full-fat coconut milk
  - 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  - 1/2 cup raw cashews
  - 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  - 1/4 cup maple syrup
  - Fresh berries and mint leaves for garnish
  - Pinch of salt
instructions:
  - Begin by chilling the can of coconut milk in the refrigerator overnight. This will allow the cream to separate from the liquid.
  - Once the coconut milk has chilled, carefully open the can and scoop out the solidified cream into a mixing bowl, leaving the liquid behind. You can save the liquid for another use, such as adding it to smoothies.
  - In a high-speed blender, combine the soaked cashews, maple syrup, cocoa powder, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  - Add the coconut cream to the blender and blend again until everything is well combined and silky smooth.
  - Taste the mousse and adjust the sweetness if necessary by adding more maple syrup, one tablespoon at a time.
  - Transfer the mousse to individual serving glasses or a large serving dish. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours to allow it to set.
  - Before serving, garnish the mousse with fresh berries and mint leaves for a pop of color and freshness.
  - Enjoy this delectable vegan wedding mousse with your loved ones and savor every spoonful of its lusciousness!
