  - almond milk
  - chia seeds
  - mango puree
instructions:
  - Mix mango puree, chia seeds, and almond milk in a coconut bowl.
  - Let sit for 10-15 minutes until the chia seeds expand.
  - Drizzle with honey and enjoy!
