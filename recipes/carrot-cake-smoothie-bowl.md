ingredients:
  - '- 1 large carrot
  - 1 ripe banana
  - 1 tablespoon of honey or maple syrup
  - 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  - 1/2 cup of almond milk
  - 1/2 cup of Greek yogurt
  - 1/2 cup of rolled oats
  - 1/2 teaspoon of ground nutmeg
  - 1/4 teaspoon of ground ginger
  - 1/4 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  - A sprinkle of cinnamon
  - chopped nuts
  - raisins
  - shredded coconut
instructions:
  - In a blender, combine the chopped carrot, banana, rolled oats, Greek yogurt, almond milk, cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, and vanilla extract. If you prefer a sweeter smoothie bowl, you can add a tablespoon of honey or maple syrup at this stage.
  - Blend the ingredients until smooth and creamy. If the mixture is too thick, you can add more almond milk to reach your desired consistency.
  - Once the smoothie base is ready, pour it into a bowl.
  - Now comes the fun part – adding the toppings! Sprinkle shredded coconut, chopped nuts, raisins, and a little extra cinnamon on top of your smoothie bowl.
  - Grab a spoon and enjoy your Carrot Cake Smoothie Bowl!
