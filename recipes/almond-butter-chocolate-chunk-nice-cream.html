Almond Butter Chocolate Chunk Nice Cream Recipe

Rich and indulgent nice cream with chunks of dark chocolate and almond butter.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 2 tbsp almond butter
  • 1/4 cup dark chocolate chunks
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  • Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
  • Place frozen bananas in a blender.
  • Add almond butter and vanilla extract.
  • Blend until smooth.
  • Stir in dark chocolate chunks.
  • Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.

Almond butter offers healthy fats; dark chocolate is high in antioxidants.
