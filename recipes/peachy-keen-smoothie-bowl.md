  - 1 tablespoon of honey
  - 1/2 cup of Greek yogurt
  - 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  - 1/4 cup of almond milk
  - 2 ripe peaches
  - Toppings of your choice
instructions:
  - In a blender, combine the sliced peaches, frozen banana, Greek yogurt, almond milk, honey, and vanilla extract.
  - Blend until smooth and creamy, adding more almond milk if needed to reach your desired consistency.
  - Pour the smoothie into a bowl.
  - Top with your favorite toppings, such as granola, sliced almonds, chia seeds, or fresh fruit.
