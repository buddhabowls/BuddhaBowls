ingredients:
  - '- 1 avocado
  - 1 lb sushi-grade ahi tuna
  - 1 tbsp sesame oil
  - 1 tsp honey
  - 1 tsp rice vinegar
  - 1 tsp sesame seeds
  - 1/2 cup edamame
  - 1/4 cup soy sauce
  - 2 cups cooked sushi rice
  - 2 green onions
  - Nori strips
  - Pickled ginger
instructions:
  - In a bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, sesame oil, rice vinegar, and honey to create the marinade.
  - Add the cubed tuna and green onions to the marinade, gently toss to coat, and let it marinate in the refrigerator for at least 15 minutes.
  - To assemble the poke bowl, divide the cooked rice among serving bowls and top with the marinated tuna.
  - Arrange the avocado slices and edamame around the tuna.
  - Drizzle any remaining marinade over the poke bowls and sprinkle with sesame seeds.
  - Garnish with pickled ginger and nori strips before serving.
