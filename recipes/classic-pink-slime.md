ingredients:
  - A few drops of pink food coloring
  - baking soda
  - contact lens solution
  - water
  - white school glue
instructions:
  - In a mixing bowl, combine the glue and water. Stir until fully mixed.
  - Add a few drops of pink food coloring and mix until you achieve your desired shade.
  - Stir in the baking soda until dissolved.
  - Gradually add the contact lens solution while stirring. The slime will start to form.
  - Knead the slime with your hands until it reaches the perfect stretchy consistency.
