instructions:
  - In a saucepan, combine the coconut milk, pink pitaya powder, red curry paste, soy sauce, brown sugar, lime juice, garlic, and ginger. Whisk until well combined.
  - Place the saucepan over medium heat and bring the mixture to a simmer. Allow it to simmer for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  - Taste the sauce and adjust the seasoning as needed, adding salt to taste.
  - Once the sauce has thickened slightly and the flavors have melded together, remove it from the heat and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
