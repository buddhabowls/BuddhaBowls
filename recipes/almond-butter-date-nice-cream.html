Naturally sweetened with dates, this nice cream combines almond butter for a rich and wholesome dessert.

Almond Butter Date Nice Cream Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas, 2 tbsp almond butter, 1/4 cup chopped dates, 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  • 1. Slice bananas and freeze them overnight. 2. Place frozen bananas in a blender. 3. Add almond butter and vanilla extract. 4. Blend until smooth. 5. Stir in chopped dates. 6. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.

Satisfy your sweet cravings with the natural sweetness of dates blended into almond butter. This nice cream is a wholesome indulgence that promotes mindfulness.

Almonds: rich in healthy fats; Dates: high in fiber, natural sweetness.
