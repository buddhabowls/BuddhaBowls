instructions:
  - **Blend the Base**: In a high-speed blender, combine the pink pitaya, banana, spinach, and almond milk. Blend until smooth and creamy. If the mixture is too thick, add a little more almond milk to reach your desired consistency.
  - **Prepare Your Coconut Bowl**: While your smoothie is blending, get your coconut bowl ready. These bowls are not only eco-friendly but also add a rustic, earthy charm to your meal, enhancing the Buddha vibe. Made from reclaimed coconut shells, they are a sustainable choice for mindful living.
  - **Pour and Top**: Pour the smoothie into your coconut bowl. Now for the fun part—toppings! Consider adding fresh berries, banana slices, granola, shredded coconut, and a sprinkle of chia seeds for added texture and nutrition.
  - **Mindful Enjoyment**: Before diving in, take a moment to admire your creation. Appreciate the vibrant colors and inviting aroma. As you eat, savor each bite, focusing on the flavors and textures. This mindful approach not only enhances your enjoyment but also promotes a deeper connection with your food.
