ingredients:
  - '- Ice cubes
  - coconut water
  - fresh lemon juice
  - honey
  - large orange
  - Mint leaves for garnish
  - pink pitaya
instructions:
  - Add pink pitaya, orange segments, coconut water, lemon juice, and honey (if using) to the blender.
  - Add ice cubes.
  - Blend on high speed for 2-3 minutes until smooth and well combined.
  - Taste and adjust sweetness if needed.
  - Pour into glasses and garnish with mint leaves.
  - Serve immediately.
notes: >
  <p>For an extra cooling effect, freeze the pitaya cubes before blending. Add a pinch of cayenne pepper for a spicy kick that can boost metabolism. This smoothie pairs well with a splash of sparkling water for a fizzy twist.</p>
