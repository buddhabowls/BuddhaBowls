ingredients:
  - bone broth/vegetable stock blend
  - garlic confit bulbs + 1 caramelized onion
  - maple syrup + 5ml apple cider vinegar
  - Spice mix: 2:1:1 ratio of smoked paprika
  - sugar pumpkin
instructions:
  - Roast pumpkin with olive oil & star anise (discard post-roast)
  - Sauté onion in browned butter until amber-hued
  - Blend solids with stock until velvet texture
  - Simmer 20min with cream swirl pattern
  - Finish with pepita oil drizzle + smoked salt
