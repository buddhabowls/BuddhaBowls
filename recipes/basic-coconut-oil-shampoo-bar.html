A simple shampoo bar that cleanses and nourishes the hair.

This basic coconut oil shampoo bar is perfect for all hair types and leaves your hair feeling soft and clean.

Basic Coconut Oil Shampoo Bar

Ingredients:

  • Coconut oil,
  • Lye,
  • Distilled water,
  • Olive oil,
  • Essential oil (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Measure lye and water separately.
  2. Slowly add lye to water, stirring until dissolved.
  3. In a separate bowl, melt coconut oil and olive oil.
  4. Combine oils and lye solution, blend until trace.
  5. Pour into molds and let cure for 4-6 weeks.
