ingredients:
  - '- Ice cubes
  - coconut milk

  - honey
  - pink pitaya
  - shredded coconut
  - vanilla extract
instructions:
  - Add pink pitaya, coconut milk yogurt, coconut milk, honey, shredded coconut, and vanilla extract to the blender.
  - Add ice cubes.
  - Blend on high speed for 2-3 minutes until smooth and creamy.
  - Pour into glasses and sprinkle with additional shredded coconut if desired.
  - Serve immediately.
notes: >
