ingredients:
  - '- Ice cubes
  - fresh carrot juice
  - fresh mango chunks
  - grated carrots
  - lemon juice
  - pink pitaya
instructions:
  - Add pink pitaya, mango chunks, and grated carrots to the blender.
  - Pour in carrot juice and lemon juice.
  - Add ice cubes.
  - Blend on high speed for 2-3 minutes until smooth and well combined.
  - Pour into glasses, creating a layered effect if desired.
  - Serve immediately.
notes: >
  <p>To enhance the 'sunrise' effect, try layering the smoothie by blending the carrot portion separately and pouring it gently over the pitaya-mango mixture. Add a pinch of turmeric for an extra antioxidant boost and golden color. Garnish with a slice of mango or a sprinkle of shredded coconut.</p>
