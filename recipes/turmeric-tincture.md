ingredients:
  - Fresh turmeric root
  - Jar
  - Vodka
instructions:
  - Chop fresh turmeric root and fill a jar halfway.
  - Pour vodka over the turmeric until fully submerged.
  - Seal the jar and store in a cool, dark place for 4-6 weeks, shaking occasionally.
  - Strain and bottle the tincture.
