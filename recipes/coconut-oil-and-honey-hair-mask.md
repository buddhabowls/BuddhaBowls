ingredients:
  - 30ml raw honey
  - 45ml coconut oil
  - Coconut bowl + bamboo brush
  - Warm towel
  - Wide-tooth comb
instructions:
  - Melt coconut oil in coconut bowl over warm water
  - Whisk in honey until emulsified (1:1 ratio)
  - Section dry hair and apply from mid-lengths to ends
  - Wrap hair in shower cap + warm towel turban
  - Process 30 minutes (or overnight for deep treatment)
  - Rinse with cool water followed by mild shampoo
  - Finish with cold water rinse for shine
