ingredients:
  - 15ml raw honey
  - 45ml coconut oil
  - Coconut shell + bamboo whisk
  - large eggs
  - Thermal hair cap
instructions:
  - Whisk eggs in coconut bowl until frothy (2 mins)
  - Melt coconut oil separately (40°C maximum)
  - Combine ingredients using folding technique
  - Apply to damp hair in three phases:
  - Phase 1: Scalp massage with egg mixture
  - Phase 2: Mid-lengths with coconut oil focus
  - Phase 3: Ends sealed with honey layer
  - Process under thermal cap (20-25 minutes)
  - Rinse with cool water (never hot!)
  - Final cleanse with mild shampoo bar
