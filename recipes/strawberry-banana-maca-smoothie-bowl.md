ingredients:
  - '- 1 cup of frozen strawberries
  - 1 ripe banana
  - 1 tablespoon of honey or maple syrup
  - 1 tablespoon of maca powder
  - 1/2 cup of almond milk
  - Almonds or other nuts
  - Banana slices
  - Edible flowers for a decorative touch
  - Fresh strawberries
  - chia seeds
  - coconut flakes
  - granola
instructions:
  - - In a high-powered blender, combine the banana, frozen strawberries, maca powder, and almond milk. Blend until smooth and creamy. If you prefer a thicker consistency, use less milk; for a thinner consistency, add a bit more.
  - - Taste the blended mixture. If you prefer it sweeter, add honey or maple syrup and blend again.
  - - Pour the smoothie mixture into a coconut bowl. The natural beauty of the coconut bowl enhances the presentation and adds an eco-friendly touch to your meal.
  - - This is where you can get creative! Arrange the fresh strawberries, banana slices, granola, chia seeds, coconut flakes, and almonds on top of the smoothie. Feel free to add any other toppings you love.
  - - Grab a spoon and dig into your Strawberry Banana Maca Smoothie Bowl. Savor each bite, enjoying the combination of flavors and textures.
