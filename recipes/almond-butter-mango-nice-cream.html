Almond Butter Mango Nice Cream

Ingredients You’ll Need:

  • 3 ripe bananas: The base of this nice cream, bananas add natural sweetness and a creamy texture.
  • 2 tablespoons almond butter: For a nutty richness and a boost of plant-based protein.
  • 1 cup diced mango: A burst of tropical flavor that pairs beautifully with almond butter.

How to Make Almond Butter Mango Nice Cream:

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Start by peeling and slicing the ripe bananas into small chunks. Place them in an airtight container and freeze for at least 4-6 hours or overnight. Similarly, ensure your diced mango is frozen for the creamiest results.
  2. Blend to Perfection: In a high-speed blender or food processor, combine the frozen banana slices, frozen mango chunks, and almond butter. Blend until smooth and creamy, stopping occasionally to scrape down the sides. Be patient—this process may take a few minutes depending on your blender’s power.
  3. Serve with Style: Once the mixture reaches a soft-serve consistency, scoop it into coconut bowls for an added touch of mindful elegance. The natural aesthetic of coconut bowls enhances the tropical vibe of this dessert and makes it even more Instagram-worthy!
  4. Optional Toppings: While this nice cream is delightful on its own, feel free to garnish it with shredded coconut, fresh fruit slices, or a drizzle of extra almond butter for added decadence.
