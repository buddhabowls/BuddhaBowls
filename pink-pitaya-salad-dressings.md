[ Print Recipe](https://buddhabowls.org/wprm_print/pink-pitaya-salad-dressings)## Pink Pitaya Salad Dressing Recipes

Today, I’m super excited to share with you my recipe for a delicious pink pitaya vinaigrette dressing. Whether you’re a fan of fresh pink pitaya or prefer the convenience of pink pitaya powder, I’ve got you covered with two variations of this vibrant and flavorful dressing. Not only will I walk you through the recipes for both versions, but I’ll also share some creative ways to use the dressing and suggest some foods that pair perfectly with its unique flavor profile.

First off, let’s talk about pink pitaya. Also known as dragon fruit, this exotic fruit is not only visually stunning with its vibrant pink hue and speckled black seeds, but it also boasts a subtly sweet and refreshing flavor. When used in a vinaigrette dressing, it adds a delightful tropical twist that’s perfect for brightening up salads and other dishes.

Now, onto the recipes!

## Fresh Pink Pitaya Vinaigrette Dressing:

**Ingredients:
**– 1/2 cup fresh pink pitaya flesh
– 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
– 2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
– 1 tablespoon [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke)
– 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
– Salt and pepper to taste

**Instructions:
**1. In a [Blender](https://amzn.to/4oSJQHl) or food processor, combine the fresh pink pitaya flesh, olive oil, white wine vinegar, [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke), and Dijon mustard.
2. Blend until smooth and emulsified.
3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
4. Transfer to a jar or container and refrigerate until ready to use.

## Pink Pitaya Powder Vinaigrette Dressing:

**Ingredients:
**– 1 teaspoon pink pitaya powder
– 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
– 2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
– 1 tablespoon [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke)
– 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
– Salt and pepper to taste

**Instructions:
**1. In a small bowl, whisk together the pink pitaya powder, olive oil, white wine vinegar, [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke), and Dijon mustard until well combined.
2. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
3. Transfer to a jar or container and refrigerate until ready to use.

### Ways to Use the Dressing

Now that you have your vibrant pink pitaya vinaigrette dressing ready to go, here are some creative ways to enjoy it:

1. Drizzle it over a mixed green salad topped with fresh berries, sliced almonds, and crumbled feta cheese for a refreshing and colorful meal.

2. Use it as a marinade for grilled chicken or tofu to infuse a burst of tropical flavor into your main dish.

3. Toss it with cooked quinoa or couscous along with diced cucumbers, cherry tomatoes, and herbs for a light and flavorful side dish.

4. Serve it as a dipping sauce for spring rolls or fresh vegetable crudités for a fun and healthy appetizer.

### Foods That Pair Well with Pink Pitaya Vinaigrette Dressing

The beauty of this dressing is that it pairs well with a wide variety of foods. Here are some ideas to get you started:

– Fresh seafood such as shrimp or grilled fish
– Leafy greens like spinach, arugula, or butter lettuce
– Tropical fruits such as mango, papaya, or pineapple
– Grilled vegetables like zucchini, bell peppers, and asparagus
– Creamy cheeses like goat cheese or burrata

So there you have it! With this pink pitaya vinaigrette dressing in your culinary arsenal, you’ll be able to add a pop of color and flavor to all of your favorite dishes. Whether you opt for the fresh pink pitaya version or the convenient pink pitaya powder variation, you’re sure to fall in love with the tropical flair it brings to the table.

Give it a try and let your taste buds take a trip to paradise!

## Pink Pitaya Salad Dressing Recipe

**Prep Time:** 5 minutes

**Cook Time:** 0 minutes

**Total Time:** 5 minutes

**Servings:** 4 servings

## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp pink pitaya powder,

- 1/4 cup olive oil,

- 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar,

- 1 tbsp [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke),

- 1 tsp Dijon mustard,

- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

- In a jar, combine all ingredients.

- Shake vigorously until well combined.

- Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.

- Use immediately or store in the refrigerator.

## Notes

This dressing pairs well with fruit-based salads or mixed greens with goat cheese. For a creamier version, add a tablespoon of Greek yogurt.
