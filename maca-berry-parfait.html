🍓 Maca Power Parfait

🕗 10min Prep | 🥛 24g Protein | 🧠 Brain Fuel

This strategic layering of functional foods combines maca's adaptogenic properties with yogurt's probiotics and berries' antioxidants - designed for sustained morning energy.

🌈 Layer Architecture

  • Base: Maca-infused Greek yogurt
  • 2nd: Antioxidant berry mix
  • 3rd: Activated grain cluster
  • Top: Nutrient-dense toppings

24g

🔬 Food Science Breakdown

  • Maca's maltol content enhances yogurt's creaminess
  • Berry anthocyanins boost cognitive function
  • Chia seeds provide omega-3s for fat-soluble vitamin absorption
  • Activated nuts improve mineral bioavailability

👩🍳 Chef's Pro Tips

🕒 Meal Prep

Prep layers separately - assemble morning-of

🌡️ Temperature

Use frozen berries as natural chill packs
