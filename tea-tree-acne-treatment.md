## Tea Tree Acne Treatment

### Ingredients:

- Tea tree essential oil,

- Carrier oil (like jojoba or coconut),

- Small container

### Instructions:

- Mix 1 drop of tea tree essential oil with 1 teaspoon of carrier oil.

- Apply directly to blemishes using a cotton swab.
