Coconut Oil & Oatmeal Mask

🌾 Skin Types: Ideal for sensitive/rosacea-prone skin
⏱ Frequency: 2-3x weekly
❄️ Storage: 3 days refrigerated in coconut bowl

🥣 Materials:

  • 3 tbsp (45g) colloidal oatmeal
  • 2 tbsp (30ml) coconut oil
  • 1 tbsp (15ml) coconut milk
  • Coconut bowl + wooden spoon
  • Muslin cloth (optional)

🧴 Preparation Method:

  1. Grind oats into fine powder (if using whole oats)
  2. In coconut bowl, mix oat powder with coconut oil
  3. Add coconut milk gradually to reach paste consistency
  4. Test on jawline - wait 10 minutes
  5. Apply using circular massage motions
  6. Leave on 15 minutes or overnight as spot treatment
  7. Remove with lukewarm water and soft cloth

💡 Oat Wisdom:
• Use cooled chamomile tea instead of milk for extra calming
• Add 1 tsp baking soda for acne-prone skin
• Mix with yogurt for gentle exfoliation
• Freeze leftovers as coconut bowl ice cubes
