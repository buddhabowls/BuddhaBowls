Crystal Bath for Relaxation

Soak your worries away with a crystal-infused bath!

✨

🛀 Soak in Crystal Magic! 🛀 Treat yourself to a crystal-infused bath! Relax in warm water with Epsom salt and let the crystals work their magic! 💎✨ #CrystalHealing #Relaxation

Materials Needed

  • Epsom salt
  • Crystals (like amethyst or rose quartz)
  • A warm bath

Steps

  1. 1

    Fill the tub with warm water and add Epsom salt.

  2. 2

    Place crystals around the tub (remove before draining).

  3. 3

    Soak and relax while visualizing stress melting away.
