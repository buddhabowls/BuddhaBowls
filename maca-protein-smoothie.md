**Maca Protein Power Smoothie Recipe | Post-Workout Recovery**

🌱 Natural adaptogen boost | 🥛 25g protein | ⏱️ 5-minute prep

Designed for post-workout recovery, this smoothie combines muscle-repairing protein with maca’s energy-boosting properties. Gluten-free and vegan-friendly when using plant-based protein.

## 💪 Maca Protein Power Smoothie

## 📋 Ingredients

- 1 ripe banana (fresh or frozen)

- 1 cup unsweetened almond milk

- 1 scoop vanilla protein powder

- 1 tbsp organic maca powder

- 1 cup fresh spinach

- ½ tsp cinnamon (optional)

## 👨🍳 Preparation Steps

- Combine all base ingredients in high-speed [Blender](https://amzn.to/4oSJQHl)

- Blend until smooth and creamy

- Adjust consistency with ice cubes as needed

- Serve immediately in chilled glass

### ⚖️ Nutrition Profile

- Calories: 292 kcal

- Protein: 25g (50% DV)

- Carbs: 35g (12% DV)

- Fiber: 5g (18% DV)

- Iron: 15% DV (from spinach)

#### 🏋️♂️ Workout Recovery

Protein supports muscle repair while maca helps restore energy

#### 🍃 Nutrient Density

Spinach provides iron + vitamin K for blood health
