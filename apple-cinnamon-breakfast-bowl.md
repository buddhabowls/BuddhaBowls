Packed with warm spices, natural sweetness, and a hearty base of oats, this recipe is not only easy to prepare but also a comforting way to kick off your morning routine.

This breakfast bowl combines the timeless pairing of apples and cinnamon—a duo that brings cozy vibes to any dish. ## Apple Cinnamon Breakfast Bowl

### Ingredients You’ll Need

To whip up this delightful breakfast bowl, you’ll need just six simple ingredients:

- **1/2 cup rolled oats**: A great source of fiber and energy to keep you full throughout the morning.

- **1 cup almond milk**: Creamy and plant-based, it adds a subtle nutty flavor.

- **1/2 apple**: Fresh and crisp, it brings natural sweetness and crunch.

- **1 tsp cinnamon**: The star spice that ties all the flavors together.

- **1 tbsp [Maple Syrup](https://amzn.to/3KUS5oa)**: A touch of natural sweetness to enhance the dish.

### How to Make It

This recipe is as simple as it is delicious. Here’s how to bring it all together:

- **Cook the Oats**
In a small saucepan, combine the rolled oats and almond milk. Heat over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the oats are soft and creamy (about 5-7 minutes).

- **Prepare the Apple**
While the oats are cooking, dice half an apple into small pieces. You can leave the skin on for added texture and nutrients or peel it if you prefer a smoother bite.

- **Add the Flavors**
Once the oats are cooked, stir in the diced apple, cinnamon, and [Maple Syrup](https://amzn.to/3KUS5oa). Mix well to ensure every bite is packed with flavor.

- **Serve and Enjoy**
Transfer your Apple Cinnamon Breakfast Bowl to a serving dish. For an extra touch, top it with additional apple slices, a sprinkle of cinnamon, or even a handful of nuts or seeds for added crunch.

### Tips for Customizing Your Bowl

One of the best things about this recipe is how adaptable it is! Here are a few ideas to make it your own:

- Swap almond milk for any milk of your choice—oat milk, coconut milk, or regular dairy milk all work beautifully.

- Add a handful of raisins or dried cranberries for extra sweetness.

- Sprinkle some [chia seeds](https://amzn.to/4q7GcdC) or flaxseeds on top for a boost of omega-3s.

- Feeling indulgent? Drizzle a bit of [Almond Butter](https://amzn.to/4oN8Mj6) or peanut butter over your bowl for a creamy finish.
