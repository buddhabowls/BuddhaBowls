## Joyful Cherry Bliss Recipe

Course: Dessert
Cuisine: Vegan
Servings: 2

## Ingredients

- 2 cups frozen cherries,

- 1 banana,

- 1/2 cup coconut milk,

- Toppings: chopped nuts, cherries

## Instructions

- Blend cherries, banana, and coconut milk until creamy.

- Serve in [Coconut Bowls](https://buddhabowls.org/product/coconut-bowls/) and top with nuts and cherries.

Add a splash of vanilla for extra flavor.
