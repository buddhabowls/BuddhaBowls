Coconut Oil & Coffee Scrub Moisturizer

🌅 Dawn Ritual Tip:
Prepare as sunrise colors change - the evolving light activates different properties in the coffee antioxidants while coconut oil absorbs vitamin D potential.

☕ Sacred Ingredients:

  • ¼ cup cold-pressed coconut oil
  • ½ cup used coffee grounds (cooled)
  • 1 tbsp raw sugar
  • 5 drops orange essential oil
  • Coconut bowl + bamboo spatula
  • Reusable linen storage pouch

🌄 Awakening Ritual:

  1. Collect morning coffee grounds - still warm with energy
  2. Mix with coconut oil in bowl using sunwise motions
  3. Add sugar while setting daily intentions
  4. Blend in essential oils during first birdsong
  5. Stir until texture resembles "earth meeting sea"
  6. Store in pouch hung near morning light
  7. Bless with three deep coffee-scented breaths
💆♂️ Multi-Sensory Application:
• Body: Circular massage starting at feet moving upward
• Face: Gentle cheekbone circles (avoid eye area)
• Hands: Focus on knuckles and cuticles
• Shower: Pre-cleanse treatment
• Weekend: Elbow/knee brightening mask

🌱 Circular Beauty Benefits:

  • Caffeine reduces morning puffiness by 40%*
  • Coconut oil seals in shower hydration
  • Orange oil boosts alertness naturally
  • Sugar gently buffs while locking moisture
  • Upcycled grounds prevent kitchen waste
