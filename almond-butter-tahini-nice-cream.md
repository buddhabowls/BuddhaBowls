## 🧘 Mindful Moment
> Explore the unique combination of almond butter and tahini in this creamy nice cream. It’s a delightful way to practice mindfulness with its rich flavors.

## 💚 Health Benefits
- Almonds: supports brain health
- Tahini: high in calcium, good for bone health.

## 🥗 Ingredients
- 3 ripe bananas
- 2 tbsp almond butter
- 1 tbsp tahini

## 👩‍🍳 Instructions
1. Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
2. Place frozen bananas in a blender.
3. Add almond butter and tahini.
4. Blend until smooth.
5. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.
