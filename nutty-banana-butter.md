## Nutty Banana Butter

**Course:** Spread

**Cuisine:** American

**Prep Time:**

**Servings:** 1 cup

### Ingredients:

- 2 cups mixed nuts (almonds, cashews)

- 1 ripe banana

- 1/2 tsp salt

### Instructions:

- Roast nuts at 350°F (175°C) for 10-12 minutes.

- Let cool, then blend in a food processor with banana until smooth.

- Add salt; blend until combined.

NOTES: Great on toast or pancakes.
