Avocado Matcha Smoothie Bowl

Course

Breakfast

Cuisine

Fusion

Servings

2 servings

Nutrition

  • Calories: 300
  • Protein: 6g
  • Fat: 20g
  • Carbohydrates: 30g
  • Fiber: 8g

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon matcha powder
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup (optional)
  • Fresh fruits and nuts for topping

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine avocado, banana, spinach, almond milk, matcha powder, and sweetener if using.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour the smoothie into a coconut bowl.
  4. Top with fresh fruits, nuts, and any other desired toppings.
  5. Enjoy your delicious Avocado Matcha Smoothie Bowl!
