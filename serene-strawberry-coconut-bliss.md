## Serene Strawberry Coconut Bliss Recipe

Course: Dessert
Cuisine: Vegan
Servings: 2

## Ingredients

- 2 cups frozen strawberries,

- 1/2 cup coconut cream,

- 1 tbsp [Maple Syrup](https://amzn.to/3KUS5oa),

- Toppings:

- shredded coconut,

- sliced strawberries

## Instructions

- Blend strawberries, coconut cream, and [Maple Syrup](https://amzn.to/3KUS5oa) until creamy. Serve in [Coconut Bowls](https://buddhabowls.org/product/coconut-bowls/) and top with coconut and strawberries.

Use full-fat coconut cream for a richer texture.
