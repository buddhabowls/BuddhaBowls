## 💚 Health Benefits
- Espresso boosts energy and metabolism
- almond butter adds creaminess and nutrients.

## 🥗 Ingredients
- 3 ripe bananas
- 2 tbsp almond butter
- 1 tsp instant espresso powder

## 👩‍🍳 Instructions
1. Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
2. Place frozen bananas in a blender.
3. Add almond butter and espresso powder.
4. Blend until smooth.
5. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.

## 💡 Tips
- Use coconut milk for a tropical twist.
