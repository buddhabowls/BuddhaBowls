Capture the moon’s magic in a bottle with moon water!

🌕 Capture Moon Magic! 🌕 Make moon water to enhance your intentions! Place a jar of water under the moonlight overnight, and use it in your rituals for extra power! 🌊✨ #MoonWater #IntentionSetting

Ingredients:

– Clean jar

– Water

– A clear night sky

Instructions:

1. Fill the jar with water.

2. Place it outside under the moonlight overnight.

3. Use the water in rituals or as a drink for intention setting.
