## 🧘 Mindful Moment
> Indulge in the creamy goodness of classic almond butter banana nice cream. This simple recipe encourages you to savor each bite, reminding you to appreciate the natural sweetness of bananas and the richness of almond butter.

## 💚 Health Benefits
- Almond butter is rich in healthy fats and protein
- bananas provide potassium and fiber.

## 🥗 Ingredients
- 3 ripe bananas
- 2 tbsp almond butter
- 1 tsp vanilla extract

## 👩‍🍳 Instructions
1. Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
2. Place frozen bananas in a blender.
3. Add almond butter and vanilla extract.
4. Blend until smooth.
5. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.

## 💡 Tips
- Incorporate a bit of almond flour for texture.
