Smoothie bowls: the perfect way to eat your smoothie with a spoon and feel fancy doing it. 

Add CBD oil to the mix, and you’ve got yourself a zen-inducing, nutrient-packed breakfast (or dessert, or snack—no judgment here). Whether you’re trying to start your day on a relaxed note or just looking for an excuse to eat something that’s both Instagrammable and delicious, these CBD-infused smoothie bowl recipes have got you covered. Bonus points if you serve them in trendy [Coconut Bowls](https://buddhabowls.org/product/coconut-bowls/) or channel your inner yogi with a Buddha-esque presentation.

## 5 Delicious CBD-Infused Smoothie Bowl Recipes to Chill Your Taste Buds

Let’s dive in, spoon first:

### 1. **Tropical Chill-Out Bowl**

*Because who doesn’t want a mini vacation in a bowl?*

**Ingredients:**

- 1 frozen banana

- 1/2 cup frozen mango chunks

- 1/2 cup frozen pineapple chunks

- 1/2 cup coconut milk (or your favorite plant-based milk)

- 1 tsp CBD oil (unflavored or citrus-flavored works great here)

**Toppings:**

- Fresh kiwi slices

- Shredded coconut

- Chia seeds

- A drizzle of [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke) (optional but highly recommended for that “I’m in the tropics” vibe)

**Instructions:**
Blend all the ingredients until smooth and creamy. Pour into a bowl, top with your tropical garnishes, and pretend you’re lounging on a beach somewhere. Bonus points if you eat it while wearing sunglasses indoors.

### 2. **Berry Zen Buddha Bowl**

*Berries + CBD = Pure Bliss.*

**Ingredients:**

- 1/2 cup frozen strawberries

- 1/2 cup frozen blueberries

- 1/4 cup frozen raspberries

- 1/2 cup almond milk

- 1 tsp CBD oil (try a berry-flavored one for extra oomph)

**Toppings:**

- Fresh blackberries

- Granola (the crunchier, the better)

- Hemp seeds (because why not double down on the chill factor?)

- A dollop of [Almond Butter](https://amzn.to/4oN8Mj6)

**Instructions:**
Blend the berries, almond milk, and CBD oil into a thick, luscious smoothie. Pour into a bowl and go wild with the toppings. This one’s perfect for when you want to channel your inner Buddha and achieve smoothie enlightenment.

### 3. **Chocolate Bliss Bowl**

*Because chocolate is the answer to everything.*

**Ingredients:**

- 1 frozen banana

- 1 tbsp cacao powder (or cocoa powder, but cacao sounds fancier)

- 1/2 cup oat milk (or any milk of your choice)

- 1 tsp CBD oil (chocolate-flavored if you’re feeling extra fancy)

**Toppings:**

- Cacao nibs (for that crunch factor)

- Sliced strawberries

- Crushed walnuts

- A drizzle of peanut butter (because peanut butter + chocolate = happiness)

**Instructions:**
Blend all the ingredients until smooth and creamy—think soft serve but healthier. Pour into a bowl, top with your chocolatey dreams, and savor every bite. Warning: this one might make you want to lick the bowl clean.

### 4. **Green Goddess Chill Bowl**

*For when you want to feel virtuous but also relaxed.*

**Ingredients:**

- 1/2 avocado (because healthy fats are life)

- 1 frozen banana

- A handful of spinach or kale (don’t worry—you won’t taste it)

- 1/2 cup coconut water or almond milk

- 1 tsp CBD oil (unflavored works best here)

**Toppings:**

- Sliced kiwi or green apple

- Pumpkin seeds

- Coconut flakes

- A drizzle of agave syrup

**Instructions:**
Blend all the green goodness until smooth. Pour into a bowl and decorate like the health guru you aspire to be. This one screams “I do yoga at sunrise,” even if you’re eating it in pajamas at noon.

### 5. **Peanut Butter & Jelly Chill Bowl**

*A nostalgic classic with a CBD twist.*

**Ingredients:**

- 1 frozen banana

- 1/2 cup frozen strawberries or raspberries (your “jelly” component)

- 1/2 cup almond milk or oat milk

- 1 tsp CBD oil (unflavored or berry-flavored)

**Toppings:**

- A generous drizzle of peanut butter (or [Almond Butter](https://amzn.to/4oN8Mj6) if you’re feeling fancy)

- Fresh berries (because aesthetics matter)

- Crushed peanuts or [Granola](https://amzn.to/4iPqYY5) for crunch

**Instructions:**
Blend everything until smooth and creamy. Pour into a bowl, top with your PB&J-inspired garnishes, and prepare for a wave of childhood nostalgia—except this time, it comes with grown-up chill vibes.

### Pro Tips for Smoothie Bowl Success:

- **Thickness is key.** You want your smoothie bowl thick enough to hold up those Instagram-worthy toppings without sinking faster than your weekend plans. Use less liquid and let your [Blender](https://amzn.to/4oSJQHl) do the heavy lifting.

- **CBD Dosage Matters.** Start small—usually around 1 tsp or the recommended dose on your bottle—and adjust as needed based on how chill you want to feel. Too much can leave you overly relaxed (or napping before noon).

- **Coconut Bowls FTW.** Serving your smoothie bowls in [Coconut Bowls](https://buddhabowls.org/product/coconut-bowls/) isn’t just trendy—it’s practically mandatory if you want bonus aesthetic points. Plus, they’re eco-friendly!

So there you have it: five delicious, CBD-infused smoothie bowls that are as good for your soul as they are for your taste buds. Whether you’re going tropical, chocolatey, or full-on zen mode, these recipes will have you spooning up good vibes in no time. 

Now go forth and blend!
