## Affirmation Wall Art

### Ingredients:

– Canvas or poster board

– Paint or markers

### Instructions:

1. Write or paint your affirmations on the canvas.

2. Hang it in a prominent place to inspire you daily.

3. Let it serve as a reminder of your goals.
