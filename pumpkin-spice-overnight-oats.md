Hey there, pumpkin spice fans! If you’re craving something warm, cozy, and full of fall flavor, these Pumpkin Spice Overnight Oats are just what you need.

## Pumpkin Spice Overnight Oats

This recipe is my ode to the classic pumpkin spice latte. It’s like having your favorite fall drink in a bowl, but without the caffeine crash.

To whip up these oats, I mix rolled oats with almond milk, pumpkin puree, and a generous sprinkle of pumpkin spice. After a quick stir, I let it chill in the fridge overnight, soaking up all those warm, spicy flavors.

Come morning, I serve it up in a coconut bowl (because it adds that extra touch of charm) and top it with pumpkin seeds and a sprinkle of cinnamon.

Every spoonful is like a taste of autumn, and I can’t help but feel all warm and fuzzy inside as I eat it. So if you’re ready to start your day with a little slice of pumpkin heaven, give these Pumpkin Spice Overnight Oats a try. You won’t be disappointed!

## Pumpkin Spice Overnight Oats

**Course:** Breakfast

**Prep Time:** 5 mins

**Cook Time:** 0 mins

**Total Time:** 5 mins

**Servings:** 1

## Ingredients

- Rolled oats, pumpkin puree, almond milk, pumpkin spice

## Instructions

- Mix oats, pumpkin puree, almond milk, and pumpkin spice. Refrigerate overnight. Top with pumpkin seeds and a sprinkle of cinnamon.
