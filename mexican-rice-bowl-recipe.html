Mexican Rice Bowl

A hearty and flavorful Mexican Rice Bowl filled with nutritious ingredients, perfect for a satisfying lunch or dinner.

Mexican Rice Bowl Recipe

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Total Time: 40 minutes

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 cup brown rice
  • 1/2 cup black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1/4 cup corn (fresh or frozen)
  • 1/4 cup bell peppers, diced
  • 1/4 cup salsa
  • Lime wedge for garnish
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Rinse the brown rice under cold water and cook according to package instructions (usually about 30 minutes).
  2. While the rice is cooking, mix the black beans, corn, and diced bell peppers in a bowl.
  3. Once the rice is cooked, fluff it with a fork and let it cool slightly.
  4. In a bowl, layer the cooked brown rice with the bean mixture and top with salsa.
  5. Garnish with a lime wedge and fresh cilantro before serving.

Nutrition

Calories: Approximately 450

Protein: 15g

Carbs: 75g

Fat: 8g

Fiber: 12g
