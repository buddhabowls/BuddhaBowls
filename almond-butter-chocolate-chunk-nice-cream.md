## 🧘 Mindful Moment

## 💚 Health Benefits
- Almond butter offers healthy fats
- dark chocolate is high in antioxidants.

## 🥗 Ingredients
- 3 ripe bananas
- 2 tbsp almond butter
- 1/4 cup dark chocolate chunks
- 1 tsp vanilla extract

## 👩‍🍳 Instructions
1. Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
2. Place frozen bananas in a blender.
3. Add almond butter and vanilla extract.
4. Blend until smooth.
5. Stir in dark chocolate chunks.
6. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.
