## Nut Butter Snack Bars

**Course:** Snack

**Cuisine:** American

**Prep Time:**

**Servings:** 12 bars

### Ingredients:

- 1 cup nut butter

- 2 cups oats

- 1/4 cup [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke)

### Instructions:

- In a bowl, mix all ingredients until combined.

- Press into a baking pan and refrigerate for 2 hours.

- Cut into bars.

NOTES: Great for on-the-go snacks!
