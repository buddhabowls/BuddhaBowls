## Veggie Stir-Fry Surprise Recipe

**Prep Time:** 10 minutes

**Cook Time:** 15 minutes

**Total Time:** 25 minutes

## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp coconut oil,

- 1 lb mixed vegetables (broccoli, bell peppers, snap peas),

- 2 tbsp [Soy Sauce](https://amzn.to/48Vf7mR),

- 1 tsp ginger, minced

## Instructions

- Heat coconut oil in a wok, add vegetables and stir-fry for 10 minutes.

- Add [Soy Sauce](https://amzn.to/48Vf7mR) and ginger, cook for another 5 minutes.
