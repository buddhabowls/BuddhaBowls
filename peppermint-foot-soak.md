## Peppermint Foot Soak

### Ingredients:

- Peppermint essential oil,

- Epsom salt,

- Warm water,

- Bowl

### Instructions:

- In a bowl, mix 1 cup of Epsom salt with 10 drops of peppermint essential oil.

- Fill the bowl with warm water.

- Soak your feet for 15-20 minutes.
