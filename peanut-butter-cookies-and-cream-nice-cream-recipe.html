Peanut Butter Cookies and Cream Nice Cream

A deliciously creamy Peanut Butter Cookies and Cream Nice Cream made with frozen bananas and crushed Oreos, perfect for a sweet treat!

Peanut Butter Cookies and Cream Nice Cream Recipe

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Freeze Time: 6 hours (overnight recommended)

Total Time: 6 hours 10 minutes

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup crushed Oreos

Instructions

  1. Slice the bananas and freeze them overnight.
  2. Place the frozen bananas in a blender.
  3. Add the peanut butter.
  4. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  5. Stir in the crushed Oreos.
  6. Serve immediately for a soft texture or freeze for a firmer texture.
