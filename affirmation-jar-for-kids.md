Teach kids positivity with an affirmation jar!

🌟 Empower Kids with Affirmations! 🌟 Help kids create an affirmation jar! Let them write positive notes to boost their confidence and self-esteem! 💖✨ #KidsAffirmations #PositiveThinking

Ingredients: – A jar – Colored paper – A pen

Instructions: 1. Have kids write or draw positive affirmations on paper. 2. Fill the jar with their creations. 3. Encourage them to pick one out daily.
