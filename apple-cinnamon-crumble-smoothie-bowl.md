## Apple Cinnamon Crumble Smoothie Bowl: A Cozy Delight

As the seasons change and the air turns crisp, there’s nothing quite like the comforting flavors of apple and cinnamon to warm your soul. Introducing the Apple Cinnamon Crumble Smoothie Bowl—a delightful blend of autumnal flavors that brings the essence of a cozy apple crumble to your breakfast table. Served in a charming coconut bowl, this smoothie bowl not only nourishes your body but also aligns with a mindful, Buddha-inspired approach to eating, emphasizing balance and appreciation for the simple pleasures in life.

The Buddha vibe is about finding peace and joy in the present moment, savoring each bite, and choosing ingredients that nourish both body and spirit. Our Apple Cinnamon Crumble Smoothie Bowl embodies this philosophy, offering a delicious fusion of wholesome ingredients that are as pleasing to the eye as they are to the palate. It’s a perfect way to start your day with mindfulness and gratitude.

**Ingredients for Your Apple Cinnamon Crumble:**

– **1 large apple, peeled and chopped**: Choose a sweet variety like Honeycrisp or Fuji for the best flavor.
– **1 frozen banana**: Adds natural sweetness and a creamy texture.
– **1/2 cup almond milk (or your preferred plant-based milk)**: For a smooth, creamy consistency.
– **1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon**: Infuses the smoothie with a warming spice.
– **1 tablespoon [Almond Butter](https://amzn.to/4oN8Mj6)**: Adds richness and a dose of healthy fats.
– **1/4 cup rolled oats**: For a hearty texture and added fiber.
– **Toppings**: Sliced apple, [Granola](https://amzn.to/4iPqYY5), chopped nuts (such as walnuts or pecans), and a sprinkle of cinnamon.

Follow these steps to prepare your Apple Cinnamon Crumble Smoothie Bowl:

1. **Blend the Base**: In a high-speed [Blender](https://amzn.to/4oSJQHl), combine the chopped apple, frozen banana, almond milk, ground cinnamon, [Almond Butter](https://amzn.to/4oN8Mj6), and rolled oats. Blend until smooth and creamy. If the mixture is too thick, add a splash more almond milk to reach your desired consistency.

2. **Prepare Your Coconut Bowl**: While your smoothie is blending, get your coconut bowl ready. These eco-friendly bowls not only reduce plastic waste but also add a natural, earthy element to your meal, enhancing the Buddha vibe.

3. **Pour and Top**: Pour the smoothie into your coconut bowl. Now for the fun part—toppings! Arrange slices of fresh apple on top. Sprinkle with [Granola](https://amzn.to/4iPqYY5) and chopped nuts for a satisfying crunch. Finish with a light dusting of cinnamon to enhance the cozy flavors.

4. **Mindful Enjoyment**: Before diving in, take a moment to admire your creation. Appreciate the warm colors and inviting aroma. As you eat, savor each bite, focusing on the flavors and textures. This mindful approach not only enhances your enjoyment but also promotes a deeper connection with your food.

**The Beauty of Coconut Bowls**

Coconut bowls are more than just a pretty serving dish. They represent a commitment to sustainability and mindfulness. Made from reclaimed coconut shells, these bowls are biodegradable and eco-friendly, aligning with a lifestyle that values harmony with nature. Plus, they add an authentic tropical touch to your meals, making every dish feel like a special occasion.

The Apple Cinnamon Crumble Smoothie Bowl is a delightful way to start your day, combining the comforting flavors of a classic dessert with the nourishing qualities of a balanced breakfast. Served in a coconut bowl, this smoothie bowl not only satisfies your taste buds but also supports a mindful, eco-conscious lifestyle. So embrace the Buddha vibe, blend up this cozy treat, and enjoy a breakfast that fuels both body and spirit.

## Apple Cinnamon Crumble Recipe

**Course: **Breakfast/Dessert

**Prep Time: **10 minutes

**Servings: **1

### Ingredients

1 apple (peeled and chopped),

1 frozen banana,

1/2 cup almond milk,

1/2 teaspoon cinnamon,

1 tablespoon [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke)

### Instructions

- Blend the apple, frozen banana, almond milk, cinnamon, and [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke) until smooth.

- Pour into a bowl and top with apple slices and [Granola](https://amzn.to/4iPqYY5).
