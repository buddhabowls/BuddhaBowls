## Nut Butter Chocolate Smoothie

**Course:** Breakfast

**Cuisine:** American

**Servings:** 1

### Ingredients:

- 1 banana

- 1 cup almond milk

- 2 tbsp nut butter

- 1 tbsp cocoa powder

### Instructions:

- Blend all ingredients until smooth.

- Serve immediately.

NOTES: Great for breakfast!
