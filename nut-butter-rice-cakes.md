## Nut Butter Rice Cakes

**Course:** Snack

**Cuisine:** American

**Prep Time:**

**Servings:** 4 servings

### Ingredients:

- 4 rice cakes

- 1/2 cup nut butter

- 1/4 cup sliced bananas

### Instructions:

- Spread nut butter on rice cakes.

- Top with banana slices.

NOTES: Great for a quick snack!
