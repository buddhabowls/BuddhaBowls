Coconut Oil & Oatmeal Hair Mask

🌾 Ideal For: Sensitive scalps, dandruff-prone hair, post-chemical treatment
⏱ Processing: 20-30 minutes under thermal cap
🧴 Storage: 1 week refrigerated in coconut bowl

🥣 Formula Components:

  • ¼ cup (30g) colloidal oatmeal
  • 3 tbsp (45ml) coconut oil
  • 2 tbsp (30ml) aloe vera gel
  • Coconut bowl + bamboo whisk
  • Scalp applicator brush

💆♂️ Application Ritual:

  1. Grind oats into fine powder using coconut bowl
  2. Melt coconut oil in separate container (40°C max)
  3. Whisk oat powder with aloe vera to form base gel
  4. Slowly emulsify coconut oil into mixture
  5. Apply to dry scalp using tint brush:
    • Section hair into 1-inch partings
    • Apply directly to scalp surface
    • Gently massage with silicone scalp massager
  6. Cover with shower cap + warm towel (20-30 mins)
  7. Rinse with cool water followed by sulfate-free shampoo

🌿 Customization Options:
• Itchy scalp: Add 5 drops tea tree oil
• Dry hair: Mix with 1 tbsp honey
• Color-treated: Use chamomile tea instead of aloe
• Fine hair: Reduce coconut oil to 2 tbsp
• Overnight treatment: Apply before bed with silk cap
