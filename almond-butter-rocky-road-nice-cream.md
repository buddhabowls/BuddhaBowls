## 🧘 Mindful Moment
> Enjoy the classic flavors of rocky road in a healthier format. This nice cream is a fun treat that invites you to savor the experience.

## 💚 Health Benefits
- Almonds: heart-healthy
- Marshmallows: quick energy
- Dark chocolate: rich in antioxidants.

## 🥗 Ingredients
- 3 ripe bananas
- 2 tbsp almond butter
- 1/4 cup mini marshmallows
- 1/4 cup chopped almonds
- 1/4 cup mini chocolate chips

## 👩‍🍳 Instructions
1. Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
2. Place frozen bananas in a blender.
3. Add almond butter.
4. Blend until smooth.
5. Stir in mini marshmallows, chopped almonds, and mini chocolate chips.
6. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.

## 💡 Tips
- Use flavored almond milk for additional taste.
