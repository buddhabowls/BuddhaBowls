What is a Brand Ambassador?

Brand Ambassadors are passionate individuals who share our mission of promoting healthy, sustainable living. They're the heart of our community—authentic voices who inspire others to embrace wellness and make eco-conscious choices.

As a Buddha Bowls Brand Ambassador, you'll receive exclusive perks, early access to new products, special discounts, and the opportunity to build a community around shared values. Whether you're a fitness enthusiast, wellness coach, content creator, or simply someone who loves our products, we want you on our team!

Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I earn as an ambassador?

Earnings depend on your sales volume and tier level. Seedling ambassadors earn 10% commission, while Elite ambassadors earn 25%. Many of our top ambassadors earn $500-$3,000+ per month. Your earnings are unlimited!

Do I need a large following to be an ambassador?

No! While a social media presence helps, we welcome ambassadors of all sizes. What matters most is your genuine passion for our products and your ability to authentically connect with your audience.

How long does the application process take?

We review applications within 5-7 business days. You'll receive an email with our decision and next steps. If approved, you'll receive your ambassador kit within 3-5 business days.

Can I promote on multiple platforms?

Absolutely! Promote on Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, your blog, email list, or anywhere you have an audience. Use your unique ambassador code to track sales from all platforms.

When do I get paid?

Commissions are calculated monthly and paid out via direct deposit on the 15th of each month. Minimum payout is $50. If you haven't reached the minimum, your balance carries over to the next month.

What if I want to stop being an ambassador?

You can pause or leave the program anytime with no penalties. We'd love to know why, but there's no obligation to continue. You'll receive your final commission payment within 30 days.

Do I have to buy products to be an ambassador?

No required purchases! However, we recommend trying our products so you can authentically recommend them. Your monthly product credit can help you do this.

Can I use my ambassador code for personal purchases?

Yes! Your personal discount applies to all your purchases. Plus, you'll earn commission on sales made through your code, even if you're the one making the purchase.
