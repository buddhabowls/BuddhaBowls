## 🧘 Mindful Moment
> Savor the warm, comforting flavors of maple and walnut in this nice cream. Let each spoonful remind you of cozy mornings and the importance of gratitude for life's simple pleasures.

## 💚 Health Benefits
- Walnuts are high in omega-3 fatty acids
- maple syrup provides antioxidants.

## 🥗 Ingredients
- 3 ripe bananas
- 2 tbsp almond butter
- 1/4 cup chopped walnuts
- 1 tbsp maple syrup

## 👩‍🍳 Instructions
1. Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
2. Place frozen bananas in a blender.
3. Add almond butter and maple syrup.
4. Blend until smooth.
5. Stir in chopped walnuts.
6. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.
