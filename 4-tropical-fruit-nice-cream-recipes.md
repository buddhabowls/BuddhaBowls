## 4 Tropical Fruit Nice Cream Recipes

## Mango Nice Cream

**Ingredients:**

- 2 ripe mangoes, peeled and chopped

- 1/4 cup coconut milk

- 1 tablespoon [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke) or agave nectar

- 1 teaspoon [Vanilla Extract](https://amzn.to/4aIhdue)
**Instructions:**

- Place the chopped mangoes in a ziplock bag and freeze for at least 4 hours.

- In a [Blender](https://amzn.to/4oSJQHl), combine the frozen mangoes, coconut milk, [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke) or agave nectar, and [Vanilla Extract](https://amzn.to/4aIhdue).

- Blend until smooth and creamy.

- Serve immediately as soft serve or transfer to a container and freeze for another 2 hours for a firmer texture.
Notes:

- You can add a squeeze of lime juice for a tangy flavor.

- Feel free to top with shredded coconut or chopped nuts for extra texture.

## Pineapple Coconut Nice Cream

**Ingredients:**

- 2 cups frozen pineapple chunks

- 1/2 cup coconut cream

- 2 tablespoons [Maple Syrup](https://amzn.to/3KUS5oa)

- 1/4 teaspoon coconut extract
**Instructions:**

- In a food processor, combine the frozen pineapple chunks, coconut cream, [Maple Syrup](https://amzn.to/3KUS5oa), and coconut extract.

- Pulse until smooth and creamy, scraping down the sides as needed.

- Transfer to a container and freeze for 1-2 hours for a firmer texture.
Tips:

- For a tropical twist, add a handful of shredded coconut to the mixture before freezing.

## Banana Papaya Nice Cream

**Ingredients:**

- 3 ripe bananas, sliced and frozen

- 1 small ripe papaya, peeled, seeded, and diced

- 1/4 cup almond milk

- 1 tablespoon [chia seeds](https://amzn.to/4q7GcdC) (optional)
**Instructions:**

- In a [Blender](https://amzn.to/4oSJQHl), combine the frozen bananas, papaya, almond milk, and [chia seeds](https://amzn.to/4q7GcdC).

- Blend until smooth and creamy, adding more almond milk if needed.

- Serve immediately for a soft serve consistency or freeze for 2-3 hours for a scoopable nice cream.
**Notes:**

- The [chia seeds](https://amzn.to/4q7GcdC) add a nutritional boost and help thicken the nice cream.

- You can garnish with fresh papaya chunks or a sprinkle of cinnamon before serving.

## Passion Fruit Banana Nice Cream

**Ingredients:**

- 4 ripe bananas, peeled, sliced, and frozen

- Pulp of 4 passion fruits

- 1/4 cup Greek yogurt

- 2 tablespoons [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke)
**Instructions:**

- In a food processor, blend the frozen bananas, passion fruit pulp, Greek yogurt, and [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke) until creamy.

- Transfer to a loaf pan and freeze for at least 3 hours before serving.
**Tips:**

- Swirl in additional passion fruit pulp before freezing for a marbled effect.

- Top with fresh passion fruit seeds and a dollop of whipped cream for an indulgent treat.

Enjoy these refreshing and guilt-free tropical fruit nice cream recipes!
