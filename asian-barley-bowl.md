## Asian Barley Bowl Recipe

**Prep Time:** 10 minutes

**Cook Time:** 30 minutes

**Total Time:** 40 minutes

**Servings:** 2

## Ingredients

- 1 cup barley

- 1/4 cup edamame, shelled

- 1/4 cup shredded carrots

- 1/4 cup bell pepper, diced

- 1/4 avocado, sliced

- 2 tbsp sesame dressing

- Sesame seeds for garnish

## Instructions

- Rinse the barley under cold water and cook according to package instructions (usually about 30 minutes).

- While the barley is cooking, lightly steam the edamame and bell pepper until tender.

- Once the barley is cooked, fluff it with a fork and let it cool slightly.

- In a bowl, layer the cooked barley with the steamed edamame, shredded carrots, diced bell pepper, and sliced avocado.

- Drizzle with sesame dressing and sprinkle with sesame seeds before serving.
