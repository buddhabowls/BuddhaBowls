## Tropical Fruit Powder Energy Balls

**Course:** Snack

**Servings:** 12 balls

### Ingredients:

- 1 cup oats 

- 1/2 cup nut butter

- 1/4 cup [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke)

- 2 tbsp tropical fruit powder

### Instructions:

- Mix all ingredients in a bowl until combined.

- Roll into balls and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
