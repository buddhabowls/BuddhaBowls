Coconut Oil & Honey Hair Mask

💇♀️ Customization Guide:
• Fine hair: 1 tbsp coconut oil + 1 tsp honey
• Curly hair: Add 1 tbsp shea butter
• Color-treated: Use 1 tsp apple cider vinegar
• Dry scalp: Mix with 2 tbsp yogurt

🍯 Ingredients:

  • 3 tbsp (45ml) coconut oil
  • 2 tbsp (30ml) raw honey
  • Coconut bowl + bamboo brush
  • Warm towel (for heat activation)
  • Wide-tooth comb

🧖♀️ Application Ritual:

  1. Melt coconut oil in coconut bowl over warm water
  2. Whisk in honey until emulsified (1:1 ratio)
  3. Section dry hair and apply from mid-lengths to ends
  4. Wrap hair in shower cap + warm towel turban
  5. Process 30 minutes (or overnight for deep treatment)
  6. Rinse with cool water followed by mild shampoo
  7. Finish with cold water rinse for shine

🌴 Pro Tips:
• Use coconut shell as applicator bowl
• Store leftovers in coconut bowl (7 days refrigerated)
• Add 1 tsp camellia oil for heat protection
• Apply before beach days for UV protection
