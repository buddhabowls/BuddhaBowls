Lemon Herb Quinoa Bowl

A fresh and vibrant Lemon Herb Quinoa Bowl packed with nutritious ingredients, perfect for a light meal or side dish.

Lemon Herb Quinoa Bowl Recipe

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 25 minutes

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1/2 cup zucchini, diced
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 2 tbsp lemon herb dressing
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions

  1. Rinse the quinoa under cold water and cook according to package instructions (usually about 15 minutes).
  2. While the quinoa is cooking, lightly sauté the diced zucchini in a pan over medium heat until tender, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. Once the quinoa is cooked, fluff it with a fork and let it cool slightly.
  4. In a bowl, layer the cooked quinoa with sautéed zucchini, halved cherry tomatoes, and chickpeas.
  5. Drizzle with lemon herb dressing and garnish with fresh basil before serving.

Nutrition

Calories: Approximately 350

Protein: 12g

Carbs: 55g

Fat: 10g

Fiber: 8g
