## Pitaya Energy Drink Recipe

**Prep Time:** 5 minutes

**Cook Time:** 0 minutes

**Total Time:** 10 minutes (including resting time)

**Servings:** 1

## Ingredients

- 1 tsp pink pitaya powder,1 cup coconut water,1/2 cup pineapple juice,1 tsp [chia seeds](https://amzn.to/4q7GcdC),1/2 tsp lime juice,Ice cubes

## Instructions

1. In a shaker bottle, combine pitaya powder, coconut water, and pineapple juice. 2. Shake vigorously until well mixed.
Add [chia seeds](https://amzn.to/4q7GcdC) and lime juice, shake again.
Pour over ice in a glass.
Let sit for 5 minutes to allow [chia seeds](https://amzn.to/4q7GcdC) to expand.
Stir before drinking.

## Notes

For a sparkling version, use carbonated coconut water. Add a sprig of fresh mint for an extra refreshing touch.
