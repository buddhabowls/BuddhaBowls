## Maca Powder Smoothie

**Course:** Breakfast

**Servings:** 1

### Ingredients:

– 1 banana –

1 cup almond milk –

1 tbsp maca powder –

1 tbsp [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke)

### Instructions:

1. Blend all ingredients until smooth.

2. Serve immediately.
