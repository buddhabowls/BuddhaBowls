## Herbal Infused Honey for Sweetness

Ingredients:

– Raw [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke)

– Dried herbs (like thyme or rosemary)

– A glass jar

Instructions:

1. Fill the jar with [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke) and herbs.

2. Seal and let it sit for a week.

3. Use it in tea or as a sweetener in your rituals.
