Buddha Bowl with Quinoa

A delicious and nutritious Buddha bowl packed with quinoa, roasted vegetables, and creamy tahini dressing.

🥗 Buddha Bowl with Quinoa & Tahini

Yield: 2 servings
Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 20 minutes
Total Time: 35 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup roasted sweet potato cubes
  • 1 cup roasted broccoli florets
  • 1/2 cup chickpeas, roasted
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1/4 cup tahini
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 clove garlic, minced

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C)
  2. Toss sweet potato and broccoli with olive oil, salt, and pepper
  3. Roast vegetables for 20 minutes until golden and tender
  4. Cook quinoa according to package directions
  5. Make tahini dressing by mixing tahini, lemon juice, garlic, and water
  6. Assemble bowls with spinach as base, then add quinoa and roasted vegetables
  7. Drizzle with tahini dressing and garnish with fresh herbs
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy!
