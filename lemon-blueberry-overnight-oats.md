## Lemon Blueberry Overnight Oats 

This recipe is a celebration of all things lemon and blueberry. It’s like a burst of sunshine in a bowl, and it never fails to brighten my day.

To create this tangy delight, I mix rolled oats with lemon yogurt and a generous helping of fresh blueberries. After a good stir, it’s time to let the fridge work its magic overnight.

In the morning, I serve it in a coconut bowl (because it makes me feel like I’m at a fancy brunch) and top it with lemon zest and fresh blueberries.

These Lemon Blueberry Overnight Oats are like a zesty hug for your taste buds, and they never fail to make my mornings a little brighter. So go ahead, treat yourself to a breakfast that’s as refreshing as you are!

## Lemon Blueberry Overnight Oats 

**Course:** Breakfast

**Prep Time:** 5 mins

**Cook Time:** 0 mins

**Total Time:** 5 mins

**Servings:** 1

## Ingredients

- Rolled oats, lemon yogurt, blueberries

## Instructions

- Combine oats, lemon yogurt, and blueberries. Refrigerate overnight. Top with lemon zest and fresh blueberries.
