## Maca Hot Chocolate

**Course:** Beverage

**Servings:** 2

### Ingredients:

- 2 cups milk

- 2 tbsp cocoa powder

- 1 tbsp maca powder

- 1-2 tbsp sugar

### Instructions:

- In a saucepan, heat milk over medium heat.

- Whisk in cocoa powder, maca powder, and sugar until well combined.

- Serve warm.
