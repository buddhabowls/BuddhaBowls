[ Print Recipe](https://buddhabowls.org/wprm_print/pink-pitaya-coconut-curry-sauce)## Pink Pitaya Coconut Curry Sauce Recipe

Are you ready to add a burst of exotic flavor to your meals? Look no further than this Pink Pitaya Coconut Curry Sauce recipe! 

This vibrant and delicious sauce is perfect for adding a touch of tropical flair to your favorite dishes. Whether you drizzle it over grilled chicken, toss it with veggies, or use it as a dipping sauce, this Pink Pitaya Coconut Curry Sauce is sure to elevate your culinary creations to a whole new level.

## Ingredients:

1 can (14 oz) coconut milk

1 tbsp pink pitaya powder

2 tbsp red curry paste

2 tbsp [Soy Sauce](https://amzn.to/48Vf7mR)

1 tbsp brown sugar

1 tbsp lime juice

2 cloves garlic, minced

1 inch piece of ginger, grated

Salt to taste

## Instructions:

1. In a saucepan, combine the coconut milk, pink pitaya powder, red curry paste, [Soy Sauce](https://amzn.to/48Vf7mR), brown sugar, lime juice, garlic, and ginger. Whisk until well combined.

2. Place the saucepan over medium heat and bring the mixture to a simmer. Allow it to simmer for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.

3. Taste the sauce and adjust the seasoning as needed, adding salt to taste.

4. Once the sauce has thickened slightly and the flavors have melded together, remove it from the heat and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
That’s it! You now have a luscious Pink Pitaya Coconut Curry Sauce ready to be enjoyed. The [Soy Sauce](https://amzn.to/48Vf7mR) and lime juice add a savory and tangy kick, while the brown sugar brings a hint of sweetness to balance out the flavors.

This versatile sauce can be used in a variety of ways. Pour it over grilled shrimp or tofu for a quick and easy weeknight dinner. Use it as a dipping sauce for spring rolls or dumplings for a fun appetizer. Toss it with cooked noodles or rice for a flavorful and satisfying meal. The possibilities are endless!

Pink pitaya, also known as dragon fruit, is rich in antioxidants and vitamin C, making it a great addition to your diet. Coconut milk is a good source of healthy fats and can help promote heart health. The garlic and ginger in the sauce provide immune-boosting properties, while the lime juice adds a dose of vitamin C.
