💪 Maca Protein Power Smoothie

🌱 Natural adaptogen boost | 🥛 25g protein | ⏱️ 5-minute prep

Designed for post-workout recovery, this smoothie combines muscle-repairing protein with maca's energy-boosting properties. Gluten-free and vegan-friendly when using plant-based protein.

📋 Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 1 tbsp organic maca powder
  • 1 cup fresh spinach
  • ½ tsp cinnamon (optional)

👨🍳 Preparation Steps

  1. Combine all base ingredients in high-speed blender
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy
  3. Adjust consistency with ice cubes as needed
  4. Serve immediately in chilled glass

⚖️ Nutrition Profile

  • Calories: 292 kcal
  • Protein: 25g (50% DV)
  • Carbs: 35g (12% DV)
  • Fiber: 5g (18% DV)
  • Iron: 15% DV (from spinach)
