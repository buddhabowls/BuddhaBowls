How to Make It (a.k.a. Breakfast Bliss in 5 Minutes)

  1. Blend like a boss: Toss the frozen acai packets, banana, almond milk, and half of the mixed berries into a blender. Hit that blend button and watch as your kitchen transforms into a tropical paradise. Pro tip: If your blender is struggling, give it a pep talk or add a splash more almond milk.
  2. Pour and admire: Once your acai mixture reaches that perfect thick-and-creamy consistency (think soft-serve ice cream), pour it into your coconut bowl. Pause for a moment to admire your handiwork. You're basically Picasso with a blender.
  3. Top it like you mean it: Now comes the fun part—toppings! Artfully arrange the remaining mixed berries, granola, almond butter, chia seeds, and honey on top of your acai base. Or just throw them on there haphazardly; it'll taste amazing either way.

Acai Granola Breakfast Bowl Recipe

Course: Brunch
Cuisine: Brazilian-inspired
Servings: 2
Prep Time: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 frozen acai packets
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1 cup mixed berries
  • 1/2 cup granola
  • 2 tbsp almond butter
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 tbsp honey

Instructions

  1. Blend acai, banana, and almond milk until smooth and creamy.
  2. Pour the acai mixture into bowls.
  3. Top with mixed berries, granola, almond butter, chia seeds, and a drizzle of honey.
  4. Serve immediately and enjoy!
Pro Tips:

Add protein powder for a more filling meal. Use coconut milk for a tropical flavor. Prepare ingredients the night before for a quick morning blend.
