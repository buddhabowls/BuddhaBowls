## Herbal Smudging for Clarity

Ingredients:

– Dried rosemary

– Dried sage

– A fire-safe bowl

Instructions:

1. Light the dried herbs until they smolder.

2. Waft the smoke around your workspace.

3. Visualize clarity and focus entering your mind.
