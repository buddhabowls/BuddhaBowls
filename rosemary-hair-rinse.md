## Rosemary Hair Rinse

### Ingredients:

- Rosemary essential oil

- Apple cider vinegar

- Water

### Instructions:

- Mix 1 cup of apple cider vinegar with 10 drops of rosemary essential oil and 2 cups of water.

- After shampooing, pour the mixture over your hair and rinse thoroughly.
