## Minty Fresh Tub Tea

### Ingredients:

- Dried peppermint, Epsom salt, Baking soda, Lemon oil (optional)

### Instructions:

- 1. Combine 1 cup of dried peppermint with 1/2 cup of Epsom salt and 1/4 cup of baking soda. 2. If desired, add a few drops of lemon oil. 3. Place in a muslin bag and hang under the faucet. 4. Soak for 20-30 minutes.
