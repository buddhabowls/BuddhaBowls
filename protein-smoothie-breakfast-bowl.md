  - 1 cup almond milk
  - 1/2 banana
  - 1/4 cup strawberries
  - 1 tbsp chia seeds

  - Blend protein powder, almond milk, and banana until smooth.
  - Pour into a bowl and top with strawberries and chia seeds.
