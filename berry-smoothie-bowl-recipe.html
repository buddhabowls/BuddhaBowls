Berry Smoothie Bowl: A Deliciously Fun Start to Your Day!

Kickstart your morning with a burst of berry goodness! This Berry Smoothie Bowl is not just a treat for your taste buds but also a feast for your eyes. Packed with nutrients and topped with your favorite goodies, it's the perfect blend of health and happiness!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen mixed berries
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup almond milk (or milk of choice)
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup (optional)
  • Toppings: granola, fresh berries, nuts, seeds, coconut flakes

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine the frozen mixed berries, banana, Greek yogurt, and almond milk.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy. Adjust the consistency with more milk if needed.
  3. Pour the smoothie into a bowl.
  4. Top with your choice of granola, fresh berries, nuts, seeds, and coconut flakes.
  5. Enjoy your beautiful and nutritious Berry Smoothie Bowl!

Course & Cuisine

Course: Breakfast

Cuisine: Healthy, Vegan-Friendly

Nutrition Information

Calories: Approximately 250 kcal per serving

Protein: 10g

Fat: 5g

Carbohydrates: 45g

Fiber: 7g
