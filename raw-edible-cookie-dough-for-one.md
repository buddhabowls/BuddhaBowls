## Homemade Edible Cookie Dough for One: A Quick and Easy Recipe

Craving a sweet treat but don’t want to spend hours baking a whole batch of cookies? Look no further! This recipe for raw edible cookie dough for one is the perfect solution. With just a few simple ingredients and a couple of minutes, you can satisfy your sweet tooth with this delicious and safe-to-eat cookie dough.

## Raw Edible Cookie Dough For One

### Ingredients:

- – 2 tbsp unsalted butter, softened

- – 2 tbsp brown sugar

- – 1/4 tsp pure [Vanilla Extract](https://amzn.to/4aIhdue)

- – 1/4 cup all-purpose flour

- – Pinch of salt

- – 2 tbsp mini chocolate chips

### Instructions:

- In a small bowl, cream together the softened butter and brown sugar until smooth.

- Add the [Vanilla Extract](https://amzn.to/4aIhdue) and mix until well combined.

- Gradually add the flour and salt, stirring until a dough forms.

- Gently fold in the mini chocolate chips until evenly distributed throughout the dough.

***That’s it! Your raw edible cookie dough for one is ready to enjoy.***

### Tips:

- If you’re concerned about consuming raw flour, you can heat treat it in the microwave before using it in this recipe. Simply spread the flour out on a microwave-safe plate and heat on high for 1 minute, stirring halfway through. Allow the flour to cool completely before using in the recipe.

- Feel free to customize your cookie dough with your favorite mix-ins such as sprinkles, nuts, or candy pieces.

- This recipe can easily be doubled or tripled if you want to make enough for a larger serving or to share with friends.

So next time you’re in need of a quick and delicious sweet fix, whip up a batch of this raw edible cookie dough for one. It’s the perfect single-serving treat that’s sure to satisfy your cravings without the need for baking an entire batch of cookies. 

***Happy snacking!***

This Coconut Flour Cookie Dough is completely edible when raw… no oven required.
Most raw edible cookie dough recipes call for all-purpose flour that needs to be heat treated before it is safe for our stomachs to consume raw. 
When you make cookie dough with coconut flour, you don’t even have to heat treat the flour!

Using coconut flour turns raw cookie dough into a delicious gluten-free treat.
