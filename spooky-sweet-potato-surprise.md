## Spooky Sweet Potato Surprise

Surprise your taste buds with this Spooky Sweet Potato Surprise! Packed with sweet and savory ingredients, it’s a fun way to enjoy a healthy meal.

## Spooky Sweet Potato Surprise Recipe

**Course:** Lunch

**Cuisine:** Healthy

**Servings:** 2

### Ingredients

- 1 cup roasted sweet potatoes,

- 1 cup quinoa,

- 1/2 cup black beans,

- 1/4 cup [Salsa](https://amzn.to/4aIhdue),

- 1/4 cup avocado

### Instructions

- Roast sweet potatoes until tender.

- Cook quinoa according to package instructions.

- Combine all ingredients in a coconut bowl.

Add a dollop of Greek yogurt for creaminess!
