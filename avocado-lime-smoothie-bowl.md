Enjoy a refreshing and nutritious Avocado Lime Smoothie Bowl that’s perfect for breakfast or a quick snack. This smoothie bowl is packed with healthy fats and vibrant flavors!

## Avocado Lime Smoothie Bowl Recipe

## Ingredients

- 1/2 ripe avocado

- 1 frozen banana

- 1/4 cup fresh lime juice

- 1/2 cup almond milk

- Lime zest and sliced banana for topping

## Instructions

- In a [Blender](https://amzn.to/4oSJQHl), combine the avocado, frozen banana, lime juice, and almond milk.

- Blend until smooth and creamy.

- Pour the smoothie into a bowl.

- Top with lime zest and sliced banana for added flavor and presentation.

## Tips for the Perfect Smoothie Bowl

For an extra creamy texture, ensure your banana is fully frozen. You can also add spinach or kale for a nutrient boost without altering the flavor significantly.
