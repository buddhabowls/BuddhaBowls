## Nut Butter Banana Bread

**Course:** Dessert

**Cuisine:** American

**Servings:** 1 loaf

### Ingredients:

- 2 ripe bananas

- 1/2 cup nut butter

- 1/2 cup sugar

- 1 egg

### Instructions:

- Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).

- Mix all ingredients until combined.

- Pour into a loaf pan and bake for 60 minutes.

NOTES: Great for breakfast!
