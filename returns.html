Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned due to their nature:

Engraved Items

Why? Engraved and personalized items are custom-made specifically for you. Once engraved, they cannot be resold or returned.

Tip: Please review your engraving design carefully before confirming your order. We offer a design preview before production.

Gift Cards

Why? Digital and physical gift cards cannot be returned or refunded once purchased.

Exception: If a gift card was purchased in error, contact us within 24 hours for a one-time exception.

Sale/Clearance Items

Why? Items marked as "Final Sale" or from clearance sections are sold as-is and cannot be returned.

Exception: If the item arrives damaged, we'll replace it at no cost.

Used/Washed Items

Why? Items that have been used, washed, or show signs of wear cannot be returned for hygiene reasons.

Exception: If there's a defect, we may offer a replacement or partial refund.

Return Conditions

To qualify for a full refund, returned items must meet the following conditions:

Unused & Unwashed

Items must not have been used or washed. They should be in the exact condition as received.

Original Packaging

Items must be in original packaging with all tags, labels, and documentation intact.

Within 30 Days

Returns must be initiated within 30 days of your purchase date. After 30 days, returns cannot be accepted.

Original Receipt

Include your order number or original receipt with your return. This helps us process your refund quickly.

No Damage from Misuse

Items damaged due to misuse, accidents, or improper care cannot be returned for a full refund.

All Components Included

All original components, accessories, and packaging materials must be included in the return.

Special Cases & Exceptions

What if I want to return an engraved item?

Engraved items are custom-made and cannot be returned. However, if the engraving is incorrect due to our error, please contact us immediately. We may offer a replacement or partial refund depending on the situation.

Can I return an item after 30 days?

Our standard return window is 30 days. However, if there's a defect or quality issue, we may make an exception. Please contact us to discuss your specific situation.

What if I used the item once?

Items that have been used cannot be returned for a full refund due to hygiene reasons. However, if there's a defect or quality issue, we may offer a partial refund or replacement. Contact us to discuss.

What about bulk or wholesale orders?

Bulk and wholesale orders have different return policies. Please contact our sales team at sales@buddhabowls.com to discuss return terms for your specific order.

Can I return items purchased on sale?

Items marked as "Final Sale" cannot be returned. However, regular sale items can be returned within 30 days if they meet all return conditions. Check your order confirmation to see if an item is marked as "Final Sale."

What if I received the wrong item?

If you received the wrong item, contact us immediately. We'll send the correct item right away at no cost. You can return the incorrect item using our prepaid label, and we'll cover all costs.
