🌾 Maca Savory Quinoa Bowl

🥄 30min Total | 🥑 320 kcal | 🌱 Vegan

This ancient grain bowl combines maca's adaptogenic properties with quinoa's complete protein profile. Perfect for meal prep or post-workout recovery.

🏔️ Andean Ingredients

Base

Tri-color quinoa
Vegetable broth

Veggies

Sweet potato
Spinach
Tomatoes

Toppings

Avocado
Pepitas
Lime

👩🍳 Cooking Protocol

  1. Toast quinoa to enhance nutty flavor
  2. Steam with broth for optimal absorption
  3. Temper maca powder with warm oil
  4. Layer components for visual appeal
  5. Finish with bright citrus acidity

⚖️ Nutritional Profile

  • Complete protein (9 essential amino acids)
  • High in manganese & magnesium
  • 3:1 omega-6 to omega-3 ratio
  • Gluten-free & dairy-free

🧪 Food Science Notes

  • Maca's maltol content enhances savory umami
  • Quinoa saponins aid mineral absorption
  • Vitamin C in lime boosts iron uptake
  • Healthy fats optimize maca's benefits
