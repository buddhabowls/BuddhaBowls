## Nut Butter Fruit Salad

**Course:** Salad

**Cuisine:** American

**Prep Time:**

**Servings:** 4 servings

### Ingredients:

- 2 cups mixed fruits

- 1/4 cup nut butter

- 1 tbsp [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke)

### Instructions:

- In a bowl, mix nut butter and [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke).

- Toss with mixed fruits.

NOTES: Great for summer!
