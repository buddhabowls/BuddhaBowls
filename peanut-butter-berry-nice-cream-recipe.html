Peanut Butter Berry Nice Cream

A refreshing and creamy Peanut Butter Berry Nice Cream made with frozen bananas and mixed berries, perfect for a healthy dessert!

Peanut Butter Berry Nice Cream Recipe

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Freeze Time: 6 hours (overnight recommended)

Total Time: 6 hours 10 minutes

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 cup mixed berries (fresh or frozen)

Instructions

  1. Slice the bananas and freeze them overnight.
  2. Place the frozen bananas in a blender.
  3. Add the peanut butter and mixed berries.
  4. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  5. Serve immediately for a soft texture or freeze for a firmer texture.

Nutrition

Calories: Approximately 320

Carbs: 50g

Fat: 10g
