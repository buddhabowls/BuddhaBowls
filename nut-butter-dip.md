## Nut Butter Dip

**Course:** Snack

**Cuisine:** American

**Servings:** 1 cup

### Ingredients:

- 1 cup nut butter

- 1/2 cup yogurt

- 1 tbsp [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke)

### Instructions:

- Mix all ingredients until smooth.

- Serve with fruits or veggies.

NOTES: Great for parties!
