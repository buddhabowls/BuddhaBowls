## Mindful Peanut Butter Coconut Oil Fudge (Zero-Waste Recipe)

## Why This Fudge Belongs in Your Buddha Bowl Lifestyle

- 5-ingredient simplicity

- Coconut oil base supports ethical harvesting

- ♻️ Plastic-free preparation

- ⏱ 10-minute active time

## Eco-Conscious Ingredients

1. 1 cup organic peanut butter (smooth, no added oil)
2. ½ cup virgin coconut oil (fair-trade certified)
3. ¼ cup [Maple Syrup](https://amzn.to/3KUS5oa) (sustainably tapped)
4. 1 tsp [Vanilla Extract](https://amzn.to/4aIhdue) (alcohol-free)
5. Pinch of Himalayan pink salt

## Earth-Friendly Preparation

- **Melting Ritual**
Combine all ingredients in a glass bowl over double boiler

- **Mindful Mixing**
Whisk until silky smooth (2-3 minutes)

- **Sustainable Setting**
Pour into silicone mold or upcycled container

- **Cold Meditation**
Freeze 1 hour vs refrigerate 3 hours

## Serving Suggestions

- Break into chunks over oatmeal in [Coconut Bowls](https://buddhabowls.org/product/coconut-bowls/)

- Gift in handmade banana leaf wraps

- Pair with meditation tea ceremony

## Storage Wisdom

- Keep in airtight glass jar

- Stable at room temp up to 72°F

- Lasts 2 weeks refrigerated
