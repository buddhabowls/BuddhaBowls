Lemon Basil Quinoa Bowl

A refreshing and vibrant Lemon Basil Quinoa Bowl that combines fresh vegetables and a zesty dressing, perfect for a light meal.

Lemon Basil Quinoa Bowl Recipe

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 25 minutes

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup zucchini, diced
  • 1/4 cup yellow squash, diced
  • 1/2 avocado, sliced
  • 2 tbsp lemon basil dressing

Instructions

  1. Rinse the quinoa under cold water and cook according to package instructions (usually about 15 minutes).
  2. While the quinoa is cooking, sauté the diced zucchini and yellow squash in a pan over medium heat until tender, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Once the quinoa is cooked, fluff it with a fork and let it cool slightly.
  4. In a bowl, layer the cooked quinoa with halved cherry tomatoes, sautéed zucchini, yellow squash, and sliced avocado.
  5. Drizzle with lemon basil dressing before serving.

Nutrition

Calories: Approximately 400

Protein: 12g

Carbs: 60g

Fat: 15g

Fiber: 10g
