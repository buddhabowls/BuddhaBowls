We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us recognize you and remember your preferences. We use cookies for:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for Site functionality and security
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how you use our Site
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Marketing Cookies: Track your activity for targeted advertising

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent. However, blocking cookies may affect Site functionality.

Tracking Technologies

We also use web beacons, pixels, and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Track user behavior and engagement
  • Measure campaign effectiveness
  • Personalize content and recommendations
  • Prevent fraud and enhance security
