## Spicy Peanut Butter

**Course:** Spread

**Cuisine:** American

**Servings:** 1 cup

### Ingredients:

- 2 cups raw peanuts

- 1/2 tsp salt

- 1 tbsp sriracha

### Instructions:

- Roast peanuts at 350°F (175°C) for 10-15 minutes.

- Let cool, then blend in a food processor until creamy.

- Add salt and sriracha; blend until combined.

NOTES: Great for savory dishes!
