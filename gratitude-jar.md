Fill your life with gratitude, one note at a time!

🙏 Gratitude in a Jar! 🙏 Start collecting gratitude notes in a jar! Write down what you’re thankful for and read them at the end of the month for a positivity boost! 🌈✨ #Gratitude #PositiveThinking

Ingredients:

– A jar

– Colored paper

– A pen

Instructions:

1. Write down things you’re grateful for on paper.

2. Fold and place them in the jar.

3. Review them at the end of the month for a positivity boost.
