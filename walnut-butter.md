## Walnut Butter

**Course:** Spread

**Cuisine:** American

**Prep Time:**

**Servings:** 1 cup

### Ingredients:

- 2 cups walnuts

- 1/2 tsp salt

- 1 tbsp [Maple Syrup](https://amzn.to/3KUS5oa) (optional)

### Instructions:

- Roast walnuts at 350°F (175°C) for 8-10 minutes.

- Let cool, then blend in a food processor until smooth.

- Add salt and [Maple Syrup](https://amzn.to/3KUS5oa) if desired.

NOTES: Perfect for drizzling over pancakes.
