## Affirmation Bath Bombs

Ingredients:

– Baking soda

– Citric acid

– Essential oils

– A mold

Instructions:

1. Mix baking soda and citric acid in a bowl.

2. Add essential oils and mold the mixture into bath bombs.

3. Write an affirmation on each bomb and let them dry.
