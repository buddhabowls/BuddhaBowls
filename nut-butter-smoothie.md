## Nut Butter Smoothie

**Course:** Breakfast

**Cuisine:** American

**Prep Time:**

**Servings:** 1

### Ingredients:

- 1 banana

- 1 tbsp nut butter

- 1 cup almond milk

- 1 tbsp [Honey](https://amzn.to/44rhrke)

### Instructions:

- Blend all ingredients until smooth.

- Serve immediately.

NOTES: Add spinach for extra nutrients.
