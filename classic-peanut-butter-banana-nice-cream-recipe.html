Classic Peanut Butter Banana Nice Cream

A delicious and healthy dessert made from frozen bananas blended with creamy peanut butter, perfect for a guilt-free treat!

Classic Peanut Butter Banana Nice Cream Recipe

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Freeze Time: 6 hours (overnight recommended)

Total Time: 6 hours 10 minutes

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Slice the bananas and freeze them overnight.
  2. Place the frozen bananas in a blender.
  3. Add the peanut butter and vanilla extract.
  4. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  5. Serve immediately for a soft texture or freeze for a firmer texture.

Nutrition

Calories: Approximately 300

Protein: 6g

Carbs: 45g

Fat: 12g

Fiber: 4g
