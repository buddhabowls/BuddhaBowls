Coconut Oil & Aloe Vera Hair Mask

🌱 Biological Synergy:
• Aloe's pH 5.5 matches healthy scalp
• Coconut oil reduces protein loss by 38%
• Fenugreek contains hormone precursors
• Peppermint increases blood flow (+27% oxygenation*)

🌿 Formula Components:

  • ½ cup (120ml) fresh aloe gel
  • 3 tbsp (45ml) coconut oil
  • 1 tbsp (15g) fenugreek powder
  • Coconut shell + bamboo spatula
  • Ice globes (post-treatment)

🧊 Application Protocol:

  1. Harvest aloe gel directly into coconut shell
  2. Whip coconut oil into gel using figure-8 motion
  3. Add fenugreek and essential oils last
  4. Apply using three-zone technique:
    • Scalp: Part hair 1cm apart with tint brush
    • Lengths: Use praying hands application
    • Ends: Dip-wrap in leftover mixture
  5. Cover with silk wrap (25-30 minutes)
  6. Rinse with cold hibiscus tea rinse
  7. Finish with ice globe massage

💧 Customization Matrix:
• Oily scalp: Add 1 tsp bentonite clay
• Dry hair: Mix with 1 tbsp honey
• Color-treated: Use chamomile tea base
• Fine hair: Substitute 1 tbsp grapeseed oil
• Storage: 5 days refrigerated in coconut shell
