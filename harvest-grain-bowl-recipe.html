Harvest Grain Bowl

A nutritious and delicious Harvest Grain Bowl filled with wholesome ingredients, perfect for a healthy lunch or dinner.

Harvest Grain Bowl Recipe

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Total Time: 45 minutes

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 cup farro
  • 1/4 cup roasted butternut squash
  • 1/4 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/4 cup pecans, chopped
  • 1/2 apple, sliced
  • 2 tbsp maple vinaigrette

Instructions

  1. Rinse the farro under cold water and cook according to package instructions (usually about 30 minutes).
  2. While the farro is cooking, roast the butternut squash in the oven at 400°F (200°C) for about 20 minutes, or until tender.
  3. Once the farro is cooked, fluff it with a fork and let it cool slightly.
  4. In a bowl, layer the cooked farro with roasted butternut squash, dried cranberries, chopped pecans, and apple slices.
  5. Drizzle with maple vinaigrette before serving and enjoy!

Nutrition

Calories: Approximately 450

Protein: 12g

Carbs: 75g

Fat: 15g

Fiber: 10g
