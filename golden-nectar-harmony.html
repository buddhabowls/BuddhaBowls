Coconut Oil & Honey Moisturizer

🌞 Solar Activation Tip:
Prepare at high noon when sunlight maximizes honey's enzymatic activity. The coconut bowl naturally filters UV rays while preserving beneficial properties.

🍯 Sacred Ingredients:

  • ¼ cup raw coconut oil
  • 2 tbsp manuka honey
  • 1 tsp royal jelly
  • 5 drops chamomile oil
  • Coconut bowl + wooden dipper
  • Amber apothecary jar

🌻 Creation Ceremony:

  1. Warm coconut bowl in sunlight for 10 minutes
  2. Layer ingredients like sun rays - oil first, then honey
  3. Stir clockwise 21 times with intention-setting
  4. Add royal jelly during golden hour light
  5. Whisk until achieving "liquid amber" consistency
  6. Store in jar wrapped in silk cloth
  7. Bless with gratitude before first use
💫 Multi-Use Glow Guide:
• Morning: Thin layer under makeup
• Noon: Lip treatment during lunch
• Evening: Massage mask before shower
• Weekend: Hair-end sealant
• Special: Cuticle renewal soak
