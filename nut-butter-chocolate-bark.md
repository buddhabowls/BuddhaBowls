## Nut Butter Chocolate Bark

**Course:** Dessert

**Cuisine:** American

**Prep Time:**

**Servings:** 1 batch

### Ingredients:

- 1 cup dark chocolate

- 1/2 cup nut butter

- 1/4 cup nuts

### Instructions:

- Melt chocolate and stir in nut butter.

- Spread on a baking sheet and sprinkle with nuts.

- Refrigerate until set.

NOTES: Great for a sweet treat!
