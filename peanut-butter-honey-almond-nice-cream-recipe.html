Peanut Butter Honey Almond Nice Cream Recipe

Overview

Servings: 2

Course: Dessert

Cuisine: American

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Freezing Time: Overnight

Nutritional Information

Calories: 220 per serving

Protein: 5g

Fat: 9g

Carbohydrates: 34g

Sugar: 14g

Fiber: 3g

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1/4 cup sliced almonds

Instructions

  1. Slice bananas and freeze them overnight.
  2. Place frozen bananas in a blender.
  3. Add peanut butter and honey.
  4. Blend until smooth.
  5. Stir in sliced almonds.
  6. Serve immediately or freeze for a firmer texture.
