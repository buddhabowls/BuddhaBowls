Curried Lentil Quinoa Bowl

A flavorful and nutritious Curried Lentil Quinoa Bowl that combines hearty ingredients for a satisfying meal.

Curried Lentil Quinoa Bowl Recipe

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 25 minutes

Total Time: 35 minutes

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1/2 cup lentils, rinsed
  • 1/4 cup spinach, chopped
  • 1/4 cup roasted chickpeas
  • 2 tbsp curry sauce
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Rinse the quinoa and lentils under cold water. Cook the quinoa according to package instructions (usually about 15 minutes) and the lentils (about 20 minutes) until tender.
  2. While the quinoa and lentils are cooking, roast the chickpeas in the oven at 400°F (200°C) for about 15 minutes, tossing them with curry sauce.
  3. Once the quinoa and lentils are cooked, fluff the quinoa with a fork and combine them in a bowl.
  4. Layer the cooked quinoa with lentils, chopped spinach, and roasted chickpeas.
  5. Drizzle with extra curry sauce and garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.

Nutrition

Calories: Approximately 480

Protein: 18g

Carbs: 75g
