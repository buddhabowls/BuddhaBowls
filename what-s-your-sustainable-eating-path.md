**What’s Your Sustainable Eating Path?**

## Discover Your Inner Eco-Foodie Persona!

Are you a kale-loving crusader or a pizza-powered planet protector? Let’s find out! Take this quiz to see where you stand in the wild world of sustainable eating. Warning: Results may cause excessive smugness at your next farmer’s market trip.

**Question 1:**
**You’re at the grocery store, and you spot a shiny, imported mango from halfway across the world. What do you do?
**A) Gasp dramatically and whisper, “Food miles, you monster!” before walking away.
B) Buy it, but only if it’s organic. You care… just not *that* much.
C) Grab it unapologetically. Life’s short, and mangoes are delicious.

**Question 2:**
**Your friend invites you to a BBQ, but the menu is all burgers and hot dogs. What’s your move?
**A) Show up with your own tofu patties and a PowerPoint presentation on methane emissions.
B) Eat the burger but spend the entire time talking about how you “usually” eat plant-based.
C) Devour the hot dog while silently thanking the pig for its noble sacrifice.

**Question 3:**
**You’re planning dinner. What’s on the menu?
**A) A locally-sourced quinoa bowl with veggies you grew in your backyard. #humblebrag
B) Pasta with jarred sauce because it’s quick, but you’ll sprinkle some fresh basil on top to feel fancy.
C) Whatever’s in the freezer. If it’s edible and doesn’t bite back, it’s dinner.

**Question 4:**
**How do you feel about food waste?
**A) Waste? What waste? I compost everything, including my ex’s love letters.
B) I try to use leftovers, but sometimes that half-eaten avocado just… dies.
C) I don’t feel bad about food waste because my dog eats everything I don’t.

**Question 5:**
**What’s your dream vacation?
**A) A farm-to-table retreat where you milk goats and learn to churn butter by hand.
B) A city with amazing restaurants that serve “sustainable” options (whatever that means).
C) A cruise with unlimited buffets because YOLO.

## What’s Your Sustainable Eating Path?

**Results:**

**Mostly A’s:** *The Green Guru* 
You’re basically a walking TED Talk on sustainable eating. You can name five different types of lentils off the top of your head and probably have a reusable straw in your pocket right now. People both admire and fear your dedication to saving the planet.

**Mostly B’s:** *The Casual Conservationist* 
You care about the environment… as long as it doesn’t interfere with your Netflix binge. You’re doing your best, but hey, nobody’s perfect. Keep sipping that oat latte and composting when you remember!

**Mostly C’s:** *The “Eff-it” Eater* 
Sustainable eating? Sounds cool, but you’ve got bigger fish to fry (literally). You might not be saving the planet single-handedly, but at least you’re honest about your love for convenience and cheese fries.

So, what’s your sustainable eating path? Whatever it is, don’t forget: every small step helps… even if that step is just eating one less burger a week. Now go forth and conquer your next meal, eco-warrior (or eco-occasionalist)!
