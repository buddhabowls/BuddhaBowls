## Pitaya Protein Shake Recipe

**Prep Time:** 5 minutes

**Cook Time:** 0 minutes

**Total Time:** 5 minutes

**Servings:** 1

## Ingredients

- 1 scoop vanilla protein powder,

- 1 tbsp pink pitaya powder,

- 1 frozen banana,

- 1 cup unsweetened almond milk,

- 1 tbsp [Almond Butter](https://amzn.to/4oN8Mj6),

- 1 cup spinach leaves,

- Ice cubes

## Instructions

- Add all ingredients to a [Blender](https://amzn.to/4oSJQHl).

- Blend on high speed until smooth and creamy.

- Add more almond milk or ice for desired consistency.

- Pour into a glass or coconut bowl and serve immediately.

## Notes

- Use chocolate protein powder for a different flavor profile.

- Add a handful of oats for extra fiber and sustained energy release.
